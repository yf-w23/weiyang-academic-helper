课程简介：
本课程以动画制作的流程为线索，对动作捕捉技术、Motionbuilder软件以及动作捕捉设备操作等进行讲解，不仅全面介绍了动作捕捉制作的流程，并且还详细讲解了动作捕捉分支技术以及相关前沿应用领域。 课程理论教学主要内容包括三部分：第一部分详细介绍动作捕捉技术的基本情况、Motionbuilder软件的基础知识、动作捕捉制作动画的流程；第二部分依次介绍了面部捕捉和眼动捕捉技术；第三部分介绍了动作捕捉在虚拟人以及科研领域的应用场景； 课程实践教学围绕将同学们的大作业进行，同学们运用所学理论知识，使用动作捕捉实验室中的设备及软件完成一部互动媒体创意作品。
英文课程简介：
his course, following the process of animation production, provides a comprehensive and detailed explanation of motion capture technology, Motionbuilder software, and operation of motion capture equipment. It not only thoroughly introduces the process of motion capture production but also delves into the sub-technologies of motion capture and related cutting-edge application fields. The theoretical teaching of the course comprises three main parts: the first part details the basics of motion capture technology, foundational knowledge of Motionbuilder software, and the process of creating animations with motion capture. The second part sequentially introduces facial capture and eye-tracking technologies. The third part discusses the applications of motion capture in virtual humans and scientific research fields. The practical teaching of the course revolves around students' major projects. Utilizing the theoretical knowledge acquired, students use the equipment and software in the motion capture laboratory to complete an interactive media creative work
