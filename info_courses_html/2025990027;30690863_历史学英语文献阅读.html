课程内容简介：
1.本课程设计目标是提高英文历史文献阅读的能力，培养从不同角度看待文献和面对文献提问题的能力。 2.本课程将以犹太文明自古至今的历史文献为主线，兼及与犹太文明有互动的其他文明的历史文献。一个重要原因在于犹太文明自古至今一直在与周遭的其他文明，尤其是古代近东、希腊罗马、基督教、伊斯兰教、欧洲、美国和中国文明互动。以犹太文明的历史文献为阅读主线，可以生动反映出世界历史上文明交流与互鉴的基本模式。 3.涉及不同的文类，比如正典、评注、重述、翻译、铭文、法典、仪轨文、寓言、自传、书信、诗歌等。
Course Description
1. This course is designed to enhance the capability to read historical texts in English, particularly the capability to examine the texts from different perspectives and to formulate questions about them. 2. The course will focus on the historical texts of Jewish civilization from antiquity to the present day, as well as on the historical texts of other civilizations that (have) interacted with Jewish civilization. An important reason for this is that Jewish civilization has interacted with other civilizations around it from time immemorial, with the civilizations of the ancient Near East, Greeks and Romans, Christianity, Islam, Europe, the United States, and China in particular. Taking the texts of Jewish civilization as the main line for the course will vividly reveal the basic patterns of inter-civilizational exchanges and mutual learning in world history. 3. Different genres will be covered, such as canonical texts, commentaries, rewritings, translations, inscriptions, codes, liturgical texts, parables, autobiographies, epistles, poems, and so on.
