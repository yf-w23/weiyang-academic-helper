课程内容简介：
本课程指导学生阅读五部莎士比亚戏剧，了解莎士比亚时期的舞台传统和文化氛围。课程核心是对莎士比亚戏剧作品原文的解析和讨论；教学内容以通读原文、讨论作品主题与人物、研读重点选段、理解戏剧手法为主，辅之以多媒体材料和前代文学评论。课程鼓励学生直接欣赏原味的莎剧，寻找每人与莎士比亚这位诗人戏剧家之间真实的联系，形成自己独特的理解并形之于创意项目。
Course Description
This course introduces students to five or six Shakespearean plays and acquaints them with the stage conventions and cultural trends of Shakespeare’s time. Supported by major critical interpretations and multimedia resources. Students are encouraged to build their own connections with the Bard through discussion, performance, presentation, and creative and academic writing. Teaching goals: 1. Students will conduct close reading and critical analysis of Shakespeare’s plays, examining their artistic techniques and exploring the relationship between the works and the socio-historical context of the Elizabethan and Jacobean eras. 2. Students will produce a creative work—such as a theatrical adaptation, artistic project, or multimedia piece—that expresses a contemporary and innovative interpretation of the plays through a personal and original artistic form. 3. Students will carry out an independent research project, which includes conducting a literature review, defining a research topic, developing a proposal and outline, and writing a formal academic paper. This process aims to cultivate in-depth analytical skills and critical independent thinking regarding Shakespeare’s drama and its cultural contexts.
进度安排：

Prelude：Introduction, theatre-going in Shakespeare’s time;
Act 1 Justice and Mercy:The Merchant of Venice,comedy, the relevance of Shakespeare, Shakespeare’s time;
Act 2 Love and Identity: Twelfth Night,As You Like It, identity, show and truth, discord and harmony; the poly-functionality of dramatic language
Mid-term project: Shakespeare and I;
Act 3 Pain and Humanity: King Lear,tragedy, discord and harmony, humanity;
Act 4 Cruelty and Conscience: Macbeth, witchcraft, imagery;
Unit 5 Shakespeare Revisited: Shakespeare in other cultures or/ and the afterlife of Shakespeare;
Epilogue: Final exam / tutorship to get you ready for the research paper
