课程简介：
课程主要讲述金融投资交易和风险管理有关基础知识，让学生了解投资交易主体和标的、投资交易机制、投资交易环境、金融市场风险的基本概念、风险管理的步骤和主要方法。
英文课程简介：
The course mainly introduces the basic knowledge of financial investment transaction and risk management, so that students can understand the main body and object of investment transaction, investment transaction mechanism, investment transaction environment, basic concepts of financial market risk, steps and main methods of risk management.
