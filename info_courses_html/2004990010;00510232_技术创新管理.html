课程内容简介：
随着信息时代的到来，各种各样的新产品层出不穷，产业的生命周期越来越短，技术创新的步伐日益加快。这种技术环境动态的加剧给企业的可持续发展既带来了威胁，也孕育着机会。如何在不断变化的技术环境中保持竞争优势是企业管理人员和技术人员所需要关注和考虑的，而如何在技术演变和变迁的过程中寻找机会也是未来创业者非常关心的问题。本课程面向未来的企业高层管理人员，通过讲授技术创新和技术战略的基本理论，结合国内外企业技术创新的实践和案例，从企业战略的高度，探讨针对外部技术环境变迁的因应之道。 通过本课程的学习，希望学生能够了解创新的基本概念，并熟悉技术创新管理的基本内容。 主要授课内容包括以下几个方面：创新的基本概念、分类和模式；创新的源泉和技术扩散；创新与产业的演变；标准之争、主导设计与企业生存；技术创新与企业战略；创新的盈利模式；进入时机；创新项目的选择；中国企业的技术获取战略；如何保护创新和从创新中获利；如何部署创新战略等。 说明： 本课程分中文讲授（课号为：00510232-91）和英文讲授（课号为：00510232-92）两个版本，相应的作业和报告的要求也分别为中文和英文，请同学们选课时注意区分。
Course Description
The aim of this course is to examine the driving forces affecting the nature and pace of technological changes and discuss managerial challenges presented by technological innovations. It is designed to provide students with a framework for analyzing industry dynamics of technological innovation, and help students understand how to formulate technological innovation strategy at firm level. Students who are interested in founding technology-based ventures, managing firms with significant technical content, or working for consulting firms / venture capitals in the future will find this course particularly useful. This is a 2-credit course and so we will meet two hours each week. The course consists of two parts. During the first eight weeks we will place an emphasis on analytic frameworks for managing technological innovation. Main topics to be covered in this part includes: Definition of innovation; Types and Patterns of innovation; Source of innovation; Diffusion of innovation; Standards battles and Dominant Design; Industry life cycle and timing of entry. For the rest weeks, we will discuss strategic and managerial challenges involved in managing various aspects of the innovation process at firm level. Important topics includes: Business strategy and technology strategy; Choosing innovation project; Protecting innovation; Collaboration Strategies; Organizing for innovation; Technical Entrepreneurship. During the course, particular consideration will be given to cases, lessons and experiences that are relevant to Chinese domestic firms.
