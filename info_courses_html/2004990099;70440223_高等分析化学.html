课程简介：
本课程由最前沿的分析化学方法学及最先进的分析化学应用技术两大部分组成，由化学系分析化学研究所一线教学科研的老师组成教学团队，内容共由8章：第1章 绪论；第2章 高灵敏度检测技术；第3章 动态单分子成像；第4章 传感分析；第5章 单细胞代谢物质谱分析；第6章 材料分析化学；第7章 生命分析化学；第8章 微流控分析。
英文课程简介：
This course consists of two parts: the most cutting-edge analytical chemistry methodology and the most advanced analytical chemistry application technology, and the teaching team is composed of teachers from the front-line teaching and research of the Institute of Analytical Chemistry of the Department of Chemistry, and the content consists of eight chapters: Chapter 1: Introduction; Chapter 2: High Sensitivity Detection Techniques; Chapter 3 Dynamic Single-molecule Imaging; Chapter 5: Mass Spectrometry; Chapter 6: Analytical Chemistry of Materials; Chapter 7: Analytical Chemistry of Life; Chapter 8: Micro Total Analysis.
