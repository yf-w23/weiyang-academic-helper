课程内容简介：
本课程与工程力学（2）作为整体，将常规理论力学以及材料力学的教授内容相互贯通，建立更加紧密的力学体系。本课程关注模型的建立过程以及应用范围。主要内容有：弾性静力学、材料力学行为和表面力学基础。通过对工程力学I的学习，学生能够掌握机械领域所必需的静力学以及材料性质方面的知识，掌握基础的建模以及力学分析的能力，提高分析解决实际工程问题的能力。
Course Description
This course, in conjunction with Engineering Mechanics (2), integrates the teaching content of conventional theoretical mechanics and mechanics of materials to establish a more cohesive mechanics system. This course focuses on the model-building process and its scope of application. The main topics include: elasticity statics, mechanical behavior of materials, and fundamentals of surface mechanics. Through studying Engineering Mechanics I, students will be able to master the knowledge of statics and material properties necessary for the field of mechanical engineering, grasp basic modeling and mechanical analysis skills, and enhance their ability to analyze and solve practical engineering problems.
