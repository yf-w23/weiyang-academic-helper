课程内容简介：
《创业思维》是一门以讲授“启发创新意识和培养创业精神”为特色的通识课程。课程贯彻国家创新驱动发展战略和我校“三位一体，双创育人”的教育理念，以培养具有创新精神和创业素养的科技领军人才为目标，锻炼同学们像创业者一样思考和行动，学习创新性地解决挑战性难题，并创造社会价值或经济价值。课程通过课上讲授、案例分析、情景模拟、小组研讨等多种手段，不断深入与锻炼，达到知识学习与能力掌握。同学们以小组为单位，研讨问题并设计创新项目，小组通过双向选择进行构建。 课程讲授场景并非仅限于创立新企业，而是“创立新事业”，学习课程后的应用场景： 1）学生科技创新活动； 2）科研型项目创新； 3）应用型项目攻关； 4）成熟组织的内部创业（政府、大企业开阔新领域）； 5）设立新企业等。 主要培养的能力包含： 1）培养创新意识：启蒙同学们的创新意识和创业精神，使学生了解创新型人才的素质要求，学习如何组建创新团队并有效合作，认识创业的本质，使同学们具备开展创新创业活动所需要的基本认知。 2）建立创业思维：解析并初步建立学生的创新思维、批判性思维、领导力思维、团队思维、产品思维、商业思维等创业思维。 3）提升创业能力：引导同学们认知当今企业及行业环境，学会辨识创新创业机会，控制创业风险，掌握商业模式开发的过程，设计策略及技巧等，提高同学们的创新创业能力。 4）深入创新实践：通过团队创业实践，引导同学们运用创业思维去思考和解决问题，做到知行合一。 正如诺贝尔和平奖获得者穆罕默德·尤努斯所说——“所有人类都是创业者，当原始人居住在洞穴，我们寻找自己的食物，自给自足，人类历史就是这样开始的……当文明到来的时候我们压抑了这种技能，我们变成了劳动者，以至于忘记了自己曾经是创业者”。为了更好地迎接未来挑战，每一位同学都应该掌握“创业思维”！
Course Description
"Entrepreneurial Thinking" is a general education course featuring "inspiring innovative consciousness and cultivating entrepreneurial spirit". The course aims to cultivate scientific and technological leaders with innovative spirit and entrepreneurial literacy. Students are trained to think and act like entrepreneurs, learn to solve challenging problems innovatively, and create social or economic value. Through various means such as in-class teaching, case analysis, scenario simulation and group discussion, the course is continuously deepened and practiced to achieve knowledge learning and ability mastery. Students work in small groups to discuss problems and design innovative projects. Course application scenarios include: 1) Student science and technology innovation activities; 2) Innovation of scientific research projects; 3) Tackling key problems in application-oriented projects; 4) Internal entrepreneurship of mature organizations (government and large enterprises to open new areas); 5) Setting up a new company. The main skills developed include: 1) Enlightens students' innovation consciousness and entrepreneurial spirit, makes students understand the quality requirements of innovative talents, and learns how to build an innovation team and cooperate effectively. 2) Analyze and initially establish students' innovative thinking, critical thinking, leadership thinking, team thinking, product thinking, business thinking and other entrepreneurial thinking. 3) Guide students to understand the current enterprise and industry environment, learn to identify innovation and entrepreneurship opportunities, and improve students' innovation and entrepreneurship ability. 4) Guide students to use entrepreneurial thinking to think and solve problems through team entrepreneurship practice, so as to realize the integration of knowledge and practice.
