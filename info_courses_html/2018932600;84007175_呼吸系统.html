课程简介：
该课程是卓越学者型医师项目课程体系中的必修课程，以器官为基础进行医学专业学习。本课程以呼吸系统疾病为基础，同时结合呼吸系统生理学、病理生理学、解剖学、药理、病理等系统的进行学习，舍去既往传统医学学习中重复学习的部分。在教学方式上，除传统理论授课模式，同时采取了病例讨论，“基于案例”教学法(Case-based learning, CBL)，实践学习方式，充分发挥研究生的主管学习能力，在充分讲解基础知识的基础上，让学生们充分思考，积极参与讨论学习，更好的掌握及自主的运用所学知识。 课程分为五个模块：感染性疾病、气道功能性疾病、肺间质与肺血管疾病、胸膜疾病、肿瘤性疾病。涵盖了执业医师考试大纲要求的疾病，重点疾病详细讲解、分析，部分内容作为自学及拓展要求。 课程同时注重实践技能及临床实践的学习，安排在技能中心使用专业模拟设备进行实际操作演练。针对重点疾病同时安排了小组病例讨论及真实患者接触内容，使学生更好的理解和掌握重点疾病。
英文课程简介：
This course is a mandatory component of the graduate medical curriculum, and is a specialized study of medicine conducted on an organ-based basis. This course is focused on the respiratory system, with a foundation in respiratory diseases, and combines the study of respiratory physiology, pathophysiology, anatomy, pharmacology, pathology, and other systems to eliminate repetitive learning from traditional medical studies. In terms of teaching methods, in addition to traditional theoretical teaching, case discussion, case-based learning (CBL), and practical learning methods are also adopted to fully utilize the graduate students' ability to lead their own learning. On the basis of thorough explanation of basic knowledge, students are encouraged to engage in thinking, active participation in discussions, and better master and apply independently the knowledge learned. The course is divided into five modules: infectious diseases, airway functional diseases, pulmonary interstitial and vascular diseases, pleural diseases, and neoplastic diseases. Covering the diseases required by the National Medical Licensing Examination (NMMLE) syllabus, with detailed explanations and analysis of key diseases, with some content used as self-study and expansion requirements. The course also emphasizes the learning of practical skills and clinical practice, and arranges practical exercises using professional simulation equipment in the skills center. Focused case discussions and real patient encounters are also arranged for key diseases to enable students to better understand and master key diseases.
