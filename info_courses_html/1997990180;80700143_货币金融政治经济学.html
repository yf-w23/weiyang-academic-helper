课程简介：
与西方主流经济学传统的“二分法”、“货币中性说”、货币面纱观和货币数量论所导向的货币银行学专注于研究货币、银行和金融市场的技术问题不同，货币金融政治经济学以马克思、凯恩斯与后凯恩斯主义的货币理论为思想来源，力求把货币和金融纳入到宏观经济学总的分析框架之中，探究有关货币与金融市场的基础概念、理论与政策问题，旨在揭示金融市场、虚拟经济与实体经济的关系，为建立稳健、有效、服务于实体经济的金融制度提供理论启迪。主要内容包括：货币的本质、内在矛盾与职能；货币供给的内生性；凯恩斯的投资融资理论；明斯基的金融不稳定假说：金融结构、金融市场的稳定性与明斯基时刻；通货膨胀、经济周期、稳定政策与中央银行的最后贷款人职责；金融制度的改革与重建。
英文课程简介：
Political Economy of Money and Finance is deferent from the traditional course of Money and finance or Money and Banking, which derives from the traditional Dichotomy of Money, Neutrality of money, money as a veil, and Quantity Theory of Money, and focuses mainly on the technical issues of money, banking, and financial markets. In contrast, this course has rich contents that originate from Marx', Keynes' and Post-Keynesian monetary theories. It tries to place money into the overall analytical framework of macroeconomics, and explores the basic concepts, theories, and policy issues of money and financial markets. The course aims to indicate the relationships between financial markets, virtual economy and real economy, and to provide theoretical enlightenment for establishing a stable and effective financial system that serves the real economy.
