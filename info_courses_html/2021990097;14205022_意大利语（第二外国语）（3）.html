课程内容简介：
该课程为基础意大利语教学。遵照“听说领先”、“急用先学”和“循序渐进”的原则，在课前、课上和课下以学生为主体，目的是强健学生自身的语言“造血机能”，使其通过一学期的意大利语学习，在听、说、读、写方面达到欧洲共同语言参考框架（Common European Framework of Reference for Languages, CEFR）意大利语B2-C1级水平，可以使用意大利语进行比较顺畅的交流，并能够在专业学术领域进行科学研究，用意大利语写学术论文、与母语者进行学术交流等。另外，本课寓文化教育于语言教学之中，使学生通过语言学习，从各个方面了解并热爱意大利文化。不仅开拓学生的视野，丰富其知识，加入一种新的思维方式，更在实际层面帮助学生拓宽选择面，如提供出国交流、访学、进一步深造的机会，在其就业方面增加竞争力。
Course Description
This course is aimed at beginners of Italian as a second foreign language. In accordance with the principles of "listening and speaking come first", "learning to be used come first" and "learning step by step", students are the leading role before class, in class and after class. The purpose is to strengthen the students' own language using capability, so that they can reach the level of Italian B2-C1（Common European Framework of Reference for Languages, CEFR）, in listening, speaking, reading and writing through one semester of Italian learning. In addition, this course incorporates cultural education in language teaching, so that students can understand and love Italian culture from all aspects through language learning. Not only broaden students' horizons, enrich their knowledge, and add a new way of thinking, but also help them broaden their choices in the practical level, such as providing opportunities for overseas exchanges, visiting schools, further education, and even increasing their competitiveness in future job applying.
