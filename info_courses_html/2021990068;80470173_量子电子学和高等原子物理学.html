课程简介：
该课程为从事原子光子以及量子信息实验的研究生和本科生讲授量子电子学和高等原子物理学的实用知识。首先，我们对高斯光束、谐振腔、非线性光学、以及调制技术进行极其传统地探讨。此外，我们将介绍一些光学实验的技巧，包括单模/保偏光纤耦合、AOM/EOM调节方法、超稳腔锁频等。其次，我们仔细探讨原子结构和原子光子作用。最后，光学放大和激光稳频谱又将之前的知识串了起来。一系列近来的科技发展成果也会被探讨，例如飞秒激光频率测量、激光冷却与囚禁、磁光阱、以及离子阱。
英文课程简介：
This course focuses on the basic knowledge needed in AMO (Atomic, Molecular and Optical physics) and quantum information experiments. First, we will introduce Gaussian beams, optical resonators, non-linear optics and classical modulating technologies. We will also discuss some techniques in optics, such as SM/PM fiber coupling, how to use AOM/EOM, and laser locking with a stable cavity. Second, we will introduce atomic structure and atom-light interactions in detail. Last, we will introduce optical amplification and laser frequency stabilization. In addition, many recent technology will be discussed,including femto-second laser, laser cooling and trapping, MOT, and ion traps.
