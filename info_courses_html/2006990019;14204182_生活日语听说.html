课程内容简介：
本课程旨在针对具有一定日语基础的学习者，提供日常生活口语对话的教学。课程设置多个日常生活场景，包括问路、乘坐交通工具、购物和用餐等。通过练习每组场景所需的表达方式，学生将了解日本社会与生活的特点，并学习日本社交的相关常识。听力训练将与各场景内容相结合。此外，课程还将安排与日本助教的对话练习，以加强学生的口语能力。学生将分成3-4个小组，每组将与一名日本助教围绕一个或多个主题展开对话。学生需要提前准备对话材料，并在对话结束后提交每次练习的心得体会。握了其单词的背景事件的基础上，要求学生能够听懂并解释音频中的内容。
Course Description
This course is designed for learners with a basic understanding of the Japanese language, focusing on teaching daily conversational Japanese. Various everyday life scenarios will be set up, such as asking for directions, using transportation, shopping, and dining. By practicing the expressions required for each scenario, students will gain insight into the characteristics of Japanese society and daily life, as well as learn various social norms in Japan. Listening exercises will be closely tied to the content of each scenario. Additionally, there will be bi-weekly conversational practice sessions with Japanese teaching assistants in the second half of the class. Students will be divided into 3-4 groups, each led by a Japanese teaching assistant to engage in conversations on one or more topics. Students are required to prepare conversation materials in advance and submit their reflections after each session.

第16周期末汇演。实际舞台演出。

选课指导：
本课程聚焦日常会话，原则上不详细讲解语法，适合对日本生活，日常用语感兴趣的同学学习。以练为主，培养学生开口“说”的能力。
