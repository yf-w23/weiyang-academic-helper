课程内容简介：
本课程《科学逻辑与思维》旨在面向生命科学专业本科生（也欢迎研生选课），系统构建科研思维方法与逻辑框架。课程内容围绕科研全流程展开，涵盖常见认知偏见与谬误、如何发现和提出科学问题、如何提出和论证研究假设、课题推进和深入、结果解读与展示技巧等关键环节。通过引入生命科学领域的经典与前沿案例，课程将抽象的逻辑思维过程具象化，帮助学生理解如何从现象中提炼科学问题、如何设计实验、如何避免常见逻辑谬误、如何合理解读数据并形成可靠结论。 课程注重理论与实践相结合，内容包括教师讲授，学生小组文献批判性阅读和展示等。 本课程不仅面对计划未来从事科研工作的生命科学专业学生，也适用于希望提升逻辑思维、系统性解决问题能力的学生。通过本课程的学习，学生将建立起有效的科研思维工具体系，为其后续专业课程学习、科研训练以及职业发展奠定基础。
Course Description
This course, "Scientific Reasoning and Critical thinking" is designed for undergraduate students in life sciences to systematically develop core competencies in scientific reasoning and research methodology. The curriculum covers the entire research process, including identifying and refining scientific questions, formulating and testing hypotheses, designing rigorous and reproducible experiments, applying statistical methods for data analysis, and adhering to academic writing standards. Using both classic and cutting-edge case studies from the life sciences, the course bridges abstract logical thinking with practical application. Students will learn to extract research questions from observations, design controlled experiments, avoid common logical fallacies, and interpret data to draw sound conclusions. The course emphasizes hands-on learning through group discussions and critical reading of scientific literature. Additional modules address research ethics, academic integrity, and collaborative teamwork, fostering a responsible and cooperative scientific attitude. This course is designed not only for students pursuing research careers but also for anyone seeking to enhance their logical reasoning and problem-solving skills within the life sciences. Upon completion, students will possess a structured framework for scientific thinking, preparing them for advanced coursework, laboratory research, and future professional endeavors.
