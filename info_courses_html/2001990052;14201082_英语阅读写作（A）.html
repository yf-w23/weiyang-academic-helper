课程内容简介：
英语阅读写作（A）的主要学习内容为英语研究论文读写，面向英语分级考试中三、四级，或者英语读写基础较好的学生，强调以项目为基础的研究论文的读写学习，帮助学生做好专业英语论文读写的准备。课程期间，学生通过小组合作调研，独立完成IMRDA（Introduction, Method, Results, Discussion, Abstract）格式的英语研究论文写作。完成项目可以使学生在理解研究论文写作框架的同时，跨越专业之间的界限共同合作，培养学生的团队意识。
Course Description
English for Academic Purposes (A) is designed for students with an upper-intermediate or advanced English proficiency level. It aims to develop students' critical thinking and academic reading and writing competencies. There is a strong emphasis on the production of a research paper (around 1500-3000 words) using a mini-project-based approach. The course will prepare students for the research genre which is required in various academic fields. The project involves collaborative research and individual academic writing that help students develop necessary academic skills in reading, writing, researching, and collaborative learning skills.
