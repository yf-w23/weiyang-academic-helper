课程内容简介：
本课程以土的渗透、变形和强度三大基本特性为核心内容，主要讲授土力学的基本概念、 理论和方法，注重培养学生的岩土工程理性思维和创新能力以及分析解决岩土工程实际问 题的能力。主要教学内容包括：土的物理性质、土的渗透性和渗流问题、土体中的应力计 算、土的压缩性和地基沉降计算、土的抗剪强度、挡土墙上的土压力、土坡稳定分析和地 基承载力等。为了加深同学对土体特性的理解，培养学生的基本土工试验技能，课程还包 括土的基本物性试验、固结试验以及三轴试验等。
Course Description
The course focuses on the fundamental concept, theory and methodology of Soil Mechanics, with the core contents of the basic characteristics about seepage, deformation and strength. The aim of this course is to cultivate students’ rational thinking, innovation capability and problem-solving ability for geotechnical engineering practice The major contents include physical characteristics of soils, hydraulic conductivity and seepage problem, stresses in soil mass, compressibility of soils and foundation settlement, shearing strength of soils, earth pressure on retaining structures, slope stability, and bearing capacity. To strengthen the students’ understanding of the fundamental characteristic of soils and promote soil testing skills training, three laboratory tests are arranged during the course including soil physical properties, consolidation, and tri-axial shearing.
