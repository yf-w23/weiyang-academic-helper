课程内容简介：
数学模型是针对一个现实对象，为了特定目的，根据其内在规律，作出必要的简化假设，运用适当的数学工具，得到的一个数学结构。数学建模是建立数学模型的全过程，简而言之就是用数学语言描述现实对象的过程。数学建模是用数学解决实际问题的第一步，也是关键一步，大量的科学发现、技术进步和经济社会发展源于创新的数学模型。数学建模贵在实践、难在创新，其能力培养主要通过案例学习。本课程从实际问题入手，介绍数学模型的一般概念、方法和步骤；以案例方式组织教学，通过大量实例研究，重点介绍一些用机理分析方法和优化、统计方法建立的非物理领域（如日常生活、社会、政治、经济、管理、科技等领域）的实际问题的数学模型及常用的数学建模方法。数学内容主要包括初等模型、简单优化模型、数学规划模型、微分方程模型、代数方程和差分方程模型、离散模型、概率模型、统计回归模型、博弈模型等。具体案例主要取自教材，课上选讲部分案例、其他案例供学生课外阅读。
Course Description
A mathematical model is the mathematical structure obtained for a real object, for a specific purpose, based on its inherent laws, using appropriate mathematical tools with necessary simplifying assumptions. Mathematical modeling is the entire process of constructing a mathematical model; in short, it is the process of describing a real object in the language of mathematics. Modeling is the first and also the crucial step in using mathematics to solve real problems. A large number of scientific discoveries, technical advances, and economic and social developments originate from innovative mathematical models. The value of mathematical modeling lies in practice and innovation, and its skill development primarily occurs through case studies. This course starts from real problems and introduces the general concepts, methods, and steps of mathematical modeling. It is organized around case-based teaching, with numerous case studies focusing on mathematical models and commonly used modeling methods for practical problems in non-physical domains (such as daily life, society, politics, economics, management, science and technology, etc.) using mechanistic analysis, optimization, and statistical methods. The mathematical content mainly includes elementary models, simple optimization models, mathematical programming models, differential equation models, algebraic and difference equation models, discrete models, probabilistic models, statistical regression models, and game-theoretic models. Specific cases mainly come from the textbook, with in-class optional discussion cases and other cases provided for extracurricular reading.
