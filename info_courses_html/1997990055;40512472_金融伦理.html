课程内容简介：
金融对收益的追求，很容易使人变得贪婪。利益诱惑下，合规意识和伦理道德的缺失，金融 丑闻不断发生，对金融业的健康发展带来不利影响。金融业的健康发展，离不开法律和金融 伦理的约束。 与法律的外部强制性约束不同，金融伦理是金融企业和从业人员的自律，是金融从业人员共 同期待并普遍采取的道德规范行为。伦理行为包括做正确的事情、履行责任或者职责、尊重 他人的权利、做事诚信和公平等。金融行业（组织和个人）需要判断什么是合乎伦理的行 为，以及应该如何开展金融业务。 作为一门经济金融专业本科生的任选课，本课程旨在帮助学生：（1）理解金融领域伦理冲 突的普遍性与原因；（2）了解金融伦理的哲学基础和分析框架；（3）建立分析框架，能够 分析金融市场、金融机构、金融监管、金融科技以及企业财务管理中的伦理问题。这门课最 重要的是希望学生认识到，学习金融伦理的目的是实践而非单纯的知识，在未来的职业生涯 中高度重视并实践金融伦理才是课程学习的成功。 本课程采用课堂讲授、案例讨论、讲座和外出活动相结合的方式，学生需要在课下阅读相关 的文献、资料，并能够参与安排的外出活动。
Course Description
People in financial service industry can easily become greed in search for return, and therefore, financial scandals or misconduct may occur frequently with the lure of interest and the lack of ethics. Financial service industry will not develop well, even not exist, without the discipline of ethical rules. Law and regulation, as compulsory formal mechanism, are not enough for finance, and ethics is needed as the rule of conduct for self-regulation. Ethical conduct involves doing what is right, fulfilling one’s duties or obligations, respecting people’s rights, being honest and acting fairly. Organizations and individuals in financial service industry need to determine what is ethical conduct in finance and how financial activity ought to be conducted. This course is a selective course for senior students majored in economics and finance in SEM, Tsinghua University. It aims to help students understand (1) why moral conflicts exist widely in finance; (2) the fundamentals and framework of finance ethics; (3) some important ethical issues in financial market, financial institutions, financial technology, financial regulation and financial management in corporations. The learning goal or the success of the course is that students realize that the most important thing is not knowledge but conduct of ethics in daily life and the future career in the finance service industry. The course is composed by lectures, case discussions, guest speaker lectures and outing activity. Students are required to read recommended reading materials, prepare for in class discussions and make time to participate outing activity if arranged.

 第1周 金融领域伦理冲突的普遍性和原因；

第2周 金融伦理的基本原则和哲学基础；

第3-6周 金融市场中的伦理问题：（1）市场公平；（2）内幕交易；（3）恶意收购；（4）金融工程

第7周 外出活动或者讲座

第8-10周 金融机构的伦理问题：（1）商业银行；（2）共同基金；（3）证券公司

第11-13周 企业财务管理中的伦理问题：（1）公司目标和公司治理；（2）风险管理；（3）破产伦理

第14周  金融科技伦理

第15周  金融监管伦理
