课程简介：
本课程主要包含核电厂动力学、运行与控制方案以及控制方法与系统三部分内容，第一部分包括：反应堆中子运动学和电厂热工水力学及其在压水堆电厂和先进反应堆电厂动态特性分析中的应用、动态系统的概念与表示、稳定性与无源性、反应堆／核电厂的稳定性分析；第二部分包括：核电厂基本运行方式、压水堆和先进反应堆核电厂的运行方式分析、动态系统的能控能观性、核电厂控制方案设计方法及其在压水堆和先进反应堆电厂中的应用；第三部分包括：线性和非线性控制理论及其在核电厂控制设计中的应用、压水堆和先进反应堆电厂的数字化控制保护系统。通过课程学习，应具有从事核电厂动态特性分析、运行方式和控制方案设计、控制系统设计与实现等多方面的基础和前沿知识。
英文课程简介：
“Nuclear Plant Operation and Control” is mainly composed of three parts, i.e. nuclear plant dynamics, nuclear plant operation and control scheme as well as nuclear plant control methods and systems. In Part 1, both neutron kinetics and plant thermal-hydraulics are introduced, which are then applied to the dynamical analysis of classical pressurized water reactor (PWR) plant and some other advanced nuclear plants. Moreover, concepts and representations of general dynamical systems are introduced, and the concepts and analyzing approach of system stability and passivity are also given, which are all applied to the stability analysis of nuclear reactors and plants. In Part 2, basic operating modes of common nuclear plants are introduced, and specific operating modes of classical PWR as well as some advanced plants are also analyzed. Both the theory of controllability and observability and the method of nuclear plant control scheme design are also given in Part 2, which is applied to the control scheme design of classical PWR plants and some advanced plants. In Part 3, both the linear and nonlinear control theory are introduced and applied to the power regulation of different nuclear plants, and the digital control and protection systems of both the PWR and advanced nuclear plants are also introduced. From the study of this course, both the basic and advanced knowledge in dynamical analysis, operation scheme planning, and control system design can be obtained.
