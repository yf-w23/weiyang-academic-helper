课程内容简介：
本课程涵盖博弈论，决策论和排队论的基本理论和分析方法，是在经济学和运筹学中非常重要的理论。课堂上讲解基本概念的同时会引入多个实际例子，在博弈论部分会结合多个领域中的问题进行讲解，在决策论部分会引入案例分析，在排队论部分会加入前沿论文的研读，通过实际应用来加深对于理论的理解。通过本课程的学习，希望同学能够应用所学知识对实际的博弈问题，单个体的决策问题和排队问题进行建模并用相应的方法有效的解决。另外，在深刻理解三部分内容的基础上，能够融会贯通，对课程的整体架构有一个更为有机的认识。 本课程主要讲授： 1、博弈论（包括：博弈论基础；纳什平衡；逆向归纳和子博弈完美均衡；重复博弈和不完全信息博弈；契约理论和机制设计），2、决策论（包括：决策分析的基本问题；风险型决策方法；决策树方法；不确定型决策方法；效用函数方法；层次分析法；多目标决策分析简介）注意：一定要具有概率和线性代数的初步知识。3、排队论（包括：引言：生灭过程和Poisson过程；M/M/s等待制排队模型；M/M/s混合制排队模型；其它排队模型简介；排队系统的优化）。
Course Description
The course consists of three main modules: queueing theory, game theory and decision theory. While covering the basic theory, we will introduce tremendous practical examples to further the understanding of the theory. In the part of decision theory, we will use case study. From this course, students should learn how to model and solve a queueing problem and a decision making problem for both one player and multiple players. In addition, based on these three modules, students should try to understand operations research from a systematic view. The topics covered in this course are: 1. Game theory (Fundamentals of game theory, Nash equilibrium, Backward induction and subgame perfect equilibrium, Repeated game and games with incomplete information, Agency theory) 2. Decision theory (Introduction to modeling; Decision trees; Treeplan; Value of information, Ventron case; Sensitivity analysis; Risk profiles, Utility theory, Gillette vs. energizer case; Probability assessment; Multi-objective decision making) 3. Queueing theory (Birth-death process and poisson process, M/M/s queueing system, Other queueing systems and system optimization, Queueing system simulation method)
