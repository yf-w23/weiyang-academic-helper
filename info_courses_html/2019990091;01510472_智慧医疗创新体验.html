课程内容简介：
阿尔伯特·爱因斯坦曾说过：“只懂得科学技术本身是不够的，关心人的本身，应当成为一切技术的主要目标。” 近年来，人工智能技术蓬勃发展，并逐步被应用于医疗健康领域（比如：医学影像辅助诊断、医用机器人、诊疗决策支持、健康检测可穿戴设备等），因此人工智能已被视为医疗健康行业未来变革的重要力量。 本课程以智慧医疗这一交叉专业领域为背景，带领同学们走进产业、走近医生、探索人工智能技术，深入探究智慧医疗发展和工程实现，感受智能技术对人类健康的影响力，建立大健康素养。为同学们呈现产业的“现实需求->技术创新->产业发展”全貌。同时，培养和锻炼同学们如何将智能技术如何运用到医疗健康行业中，提供人工智能的应用能力。 本课程首先在科学技术方面，学习运用人工智能技术解决医疗健康问题的思路；其次，在产业前沿方面，学习从现行医疗行业中发现问题、研究AI技术、探究解决之道，培养医疗健康与人工智能的技术交叉思维方式，提高运用创新技术解决问题的能力；第三，在个人发展方面，帮助同学了解大健康产业前沿和发展趋势，思考复合型人才的成才之路。最后，在价值塑造方面，帮助同学们树立终生受益的人文关怀观念：关爱生命，佑护健康，健康工作，科学生活。
Course Description
Albert Einstein once said, "It is not enough to understand science and technology by itself. Caring for human beings should be the primary goal of all technology." In recent years, artificial intelligence technology has been booming and gradually applied to the medical and health field (such as medical imaging assisted diagnosis, medical robots, diagnosis and treatment decision support, health detection wearable devices, etc.). Therefore, artificial intelligence has been regarded as an important force in the future reform of the medical and health industry. Based on the background of intelligent medicine, this course leads students to walk into industry, approach doctors, explore artificial intelligence technology, and deeply explore the development and engineering realization of intelligent medicine.Present the whole picture of "realistic demand -> technology innovation -> industry development" of the industry for students.At the same time, the students will be trained to apply intelligent technology to the medical and health industry and provide the application ability of artificial intelligence. Firstly, in terms of science and technology, this course will learn how to solve medical and health problems with artificial intelligence technology.Secondly, in the front of the industry, learn to find problems from the current medical industry, research AI technology, explore solutions, improve the ability to use innovative technology to solve problems;Thirdly, in terms of personal development, we should think about the path of inter-disciplinary talents.Finally, we will help students to establish the concept of humanistic care that will benefit them all their lives: caring for life and protecting health.
