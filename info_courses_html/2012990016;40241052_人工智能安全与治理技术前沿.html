课程内容简介：
随着我们将越来越多的任务委托给人工智能并将其深度融入社会决策过程中，我们面临着一 个重要的挑战：如何确保AI系统的安全性、遵循伦理原则并与创建者的意图保持一致。当 前，学术界和工业界的AI专家一致认为，在AI对齐、伦理和治理方面取得技术和社会进步， 以理解和减轻日益强大的AI系统带来的系统风险，并确保其贡献惠及整个社会，是一项极其 紧迫的任务。 《人工智能安全与治理技术前沿》课程旨在通过一系列讲座、针对性阅读材料、每周测验和 讨论，深入探讨这些关键问题。课程将首先介绍当前和未来AI系统的能力和局限性，帮助学 生理解为什么确保现有AI系统的可靠性如此困难。我们将探讨AI系统在不同应用领域中的表 现，包括强化学习、计算机视觉和自然语言处理等。此外，课程还将涵盖正在进行的研究工 作，这些研究致力于解决AI安全和治理问题。学生将学习AI系统的可解释性、鲁棒性和治理 等方面的最新进展，了解如何通过技术手段和政策措施来提高AI系统的安全性和可靠性。
Course Description
As we delegate more tasks to artificial intelligence (AI) and deeply integrate it into societal decision-making processes, we face a significant challenge: how to ensure the safety of AI systems, adhere to ethical principles, and align with the creator’s intent. Currently, AI experts in academia and industry unanimously agree that achieving technical and societal progress in AI alignment, ethics, and governance to understand and mitigate the systemic risks posed by increasingly powerful AI systems, and ensuring their contributions benefit society as a whole, is an extremely urgent task. The course “Frontiers in AI Safety and Governance Technologies” aims to explore these critical issues through a series of lectures, targeted readings, weekly quizzes, and discussions. The course will first introduce the capabilities and limitations of current and future AI systems, helping students understand why ensuring the reliability of existing AI systems is so challenging. We will explore the performance of AI systems in various application domains, including reinforcement learning, computer vision, and natural language processing. Additionally, the course will cover ongoing research efforts dedicated to addressing AI safety and governance issues. Students will learn about the latest advancements in the interpretability, robustness, and governance of AI systems, understanding how to enhance the safety and reliability of AI systems through technical means and policy measures.
