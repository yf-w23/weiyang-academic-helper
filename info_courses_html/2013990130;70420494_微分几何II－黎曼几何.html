课程简介：
Hopf-Rinow Theorem (link geometry with analysis), Bonnet-Myers Theorem (study geometry by ODE), Bishop-Gromov Theorem (study geometry by PDE), Uniformization Theorem (the best metric in the deformation class of metrics on surface), Mostow rigidity (topology determine geometry in hyperbolic case), Cheeger-Gromoll splitting Theorem (linear harmonic function leads to splitting of manifolds in positive curverd manifolds) etc.
