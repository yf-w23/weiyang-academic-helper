课程内容简介：
本课程按照高校教学大纲，使用《综合日语》系列教材，主要针对外文系日语专业一年级学生（第二学期）所设置。教学内容覆盖以下内容。1.词汇方面：基本词汇量的掌握；常用词汇的使用方法；近义词的区别使用；复合词的基本规律；2.语法方面：掌握教学大纲规定的基本语法和句型。具体目标为基本掌握叙事文、说明文、书信、日记等文体的写作方法；礼貌语、敬语及简体语的区分及使用方法；时态以及复句的种类和结构等；日常对话、正式场合的语言使用方法；3.文章理解方面：熟悉日语惯有句式、语言习惯、文化传统以及日式省略等特点，加深对语言文化的理解。4.语流语感方面：鼓励学生通过跟读、背诵、课外阅读等方式熟悉日语口语的习惯特征，培养对日语的语流、语感的熟悉度，全方位加强日语语言能力。。
Course Description
This course is designed for first-year Japanese major students (second semester) in the Foreign Languages Department, following the college teaching syllabus and using the "Comprehensive Japanese" series of textbooks. The teaching content covers the following aspects: 1. Vocabulary: Mastery of basic vocabulary; usage of common vocabulary; distinction and application of synonyms; basic rules of compound words. 2. Grammar: Mastery of the basic grammar and sentence patterns stipulated in the teaching syllabus. Specific goals include basic proficiency in writing methods for narrative, expository, letter, and diary genres; differentiation and usage of polite language, honorifics, and plain forms; understanding of tenses, types, and structures of complex sentences; language usage in daily conversations and formal settings. 3. Text comprehension: Familiarity with Japanese idiomatic expressions, language habits, cultural traditions, and characteristics of Japanese ellipsis to deepen understanding of language and culture. 4. Fluency and sense of language: Encouraging students to become familiar with the characteristics of Japanese oral language through reading aloud, recitation, and extracurricular reading, to cultivate fluency and sense of Japanese language, and to comprehensively enhance their Japanese language abilities.
