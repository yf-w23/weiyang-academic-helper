Course Description
[Students who are not native speakers of Chinese are advised not to take this course] Do you have nothing to say or often quarrel with your family? Do you have unresolved issues with your roommate? Do you have unresolved issues with your friends? Do you not know how to deal with your teachers? If you have any of these problems, this course may suit you. This course is based on neuro-linguistic programming and helps students identify communication barriers, correct external listening and speaking skills, further examine internal values, and finally introduce family of origin therapy, hoping to change interpersonal communication outcomes from the root. The course is progressive, coherent, and practical. Spring Semester theme: Listen quickly + Speak slowly= Find a better self. The main contents include: 1. Communication barriers; 2. Self-correction communication skills (including listening and oral expression); 3. Values and family of origin that shape them in interpersonal communication processes.
进度安排：

 

 

 

  1. 我习以为常的沟通姿态 -> 你不知道的沟通秘密：你的姿态决定了一切
  2. 划破沟通的僵局 -> 如何打破沟通的死锁？这些技巧让你轻松应对
  3. 越聆听越想说 -> 聆听的艺术：如何让对方更愿意对你吐露心声
  4. 我说对话了吗？ -> 你以为你在对话，其实你在自说自话
  5. 用不同的视角转换情绪 -> 情绪管理的关键：如何用换位思考来调节自己的情绪
  6. 识根源.弱影响 -> 原生家庭对你有多大影响？如何摆脱束缚，找回真正的自己
  7. 总结报告 -> 输出倒逼输入：我学到了什么，我改变了什么
