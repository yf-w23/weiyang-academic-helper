课程内容简介：
作为教学改革的重要内容之一，2016年春节学期开始，《工业数据挖掘与分析》的讲授内容做出了重大调整，主要针对当前互联网领域产品的快速发展变化，重点讲授“网络产品设计”相关方面的内容。本课程将从移动互联网产品设计为切入口，融合数据分析方法与思想，讲授网络产品的设计及其基本知识，并贯穿身边的应用实例进行讲解，帮助我们掌握优秀的网络产品设计的思想方法。 本课程2017年入选微软全球TOP30高校创新人才培养计划课程，2018年入选教育部—微软产学合作协同育人项目。 本课程主要针对未来立志从事网络产品设计与研发的同学而开设，全校本科生和研究生均可选学。
Course Description
As one of the important contents of the teaching reform, since the Spring Festival semester in 2016, the teaching content of Industrial Data Mining and Analysis has been significantly adjusted, mainly focusing on the content related to "network product design" in view of the rapid development and changes of current products in the Internet field.This course will start from the design of mobile Internet products, integrate data analysis methods and ideas, teach the design of network products and its basic knowledge, and explain through the application examples around us, to help us master the excellent ideas and methods of network product design. In 2017, this course was selected as one of Microsoft's Global TOP30 Innovative Talents Training Program for universities, and in 2018, it was selected as one of the Ministry of Education -- Microsoft Industry-University Collaborative Education Project. This course is designed for students who aspire to be engaged in the design and development of network products in the future. It is optional for undergraduate and graduate students of the university.
