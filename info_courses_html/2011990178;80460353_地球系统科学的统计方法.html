课程简介：
本课程面向地球系统科学系研究生开设，同时也可支撑相关学科研究生对统计方法的应用需求（如环境和水利等），主要讲授全球变化研究、大气和海洋科学等学科中的常用统计学方法。采用基础概念和关键科学应用相结合，通过代表性论文阅读和全球变化实际问题的实例讲解等方式、辅以研究生具体科研任务研讨，开展方法讲授和应用训练。讲授内容主要包括：单随机变量与多随机变量气候数据分析方法，统计假设检验，时间序列分析与应用，空间统计与预测，数据同化方法及其应用，常用统计工具软件使用，以及典型气候数据集介绍。
英文课程简介：
This course is titled "statistical methods in earth system science", and targets at postgraduate students in global and climate change, atmospheric science and oceanic science. It also welcome students from environmental and hydrological sciences who share the similar needs for statistical methods in treating spatial-temporal datasets. It includes introduction to key and basic concepts in probability and statistics, including: univariate and multi-variate statistics and application in climate studies, statistical hypothesis testing, time-series analysis, spatial statistics and prediction, basics of data assimilation and applications, as well as introduction to commonly used climate datasets.
