课程内容简介：
本课程是清华医学院国际医学选修课程的核心模块。在为期3-4周的临床学习中，将帮助学生 学习神经外科脑肿瘤、脊柱脊髓相关疾病、脑血管疾病、癫痫和帕金森等疾病；学习常见精神 疾病的诊治。掌握基本的腰穿、脑穿等临床技能，并在医生与患者的交流对话中提高中文口语 水平。课程将突出“中西医结合”“医工结合”等特点，利用我院建设的虚拟仿真平台提高学 生的兴趣和对知识的理解。
Course Description
This course is the core module of the International Medical Elective Course at Tsinghua Medical University. Students will study related diseases with brain, spine and spinal cord diseases, epilepsy and Parkinson's syndrome in neurosurgery, as well as some common mental diseases. Enhancing clinical skills: We have about 4000 patients in neurosurgery and neuropsychiatry department, and about 3000 operations related with brain tumor, spine and spinal cord related diseases each year. Students will have quite a lot of chances to study these diseases, and improve their clinial skills such as nervous system examination, lumbar puncture, etc. Students will have opportunity to observe and join procedures, and have a lot of chances to observe operations.
