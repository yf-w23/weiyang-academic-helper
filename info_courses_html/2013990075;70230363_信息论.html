课程简介：
《信息论》是清华大学电子工程系的研究生基础课程之一。它涵盖了压缩、编码、通信、网络和人工智能中信息度量和容量的各个方面。本课程中发展起来的理论和工具几乎可以应用于电子工程系所有研究主题的各个方面。《信息论》的中文课程已在系内开设近30年，英文课程则面向外国留学学生开放，包括研究生和高年级本科生。此外，感兴趣的中国学生也欢迎选修。
英文课程简介：
"Information Theory" is one of the fundamental graduate courses offered by the Department of Electronic Engineering at Tsinghua University. It covers various aspects of information measures and capacity across compression, coding, communications, networking, and artificial intelligence. The theories and tools developed in this course can be applied to almost every aspect of all research topics within the Department of Electronic Engineering. The Chinese-language version of this course has been offered by the department for nearly 30 years, while this English-language version is open to international students, including both graduate students and senior undergraduates. Additionally, interested Chinese students are also welcome to enroll.
