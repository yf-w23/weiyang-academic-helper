课程内容简介：
本课程《生医机械工程探索（2）》是未央书院“数理基础科学+机械工程”双学位本科课程中机械方向的探索式学习前沿课程，授课对象为已接受两年基础课程和专业基础课程教育的大三春季学期学生。本课程采用理论教学和专题实验/实践相结合的教学方式，主要课程内容包括12学时的课堂理论授课和36学时的分组专题实践探究。课堂授课以绪论开始，简介绍仿生机械、生物制造、脑机接口外骨骼领域的学科背景和前沿研究，之后3周分主题讲授仿生机械、生物制造、人机交互等方向的基础知识与前沿动态，后12周分为三组，分别选择仿生机器人、生物打印机以及人机交互关键模块设计与制造三个专题，进行实践探究，培养并提高学生运用所学知识分析和解决生医机械交叉学科领域问题的能力，从而掌握用工程手段解决生物医学问题的基本原理和方法。最终以课题报告及交流展示的形式进行课程考察。
Course Description
This course is an exploratory learning course in the mechanical engineering direction of the "Basic Science + Mechanical Engineering" double-degree undergraduate course of Weiyang College. This course adopts a combination of theoretical teaching and thematic experiments/practice teaching methods. The main course includes 12 class-hours of theoretical teaching and 36 class-hours of grouped thematic practice inquiry. The class starts with an introduction, which introduces the concept, connotation, trend and application of biomechanical engineering, following with the topics such as mechanical bionics, biomanufacturing, Bio-Mecha interface, etc. In the experimental practice part, three topics will be selected by the students, such as the design of the development and experiment of bionic instruments, the design and manufacture of key modules for cell 3D printing, the key systems for brain-controlled robots, etc. The objectives are to improve students' ability to analyze and solve problems in the interdisciplinary field of biomechanical engineering by using the knowledge they have learned, so as to master the basic principles and methods of solving biomedical problems via engineering methods, and to lay the foundation for the training of scientific and innovation research.
