课程内容简介：
《高技术战争》课程从解析高技术局部战争入手，阐述新军事变革中战争形态与作战方式的变化，高技术战争的起源、现状、特点及向信息化战争、智能化发展的趋势。高技术条件及信息化条件下的现代局部战争对军事革命和世界格局的重要影响，对我国国防和国家安全带来的机遇和挑战。同时介绍高新技术武器装备的现状及近期发展，我军新时代的军事战略、国防与军队建设和军事斗争准备和强军目标等内容。
Course Description
The course " High Technology in Modern Local War " starts by analyzing high-tech local wars, discussing the changes in the forms and methods of warfare in the context of new military transformations, the origins, current status, characteristics of high-tech warfare, and the trend towards the development of information warfare and intelligentization. It also addresses the significant impact of modern local wars under high-tech and information conditions on military revolutions and the global structure, as well as the opportunities and challenges they pose to our country's national defense and security. Furthermore, it introduces the current status and recent developments of high-tech weaponry and equipment, the military strategy of our armed forces in the new era, national defense and military construction, military struggle preparation, and the goal of strengthening the military。
