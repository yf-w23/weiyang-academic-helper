课程简介：
近年，随着信息化、数字化、智能化技术的普及，污水处理领域的智能化也得到了快速发展。大量的数据信息要得到有效利用，就必须找出其内在规律，并提升为数学模型。因此，数学模型是实现智能化的核心。本课程以化学计量学和酶催化反应动力学为基础，系统地介绍了污水生物处理的经典模型、国际水协的ASM系列模型、厌氧生物处理反应动力学和生物膜反应器动力学。为了使学生能够更好地理解和运用污水生物处理的数学模型及其软件来解决工程设计与污水处理工艺运行中的问题，课程详细介绍了污水处理工艺模型的建立与模型求解方法，以及运用ASM模型软件进行模拟仿真，以便掌握构建污水处理数学模型的基本思路与方法。课程还采用报告讨论的互动方式，学习和研讨近年污水生物处理的新技术和发展趋势。因此是一门理论与工程实际相结合，提升科研新思维的课程。
英文课程简介：
In recent years, with the popularization of informatization, digitization, and intelligence technologies, the field of wastewater treatment has also witnessed rapid advancements in intelligence. To effectively utilize the vast amount of data information, it is essential to uncover its inherent patterns and elevate them into mathematical models. Therefore, mathematical models are the core of achieving intelligence. Based on chemometrics and enzyme-catalyzed reaction kinetics, this course systematically introduces classic models of biological wastewater treatment, the Activated Sludge Model (ASM) series from the International Water Association (IWA), kinetics of anaerobic biological treatment, and kinetics of biofilm reactors. To enable students to better understand and apply mathematical models and software for biological wastewater treatment to solve problems in engineering design and process operation, the course elaborates on the establishment and solution methods of wastewater treatment process models, as well as the use of ASM model software for simulation, so that students can grasp the fundamental ideas and methods for constructing mathematical models of wastewater treatment. The course also adopts an interactive approach through report discussions to study and explore recent advancements and trends in biological wastewater treatment technologies. Thus, it is a course that integrates theory with engineering practice and fosters innovative scientific research thinking.
