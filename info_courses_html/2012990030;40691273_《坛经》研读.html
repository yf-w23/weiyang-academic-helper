课程内容简介：
通过深入研读《坛经》，探讨禅宗的基本观念、初期禅宗史、慧能的生平、《坛经》的版本、 《坛经》思想等，帮助同学理解禅宗作为佛教中国化的理路与脉络，为进一步把握中国佛教乃 至中华传统文化的基本观念奠定基础。
Course Description
Course Objectives: Through an in-depth study of the Platform Sutra, this course aims to help students understand the basic concepts and early history of Chan Buddhism, the life of Hui Neng, and the versions and thoughts of the Platform Sutra, understand the important position of Chan Buddhism in the process of Sinicization of Buddhism, and lay a foundation for further understanding of the basic concepts of Chinese Buddhism and the whole traditional Chinese culture.
