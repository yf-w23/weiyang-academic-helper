课程简介：
依托于先进半导体材料外延技术的低维半导体量子结构具有丰富的物理内涵和巨大的应用价值，是新一代半导体微电子器件和光电子器件的核心基础。本课程以典型的低维半导体量子结构和器件为对象，系统介绍相关的材料、物理和器件问题。主要讲授低维半导体量子结构的能带结构、电子状态、载流子输运性质、光学性质；介绍典型的低维半导体量子结构的制备方法；介绍高电子迁移率晶体管、共振隧穿电子器件、单电子输运器件、自旋电子器件、量子阱红外探测器、量子级联器件、基于量子点的单光子器件等代表性低维半导体量子器件中的量子效应、工作原理和器件特性。本课程的目标是使学生掌握与半导体量子器件相关的材料物理和器件物理的核心要素，为发展面向下一代信息技术的高性能半导体器件打下基础。
英文课程简介：
Low-dimensional semiconductor quantum structures based on the advanced semiconductor epitaxy technologies possess rich physics connotations and huge application potentials, and they are the building block of the new-generation semiconductor electronic devices and optoelectronic devices. This course will introduce the material, physics and device topics related to the typical low-dimensional semiconductor quantum structures and devices including: 1) the band structures, electron states, carrier transportation and optical properties of low-dimensional semiconductor quantum structures; 2) the preparation methods of typical low-dimensional semiconductor quantum structures; 3) the quantum effects, operation mechanism and device characteristics of typical low-dimensional semiconductor quantum devices such as HEMTs, RTDs, single-electron devices, spintronic devices, QWIPs, quantum cascade devices and single photon devices.
