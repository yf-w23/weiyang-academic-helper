课程内容简介：
本课程是在电气工程及其自动化本科专业核心课《电力电子技术基础》的教学基础上，开展 各类电力电子变换器及电机控制的探索性设计教学。基于《电力电子技术基础》的学习内容 和课外自学，调研并理解AC-DC整流器、DC-AC逆变器、隔离型/非隔离型DC-DC变换器和异步 电机/永磁同步电机驱动控制的技术方案，并学习、掌握各类变换器的最新技术进展及工程 应用情况。 学习并掌握主要类型电力电子变换器的仿真分析和软硬件设计方法，包括：1）基于 MATLAB/SIMULINK和国产自主仿真软件DSIM的变换器及电机控制仿真方法；2）AC-DC整流 器、DC-AC逆变器、隔离型/非隔离型DC-DC变换器的功率电路、控制电路、采样保护电路的 硬件设计方法，包括Altium Designer电路设计软件使用方法；3）AC-DC整流器、DC-AC逆变 器、隔离型/非隔离型DC-DC变换器以及异步电机调速、永磁同步电机调速的DSP控制程序设 计方法。基于上述分析和设计方法，开展三相PWM整流器、单相/三相PWM并网逆变器、双向 AC-DC变换器、开关电源变换器、异步电机调速系统、永磁同步电机调速系统等项目的整体 设计，包括硬件电路设计和软件程序编写。 根据设定的题目和要求，实际动手研制三相PWM整流器、单相/三相PWM并网逆变器、双向AC- DC变换器、开关电源变换器、异步电机调速系统、永磁同步电机调速系统等实验样机，并进 行系统调试和实验测试，针对测试得到的实验数据，计算性能指标，并与设计方案与仿真结 果进行对比，研究进一步改进和提升性能的方案。根据研制的电力电子变换器及电机驱动控 制样机，探索先进电力电子技术在新能源发电、电力系统、高端电源、电气化交通装备等领 域的最新应用，探讨电力电子技术在实现国家“双碳”目标和建设新型电力系统进程中的重 要作用。
Course Description
This course is to carry out exploratory design teaching of various power electronic converters and motor control based on the teaching of “Fundamentals of Power Electronics Technology”, which is one of the core course of Electrical Engineering and Automation Major. Investigate and understand the technical schemes of AC-DC rectifier, DC-AC inverter, isolated/non-isolated DC-DC converter and induction motor/ permanent magnet synchronous motor drive control, and learn and master the latest technical progress and engineering application of various converters. Learn the simulation analysis and software and hardware design methods, including: 1) Converter and motor control simulation methods based on MATLAB/Simulink and domestic independent simulation software DSIM; 2) Hardware design methods of power circuit, control circuit and sampling protection circuit of AC-DC rectifier, DC-AC inverter and isolated/non-isolated DC-DC converter; 3) Design method of DSP control program for AC-DC rectifier, DC-AC inverter, isolated/non-isolated DC-DC converter, induction motor adjustable speed drive and permanent magnet synchronous motor adjustable speed drive.. According to the required topics and requirements, the three-phase PWM rectifier, single-phase/three-phase PWM grid-connected inverter, bidirectional AC-DC converter, switching power supply, induction motor drive system, permanent magnet synchronous motor drive system and other experimental prototypes are actually developed. According to the developed power electronic converter and motor drive prototypes, explore the latest applications of advanced power electronic technology in renewable generation, power system, high-end power supply, electrified transportation equipment and other fields, and explore the important role of power electronic technology in realizing the “Carbon peak, Carbon neutrality” goal and building a new power system.
