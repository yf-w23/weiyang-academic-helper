AI制药方法与实践

课程简介：
药物发现是生物学、化学、医学、药学等基础研究与工业转化的重要窗口。近年来，AI技术的发展，为高投入、高失败率的制药行业带来了全新机遇，或将彻底改变传统制药的研究范式。本课程针对AI制药这一领域的基础与前沿问题，深入学习制药相关的AI算法，融合化学、生物学、医学、药学、数学与计算机等多个学科，引导学生进行学科交叉，并提高解决实际问题的能力。本课程的目标是使学生通过学习人工智能在药学应用的案例（包括计算机实验课程），理解数据驱动的建模方法，结合自身专业背景（如生物学、化学、医学、药学等），针对特定科学问题（如化合物性质预测，药物设计，多组学数据分析，实验条件优化等），具备利用已有数据优化实验的能力，探索更经济的实验方案来完成研究生期间的科学课题。本课程将简单回顾线性代数与概率论的基本知识，讨论制药相关的数据类型（包括小分子、蛋白、组学数据等）以及编程方法，深入分析当前主流的AI算法包括Transformer、GNN等方法。本课程也讨论AI制药技术的发展趋势以及应用，并结合实际案例锻炼学生自主解决实际问题的能力。
英文课程简介：
Drug discovery is an important window for basic research and industrial transformation in biology, chemistry, medicine, and pharmacy. In recent years, the development of AI technology has brought new opportunities to the pharmaceutical industry with high investment and high failure rate, which could completely change the research paradigm of traditional drug discovery. This course will focus on the basic and cutting-edge problems in the field of AI-based drug discovery. We will discuss in-depth study of AI algorithms related to drug discovery, integrating chemistry, biology, medicine, pharmacy, mathematics, computer science and other disciplines, guiding students to cross disciplines, and will help students improve their abilities of solving practical problems. The goal of this course is to enable students with different background (such as biology, chemistry, medicine, pharmacy, etc.) to understand data-driven modeling methods by learning cases of AI-based drug discovery (including computer experiment courses), and solve specific scientific problems (such as compound property prediction, drug design, multi-omics data analysis, experimental condition optimization, etc.). We help student develop the ability to utilize existing data to optimize experiments, explore more economical experimental plans to complete scientific goals during graduate school. This course will briefly review the basic knowledge of linear algebra and probability theory, discuss pharmaceutical-related data types (including small molecules, proteins, omics data, etc.) and programming methods, and deeply analyze the current mainstream AI algorithms including Transformer, GNNs. We also discuss the trends and applications in AI-based drug discovery, combined with practical cases to exercise students' ability to solve practical problems independently.
