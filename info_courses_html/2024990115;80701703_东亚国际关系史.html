课程简介：
本课程旨在探讨 19 世纪末至今东亚国际关系的演变。课程将从古代、殖民期、战争期、冷战期和后冷战期五个阶段介绍东亚国际关系与地区秩序的变化。课程将讨论有关中国、日本、韩国和外部世界之间关系的几场经典学术辩论，这些学术成果不仅包括正统国关学术成果，还有近年国关学者与全球史学家的最新研究成果。 探索和分析一手档案资料是研究国际关系史必备的基础技能。通过撰写和展示课程论文，本课程力图提升学生基于一手档案撰写国际关系领域学术论文的能力
英文课程简介：
This course aims to explore the evolution of international relations in East Asia from the late 19th century to the present day. It introduces the changes in international relations and regional order in East Asia in five phases, namely the pre-modern period, colonial period, war period, Cold War period, and post-Cold War period. We will engage in several fundamental debates about the relationship between China, Japan, Korea and the outer world and introduce not only orthodox IR analyses but also newer approaches by IR scholars and global historians. The exploration and analysis of archival materials is a fundamental skill that needs to be acquired for the study of IR history. Through the writing and presentation of a research paper, this course provides an opportunity to improve your ability to write academic papers based on primary sources.
