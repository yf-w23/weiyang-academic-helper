课程内容简介：
基础工程（英）于2008年在土木系开设，主旨是吸收和借鉴国外同类课程的经验，提高三年级学生对基础工程专业课程的学习兴趣，强调培养学生如何运用先修课—土力学（一）的知识来分析、解决基础工程问题的能力。我们编译了一本英语世界中久负盛名的简明“基础工程”教材(Atkinson J.教授)作为教材，强调课程是教授如何运用土力学和工程力学的基本知识和方法，进行各类地基基础的设计与分析，以及影响的主要因素分析。每章均配有典型例题讲解。这种教材与教学方式，可以说与国外同类课比较接轨。与此同时，也与中文班的教学内容基本协调一致。
Course Description
The objective of the course is to enhance the interest of third-year students in the foundation engineering by drawing on the experience of similar courses abroad, and to emphasize the development of students' ability to solve foundation engineering problems by applying their knowledge from the prerequisite course, basic Soil Mechanics. We translated and complied a textbook "Foundation Engineering" (Prof. Atkinson J.), which is well known in the English-speaking world, and emphasized that the course teaches how to apply the basic knowledge and methods of geotechnics and Engineering mechanics to design and analyze various types of foundations, as well as to analyze the main factors affecting them. This kind of teaching materials and teaching methods can be said to be more in line with similar classes abroad. At the same time, it is also basically coordinated with the teaching contents of Chinese classes.
