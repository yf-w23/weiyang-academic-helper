课程简介：
本课程围绕人工智能大模型的安全与对齐展开，系统介绍大模型在快速发展过程中面临的安全风险与治理挑战。主要内容包括：对抗鲁棒性与模型脆弱性分析，大模型幻觉与可控性问题，数据安全与隐私保护，模型滥用与社会风险，价值观与人类意图对齐，安全评测方法与国际治理框架等。课程注重结合经典研究与最新进展，选取GPT-4、Claude、DeepSeek等前沿模型案例进行讲解，并通过论文导读与实验练习帮助学生理解方法原理与实践应用。通过学习，学生不仅能够掌握大模型安全与对齐的基本理论与关键技术，还能具备独立分析、发现并解决前沿安全问题的能力，为后续科研探索和产业应用打下坚实基础。课程同时强调跨学科视角，引导学生理解人工智能技术、伦理和政策的互动关系，提升综合判断与学术表达能力。
英文课程简介：
This course focuses on the safety and alignment of large-scale AI models, providing a systematic overview of the emerging risks and governance challenges accompanying their rapid development. Topics include adversarial robustness and model vulnerabilities, hallucination and controllability issues, data security and privacy protection, misuse and societal risks, value alignment with human intent, safety evaluation methodologies, and international governance frameworks. The course combines classical research with the latest advances, using case studies of cutting-edge models such as GPT-4, Claude, and DeepSeek to illustrate concepts. Students will engage in paper reading, discussions, and hands-on experiments to deepen their understanding of both theoretical foundations and practical applications. By completing this course, students will acquire essential knowledge and techniques in AI safety and alignment, develop the ability to independently analyze and address frontier problems, and build a solid foundation for future research or industrial applications. Emphasis is also placed on interdisciplinary perspectives, helping students understand the interplay between technology, ethics, and policy, and enhancing their capacity for critical judgment and scholarly communication.
