课程简介：
ChatGPT，DeepSeek等的诞生标志着人工智能革命的正式开始。但这一类的大模型的迭代升级对硬件算力的需求达到了惊人的2-3个月翻一番的程度，已经远超摩尔定律下的硬件算力增长速度。此外，大模型的一次训练使用成千上万块GPU（每块数百瓦），产生的碳排放甚至高达从地球开车去月球往返6趟的程度。相比于大模型，人脑的算力有过之而无不及，但功耗只有区区20瓦。这背后高度智能和超低能耗的原因，与人脑中具有自适应性的神经组织有密切关联。具备自适应性、可重构性的智能半导体芯片技术是“后摩尔”时代的典型技术，与人工智能技术的发展相辅相成。相比传统的半导体器件，自适应半导体器件的物理特性可以针对不同的信息处理任务进行改变，使得信息的处理效率、智能程度等都大大提高。 在全球半导体芯片技术竞争白热化的背景下，清华的同学更应该及时掌握半导体技术的最新发展趋势。 在人工智能重塑生产生活方式的背景下，本课程旨在培养能适应时代发展需求的学生。选修该课程的学生将掌握自适应半导体器件原理、相应的材料微观机理、基础应用以及其它与半导体技术相关的前沿拓展。课程将辅以自适应半导体器件与脑科学、与人工智能相交叉的主题，吸引同学关注科学技术前沿、深入体会学科交叉的优势、促进对主体知识的理解。
英文课程简介：
The birth of ChatGPT marks the official beginning of the artificial intelligence revolution. Models like GPT have demonstrated an astonishing rate of growth, doubling in size every 2-3 months, far surpassing the hardware computing power growth predicted by Moore's Law. Furthermore, training GPT requires thousands of GPUs, each consuming hundreds of watts of power, reaching energy consumption levels equivalent to making six round trips from Earth to the Moon by car. In comparison, the computational power of the human brain is on par with or even surpasses that of GPT, but it operates at a mere 20 watts of power. The remarkable intelligence and ultra-low power consumption of the human brain are closely associated with its adaptive neural organization. Adaptive and reconfigurable intelligent semiconductor chip technology represents a typical development in the post-Moore's Law era and complements the advancement of artificial intelligence technology. Compared to traditional semiconductor devices, adaptive semiconductor devices emphasize autonomous coordination between physical parameters and application scenarios, greatly enhancing information processing efficiency and intelligence. This course aims to cultivate students who can adapt to the needs of the times. Students who choose to take this course will acquire knowledge of the principles of adaptive semiconductor devices, corresponding material micro-mechanisms, fundamental applications, and other cutting-edge developments related to semiconductor technology. The course will incorporate interdisciplinary themes such as adaptive semiconductor devices in conjunction with neuroscience and artificial intelligence, attracting students' attention to the forefront of science and technology, providing a deep understanding of the advantages of interdisciplinary studies, and facilitating comprehension of the core knowledge in this field.
