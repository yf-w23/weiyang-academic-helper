课程内容简介：
讲授电磁场的基本定律，麦克斯韦方程组，静电场，标量电势，静电场问题的求解，恒定磁场，矢量磁势，标量磁势，时变电磁场，波动方程，坡印廷定理，能量和能流，平面电磁波，反射与折射，波导与谐振腔，时变场的标势与矢势，达朗伯方程，偶极辐射，天线辐射。
Course Description
In this course, we study the basic laws of electromagnetic fields, including Maxwell equations, electrostatics, scalar electric potential, solution of boundary problems in electrostatics, magnetostatics, vector magnetic potential, time-varying electromagnetic fields, wave equations, Poynting theorem, electromagnetic energy and power flow, plane electromagnetic waves, reflection and refraction, waveguides and resonators, scalar and vector potentials of time-varying fields, d'Alembert equations, and electromagnetic radiation by dipoles and antennas.
