课程简介：
　本课程旨在通过系统梳理具有代表性的日本近代文学批评文章及其相关论争，让学生不仅系统地把握到日本近代文学批评的发展史脉络及流变的过程，而且深入探讨近代日本社会及其各种思潮、小说·诗歌·散文等文学作品和评论之间的互动关系。本课以《日本近代文学评论选》（明治·大正篇、昭和篇）、日文重要相关文献以及其中文译本为抓手，依据该书的编年顺序，对日本近代文学的批评及评论史做一具体的研究。 　课程内容主要讨论的文本包括：明治时期的夏目漱石《文学评论》、森鸥外《言文论》、片上伸《无解决的文学》、石川啄木《时代闭塞之现状》、平冢雷鸟《元始、女性是太阳》；大正时期的武者小路实笃《关于新村》、厨川白村《出了象牙之塔》、有岛武郎《宣言一篇》；昭和时期的藏原惟人《通往无产阶级写实主义之路》、宫本显治《“败北”的文学》、小林秀雄《种种意匠》、中野重治《文学上的新官僚主义》、萩原朔太郎《回归日本》、中村光夫《对“近代”的疑惑》。
英文课程简介：
　This course aims to systematically review important works of modern Japanese literary criticism and the related debates, guiding students not only to understand the historical development and evolutionary process of modern Japanese literary criticism, but also to conduct an in-depth exploration of the interactive relationships among modern Japanese society, its various intellectual currents, literary works (including novels, poetry, and essays), and literary criticism. Using Selected Works of Modern Japanese Literary Criticism (Meiji-Taisho Volume and Showa Volume), essential Japanese reference materials, and their Chinese translations as main resources, it will follow the chronological order of the texts to concretely research the history of criticism and commentary in modern Japanese literature. 　The main texts discussed in the course include: from the Meiji period, Natsume Soseki's Literary Criticism, Mori Ogai's “On Spoken and Written Language”, Katakami Noburu's “Literature of No Solution”, Ishikawa Takuboku's “The Current State of Social Stagnation”, and Hiratsuka Raichō's “In the Beginning, Woman Was the Sun”; from the Taisho period, Mushanokoji Saneatsu's “About the New Village”, Kuriyagawa Hakuson's Out of the Ivory Tower, and Arishima Takeo's “A Manifesto”; and from the Showa period, Kurahara Korehito's “The Path to Proletarian Realism”, Miyamoto Kenji's “The Literature of ‘Defeat’”, Kobayashi Hideo's “Design in Different Forms”, Nakano Shigeharu's “The New Bureaucratism in Literature”, Hagiwara Sakutarō's Return to Japan, and Nakamura Mitsuo's “Doubts Regarding ‘Modernity’”.
