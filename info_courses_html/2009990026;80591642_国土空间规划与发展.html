国土空间规划与发展

课程简介：
长期以来，在规划理论的指导下，人们运用规划技术和方法来管理和引导物质空间的开发，对经济、社会、文化的发展和生态环境状况产生了重大的影响。国土空间规划是国家空间发展的指南、可持续发展的空间蓝图，是各类开发保护建设活动的基本依据，是落实发展规划和主体功能区战略的重要载体。本课程从我国新旧规划体系转型的时代背景出发，从公共政策的视角和基本特征切入，通过对我国发展规划、土地利用规划、城乡规划的发展历程和国外国土空间规划的趋势分析，关注我国新型城镇化进程中国土空间规划体系的建立与完善问题，培养研究生理解国土空间规划的重大理论和实践意义，掌握其规划编制审批体系、实施监督体系、法规政策体系和技术标准体系的基本知识点，以及国土空间规划统一用途管制的主要原理和实施途径。在此基础之上，能够对国土空间规划在城市治理中的作用进行研讨和分析。
英文课程简介：
With the guidance of planning theories, planning technologies and methods have been applied to manage and regulate the development of physical spaces, which has had significant impacts on economy, society, culture and ecological environment. China’s territorial spatial planning would regulate spatial development nationwide and be an official basis of all sorts of spatial development and conservation. It may also facilitate the sustainable development and be important carriers of development plans. In the transitional era of Chinese planning systems, development planning, land use planning and urban-rural planning in China, as well as international spatial planning systems will be analyzed from a perspective of public policy. The course will focus on the establishment and improvement of Chinese territorial spatial planning system in the process of new-type urbanization. Students will be trained to understand the great theoretical and practical significance of territorial spatial planning, master the basic knowledge of relevant planning formulation and approval system, implementation and supervision system, laws and regulations, and technical standards system. Moreover, main principles and approaches of development regulation will be introduced to enable students to independently study the function of territorial spatial planning in urban governance.
