课程内容简介：
本课程作为面向几乎零基础新生的专业选修课，旨在让信息类学生初步掌握大语言模型的独特研发模式、应用场景和实践动手能力。包括：大语言模型的上下文学习应用能力，基于大模型的端到端开发能力，大模型精调能力，数据标注 与整理能力， 课程包括讲授和大量的编程实践。学生将在助教的指导下完成若干次独立进行的小实验，并且组队开发完成一项大模型的真实应用。 通过本课程学生将学到： 1）掌握采用预训练大语言模型进行推理和上下文学习的方法； 2）掌握大语言模型的精调能力，分布式训练系统的使用和大模型质量的评估方法； 3）掌握大语言模型应用的端到端实践开发能力，完成一个大语言模型应用项目端到端的开发与演示； 4）了解大语言模型当前最新的应用场景以及前沿的研究问题； 5）锻炼进行团队合作能力，和展示应用项目的能力。
Course Description
As a professional elective course for new students with almost zero AI background, this course aims to enable information-related majors to initially grasp the unique R&D models, application scenarios, and practical hands-on coding skills of large language models (LLMs). It covers: the few-shot learning capabilities of LLMs, end-to-end development skills based on large models, LLM fine-tuning abilities, and data annotation and organization skills. The course consists of lectures and extensive programming practices. Under the guidance of teaching assistants, students will complete several independent small experiments and work in teams to develop a real-world application of large language models. Through this course, students will learn to: Master methods for inference and few-shot learning using pre-trained large language models; Acquire LLM fine-tuning skills, proficiency in distributed training systems, and methods for evaluating LLM quality; Develop end-to-end practical development capabilities for LLM applications, completing the end-to-end development and demonstration of an LLM application project; Understand the latest application scenarios of large language models and cutting-edge research issues; Enhance teamwork skills and the ability to present application projects.
