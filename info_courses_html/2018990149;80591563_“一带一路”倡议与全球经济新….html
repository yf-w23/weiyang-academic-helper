课程简介：
本课程谨以下列因素为背景组织“一带一路”倡议课程教学：古丝绸之路历史、工业文明史、中国关于发展、改革、开放、创新的理论与实践。本课程旨在描绘金融危机后的世界经济，分析中国在全球经济中的作用，并阐述中国和伙伴国家在推动“一带一路”建设过程中重要的经济理论。
英文课程简介：
This course endeavors to carry out the series of lectures on the “Belt and Road Initiative”, in accordance with the history of the Chinese nation, the ancient silk road, the industrial civilization, the theories and practices on Chinese national development, economic reform, opening-up, as well as innovation. This course intends to depict the world economy during the post-financial crisis period, to illustrate China’s role in the global economy, and to dwell upon the relevant economic theories that China and partner countries need to take stock from when they push forward the joint construction of the BRI.
