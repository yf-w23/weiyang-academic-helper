课程简介：
本课程主要讲授机器学习在通信中的应用，以通信技术为主线介绍了信息处理与传输、机器学习的理论与方法，具体内容有：1）基础部分：监督学习、无监督学习、深度学习、强化学习、联邦学习等；2）信道部分：信道建模、信道估计、CSI压缩、信道编码及调制等；3）接收机设计部分：OFDM信号检测、MIMO检测等；4）语义编码与语义通信部分：语义编码、语义-信道联合编码等。课程还包含2次智能通信前沿讲座和1个学生项目展示。
英文课程简介：
This course primarily focuses on the application of machine learning in communications, presenting theories and methods of information processing and transmission, as well as machine learning, with communication technology as the central theme. The specific content includes: 1) Fundamentals: supervised learning, unsupervised learning, deep learning, reinforcement learning, federated learning, etc.; 2) Channel aspects: channel modeling, channel estimation, CSI compression, channel coding and modulation, etc.; 3) Receiver design: OFDM signal detection, MIMO detection, etc.; 4) Semantic coding and semantic communication: semantic coding, joint semantic-channel coding. The course also includes two communication frontiers lecture and a student project presentation.
