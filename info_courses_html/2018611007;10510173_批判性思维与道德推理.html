课程内容简介：
习近平总书记在《深入实施新时代人才强国战略》中谈到人才自主培养之路时，他说：“突破常规，创新模式，更加重视科学精神、创新能力、批判性思维的培养教育。” 本着“扎根中国大地办大学”的基本精神，着力开出具有以下本土化特色的“批判性思维与道德推理”课程。 本课程的主要目标有两个：一个是如何读懂一部伦理经典著作；另一个是针对具体情境中的选择和决策，如何依据不同的道德原则得出最佳的道德判断。通过掌握以问题为导向的探究法、最佳解释推理、论证的微观布局图尔明模型、论证的宏观布局“正反正”框架，谬误分析法、假设分析法、关键性问题分析法和选择性解释分析法，以及撰写读书报告的CCPAAI框架，训练学生理解、构建和评估道德推理的思维技能。在训练思维技能的过程中，培养学生独立思考、公正客观、开放兼容、清晰准确、谦逊诚实等思维习性。 本课程的理念是：知识量×批判性思维=创新潜力。教学法采用大任务驱动的探究法。大任务是在实践中需要完成的真实任务，旨在解决课堂训练与实际应用的能力迁移问题。观念的变化牺牲最小，成就最大。批判性思维依靠得到充分根据支持的判断来改善自己的观念，迈向成功的人生。
Course Description
Secretary General Xi Jinping said in “Deeply Implementing the Strategy on Developing a Quality Workforce in the New Era” when he talked about the road of independent cultivation of talents: “Break through the routine, innovate the model, and pay more attention to the cultivation and education of scientific spirit, innovation ability and critical thinking.” In line with the basic spirit of “running a university rooted in the land of China”, we will focus on developing CTMR with the following localized characteristics. This course has two goals: one is how to read classical ethics; the other is how to make the best moral judgment based on different moral principles for choices or decisions in specific situations. By mastering problem-oriented inquiry, inference-to-best-explanation, the Toulmin model of micro-argument layout, the “A-B-A” framework for macro-argument layout, fallacies analysis, assumptions analysis, critical problems analysis and alternative explanations analysis, and CCPAAI framework for writing book reports, students’ thinking skills to understand, construct and evaluate moral reasoning are trained. In the process of training skills, students should develop thinking dispositions such as independent thinking, fairness and objectivity, openness and compatibility, clarity and accuracy, humility and integrity. The idea of this course is: Amount of Knowledge × Critical Thinking = Innovation Potential. The pedagogy adopts a big task-driven inquiry method. Big tasks are real tasks that need to be completed in practice, aiming to solve the problem of ability transfer between classroom training and practical application. The change of thinking mode has the least sacrifice and the greatest achievement. Thoughts lead to action. Critical thinking relies on well-founded judgment to improve one’s own perceptions and lead to a successful life.
