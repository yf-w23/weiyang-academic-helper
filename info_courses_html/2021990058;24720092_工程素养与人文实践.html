工程素养与人文实践

课程内容简介：
本课程为日新书院本科生暑期人文实践环节的建设内容。立足现代文明与工业制造，回看创造性（手工）工艺技术，反思人类文明的进步流变，将东西方对照比较工程与人文之间的关联，积极展望未来文明的发展态势，探索人文与工程融合的发展方向。 通过设计建立人文与工程的连接，基于文化、历史、哲学背景的思考，培养学生的思辨能力、动手能力，感受工程中的力量与方向，提升人文和工程融合的素养，同时在实践过程中反思制造、科技、设计与文明之间错综复杂的关联与牵绊。课程以相关理论为基础，伴以动手实践、研讨、课外自学等多种学习方式。在实践和理论的紧密连接过程中逐步感受工程的力量与人文的温度之间的平衡。
Course Description
This course is the construction content of humanistic practice link for undergraduates in Rixin Academy in summer vacation. Based on modern civilization and industrial manufacturing, review creative (manual) technology, reflect on the progress and evolution of human civilization, compare the relationship between engineering and humanities between the East and the West, actively look forward to the development trend of future civilization, and explore the development direction of the integration of humanities and engineering. Establish the connection between humanities and engineering through design, based on the thinking of cultural, historical and philosophical background, cultivate students' thinking ability and practical ability, feel the strength and direction in engineering, improve the quality of integration of humanities and engineering, and reflect on the complex relationship and tie between manufacturing, science and technology, design and civilization in the process of practice. The course is based on relevant theories, accompanied by hands-on practice, discussion, extracurricular self-study and other learning methods. In the process of close connection between practice and theory, gradually feel the balance between the strength of engineering and the temperature of humanity.
