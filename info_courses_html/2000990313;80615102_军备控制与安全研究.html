课程简介：
The course teaches the concepts, issues, ideas, theories and analytical methods in arms control from the perspective of international relations. It explains the debates in arms control and helps the students learn to analyze arms control issues.

Content (may be adjusted)

Unit 1. Concepts in Arms Control and Security Studies

       1.1 course arrangement

       1.2 the use of terms in arms control and security study

       1.3 arms control measures      

Unit 2. Calculation of National Interests in Arms Control

       2.1 rational analysis in national security policy making

       2.2 security dilemma and its cooperative solution

       2.3 power competition     

Unit 3. Strategic Stabilities -- Rational Analysis

       3.1 nuclear deterrence

       3.2 strategic stability

       3.3 offense & defense      

Unit 4. Strategic Stabilities -- Accidents and Miscalculations

       4.1 inadvertent nuclear launch

       4.2 avoiding inadvertent nuclear war      

Unit 5. Beyond National Security

       5.1 negative correlations between weapons and security

       5.2 non-traditional security

       5.3 global security

       5.4 human security   

Unit 6. The Constructivist Views

       6.1 social construction of preferential order in national security

       6.2 research methods

       6.3 ideational basis in arms control decision making

       6.4 applications

Unit 7. Domestic Politics in Arms Control

       7.1 sub-state actors in arms control policy making

       7.2 multiple channels of arms control diplomacy

       7.3 arms control as a subject of public policy      

Unit 8. Arms Control and International Law

       8.1 legal aspects

       8.2 research methods

       8.3 jus ad bellum & jus in bello       

Unit 9. Nuclear Security

       9.1 nuclear terrorism

       9.2 nuclear security & related regimes      

Unit 10. Nuclear Nonproliferation

       10.1 Nuclear Non-Proliferation Treaty

       10.2 other regimes

       10.3 cases   

Unit 11. Missile and Space Issues

       11.1 missile proliferation and control

       11.2 missile defense

       11.3 outer space 

Unit 12. Nuclear Arms Control and Disarmament

       12.1 strategic arms control: limitations and reductions

       12.2 nuclear test ban & fissile material cut-off

       12.3 security assurance

       12.4 confidence building 

Unit 13. Biological, Chemical and Conventional Arms Control

       13.1 chemical disarmament

       13.2 biological weapons & biosecurity

       13.3 conventional arms control      

Unit 14. Arms Control Related to Cyber & AI  

       14.1 arms control in cyber space

       14.2 artificial intelligence & arms control  

Unit 15. Summary
