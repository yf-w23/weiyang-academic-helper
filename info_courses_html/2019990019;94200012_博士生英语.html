课程简介：
《学术论文写作与发表》是一门持续16周，包含32课时的英语必修课程，主要面向准备或正在用英文撰写学术论文的同学。基于“产出导向”的教学理念，本课程引导研究生同学结合自己的研究课题进行英文学术论文的阅读、写作及发表，拓展同学们对英文学术写作的认知，培养用英文撰写研究论文的能力，并增强国际学术交流与发表的自信心，旨在帮助同学们学会用简洁、清晰、准确、恰当的学术英语将前沿的科研成果及学术观点传递出去。
英文课程简介：
Guided by the production-oriented approach, this course aims to scaffold doctoral students in the process of writing and publishing English academic papers based on their undergoing research projects, and to help students effectively develop academic literacy to deliver their voice as novice researchers in the international academic arena.To be more specific, this course intends to provide doctoral students with: an improved understanding of the overall structure and underlying logic of research articles published in English in the international literature; skills for analyzing the moves of each section of published articles in their own disciplines, and for using the results of this analysis to improve their own writing; knowledge and mastery of the specific English language features commonly used in each section of published articles; strategies and tools for improving your own drafts, such as structured peer review checklists, ways to re-use relevant language elements, and special-purpose software; a process for completing a draft of an article on their own research projects, prepared in the style of the journals or conferences to which they wish to submit; and strategies for presenting at international conferences, such as learning from TED talks, visual elements, establishing a bond with the audience, and creating posters.

课程主要学习内容包括：

ü英文研究论文的整体结构和基本逻辑

ü学术英语的特点

ü通用学术词汇的训练

ü常见语法问题的诊断

ü论文主要部分（包括标题、摘要、引言、方法、结果和讨论）的功能、语步及语言特征分析

ü写作技巧和工具（如语料库建设、写作辅助软件、文献引用、句式挖掘、衔接和连贯、避免冗余等）

ü国际会议发言技巧（如结构条理、语速语调、身体语言、观众交流、视觉元素、海报展示等）
