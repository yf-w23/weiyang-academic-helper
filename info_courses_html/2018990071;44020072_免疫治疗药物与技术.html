课程内容简介：
近年来，免疫治疗药物的研发取得了长足的进展，并且在临床上得到了广泛的应用。与传统药物相比，免疫治疗类药物在研究方法、作用机理、应用技术、疗效评估、毒理等方面均存在显著的不同。本课程将介绍免疫治疗药物的作用机理、临床应用以及相关研究的技术方法，并结合当前免疫治疗药物开发的前沿研究与成果进行深入的讲解与讨论。课程涵盖范围包括免疫学的相关基本概念、免疫治疗药物的作用机理、基础与应用研究中涉及的方法技术、免疫治疗药物最新的研发进展、未来的发展趋势等等。本课程旨在通过学习了解免疫治疗与药物研发的相关理论与技术方法，在该过程中培养学生发现问题与解决问题的能力。
Course Description
During the last decade, significant progresses have been achieved in the development of drugs targeting the immune system. Several immunotherapy drugs have been widely used in the clinic. Comparing to conventional therapeutics, immunotherapy drugs are significantly different in multiple levels, such as methodology, mechanism, technology, assessment of efficacy, and toxicology. The course will introduce and discuss about the mechanisms of immunotherapy, clinical applications, related technologies and methodologies. The contents include basic concepts in immunology, applications and mechanisms of immunotherapy drugs, technologies involved in immunotherapy research and industry, advances and future directions in drug developments for immunotherapy. The course aims to help students gain a comprehensive knowledge about the development of immunotherapy drugs. During this process, they will build the capabilities to find and solve certain questions.
