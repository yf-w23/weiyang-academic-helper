课程简介：
健康心理学由四部分内容组成。第一部分介绍健康心理学的基础知识，包括：健康心理的标准；我们的内心世界是怎样构成的；我们是用“认识”去认识他人的等具体内容。第二部分介绍情绪及其情绪调节，包括：情境究竟是什么；情绪困扰的总根源；情绪调节策略等具体内容。第三部分介绍不同层次的心理问题及其治疗，包括：心灵层次的问题及其治疗（精神分析治疗，认知行为治疗和理性情绪治疗），生命层次的问题及其治疗（瑜伽治疗）。第四部分介绍健康心理学的应用，包括：情绪与决策；治疗不知足和说谎等具体内容。
英文课程简介：
Health Psychology includes four parts: The first part is introduction, which is consisted of four lectures: what is the criteria of health psychology; how is our mind constructed (2); how do we learn others. In the second part, we introduce emotion, which is consisted of four lectures: what is emotion (2); what is the fundamental cause of emotional disturbance; how do we regulate emotion. In the third part, we introduce psychological problems and therapy at different conscious levels, which is consisted of four lectures: psychoanalytic treatment; cognitive-behavioral therapy; rational-emotive therapy; yoga therapy. The final part is applications of health psychology, in which we discuss about emotion and decision-making; treatment of greedy and lying.
