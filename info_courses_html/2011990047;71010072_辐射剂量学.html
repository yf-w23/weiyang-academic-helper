课程简介：
辐射剂量学，也称电离辐射剂量学，是核科学与技术领域辐射安全及辐射防护方向的核心内容，是一门研究辐射与物质的相互作用及有效定量辐射效应的应用基础学科。本课程重点阐述辐射与物质相互作用、辐射剂量与辐射效应定量关系以及辐射防护基本原则，讲解内、外照射剂量计算和辐射剂量测量，分析三次重大核事故辐射环境影响，明确辐射剂量学在核科学与核工程中的重要角色和作用。 本课程旨在提高研究生的辐射安全学术素养，致力于三个层面的目标： （1）知识层面： 使学生能够熟练掌握电离辐射场特征、辐射与物质相互作用、辐射剂量与生物效应等基本知识和理论，科学评价三次重大核事故的辐射后果，建立电离辐射场-辐射剂量-辐射效应的知识框架体系。 （2）能力层面： 加强学生对物理概念和物理图像的理解，培养学生的物理直觉，具有较强的分析计算能力、动手实践能力、凝练关键科学问题的能力，能够运用所学的知识方法分析和解决生活工作中与辐射相关的问题。 （3）价值层面： 注重熏陶学生对学术大师的为人为学和科技进步的历史过程认识，使学生能够科学全面的看待事物，理解核能核电发展的复杂性和重要性，增强自身对从事核能科学研究的价值感和使命感。
英文课程简介：
Radiation dosimetry, also known as ionizing radiation dosimetry, is the core content of radiation safety and radiation protection in the field of nuclear science and technology. It is an applied basic discipline which studies the interaction between radiation and matter and the effective quantification of radiation effect. This course emphasizes on the interaction between radiation and matter, quantitative relationship between radiation dose and radiation effect, as well as the basic principles of radiation protection, illustrates the dose calculation of internal and external exposure and radiation dose measurement, analyzes the radiation environmental impacts from the three major nuclear accidents, and indicates the important role of radiation dosimetry in nuclear science and engineering. This course aims to enhance the academic literacy of students in radiation safety: (1) Knowledge level To enable students to master the basic knowledge and theory of the characteristics of ionizing radiation field, interaction between radiation and matter, radiation dose and biological effect, scientifically evaluate the radiation consequences of the three major nuclear accidents, and establish the knowledge framework system of ionizing radiation field-radiation dose-radiation effect. (2) Competence level To enable students to strengthen their understanding of physical concepts and physical images, cultivate their physical intuition, have strong ability of analysis and calculation, hands-on practice and summarizing key scientific problems, and use the knowledge and methods learned to solve problems related to radiation in life and work. (3) Value level To enable students to cultivate the humanism from the academic masters and historical process of science and technology, view things in a scientific and comprehensive way, understand the complexity and importance of the development of nuclear power, and enhance their senses of value and mission in nuclear energy research.
