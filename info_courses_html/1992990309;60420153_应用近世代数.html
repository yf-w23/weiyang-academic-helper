课程简介：
近世代数是现代数学的重要基础之一，在计算机科学、信息与通讯、物理、化学等领域有广泛的应用。 本课程讲授群、环、域的基本理论。希望通过本课程的学习使学生比较熟练地掌握抽象代数的基本 理论与方法，结合具体的例子理解抽象代数中的数学思想和思维方法，培养工科学生应用代数方法建立数学模型、分析与解决有关学科中的理论问题的能力，使学 生的抽象思维能力得到系统的训练和提高。 主要内容有集合与映射、二元关系、整数与多项式、群与子群、Lagrange定理及其应用、正规子群和共轭子群、组合计数理论、环与域、整环中的因式分解、欧氏环、域的扩张、有限群、密码学等。
英文课程简介：
Modern algebra is one of the fundamental pillars of contemporary mathematics and finds extensive applications in fields such as computer science, information and communication, physics, chemistry, and others. This course covers the basic theories of groups, rings, and fields. The aim is to enable students to achieve a relatively proficient grasp of the fundamental theories and methods of abstract algebra through this course. By integrating concrete examples, students will comprehend the mathematical ideas and thought processes in abstract algebra, cultivate the ability to apply algebraic methods to construct mathematical models, analyze and solve theoretical problems in related disciplines, and systematically train and enhance their abstract thinking abilities. The main contents include sets and mappings, binary relations, integers and polynomials, groups and subgroups, Lagrange's theorem and its applications, normal subgroups and conjugate subgroups, combinatorial enumeration theory, rings and fields, factorization in integral domains, Euclidean domains, field extensions, finite groups, cryptography, and more.
