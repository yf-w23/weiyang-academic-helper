课程内容简介：
课程总体框架确定为“系统思维、实践本位、文化视角”，采用大班上课+小班实 践研讨的方式进行教学组织，课程分三个层次同步进行，（1）梳理工业文明，了解工 程文化；（2）剖析实体产业案例，体验系统科学思维；（2）开展团队研讨，实践工 程项目。 通过工业文明史梳理、实体产业案例研讨、小班探究实践等形式展开探究式自 主学习，有助于学生了解工业文明、接触产业实际、获得工业体验、感受工程文化、 熏陶系统思维，培养学生自主学习的能力，强化严谨的工作作风、逻辑思维和团 队合作精神。
Course Description
The overall framework of the course is determined as "system engineering thinking, practice standard and cultural perspective". The teaching organization is carried out in the way of large class + small class practice discussion. The course is carried out synchronously at three levels: (1) sort out industrial civilization and understand engineering culture; (2) Analyze the cases of real industry and experience systematic scientific thinking; (2) Carry out team discussion and practice engineering projects. Through combing the history of industrial civilization, case study of physical industry, small class inquiry practice and other forms to carry out inquiry based autonomous learning, it is helpful for students to understand industrial civilization, contact industrial reality, obtain industrial experience, feel engineering culture, edify system engineering thinking, cultivate students' ability of autonomous learning, and strengthen rigorous work style Logical thinking and teamwork spirit.
