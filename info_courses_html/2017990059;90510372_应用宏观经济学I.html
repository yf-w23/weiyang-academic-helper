课程简介：
本课程旨在为学生提供研究经济活动空间分布的宏观经济学理论基础和定量分析工具，强调区域间和区域内的异质性。课程由三部分构成。第一部分基于定量贸易、定量空间和定量城市经济学文献，讲授引力模型、空间均衡、投入产出关联、交通网络和动态空间模型。第二部分引入异质性个体模型，研究区域内的不平等和生命周期动态。第三部分将前两部分有机结合，讲授含有区域内异质性的空间模型、不确定性下的贸易、贸易与技术的互动，以及非合作贸易与产业政策。课程应用聚焦中国区域经济，涵盖跨区域溢出效应、伴随不平等的增长、老龄化、人口迁移、基础设施、产业政策和地缘政治等议题。
英文课程简介：
The aim of this course is to prepare students with theoretical and quantitative tools for macroeconomic research on the spatial distribution of economic activities, emphasizing both inter-region and within-region heterogeneity. The first part builds on the quantitative trade, quantitative spatial, and quantitative urban literatures, covering gravity models, spatial equilibrium, input-output linkages, transportation networks, and dynamic spatial models. The second part introduces heterogeneous agent models for studying within-region inequality and lifecycle dynamics. The final part integrates these two strands, covering spatial models with within-region heterogeneity, trade under uncertainty, and the interaction between trade and technology. Applications focus on China's regional economy, including cross-region spillovers, growth with inequality, aging, migration, infrastructure, industrial policy, and geopolitics.
