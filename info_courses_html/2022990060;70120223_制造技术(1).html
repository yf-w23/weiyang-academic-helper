课程简介：
面向工程技术专业的同学，重点讲授现代制造技术所涉及到的关键概念、技术原理、典型应用以及未来发展趋势等。在以课堂授课为主体的基础上，配套有必要的技术实验和生产观摩课时，以增强学生对现代制造技术及其工程运用的理解，强化学生对于工程领域应用知识的能力，培养学生国际化思维以及团队协作管理的复合能力。
英文课程简介：
This course introduces a wide range of manufacturing processes used in modern industry, including machining and assembly. For each process, I will explain the underlying physical principles, provide some practical examples and demonstrations, and summarize the manufacturing principles. In addition, we will also discuss how the material properties of a product affect the performance of manufacturing processes. Together, this knowledge will enable you to plan a manufacturing process for a multi-part product, evaluate its performance, and recognize critical constraints and tradeoffs. Students of all engineering disciplines are encouraged to take this course.
