课程简介：
通过本课程学习，可以使学生掌握发表国际期刊论文的基础知识和规范，并学习如何在国际会议上作学术报告。
英文课程简介：
The course will cover those of aspects of grammar and style most relevant for the preparation of scientific publications. The objective of the course is to make your writing clearer and stronger. Other topics covered include the review process and how to proof-read a paper. The course content will be delivered in English.
