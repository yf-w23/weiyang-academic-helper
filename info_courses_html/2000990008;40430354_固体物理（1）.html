课程内容简介：
讲述固体量子理论的基本概念，重点是晶体结构和周期结构中的电子态。主要内容包括：晶体的周期结构和对称性，倒易点阵和衍射，晶体的分类和结合，晶格振动，声子，晶体热学性质，自由电子费米气体，能带理论，计算能带的赝势方法和紧束缚近似，金属的电子论，外电场和外磁场中的电子态，半导体电子论，晶体的光学性质和激子等。
Course Description
This course covers the fundamental theories and knowledge of solid-state physics, focusing on the following main topics: crystal structure, crystal bonding, lattice vibrations, phonons and thermal properties, electronic band theory of solids, electronic states of solids in external electric and magnetic fields, metallic theory, semiconductor theory and optical properties.
进度安排：

1-8周：Periodic Structure and Symmetry of Crystalsl;Diffraction, Reciprocal Latticel; Chemical Bonding; Lattice Dynamics; Phonons;Thermal Properties; Free Electron Gas; Bloch Theorem and Band Structure; Nearly Free Electron Approximation：Psedopotential Method; Tight Binding Method;

9-16周：Metalsl;Fermi Surface;Semiconductors; Electrons and Holes; Impurities; Optical Properties; Excitons; Superconductors (mainly in solid state II); Magnetism(mainly in solid state II)
