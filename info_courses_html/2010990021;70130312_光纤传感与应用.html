课程简介：
课程首先介绍了光纤光学的一些基础知识，包括：光纤传输基本理论、光纤的色散和损耗以及模式分布特性；接着介绍常用的一些光纤器件，在此基础上，概括性介绍光纤传感的基本原理和应用中所涉及的各种调制技术。选取并深入分析5个典型的应用；每个应用课程后，配套有2学时的讨论课或实验课，由学生根据前一课堂的知识点，选择对应的文献开展调研和准备课堂汇报与讨论，或者开展相应的实验加深对课程内容的理解。课程最后，由学生结合课程内容与研究课题，设计一个光纤传感系统，详细分析其原理和技术特点及性能指标。本课程注重对学生阅读文献、分析问题、多学科融合、创新设计与深入研究等综合能力的培养。
英文课程简介：
The curriculum will introduce the basic knowledge of fiber optics, including theory of field transmission, dispersion, loss and mode distribution characteristics in optical fiber. Then we will introduce several common fiber devices. And bases on that, the principles of optical fiber sensing techniques and related modulation methods are presented in brief, accompanying with five typical and thoroughly illustrated applications. After each applications-oriented course, there will be two class hours for discussion, where students will give a presentation according to the relative literature researches. At the ending of this curriculum, students are required to design an optical fiber sensing system based on the class and research, as well as analyze the principle and performance specifically. This curriculum concentrates on cultivating the comprehensive capability in terms of references reading, problem analyzing, multidisciplinary integration, innovative design and profound researching.
