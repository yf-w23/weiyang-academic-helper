课程简介：
本课程旨在培养研究生的基本科学规范以及科学表达能力（包括写作和口头表达），为研究生成为一个严谨的、出色的科学工作者打下基础。课程内容偏重自然科学和交叉学科研究方面，以生物信息学和机器学习方向的科学研究为主要背景，对于其他专业的研究生也有一定参考价值。课堂教学将中英文混合，适合中国学生和国际学生学习。
英文课程简介：
This course aims to provide an introduction to some basic codes in scientific research and expositions, to help graduate students to form a solid base for the future academic success. Skills in both written and oral presentations will also be practised through the course. The course materials will be mostly from the field of bioinformatics and machine learning, which students from other fields can also find valuable contents. The course will be given in mixed English and Chinese, and will welcome International students whose Chinese skills are challenged.
