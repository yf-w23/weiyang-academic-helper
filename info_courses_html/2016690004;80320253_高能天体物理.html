课程简介：
本课程主要讲授高能天体物理相关的观测方法、天文现象和涉及的基本物理过程。可能的具体内容包括天文X射线和伽马射线探测和数据处理方法；X射线和伽马射线源包括X射线双星、脉冲星、超新星遗迹等；高能暂现暴发现象包括潮汐瓦解事例、快速射电薄、伽马射线暴等；及吸积、辐射、粒子加速等物理过程。
英文课程简介：
The course covers key observational techniques, astrnomonical phenomena, and related physical processes in the field of high energy astrophysics. The topics may include X-ray and gamma-ray detection and data analysis methodology; X-ray and gamma-ray sources including X-ray binaries, pulsars, and supernova remnants; high-energy transient phenomena including tidal disruption events, fast radio bursts, gamma-ray bursts; and physical processes including accretion, radiation, and particle acceleration.
