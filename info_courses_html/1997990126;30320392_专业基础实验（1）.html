课程内容简介：
该课程主要针对与工物系目前在核科学与技术、物理学以及安全科学与技术三大研究方向而设立的相应专业基础实验。在保证基本实验技能训练的基础上，充分考虑不同研究方向在实验技能及基本专业知识上的要求。整个课程目前包括了十几个大项专业基础实验，在条件成熟时还将包括其它实验。学生在完成辐射物理与探测以及反应堆物理实验课的基础上，可以根据兴趣选修其它方向的实验。
Course Description
This course is an experimental class, requiring students to choose at least 64 experiment hours, the experiment covers 11 major fields of our department, Radiation detection experiments (32 experiment hours) is compulsory, the other for elective. Over part can be used for Specialized Fundamental Experiments （2）. The major subjects involved in the experiment include: Radiation Detection, Nuclear Electronics, Strong Electromagnetic Environment Measurement, Radiation Protection, Plasma Photoelectric Interesting Experiments, Magnetic Confinement Fusion and High Temperature Plasma Physics, High Energy Cosmic Ray Particles Detected , Measurement Technology, Nuclear Technology Application ,Passive Thermal Hydraulic and Safety, Nuclear Fuel Cycle and Materials Plasma Science and Technology.
