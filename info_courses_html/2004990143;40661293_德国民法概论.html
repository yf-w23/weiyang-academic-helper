课程内容简介：
“德国民法概论”是关于德国民法的入门课程，其目的在于使选课本科生和研究生通过一个学期的学习，获得了解德国民法主要内容和基本方法的门径，为进一步学好民法原理、规则和方法打下坚实的基础。 主要讲授内容包括：德国私法概说；法律行为；权利；权利主体；权利客体；债务关系；法学方法论。 主要学习材料是德国民法典，但不限于法典条文的解释。要求选课学生熟悉德国民法典最重要的条文，了解请求权基础，能够运用所学原理解决实际问题，尤其是严格地使用“专家意见书”格式分析民法案例。 自本课程于2005年春季学期开设以来，授课教师已讲授十余次。拟自2018年春季学期起继续开课。授课教师将运用自己在德国马普外国私法与国际私法研究所学到的知识为大家讲解真实、地道和现行的德国民法，并加强同德国民法教授的合作，每年邀请德国汉堡大学法学院教授博尔克（Prof. Dr. Reinhard Bork）等德国民法学家来清华大学法学院授课或做讲座，为选课学生带来原汁原味的德国民法课。
Course Description
"An Introduction to German Civil Law is an undergraduate course taught at Tsinghua University School of Law since February 2005 by Prof. Dr. Dr. Chen Weizuo who is the translator of the Chinese version of the German Civil Code. On the basis of the provisions in force of the German Civil Code, the course guides the students to the fundamentals of German civil law, aiming to teach them the genuine, elementary and useful principles, rules and methodologies relating to the General Part of the German Civil Code within a relatively short time and in a scientific manner. In addition, the course provides the newest materials on German civil law for the students so that they are informed of the latest development of German civil law. Focusing on the General Part of the German Civil Code, the course also takes account of the Special Part of the German Civil Code. A large number of typical cases are to be analyzed, requiring the students to be familiar with the most important provisions of the German Civil Code and their application in judicial practice. "
