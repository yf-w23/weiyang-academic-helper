课程简介：
本课程主要讲授二十世纪迅速发展起来的固体表面分析技术，包括：电子显微镜(SEM)、扫描隧道显微镜(STM)、原子力显微镜(AFM)等微、纳尺度下形貌分析技术；俄歇电子能谱(AES)、X光电子能谱（XPS）、二次离子质谱(SIMS)等材料成分分析技术；以及X射线衍射(XRD)、高能及低能电子衍射等固体结构分析技术。 目前它们已被广泛应用，并将对未来科学技术（包括信息光电子学、微纳电子学、纳米科学技术、材料科学、生命科学等）的发展起到重要的支撑作用。 本课程的主要目标是让学生掌握： （1）各种现代分析技术的基本原理、发展思路、特点、主要分析方法及应用范围。 （2）从要求得到的材料特性，会寻找并确定应采用的分析手段。从测量的结果，会正确提取有关材料特性的信息（会读识文献中提供的有关图象及谱图）。 本课程将安排学生在课下时间，参观课堂上所讲授的分析仪器，了解世界最先进的测试技术，向最有经验的一线工作者请教分析技术。
英文课程简介：
This course focuses on modern analytical techniques that have been rapidly developed in the 20th century, including micro- and nano-scale topography analysis techniques such as electron microscopy (SEM), scanning tunneling microscopy (STM), and atomic force microscopy (AFM); Auger electron energy AES, X-ray photoelectron spectroscopy (XPS), secondary ion mass spectrometry (SIMS) and other material analysis techniques; and X-ray diffraction (XRD), high-energy and low-energy electron diffraction based solid structure analysis technology. The main goal of this course is for students to master the followings: (1) The basic principles, development ideas, characteristics, main analysis methods and application scope of various modern analytical techniques. (2) The characteristics of the material obtained from the request will find and determine the analytical means to be used. From the results of the measurement, information on the material properties will be extracted correctly (the relevant images and spectra provided in the literature will be read). This course also arranges students to spend time to visit some labs, where there are the analytical instruments taught in the class, to help them understand the world's most advanced techniques, and consult the most experienced engineers about using analytical techniques.
