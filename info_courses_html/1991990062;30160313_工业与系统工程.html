课程内容简介：
1.定义工业与系统工程，介绍这个专业的历史沿革和未来趋势。介绍中国和其它国家的工业工程专业； 2.工业工程主要分支介绍： a) 运筹与统计 b) 人因工程 c) 生产系统 d) 医疗系统 3.课程总体结构：本课程帮助各专业背景学生了解什么是工业工程，并掌握工业工程师所需的相关技能。课程中将设计一个团队竞赛项目，学生以小组形式，将在课程中学到的知识用于解决竞赛问题。课堂主要环节包括： a)通过讲座/讨论了解基本概念。 b)分小组实践完成特定的团队项目。 c)问答环节。
Course Description
1. Define Industrial and Systems Engineering and introduce the historical development and future trends of this profession. Introduce industrial engineering majors in China and other countries; 2. Introduction to the main branches of industrial engineering: a) Operations Research and Statistics b) Human factors and ergonomics c) Production system d) Healthcare system 3. Course structure: This course helps students from various major backgrounds understand what industrial engineering is and master the relevant skills required for industrial engineering. A team competition project will be designed in the course, where students will use the knowledge learned in the course to solve competition problems in small groups. The main classroom activities include: a) Understand basic concepts through lectures/discussions. b) Practice in small groups to complete specific team projects. c) Q&A session.

10-15周：为竞赛周，针对企业实际问题，分组开展研究

15-16周：比赛和总结周
