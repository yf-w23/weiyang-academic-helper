课程简介：
本课程面向天线新的系统需求和人工电磁媒质的前沿技术，以天线关键技术的方式展开对天线理论的讲解；包括三大方面： 面向系统需求包括：移动终端天线、基站天线、MIMO天线、机载\车载天线、封装天线、雷达天线、及毫米波、太赫兹频段的天线阵列； 重点讲解的关键技术包括：可重构技术、集总\分布参数匹配技术、多极化天线技术、行波天线技术、复杂馈电网络技术、基于新型人工电磁媒质天线技术，例如波导集总电路、左右手传输线、近零指数媒质等； 天线设计流程方面包括：天线仿真软件、天线加工工艺、天线测试系统及方法。
英文课程简介：
This course is aimed at the demand for the antenna engineering and explains antenna theory in the form of antenna-based key technologies. It includes three major aspects: Engineering object-oriented includes: mobile terminal antennas, base station antennas, MIMO antennas, airborne/car antennas, packaged antennas, radar antennas, and antenna arrays in the millimeter-wave and terahertz bands. The key technologies to be explained include: reconfigurable technology, lumped or distributed parameter impedance matching technology, multi-polarized antenna technology, traveling wave antenna technology, complex feed network technology, and antenna technology based on the novel artificial electromagnetic medium, such as waveguide lumped circuits, right/left-handed transmission lines, near-zero-index(NZI) media, etc. The antenna design processes include: antenna simulation software, antenna processing technology, antenna testing system and method.
