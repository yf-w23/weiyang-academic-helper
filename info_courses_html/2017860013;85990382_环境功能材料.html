课程简介：
环境功能材料课程围绕着环境功能材料设计、制备及应用原理来开展课程，实验的内容包括微滤/超滤/纳滤/反渗透膜分离材料的设计、制备及应用，基于静电纺丝的多功能吸附材料的设计、制备及应用， 类芬顿/臭氧催化氧化/光催化催化材料的设计、制备及应用，及电化学用材料的设计、制备及应用等，开展的应用包括水污染控制、药物及个人护理品的去除、难降解有机物的去除、高盐高COD废水的处理等，从原理、设计及实际效果三方面系统地讲授环境功能材料。
英文课程简介：
The course of environmental functional materials focuses on the design, preparation and application principles of environmental functional materials. The experimental contents include the design, preparation and application of microfiltration / ultrafiltration / nanofiltration /reverse osmosis membrane separation materials, the design, preparation and application of multifunctional adsorption materials based on electrospinning, the design, preparation and application of Fenton-like / catalytic ozonation / photocatalysis materials, and design, preparation and application of electrochemical materials. The applications include water pollution control, removal of pharmaceuticals and personal care products, removal of refractory organics, treatment of high salinity and high COD wastewater, etc. The environmental functional materials course will be systematically taught from three aspects of principle, design and application.
