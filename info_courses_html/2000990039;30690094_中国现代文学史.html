课程内容简介：
讲述中国现代文学的发生与发展过程——从“五四”文学革命直到新中国成立前夕的“人民文学”，介绍重要的文学思潮、文学流派，分析重要的作家及其代表作品，如鲁迅、郭沫若、茅盾、巴金、老舍、曹禺、沈从文等，探讨现代文学发展的经验与教训，以培养学生对文学和历史的理解能力。
Course Description
This course offers an introduction of the origin and developmental trajectory of modern Chinese literature, e.g. from the May Fourth Literary Movement to the "People’s Literature" in the eve of the P. R. C. It explores influential literary thoughts and societies, for example, “Human Being's Literature”, Association for Literary Research, Creation Society, Crescent School, Yusi School, School of Modern Poetry, New Sensation School, Peking School, July School, “Revolutionary Literature”, Yen-an Talk on Literature and Arts, and so son. In addition, this course gives a critical analysis of representative pieces by major writers, for instance, Lu Xun, Guo Moruo, Mao Dun, Zhou Zuoren, Ai Qing, Feng Zhi, Shen Congwen, Zhao Shuli, etc. This course aims to examine the landscapes of modern Chinese literature in the hope of nurturing students’ appreciation of literary canons.
