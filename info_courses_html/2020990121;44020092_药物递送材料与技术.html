药物递送材料与技术

课程内容简介：
药物能够高效地到达靶点是发挥其生物学活性的重要前提，然而多重递送屏障的存在会妨碍药物到达靶点，从而制约其活性。在本课程中我们将首先介绍药物到达靶点前可能面临的递送屏障（如药物降解、单核吞噬系统清除、血管内皮屏障、细胞外基质屏障、细胞内吞体/溶酶体隔离等），然后使用研究实例讲授如何根据不同类别药物（小分子、蛋白/多肽、核酸分子）的特点开发能克服相应生理屏障的递送材料和递送技术，阐明这些递送方案对药物的有效性和安全性的影响，并讨论其面临的机遇与挑战。通过这种研究型教学的模式，学生将掌握与药物递送有关的代表性的材料和技术及其工作原理，了解不同的递送材料和技术的设计开发原则及未来发展趋势，为今后进一步深入学习和研究奠定坚实的基础。
Course Description
Efficiently reaching the target is an important prerequisite for a drug to exert its biological activity, but the presence of multiple levels of delivery barriers can prevent the drug from reaching the target, thus compromising the activity. In this course, we will (1) introduce key delivery barriers (e.g., drug degradation, clearance by the mononuclear phagocytic system, endothelial barrier, extracellular matrix, and sequestering in endosomes/lysosomes), (2) use case studies to discuss how to develop drug delivery materials and technologies based on the properties of different types of drugs (e.g., small molecules, peptides/proteins, and nucleic acids), and (3) elucidate the impact of delivery strategies on the efficacy and safety of different drugs and discuss emerging opportunities and challenges. Through the research-based teaching style, the students will have a deep understanding of drug delivery-related materials and technologies, the mechanism of action for such strategies, and the emerging opportunities and challenges, which will pave the way for further study and research.
