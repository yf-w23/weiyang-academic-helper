课程内容简介：
哲学的哲学（philosophy of philosophy），或者被称为元哲学（metaphilosophy），顾名思义就是以哲学本身为研究对象的哲学，是对哲学的性质（nature）进行自我反思性研究的哲学分支，主要聚焦在研究哲学的目标（aims）、方法（methods）、边界（boundaries）、好坏标准（standard）、类型（style）、进步（progress）等方面。处理如下中心问题：哲学是什么？怎么做哲学？哲学有什么用处？哲学与其他学科（数学、经验科学、宗教、艺术、心理学等）的边界和区别？如何比较不同哲学之间的好坏？哲学是否有进步？本课程会学习元哲学的各个核心主题。
Course Description
The philosophy of philosophy, generally called metaphilosophy, is the self-reflective investigation of the philosophy itself, namely, the philosophical branch of inquiring the nature of philosophy. Its topics include the aims of philosophy, its methods, its boundaries, its standard, its style, its progress, etc. The sub-discipline of philosophy focuses on the following questions: What is philosophy? How to philosophize? What good is philosophy? What is good philosophy? What is the difference between philosophy and other disciplines such as sciences, mathematics, art? Whether or not does philosophical progress occur and more so, whether is such progress in philosophy even possible? This course will discuss core issues of metaphilosophy.
