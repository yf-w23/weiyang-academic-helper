课程内容简介：
一、课程目标 培养学生在AI时代成为"教育+X"复合型人才: 1. 理解并应用教学设计基本原理和方法,在AI赋能与"真人在场"的协同中构建有效且富有吸引力的学习环境。 2. 辩证把握"AI赋能"与"AI暴露",战略性运用AI优化高自动化任务,将精力投入AI难以替代的高价值教学环节。 3. 创造性地设计和实施"人机协同"教学方案,从传统"知识接受者"转向"教育创新者",实现价值引领与人文关怀。 4. 增强跨学科整合与团队合作能力,通过"教学+专业+AI"的融合项目深化对教学设计的理解和应用,培养问题创造力。 二、课程特色 (1)"以教为学": 采用对话式、体验式与合作探究式教学方法,设计系列"做中学"活动,引导学生在师生换位中以教学设计、实施和评价为学习方法。课程强调从"知识供给"转向"问题创造"能力培养,促进学生从理论到实践深入理解教学全过程,实现知行合一。学生不仅学习运用AI工具完成常规性教学任务,更要聚焦教育的人文本质,实现AI难以替代的价值引领。 (2)"AI赋能"+"真人在场"融合: 本课程构建"AI辅助-人机协同-智能增强"三阶AI赋能体系,引导学生辩证理解教学中的"AI赋能"与"AI暴露"。强调教师聚焦启发对话、价值引导等任务,AI承担标准化工作,实现教学"人机协同"与"真人在场"的优化组合,培养学生在AI时代做AI难以替代的事的能力。学习使用AI智能体和多种AI工具为教学和学习提质增效。 (3)跨学科视野: 设置教育经典对话、镜头语言实验、教学示范课等环节, 培养"教学+专业+AI"跨学科融合思维。研读《论语》《礼记》《理想国》等中外经典以及电影中的教育主题选篇，研讨教育经典理论与认知语言学、具身认知科学、视觉人类学等前沿理论，探讨学习教学设计的艺术和技术,从多学科教师实践中获得启发。这种跨学科融合不仅扩展思维视野,更增强学生将教学设计方法创造性迁移至本专业学习的能力。 三、学习评价 四维评价: • 理论理解:案例分析、课堂研讨、概念辨析的深度 • 设计创新:教学方案原创性、AI赋能策略合理性、跨学科融合度 • 实施效果:真人在场教学的对话质量、学习者反馈、即兴应对能力 • 反思深度:教学理念阐述、AI暴露度自评、成长规划的质量 持续反馈:即时反馈(AI雨课堂个性化互动)、同伴反馈(互评)、专家反馈(教师和助教) ,支持学生自我发现与发展。
Course Description
Course Objectives This course cultivates interdisciplinary "Education+X" talents for the AI era, enabling students to: 1. Master instructional design principles and construct engaging learning environments through AI empowerment and human presence synergy 2. Dialectically understand "AI empowerment" versus "AI exposure", strategically utilising AI for automatable tasks whilst focusing on high-value teaching activities AI cannot replace 3. Design and implement human-AI collaborative teaching programmes, transforming from knowledge recipients to educational innovators who provide value leadership and humanistic care 4. Enhance interdisciplinary integration through "teaching+discipline+AI" projects, cultivating problem-creating abilities Course Features (1) Learning By Teaching: Adopting dialogic, experiential, and collaborative inquiry methods, the course guides students through "learning by doing" activities. Role reversal between teachers and students facilitates deep understanding from theory to practice. Emphasis shifts from knowledge supply to problem-creating abilities, achieving unity of knowledge and action. Students learn to utilise AI tools whilst maintaining education's humanistic essence. (2) AI-Empowerment + Human Presence: A three-tier system—"AI assistance, human-AI collaboration, intelligent augmentation"—guides students to optimise teaching. Teachers focus on inspiring dialogue and value guidance whilst AI handles standardised work, cultivating abilities to do what AI cannot easily replace. Students master AI agents and tools to enhance teaching quality and efficiency. (3) Interdisciplinary Perspective: Integrating educational classics (Analects, Book of Rites, Republic), cinematic analysis, and teaching demonstrations, students engage with theories from cognitive linguistics, embodied cognition, and visual anthropology. This broadens horizons and strengthens creative transfer of instructional design to their own disciplines.
进度安排：

采用“以教为学”模式，通过"能力递进+AI赋能"四个模块设计，实现从"理解教学"到"创造教学"的能力发展：

模块1. 善喻与善问：认知隐喻与对话式教学法

第1-4周：中外教育经典研读与教学启示研讨

学习内容：

  • 中外教育经典选读：《论语》《礼记》《理想国》《爱弥儿》等
  • 认知隐喻理论：概念隐喻、体验哲学与教学语言艺术
  • 对话式教学法：苏格拉底式提问、启发式对话、问题创造能力
  • AI赋能初探：大语言模型在文本分析与学习支持中的应用

 

模块2. 善察与善述：镜头语言与影像式教学法

第5-8周：中外教育主题电影赏析与教学启示研讨

学习内容：

  • 教育主题电影赏析：《十二公民》等
  • 镜头语言基础：景别、构图、光影、色彩、剪辑与教学隐喻
  • 影像式教学法：具身认知理论、视觉人类学、导演思维在教学中的应用
  • 教学设计进阶：学习目标分类（认知/情感/技能）、教学策略与评价设计

 

模块3. 善游与善造：创意思维与游戏式教学法

第9-12周：经典游戏分析与学习型游戏设计

学习内容：

  • 游戏化学习理论：心流体验、即时反馈、挑战进阶、叙事设计
  • 学习型游戏设计：教育游戏的机制设计与学习目标融合
  • AI赋能教学的理论探索：教学任务的自动化程度分析、战略性AI使用
  • 教学智能体开发：AI工具链（大语言模型、智能体、AI雨课堂）的综合应用

 

模块4. 综合教学设计："设计-实施-评价-改进"全流程教学体验

第13-16周：融会贯通一学期教学设计成果的综合实践

核心能力：整合四个模块所学，实现"设计-实施-评价-改进"全流程

学习内容：

  • 真人在场教学艺术：即兴应对、情感连接、价值引领
  • 综合教学设计：融合对话式、影像式、游戏式教学法
  • 教学反思与改进：基于多源反馈的迭代优化
  • 人机协同优化：AI赋能与真人在场的最佳结合模式

 

Module 1. Skilful Metaphor and Skilful Questioning: Cognitive Metaphor and Dialogic Pedagogy

Weeks 1-4: Reading Chinese and Western Educational Classics and Discussion of Teaching Insights

Learning tasks:

  • Selected readings from Chinese and Western educational classics: The Analects, The Book of Rites, The Republic, Émile, etc.
  • Cognitive metaphor theory: conceptual metaphor, experiential philosophy, and the art of pedagogical language
  • Dialogic pedagogy: Socratic questioning, heuristic dialogue, and question-creation capability
  • Initial AI empowerment: applications of large language models in textual analysis and learning support

 

Module 2. Skilful Observation and Skilful Narration: Cinematic Language and Visual Pedagogy

Weeks 5-8: Appreciation of Chinese and Western Educational Films and Discussion of Teaching Insights

Learning tasks:

  • Appreciation of education-themed films: 12 Citizens, etc.
  • Fundamentals of cinematic language: shot scale, composition, lighting and shadow, colour, editing, and pedagogical metaphor
  • Visual pedagogy: embodied cognition theory, visual anthropology, and the application of directorial thinking in teaching
  • Advanced instructional design: classification of learning objectives (cognitive/affective/psychomotor), teaching strategies, and assessment design

 

Module 3. Skilful Play and Skilful Creation: Creative Thinking and Game-Based Pedagogy

Weeks 9-12: Analysis of Classic Games and Design of Educational Games

Learning tasks:

  • Gamification learning theory: flow experience, immediate feedback, progressive challenge, and narrative design
  • Educational game design: mechanism design of educational games and integration with learning objectives
  • Theoretical exploration of AI-empowered teaching: analysis of automation levels in pedagogical tasks, strategic AI usage
  • Development of teaching agents: comprehensive application of AI tool chains (large language models, agents, AI Rain Classroom)

 

Module 4. Comprehensive Instructional Design: Full-Cycle Teaching Experience of "Design-Implementation-Evaluation-Improvement"

Weeks 13-16: Integrated Practice Drawing Together the Semester's Instructional Design Achievements

Core Competency: Integrating learning from all four modules to realise the complete cycle of "design-implementation-evaluation-improvement"

Learning tasks:

  • The art of face-to-face teaching: improvised responses, emotional connection, and value guidance
  • Comprehensive instructional design: integrating dialogic, visual, and game-based pedagogies
  • Teaching reflection and improvement: iterative optimisation based on multi-source feedback
  • Human-machine collaborative optimisation: optimal combination models of AI empowerment and human presence
