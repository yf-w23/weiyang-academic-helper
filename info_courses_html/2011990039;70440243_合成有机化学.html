课程简介：
通过本课程的学习，要求学生掌握氧化、还原、取代、缩合、消除、重排等各单元反应，掌握各类有机物的合成方法，学会具有一定难度的化合物合成路线的设计，熟悉不对称合成设计的基本方法，了解一些天然有机物的合成方法。
英文课程简介：
Various fundamental reactions including oxidation, reduction, substitution, condensation, elimination and rearrangement reactions are presented. The design and synthesis of complicated organic and/or chiral compounds are ilustrated. Some typical natural products are exemplied.
