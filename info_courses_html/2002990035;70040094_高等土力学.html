课程简介：
本课程是岩土工程及相关专业研究生的专业课，主要内容包括土工试验、土的本构关系、土的强度、土中渗流、土体的压缩与固结、土工数值计算等。 本课程可使得研究生能够理解掌握土力学研究的基本知识并锻炼相关技能，为开展学术研究奠定基础，包括： （1）系统理解并掌握土力学的基础理论和学术思想。 （2）了解土力学研究前沿方向，开阔学术视野。 （3）培养运用土力学理论和试验技术解决岩土工程问题的能力。 为达到上述目标，拟采取如下方式： （1）将课堂讲课、实验、案例教学、程序应用和前沿学术讲座等教学环节紧密结合。 （2）突出土力学的实践性特点，从工程实例和科研课题中提炼出符合研究生学习特点的作业并加强过程指导。 （3）任课教师均在科研一线工作，在教学中注重嵌入学科前沿成果及发展趋势评述，兼顾课程的系统性和前沿性。
英文课程简介：
This is an advanced course for graduate students majoring in geotechnical engineering and related subjects, covering topics including soil testing, strength and constitutive modelling of soil, seepage and filtration, compression and consolidation, geotechnical numerical computation, etc. This course aims to provide students with the knowledge and understanding of advanced soil mechanics behavior, building up a basis for further research, and targets to: (1) enhance system understanding of the basic theories and concepts of soil mechanics; (2) gain knowledge of recent research advances, broaden academic vision; (3) develop students’ ability of applying theoretical and experimental advancement in solving practical geotechnical engineering problems. To achieve these targets, the following methods will be adopted: (1) close integration of class lectures, experiments, case studies, program application and lectures on recent advances in geotechnical engineering; (2) assign homework suitable for graduate level students based on engineering cases and research projects, highlighting the practical nature of geotechnical engineering, and provide guidance during the homework completion process; (3) course instructors will all be active researchers with the ability to integrate state of the art research with class lectures, keeping the course systematic and up-to-date.
