课程内容简介：
课程“光的技术: 提升人类生活”是电子工程系信息光电子研究所教师团队为大学一年级本科生开设的介绍光电子技术专业方向的新生研讨课。课程名“光的技术”来自2015年国际“光”年的主题“光与光的技术”，“提升人类生活”来自诺贝尔奖奖章的铭文。这门课程的主要内容是介绍光的技术发展历史及其在现代社会各个方面发挥的重要作用。通过课程讲座，实验室参观，座谈交流等多种灵活的形式使学生深入了解光电子技术和产业领域，激发学生在该领域深造的兴趣。
Course Description
The course "Light based technologies: bettered life on earth" is a freshman seminar for first-year undergraduate students to introduce the field of optoelectronics. The title of the course, "Light based technology", comes from the theme of the 2015 International Year of Light, "Light and Light based Technology", and "bettered life on earth" comes from the inscription of the Nobel Prize. The main content of this course is to introduce the development of light based technology and the important role it has played in modern humen life. Through course lectures, laboratory tours, seminars, etc., students can have an in-depth understanding of acedamic and industrial fields of optoelectronics technology, and stimulate students' interest in further study in this field.
