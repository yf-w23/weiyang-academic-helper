课程内容简介：
媒体与认知课程以媒体与认知相互作用为主线，为学生介绍媒体和信息技术领域的研究对象、经典理论与前沿进展，包括其与认知科学、心理学、媒体传播学等学科的内在联系。通过本课程的学习，使学生在充分了解领域知识的前提下，用文、理、工相融合的眼光看待其发展历史、研究现状、以及未来课题，为今后开展高水平、创新性研究铺平道路。本课程致力于为国家培养具有人文素养、创造力丰富的科技人才，为清华成为国际一流大学打好坚实基础。 媒体与认知课程的核心内容如下： 一、媒体与信息： 媒体的概念、媒体信息的获取与表现形式等； 二、认知心理学与认知科学： 人类感知与认知机理等； 三、人工智能：模式识别与机器学习、深度学习、媒体认知相互作用及应用。
Course Description
The course Media and Cognition will introduce the research scope, classical theories and frontier progresses in the field of media and information technologies, including their internal relations with cognitive science, psychology, media communication and other disciplines. Through this course, students can fully understand the history, current and future topics in this field from the perspective of the interaction of humanity, science and industry, paving the way for high-level and innovative research in the future. This course aims at cultivating top-level talents with both humanistic leadership and rich creativity required by our country, laying a solid foundation for Tsinghua to become a world-level top university. The core content of this course is as follows: (1) Media and Information: The concepts of media and information, media information acquisition and representation; (2) Cognitive Science: Human perception and cognitive mechanisms; (3) Artificial Intelligence: Pattern recognition and machine learning, deep learning, interaction of media and cognition and its applications.
