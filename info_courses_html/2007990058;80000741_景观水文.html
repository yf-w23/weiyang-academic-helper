课程简介：
作为一个研究领域，景观水文学（Landscape Hydrology）是风景园林学与涉水自然科学的交叉领域，广泛面向从自然保护地到城乡人居环境建设的各类涉水问题，强调整合科学研究与风景园林规划设计的交叉创新解决策略。 作为一门课程，景观水文学是清华大学景观学系应用自然科学板块的核心课程。目前在本科与硕士阶段均开设。本科课程的目的是训练水景观规划设计的基本能力，并学习、理解关键性的水文、水利、水环境、水生态等自然科学知识点。研究生课程的目的是基于上述能力，引导学生对国际涉水前沿科学问题与国家战略需求实践问题的探讨与思考，侧重对交叉应用与问题创新解决能力的培养。
英文课程简介：
As a research field, Landscape Hydrology is an interdisciplinary field of Landscape Architecture and water-related natural science. It is widely oriented to all kinds of water-related problems from natural reserves to urban and rural human settlements construction, and emphasizes the cross-innovation solution strategy of integrating scientific research and landscape planning and design. As a course, Landscape Hydrology is the core course of applied natural science in the Department of Landscape Architecture of Tsinghua University. At present, it is offered at both undergraduate and master levels. The purpose of the undergraduate course is to train the basic ability of water landscape planning and design, and to learn and understand key natural science knowledge points such as Hydrology, Hydraulic-engineering, Environmental Science, Water Ecology, etc. The purpose of the postgraduate course is to guide students to explore and think about international cutting-edge scientific issues and practical issues of national strategic needs based on the above capabilities, focusing on the cultivation of cross-application and innovative problem-solving capabilities.
