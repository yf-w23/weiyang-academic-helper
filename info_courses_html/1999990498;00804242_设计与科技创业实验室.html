课程内容简介：
课程介绍： 《设计与科技创业实验室》是一门面向AI时代的创新创业型设计课程，旨在培养学生的创新创业素养与未来塑造力。课程倡导“与AI共学（Learning by Co-Creating with AI）”的教学理念，通过AI助教与生成工具，引导学生完成从趋势洞察、机会识别到原型构建与商业表达的全过程。课程融合设计思维、未来思维、精益开发与以人为本的创新方法，采用开放式项目制学习模式，促进跨领域协作与快速迭代。课程依托AI Agent共创平台构建智能学习与实验生态，支持学生实现从概念生成到原型验证的系统探索。最终成果为个人创新创业作品集，展示学生如何在AI赋能的设计实践中展现创造力、批判性思维与社会责任感，回应未来社会与产业的复杂挑战。
Course Description
Design and Technology Venture Lab is an innovation- and entrepreneurship-oriented design course for the AI era, aimed at cultivating students’ creative entrepreneurship literacy and future-making capabilities. The course advocates the pedagogical concept of “Learning by Co-Creating with AI,” guiding students—through AI teaching assistants and generative tools—to complete the entire process from trend insight and opportunity identification to prototyping and business expression. Integrating design thinking, futures thinking, lean development, and human-centered innovation methods, the course adopts an open, project-based learning model that encourages cross-disciplinary collaboration and rapid iteration. Supported by an AI Agent co-creation platform, it builds an intelligent learning and experimental ecosystem that enables students to systematically explore from concept generation to prototype validation. The final outcome is an individual innovation and entrepreneurship portfolio, showcasing how students demonstrate creativity, critical thinking, and social responsibility through AI-empowered design practices in response to the complex challenges of future societies and industries.
