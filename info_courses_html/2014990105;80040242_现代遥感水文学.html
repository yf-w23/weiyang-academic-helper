课程简介：
遥感对水循环变量和过程的观测和模拟不仅是地球科学领域面临的重大问题，也对水资源管理、水旱灾害防治、生态系统健康、农业和粮食生产等领都有重要的意义。本课程介绍一系列的水卫星和传感器观测遥感水循环要素的基本原理和方法，尤其是对目前最成熟最先进的相关算法进行系统介绍，包括主被动微波和红外降水反演、热红外蒸散反演、卫星雷达测高和光学河道径流反演、多源遥感观测地表水储量反演、主被动微波和热红外土壤表面水分反演、重力卫星地下水的遥感反演和观测方法做系统介绍和辨析。同时，本课程还会介绍水循环要素的相关产品及其误差特征，遥感数据和信息的检验方法和策略等。在传感器和平台方面，本课程主要侧重卫星遥感方法；对其他遥感平台例如空载遥感和车载遥感平台等不做详细介绍。 通过本课程的学习要达到三个主要目标：(1)对遥感基本理论和知识有一定重温或了解；(2)对遥感水循环变量的反演算法和模型有系统了解；(3)掌握如何使用和处理相关遥感数据。
英文课程简介：
Remote sensing measurement of hydrological variables and processes represents one of the most challenging research problems in Earth science. This course overviews various satellite platforms/sensors and introduces advances in remote sensing of hydrology from spaceborne observations, state-of-the-art retrieval algorithms for hydrological variables, and ground validation strategies. Various applications of remote sensing to hydrology are introduced to improve our understanding of hydrologic processes at various spatial and temporal scales (e.g., precipitation, ET, river discharge, soil moisture, and groundwater). Each of these hydrologic variables or processes is discussed individually with an emphasis on the use of remote sensing data and understanding its applicability and uncertainty. Particular emphasis is also given to science and techniques used for spaceborne estimation, validation, and its application in hydrometeorology. The objectives of this course are threefold: (1) understanding the basics of remote sensing; (2) understanding how hydrological variables are derived (retrieved) from remote sensing; and (3) understanding how to use remote sensing-based retrieval algorithms or products.

This course spans the second half of this semester:

Week 9: Basics of Remote Sensing and Its Applications

Week 10: Labor Day Break

Week 12: Remote Sensing of Evapotranspiration and Its Products and Applications: Global ET Data Release

Week 13: Total Water Storage and Groundwater Storage Changes from GRACE Satellites

Week 14: Water Level and River Discharge Estimation from Multisource Remote Sensing

Week 15: Remote Sensing of Land Surface Temperature and Soil Moisture

Week 16: International Water-Related Missions, and ENVI Workshop

Week 17: Final Examination: Presentation + Final Report Submission
