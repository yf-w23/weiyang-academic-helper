科学博物馆学与科学传播

课程简介：
本课程以科学博物馆的历史、收藏、策展等内容以及科学传播的理论与应用为主题，对其中的关键问题进行讨论与实践。课程共分为四个部分：第一部分介绍科学博物馆学基础，包括科学博物馆的发展史与职能。第二部分探讨科学博物馆在收藏和策展方面的理论与方法。第三部分重在讲授科学传播的基本概念、传播过程中的模式与技巧。而第四部分的目的是将之前主讲的内容结合并落实到实践层面，在分享科学博物馆经典案例的同时要求学生进行展览策划并在课堂展示。通过课程的整体学习，学生将对科学博物馆的运行方式有着一定的掌握，并有一定能力承担科学传播相关工作。
英文课程简介：
This course focuses on the science museums studies as well as the theory and application of science communication. It discusses issues in these areas and conducts research at the practical level. The course is divided into four parts. The first part introduces the basic knowledge of science museum studies, including the development history and functions of science museums. The second part explores the theories and methods of science museums in terms of collection and curation. The third part focuses on teaching the basic concepts of science communication, as well as the modes and techniques involved in the communication process. The purpose of the fourth part is to combine and put into practice the knowledge taught in the previous lectures, while sharing classic cases from science museums, and requiring students to curate exhibitions. Through comprehensive study of this course, students will have a certain grasp of the operation mode of science museums and have the ability to undertake related work in science communication.
