课程内容简介：
本课程介绍随机过程的基本理论与典型模型，课程主要内容包括：离散时间马尔可夫链的基本性质与应用、转移概率分析、状态分类、平稳分布、采样算法、返回时间、一步分析方法、随机游走、分支过程；泊松过程的性质与应用、几种常用的变换、独立增量过程、更新过程与大数定律；连续时间马尔可夫链、转移率矩阵、前向后项方程、生灭过程；鞅的基本概念、条件期望、集中不等式、停时定理、退出时间与退出分布分析；布朗运动的基本性质、扩散过程、随机微积分简介。
Course Description
This course provides a systematic introduction to the theory and applications of stochastic processes, focusing on both fundamental concepts and representative models. Topics include discrete-time Markov chains, their basic properties, transition probabilities, state classification, stationary distributions, sampling algorithms, return times, random walks, and branching processes. We will also cover Poisson processes, independent increment processes, renewal theory, and the law of large numbers, highlighting their properties and practical applications. Another key topic is the continuous-time Markov chains, including transition rate matrices, forward and backward equations, and birth–death processes. The course also covers the basics of martingales, including conditional expectation, concentration inequalities, optional stopping theorem, and the analysis of exit times and exit distributions. Time permitting, we will introduce the Brownian motion and diffusion processes, and a brief overview of stochastic calculus.
