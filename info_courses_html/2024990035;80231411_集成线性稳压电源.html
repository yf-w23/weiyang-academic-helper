课程简介：
本课程涵盖各类线性稳压电源的设计与分析，包括线性低压差稳压电源、电感型开关电源转换器、开关电容功率转换器、开关电感电容混合型DC-DC功率转换器、无线能量传输电路与系统、功率芯片的版图设计与测试等内容。本课程背景知识涉及模拟电路、数字电路、功率电子、半导体器件、控制理论等多个电子信息科学的子学科方向。课程将重点讲授线性稳压电源的工作原理、拓扑架构、环路控制、及其设计流程。通过本课程的学习，学生们将掌握高集成度线性稳压电源的设计与分析技能；熟悉各类稳压电源的系统架构和环路控制机理；通过学术前沿设计案例的讨论分析提升功率芯片方向创新的学术能力；通过课程设计和辅导课程提升功率芯片方向的工程能力。
英文课程简介：
This course covers the design and analysis of various integrated linear voltage regulators, including analog low-dropout (LDO) regulators, digital LDO regulators, capacitor-less LDO design, PID loop analysis, LDO power supply ripple rejection (PSRR) enhancement techniques, layout design and measurement considerations of the linear voltage regulators. The background knowledge of this course involves a number of sub-areas of the electronic and information subject, includes analog circuits, digital circuits, power electronics, semiconductor devices, and control theory. The emphasis of this course will be put on the operation principle, topology architecture, control loop, and design flow of the integrated linear voltage regulators. Through this course, the students will grasp the capability of designing and analyzing the highly-integrated linear voltage regulators; will be familiar with the circuit and system architectures and loop-control mechanisms of various voltage regulators; are expected to get improvements on their academic innovation capability through cutting-edge design case studies, and to get more hands-on power IC engineering experiences through design projects and tutorials.
