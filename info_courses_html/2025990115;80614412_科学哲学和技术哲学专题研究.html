课程简介：
阅读科学哲学或技术哲学的新文献，探讨科技哲学的前沿问题。本学期课堂重点讨论与演化生物学哲学相关的6个议题，分别涉及模型、科学解释、因果性、自然选择的单元与所在层次、演化元伦理学、对现代综合的挑战。选课者须平均每周阅读约30页指定英文文献，而后参与课堂讨论。每次研讨会安排1至2位报告人和相应评论人（第1次课除外）。
英文课程简介：
The seminar in this semester will focus on six topics in the philosophy of evolutionary biology, which concern models, scientific explanation, causality, the units and levels of natural selection, evolutionary meta-ethics, and challenges to the Modern Synthesis, respectively. Students are expected to read about 30 pages (on average ) of assigned literature in English per week and then participate in class discussions. Every week (except for the first one), before the session of free discussion, we may need one or two presenters and corresponding commentators to volunteer to provide an introduction of --and some comments on-- the assigned articles or book chapters.

 因“五一”劳动节停课，所以实际共计上课10周 ，每周3小节课，时间为周二第二大节。各周所要讨论的主题和所需阅读的材料暂定如下（可能根据课堂情况和学生兴趣做调整；教师会提供阅读材料的电子版）。

1. 课程导论：第一周 (程志翔主讲)

2. Models

 

第二周：

Odenbaugh, J. (2008). Models.  In Sarkar, S. and A. Plutynski (eds), A Companion to the Philosophy of Biology (pp. 506-524). Blackwell Publishing.

Levins, R. (1966). The Strategy of Model Building in Population Biology. American Scientist, 54(4), 421-431. 

 

第三周：

Godfrey-Smith, P. (2007) Conditions for Evolution by Natural Selection. Journal of Philosophy, 104 (10), 489-516.

 

3. Explanation

 

第四周：

Reutlinger, A. (2017). Explanation Beyond Causation? New Directions in the Philosophy of Scientific Explanation. Philosophy Compass, 12(2), e12395, 1-11. （正文8页）

Rice, C.. (2015). Moving Beyond Causes: Optimality Models and Scientific Explanation. Nous, 49, 589–615.  (正文21页)

4. Causation

第五周：

Pence, C. H. (2021). The Causal Structure of Natural Selection (Cambridge Elements in the Philosophy of Biology). Cambridge University Press. (导言加第1、2节， 第1-34页)

 

第六周：

Pence, C. H. (2021). The Causal Structure of Natural Selection (Cambridge Elements in the Philosophy of Biology). Cambridge University Press. (第3-5节，第 34-64页。)

 

5. Units and Levels of Selection

第七周：

Okasha, S. (2008). The Units and Levels of Selection. In Sarkar, S. and  Plutynski, A. (eds.), A Companion to the Philosophy of Biology (pp.138-156). Blackwell Publishing.

Bourrat, P. (2025). Moving Past Conventionalism About Multilevel Selection. Erkenntnis, 90, 1363–1376. 

 

6. Evolutionary Meta-ethics

第八周：

Street, S. (2006). A Darwinian Dilemma for Realist Theories of Value. Philosophical Studies, 127, 109–166. （阅读第1-6节，即第109-135页；学有余力者可读完全文）

 

第九周：

Brosnan, K. (2011). Do the Evolutionary Origins of Our Moral Beliefs Undermine Moral Knowledge?. Biology & Philosophy, 26(1), 51-64.

McKay, N. (2024). Can Rational Reflection Save Moral Knowledge from Debunking?. Episteme, 21(4), 1418-1433.

7. Challenging the Evolutionary Synthesis

第十周：

Laland, K. N., et al. (2015). The Extended Evolutionary Synthesis: Its Structure, Assumptions and Predictions. Proceedings of the Royal Society B: Biological Sciences, 282(1813), 20151019. （约10页）

Smocovitis, V. B. (2023). Every Evolutionist Their Own Historian: The Importance of History, Context, and the Extended Evolutionary Synthesis. In Dickins, T. and Dickens, B. (eds.), Evolutionary Biology: Contemporary and Historical Reflections Upon Core Theory (pp. 25-54). Cham: Springer International Publishing. （偏历史学，相对易读）
