课程内容简介：
《科技创新与挑战2C》是为先书院创新实践类课程《科技创新与挑战》的初级课程，面向为先书院大一和大二学生开设。《科技创新与挑战》课程以问题为导向，以项目为载体，指导学生从大一开始，纵深贯穿本科阶段，开展进阶式科研探索和科技创新研究，赋予学生严谨求实、创新融合的理工思维。 《科技创新与挑战2C》要求学生深度体验生物医学工程学科领域的实验室，在教师指导下开展实验室入门科研训练，通过走进实验室深度体验、实践、交流和讨论，进一步培育学生基本的科研素养，培养探索未知能力等，为《科技创新与挑战》后续进阶课程奠定基础。 本课程实验涵盖医学影像、神经工程、微纳医学与组织工程3个方向，学生沉浸式学习实践12周，包括实验室安全、实验仪器、实验技术和验证实验等内容。 该课程1学分，课内、实践、课外等环节共48学时。
Course Description
Technology Innovation and Challenge 2C is an elementary course for Weixian College's innovative practice course Technology Innovation and Challenge, which is open to freshmen and sophomore of Weixian College. The course Technology Innovation and Challenge is problem-oriented and project-based, guiding students to carry out advanced scientific research exploration and scientific and technological innovation research from the beginning of the freshman year, through the undergraduate stage, and endow students with rigorous, realistic and innovative integration of science and engineering thinking. Technology Innovation and Challenge 2C further guides students to cultivate basic scientific research literacy and exploration ability through in-depth experience, practice, communication, and discussion in the laboratory, including the cognition and understanding of engineering laboratories and learning the commonly used laboratory safety issues, experimental equipment, methods and techniques with improved experimental skills, manipulative ability, and field exploration ability. Students will have deep experience at the laboratories in the field of biomedical engineering, and carry out introductory scientific research training in laboratories under the guidance of tutors. It lays a foundation for the follow-up advanced courses in Technology Innovation and Challenges. The theme of this course includes: deep experience, learning, and practice in laboratories of biomedical engineering covering the following different fields for up to 12 weeks for laboratory safety issues, experimental equipment, methods and techniques, and a verification experiment. Field 1: Medical imaging; Field 2: Neural engineering; Field 3: Micro/nano biomedicine and tissue engineering; The course is 1 credit, totally 48 class hours of in-class, practical and extra-curricular links.
