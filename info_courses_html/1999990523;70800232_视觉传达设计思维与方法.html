课程简介：
《视觉传达设计思维与方法》为美术学院视觉传达专业研究生必修课程。现代设计教育中越来越注重思维与方法论的培养，因为这样才能不断培养出具有创造性、前瞻性的艺术设计新生力量，从而避免学生停留在单纯的技法教学层面，开始他们得以独立思考的设计人生。设计思维与设计表现的训练课程共同构筑了一个人完整的设计能力，如果说设计表现的训练是针对语言方面的继承与探索，那么设计思维课程就应该是针对于同学在设计过程中的创造力、反映力、分析力、综合力、联系力、判断力等思维方式的训练。其中特别注重逻辑思维与跳跃性思维并重的教学与训练，突出研究型设计与跨界设计等重点内容。
英文课程简介：
Research on design thinking and methods is a compulsory course for the visual communication postgraduates in the Academy of Art & Design. In modern design education, more and more attention is paid to the cultivation of thinking and methodology, because this can continue to cultivate creative and forward-looking art design students, so as to avoid the students from stay in the simple technique teaching level, and start their own independent thinking design life. The design thinking and the design performance training courses jointly build a person’s complete design ability, if the training of design performance is aimed at language inheritance and exploration, then the design thinking course should be aimed at the students’ ability of creativity, reflection, analysis, synthesis, connection, judgment and other thinking methods in the design process. Particular attention is paid to the teaching and training of rational thinking and non-linear thinking, and highlights key contents of research design and cross-disciplinary design.

第一课：设计哲学（讲授）

1.不同的设计哲学与不同的评价标准

2.现代设计学术文脉梳理及我校学术文脉的梳理

1.格与格局

3.格律设计论的解读与案例分析

3.设计的多条思考路径

4.布置课后作业课题：第二阶段
