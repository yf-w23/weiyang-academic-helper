课程内容简介：
植物是地球生物圈的重要组成部分，在生物圈的生态系统、物质循环、能量流动中处于最 关键的地位。在地球生命的演化过程中，陆生植物的出现是具有决定性意义的事件。 绝大多数植物包括根、茎、叶、花、果实、种子六大器官，每种器官又由功能各异的组织 组成，而每一种组织又是由生命体最基本的单位——细胞组成。植物的身体结构和发育过程 随着对陆生生活的适应而逐渐复杂，各种器官的功能特化是植物表现出千姿百态的生活习 性，形成令人叹为观止的多样性。 植物和动物不同，一旦落地生根，此生无法迁移，但植物也是充满智慧的：植物能设计出 复杂的传粉路线让昆虫为它传粉；含羞草为保护自己迅速合拢叶片；植物能分泌吸引昆虫的 天敌的物质来保护自己；捕蝇草以昆虫为生等等。 植物的智慧是植物在生存压力的胁迫下，不断的改变自己而得到更好的发展。很多植物的 智慧是和动物协同进化的结果，也离不开自然选择的法则。也有很多植物的智慧我们还无法 确定其演化途径，有待进一步研究。 本课程围绕植物的六大器官，以理论课配合实践课、实验课的模式，激发学生学习兴趣， 促进不同专业同学相互交流，共同探讨植物的智慧及其背后的机制。
Course Description
Plants are an important part of the earth's biosphere and play a key role in the ecosystem, material circulation and energy flow of the biosphere. The emergence of land plants is a decisive event in the evolution of life on earth. Most plants include six organs: roots, stems, leaves, flowers, fruits and seeds. Each organ is composed of tissues with different functions, and each tissue is composed of the most basic unit of life - cells. As plants adapt to terrestrial life, their body structures and developmental processes gradually become more complex. The functional specialization of various organs results in a remarkable diversity of life habits among plants, forming a breathtaking diversity. Plants, unlike animals, cannot migrate for the rest of their lives once they have established themselves. However, plants exhibit a remarkable intelligence. They can design intricate pollination routes to attract insects for pollination; Mimosa quickly closes its leaves to protect itself; Plants protect themselves by secreting substances that attract natural enemies of insects; Carnivorous plants even rely on insects as a source of nutrition. The wisdom of plants is a result of continuous adaptation to survival pressures, often co-evolving with animals and subject to the principles of natural selection. Many aspects of the wisdoms is the result of co-evolution with animals and the laws of natural selection. Many aspects of plant widsom are still not fully understood and require further research.
