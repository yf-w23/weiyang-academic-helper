课程简介：
本课程共32学时，是为全校国际研究生开设的汉语初级课程。该课程实际分为两类，尽管用同一课程编号但有不同的先修要求：（一）一类是与初级汉语（A）课程（课程编号64203012)的内容衔接，向已经了解汉语入门知识的各院系国际学生开放，要求学生至少学习过48小时汉语，掌握约200个汉语词汇。通过课程学习，学生将进一步扩大词汇量，加强对中国文化的觉知，掌握更多的复杂语法，流利地与他人就天气、送礼、做客等话题展开交际。（二）另一类则是零起点汉语课程，仅向公管学院SPPM、经管学院EMBA、GMBA等项目的国际学生单独开放选课。通过课程学习，学生能掌握拼音基本知识，了解汉字的基本结构和书写规则，掌握基础入门阶段的词汇及语法结构，能够使用所学词汇做自我介绍、问答时间、方向、日程安排等。
英文课程简介：
This course has a total of 32 class-teaching hours and is an elementary Chinese language course for international graduate students throughout the school. This course is divided into two categories with different prerequisite expectations and enrollment procedure although sharing a same course code: (1）One is a successive course of the Elementary Chinese A Course (Course code 64203012)，which requires students to have learned Chinese for at least 48 hours and to have mastered about 200 Chinese vocabulary written in characters. Through the course learning, students will further expand their vocabulary and cultural awareness, master more complex grammar, and fluently communicate with others on topics such as weather, gift giving, and being guests. （2）The other type is a real beginner course, only opened enrollment to those students who is studying in certain departments such as SPPM, EMBA and GMBA etc. The learner who enrolled this type of course needn't know Chinese language but are expected to build up basic and solid foundation of Mandarin Chinese linguistic knowledge and cultural awareness. Its learning objects and outcomes include to master the basic knowledge of Pinyin and pronunciation, the basic structure of Chinese characters and the proper stroke order of character writing, sentence structure, and be able to use the learned vocabulary and patterns to communicate on topics such as self-introduction, time, direction and schedule etc.

初级汉语B第一类课程，是针对有一定中文基础的学生设计的基础汉语A的接续课程。该类课程从教材的第11课开始学习。

初级汉语B第二类课程，是零起点汉语课程，仅向某些学院某些项目的国际学生单独开设。该类课程从教材的第1课开始学习，也就是从最基础的汉语拼音、发音和汉字认读与书写开始。

Elementary Chinese B Category 1 is a continuation course designed for students who have completed Elementary Chinese A or possess a foundational understanding of the language. The course begins at Lesson 11 of the textbook and is intended for learners already familiar with basic elements such as Pinyin, Chinese characters (Hanzi), and simple conversational phrases. 

Elementary Chinese B Category 2 is a beginner-level course offered exclusively to international students enrolled in specific programs within select departments. This course starts from Lesson 1 of the textbook, introducing the fundamentals of Mandarin, including Pinyin, pronunciation, character recognition, and writing. It is designed for learners with no prior background in Chinese and aims to build a strong and comprehensive foundation in both linguistic skills and cultural understanding.
