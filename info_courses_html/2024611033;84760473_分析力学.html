课程简介：
本课程将首先简要回顾牛顿力学，随后讲解最小作用量原理、拉格朗日力学、变分法以及欧拉–拉格朗日方程。我们将讨论若干典型例子，包括开普勒问题、刚体运动和振动问题。同时，引入守恒量的概念，并阐明其与拉格朗日函数对称性之间的深刻联系——即诺特定理。接下来将介绍哈密顿力学，包括泊松括号、刘维尔定理以及正则变换。最后，在哈密顿力学部分，我们将讲解哈密顿–雅可比方程及变量分离方法。除上述偏物理的经典力学内容外，本课程还将引入一些辛几何的基本概念，帮助学生从几何角度理解经典力学。最后，我们将通过引入刘维尔可积性，简要讨论可积系统。
英文课程简介：
In this class we will first shortly review Newtonian mechanics, after that we will talk about principle of least action, Lagrangian, calculus of variations, Euler-Lagrange equation. We will talk about various examples like Kepler problem, rigid body and oscillation. We will also introduce the concept of conserved quantity and its deep relation to symmetry of Lagrangian via Noether’s theorem. We will introduce Hamiltonian mechanics, Poisson brackets, Liouville’s theorem and canonical transformation. Finally in this Hamiltonian formulation part we will introduce Hamilton-Jacobi equation and the technique of separation of variables. Apart from the above pure physics perspective of classical mechanics, we will also introduce some basics concepts of symplectic geometry to let the students understand geometric pictures of classical mechanics. Finally we will talk about integrable system via introducing Liouville integrability.
