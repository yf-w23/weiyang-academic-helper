课程简介：
《脑与机器智能》是一门研究生课程，聚焦脑科学与人工智能的交叉。课程以“编码、计算、学习”为主线，首先介绍脑科学与人工智能的发展脉络，并比较人脑与大模型在结构、功能与Scaling Law上的差异。随后系统讲授神经编码与计算机制，包括脉冲神经网络、群体编码、突触可塑性与生物可行学习算法。课程还讨论视觉、语言、运动与记忆等认知功能，并引入表征对齐、Theory of Mind等心理认知基准，分析大模型与人脑在感知、认知、学习等方面的差距。在学习机制方面，内容涵盖持续与终身学习、预测编码及多时间尺度适应，突出大脑的可塑性与鲁棒性。课程也将初步介绍脑机接口的基本原理与脑机对齐的应用潜力。后期专题聚焦大模型的可解释性、幻觉与意识等进阶问题，探讨其与大脑机制的异同。教学上强调理论与实践结合，通过讲授、研读、实验和项目，使学生既能理解脑科学原理，又能设计并实现类脑算法，为未来NeuroAI与设计类脑智能系统奠定基础。
英文课程简介：
Natural Intelligence and Artificial Intelligence is a graduate course at the intersection of neuroscience and AI. Structured around coding, computation, and learning, it begins with an overview of both fields and compares the brain with artificial intelligence frameworks such as large language models in structure, function, and scaling laws. It then explores neural coding and computation, covering spiking neural networks, population coding, synaptic plasticity, and biologically plausible learning rules. Cognitive functions such as vision, language, motor control, and memory are examined alongside benchmarks like brain-machine alignment and theory of mind, highlighting gaps between human cognition and large language models. Learning mechanisms including continual and lifelong learning, predictive coding, and multi-timescale adaptation emphasize brain plasticity and robustness. The course also introduces basic applications of brain–computer interfaces. In later weeks, frontier topics such as interpretability, hallucinations, and consciousness in large language models are addressed, drawing parallels and contrasts with brain mechanisms. Combining lectures, readings, labs, and projects, the course integrates theory with practice, enabling students to understand neural principles, implement brain-inspired algorithms, and pursue independent research in NeuroAI and intelligent systems.
