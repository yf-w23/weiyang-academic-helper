课程内容简介：
生物地球化学是一门系统的科学，研究影响自然环境（生物圈、岩石圈、水圈、土壤圈和大气圈）的组成与反应的各种化学、物理、地质和生物过程，重点是化学元素（特别是对生命而言十分重要的C、N、P和S）在不同的时空格局下的循环，以及人类活动对环境的扰动。本课程从认识环境问题的成因和影响出发，关注相关的生物地球化学过程与机制，包括全球碳循环（与气候变化）、陆地生态系统氮循环（与生物多样性）、大气化学反应（与大气污染）、土壤退化以及重金属迁移（土壤污染）等。
Course Description
Biogeochemistry is a system science, studying chemical, physical, geological, and biological processes that govern reactions and composition of the natural environment (biosphere, cryosphere, hydrosphere, pedosphere, atmosphere and lithosphere). It involves the study of the cycles of major chemical elements, generally the lighter ones, that are important for life, such as C, N, P and S, at different spatial and temporal scales, which are either driven by or have an impact on biological activity. The ultimate goal of this course is to understand the effects of human perturbations, especially anthropogenic pollution, on the environment in which we live. The main processes concerned include global carbon cycle (and climate change), nitrogen cycle on land (and biodiversity), atmospheric reaction (and air pollution), soil degradation, trace metal transportation, and so on.
