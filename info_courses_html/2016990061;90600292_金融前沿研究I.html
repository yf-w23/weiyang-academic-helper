课程简介：
连续时间金融理论是现代金融经济学的重要分支，许多重要的研究领域如金融衍生品的定价、实物资产的投资正是建立在连续时间金融理论之上。本课程旨在运用连续时间模型的分析方法，阐释最优消费和投资组合选择、认股权证和期权定价理论、公司财务和金融中介理论的未定权益分析、金融跨期均衡理论、以及金融市场中的信息摩擦等问题。 学生学完本课程后，预期达到如下要求：正确理解随机微分方程和伊藤过程的基本概念，掌握连续时间下最优化问题的求解方法；在给定资产收益函数的情况下能够对该资产进行定价和对冲分析；掌握连续时间下资产市场的一般跨期均衡模型。
英文课程简介：
Continuous-time finance is a significant branch of modern financial economics, underpinning many critical research areas such as the pricing of financial derivatives and investment in real assets. This course aims to utilize the analytical methods of continuous-time models to elucidate optimal consumption and portfolio selection, the theory of warrant and option pricing, the contingent claims analysis in corporate finance and financial intermediation theory, the intertemporal equilibrium theory of finance, and issues of informational friction in financial markets. Upon completing this course, students are expected to achieve the following objectives: correctly understand the basic concepts of stochastic differential equations and Itô processes, master the methods for solving optimization problems in continuous time; be able to price and perform hedging analysis on assets given their return functions; and grasp the general intertemporal equilibrium models of asset markets in continuous time.
