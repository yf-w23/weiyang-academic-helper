课程简介：
有限元分析既需要学生掌握其基本原理，更需要学生掌握其在工程和科学问题中的具体建模方法和分析过程，因此，这是一门实践性很强的课程。课程教学结合教师专题讲解，课后学生自主学习、选题，与教师交流，课堂上学生阐述，教师点评相结合的方式。在教学中，非常明确地将教师定位于引导性角色，将学生定位于自主研究型角色，充分发挥出学生的主体性与主动性作用，从课程的开始，就实现了学生角色的主动转变。实践证明，这种教学方式受到学生的好评，对研究生的教学模式是一种有益的探索。引导式自主研究型教学模式所期望的目标为：在课堂中实现教师引导下的研究生自主专题研究，培养学生的进行专题研究的综合能力并按照引导性自主研究型教学模式进行不断的改进及完善。将考核方式服务于引导性的自主研究型教学模式，主要采用口头交流与书面报告的形式来进行考核，涉及自主研究的各个环节。在交流过程中拓展除本人研究专题以外的一些领域，该课程中研究生所开展的研究专题领域非常广泛，涉及重大装备结构、重大科学工程、汽车、制导、纳米材料、航天结构、电机工程、快速成形、超导材料、成形工艺等。
英文课程简介：
Finite element analysis requires students not only to grasp its fundamental principles but also to master specific modeling methods and analysis processes applied to engineering and scientific problems. Therefore, this course is highly practice-oriented. The teaching approach combines thematic lectures by the instructor, independent after-class learning and topic selection by students, discussions with the instructor, in-class presentations by students, and feedback from the instructor. In this process, the teacher is clearly positioned as a guiding role, while students take on the role of autonomous researchers, fully leveraging their initiative and active participation. From the very beginning of the course, students proactively transition into this active role. Practical experience has shown that this teaching approach is well received by students and represents a valuable exploration of graduate-level teaching models. The goal of the guided autonomous research-based teaching model is to enable graduate students to conduct independent thematic research under the instructor's guidance, cultivate their comprehensive research capabilities, and continuously refine and improve the model. The assessment method aligns with this guided autonomous research-based teaching model, primarily relying on oral presentations and written reports, covering various aspects of independent research. Through these exchanges, students expand their knowledge beyond their specific research topics. The research topics undertaken by graduate students in this course span a wide range of fields, including major equipment structures, large-scale scientific engineering projects, automotive engineering, guidance systems, nanomaterials, aerospace structures, electrical engineering, rapid prototyping, superconducting materials, and forming processes.
