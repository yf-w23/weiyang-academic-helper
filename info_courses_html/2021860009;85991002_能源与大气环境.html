课程简介：
课程重点讲授能源与大气环境的基础科学知识、研究方法和学科前沿。课程内容涵盖能源资源禀赋、生产与消费特征，能源利用中的大气环境问题（大气污染和气候变化），气候环境变化对能源供需的影响，能源系统绿色低碳转型的技术、路径及其环境影响等。本课程旨在帮助学生掌握能源环境问题的系统分析和控制方法，从绿色低碳转型视角建立解决能源环境问题的宏观思路。
英文课程简介：
The course focuses on the basic scientific knowledge, research methods and frontiers of energy and atmospheric environment. The course covers energy resource endowment, production and consumption characteristics, atmospheric environmental problems related to energy use (air pollution and climate change), the impact of climate and environmental changes on energy supply and demand, technologies，pathways of green low-carbon transition of energy systems and their environmental impacts. This course aims to help students master the systematic analysis and control methods of energy and environmental problems, and establish macroscopic ideas to solve energy and environmental problems from the perspective of green and low-carbon transition.
