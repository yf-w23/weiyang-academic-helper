课程简介：
“中国发展实践考察”课程是清华大学公共管理学院可持续发展公共政策双学位硕士（MPP-SDG）、国家援外国际发展与治理国际硕士项目（MIDG）、国际发展国际硕士项目（MID）的必修环节，旨在将课程学习和发展实践相结合。国家援外硕士项目的中国发展实践考察将提升学生对于中国政治、经济、社会和文化发展的认知水平，并提升他们的领导力能力。可持续发展公共政策双学位硕士生的中国发展实践考察将聚焦于增进他们在可持续发展各个领域的知识。 “中国发展实践考察”课程有助于学生了解中国发展的经验和挑战。社会实践从不同视角为学生提供学习实际发展经验的机会，使得他们形成对于中国发展的正确认知。学生将与来自政府、企业、非政府组织的实务人士交流，甚至有机会走进农村和城市社区。学生可以将前期课程所学的知识和技能运用到对社会实践所得数据的分析当中，并为硕士论文工作提供准备。
英文课程简介：
The course "China Development: Field Trip Series" is a compulsory component of the Master's education curriculum in the area of sustainable development at the School of Public Policy & Management, Tsinghua University. Knowledge learning would be linked with development practice. Field trips of foreign aid Master's students would be designed to improve their understanding of China's political, economic, social, and cultural development, as well as develop their leadership. Field trips of dual master degree students would be designed to enhance their knowledge in various areas of sustainable development. The field trip series is designed to help students explore development experience and challenges that China are facing. The trips will provide students with first-hand development experiences from different perspectives, and help students form the understanding of China’s development. Students will meet with practitioners from government, business, NGOs, and even visit villages and urban communities. They can test their knowledge and skills they have learned in previous courses, which can then help them prepare and consolidate the dissertation.

进度安排：


Based on specific themes of study, places and route of site visits would be selected and organized. Students are required to attend a field trip which contains an equivalent workload of 48 class hour study. Attendance of all site visits will be a prerequisite of completing the course study. Pre-research, on-site visits, seminars, lectures, and summary will be the main forms of learning. Field trip reflection is required, which serves as the basis for performance evaluation. For foreign aid Master's students, the subjects of study could include Chinese history of revolution, Chinese history and culture, leadership, economic development, social development, ecological and environmental protection, urban planning, etc. For other Master's students, the subjects of study may focus on sustainable development, which could be development practices in one or several UN SDGs fields. Students are encouraged to develop the knowledge application and analytical capacity.﻿
