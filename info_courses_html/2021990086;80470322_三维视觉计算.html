课程简介：
本课程将深入探讨大数据时代三维视觉计算中的先进技术。课程将涵盖对几何分析基本概念的简单介绍，覆盖曲线、曲面、三维表征、三维表换等基础，帮助学生快速建立对三维数据的几何理解。在建立基本的几何概念之后，课程将按照从三维重建到三维识别的思路展开，一方面介绍三维数据的获取，涵盖多视角立体三维重建、单图像三维重建、神经辐射场、表面重建等技术，另一方面深入介绍三维数据的识别理解，解析最新的三维深度学习技术并介绍其在诸如三维目标检测、三维分割、位姿估计、内蕴分析等一系列问题中的应用思路。同时本课程将简要介绍三维视觉计算中的最新研究方向与进展。通过本课程的学习，学生将理解三维视觉计算领域的关键性问题、前沿解决方案以及现阶段的发展局限性，对三维感知系统形成更完整的认识并理解其与认知、决策、控制等相关问题的丰富交互及其在人工智能系统中的重大价值。
英文课程简介：
This course will focus on the advanced technologies in 3D visual computing in the era of big data. The course will cover a brief introduction to the basic concepts in the geometric analysis including curve, surface, 3D representations, 3D transformations, etc., with the goal of fostering students’ geometric understanding of 3D data. After establishing the geometric foundations, the course will go from 3D reconstruction to 3D recognition. On one hand, the course will explain popular ways of reconstructing 3D data, covering multi-view stereo, single image-based 3D reconstruction, NeRF, surface reconstruction, etc. On the other hand, the course will delve deep into 3D recognition and understanding, analyzing recent 3D deep learning techniques and introducing their applications in a range of important 3D perception problems including 3D object detection, 3D segmentation, pose estimation, intrinsic analysis, etc. The course will also briefly introduce the recent research directions for 3D visual computing. Through this course, students will understand the key problems, state-of-the-art solutions as well as limitations in the current 3D visual computing area. Students will form a more comprehensive landscape of 3D visual computing in modern AI systems and understand their interactions with planning, decision making, and control.
