课程内容简介：
作为面向物理专业和相关的科学工程专业的第一门基础物理课程， 将系统地讲授力学，狭义相对论和波动光学（干涉，衍射）。 为今后的物理及相关学科的学习打下坚实的基础。
Course Description
This class is for students in physics or engineering with close relation with science. There are two parts in this class. Part I classical mechanics: particle kinematics，momentum conservation，particle dynamics，mechanical energy conservation，angular momentum conservation，rigid body mechanics，continuum mechanics，vibration and wave，gravity， the theory of relativity. Part II optics: geometrical optics and interference of light.
