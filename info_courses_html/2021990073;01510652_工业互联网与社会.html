课程内容简介：
2025年，工业互联网已连续8年被写入《政府工作报告》。作为人工智能时代的数字底座，DeepSeek等前沿技术重要应用领域之一的工业互联网是第四次工业革命的重要基石和关键支撑，也是我国新基建的重要建设内容。工业互联网作为新兴的交叉学科，市场的人才需求强烈和创新创业的机会较多。工业互联网在传统的工业自动化技术之上融合了数字化技术比如5G/6G, 物联网，云计算，大数据，大语言模型这些新兴的技术。本课程从消费互联网这个“互联网+”的原点出发，使同学们在理解产业互联网思维的同时，结合在基础工业训练中心的工程实验，理解相关技术点，并逐渐理解国家在制造业实体经济的战略布局，和工业互联网带来的技术和商业创新机会。本课程没有先修要求和专业要求，对于技术概念通过动手实践让同学们有基本的了解，对于涉及到的商业模式和运营环境通过走进企业，案例讲解和互动讨论现场“还原”，对于产业的概念通过图形示例和与消费互联网的对比教学讲解清楚。本课程强调产业互联网思维方式的建立与思辨，和站在产业角度的整体思考，注重学科交叉创新能力与协作力的培养，重视真实应用体验。
Course Description
By 2025, the industrial Internet had been included in the Government Work Report for eight consecutive years. As the digital foundation of the era of artificial intelligence, the industrial Internet, one of the important application fields of cutting-edge technologies such as DeepSeek, is an important cornerstone and key support of the Fourth Industrial Revolution, and also an important part of China's new infrastructure construction. As an emerging interdisciplinary field, the industrial Internet has a strong demand for talents in the market and offers numerous opportunities for innovation and entrepreneurship. The industrial Internet integrates digital technologies such as 5G/6G, the Internet of Things, cloud computing, big data, and large language models on top of traditional industrial automation technologies. This course starts from the consumer Internet, the origin of "Internet +", enabling students to understand the thinking of industrial Internet while combining the engineering experiments in the Basic industrial training center to understand the relevant technical points, and gradually understand the strategic layout of the country in the manufacturing real economy and the technological and business innovation opportunities brought by industrial Internet. This course has no prerequisite or major requirements. Through hands-on practice, students will gain a basic understanding of technical concepts. The business models and operational environments involved will be "restored" on-site by entering enterprises, case studies, and interactive discussions. The concepts of industries will be clearly explained through graphic examples and comparative teaching with the consumer Internet. This course emphasizes the establishment and critical thinking of industrial Internet thinking patterns, as well as overall thinking from an industrial perspective.
