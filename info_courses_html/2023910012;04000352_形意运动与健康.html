课程内容简介：
本课程符合清华大学“三位一体”的教育理念，旨在深入探讨中医哲学理念与健康的本质， 传播中医药优秀传统文化，树立学生文化自信与民族自信。本课程从传统哲学理念出发，阐 述中医原创思维方式及健康之道，包括形神一体观、顺应四时阴阳养生、调节脏腑功能等， 解析中医哲学如何影响现代医疗与健康观念。课程将介绍中医整体生命观、中医健康观、中 医养生观、中医防病观，并在“形神一体观”理论指导下进行具体运动健康的实践，比如五 禽戏、八段锦、气功等，使学习方式更加生动。课程能够帮助学生理解中医健康之道背后蕴 含的原理，培养健康意识，提升身心健康水平，实现具有中医特色的自我健康管理。在知识 讲授的同时注重培养学生发散思维能力，提升学生健康生活能力。并通过此课程的开设，树 立学生对中国传统文化的热爱与自信，领悟生命的内涵，规范意志行为。
Course Description
The course is in line with the educational philosophy of "Trinity" of Tsinghua University, aiming to deeply explore the philosophy of traditional Chinese medicine and the essence of health, spread the excellent traditional culture of traditional Chinese medicine, and establish students' cultural and national confidence. Starting from traditional philosophical concepts, this course elaborates on the original thinking methods and health methods of traditional Chinese medicine, including the concept of integrating form and spirit, conforming to the four seasons of yin and yang for health preservation, and regulating the functions of organs. It analyzes how traditional Chinese medicine philosophy affects modern medical and health concepts. The course will introduce the overall life view, health view, health preservation view, and disease prevention view of traditional Chinese medicine. Under the guidance of the theory of "unity of form and spirit", specific exercise and health practices will be carried out, such as the Five Animal Play, Eight Section Brocade, and Qigong, to make the learning method more vivid. The course can help students understand the principles behind the path of traditional Chinese medicine health, cultivate health awareness, improve physical and mental health levels, and achieve self health management with traditional Chinese medicine characteristics. While imparting knowledge, emphasis is placed on cultivating students' divergent thinking abilities and enhancing their ability to live a healthy life. And through the establishment of this course, students can establish their love and confidence in traditional Chinese culture, understand the meaning of life, and regulate their willpower and behavior.
