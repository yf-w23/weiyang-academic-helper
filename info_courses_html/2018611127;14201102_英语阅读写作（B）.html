课程内容简介：
为适应大学生对英语和学术科研的需求，语言教学中心推出学术英语阅读写作系列课程。本课程，B级，即学术文章读写，面向英语分级考试中一二级的学生，强调以主题为基础的学术类文章的阅读和写作，帮助学生做好一般性学术英语读写的准备。课程期间，学生独立完成符合学术写作规范的一般性学术文章，同时本课程鼓励学生跨越专业之间的界限共同合作，培养学生的团队意识。
Course Description
Globalization and the development of Internet and AI make cross-cultural commnication more frequent and necessary. In order to facilitate foreign language acquisition and English composition,this course provides assistance in the mastery of basic English writing skills, a comprehensive survey in writing theories and practice, and enhencement of language proficiency and cultural awareness.The academic term will complete the following targets: 1 the purpose, features and format of English titles; 2 the three basic elements for title-writing, its position and related writing skills; 3 the structure of English paragraph, its unity, coherence, and cohesion such as parallelism,use of pronouns, reiteration of key words,use of synonyms and antonyms,use of conjunctions; 4 the methods for the development of English paragraph such as definition, classification, comparison and contrast, causes and effects; 5 cross-cutural and cross-lingual differences, and those in transcultural thinking modes; 6 writing of research paper and ways of argumentartion; 7 the student-centered classroom with timely supervision from the professor.
进度安排：

 教学周内完成下列内容：

1 作文标题写作的目的、特点、形式；2主题句(topic sentence)的三要素，主题句的位置和写作方法； 3英语段落的层次结构；段落的统一性、连贯性，段落中过渡与照应的方法；重复重点词，运用代词，运用平行结构，运用过渡连接词，围绕中心展开段落，运用照应、省略、重复，运用同义词、反义词；4 展开段落的方法：定义法、分类法、例举法、因果法、对比法、比较法、过程描述法等 5英汉语言文化差异和思维模式差异 6 论文写作方法：论点、论据、演绎、归纳、量化、直接论证、间接论证等。7学生中心，教师引导，定期写作。
