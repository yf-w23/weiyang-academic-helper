课程内容简介：
本课程主要围绕日语论文“写什么、怎样写”的问题，教授本科生学习学位论文和学术论文的内容和形式。内容包括学习日语论文写作的步骤，要求和格式。（一）通过研讨日语语言文学专业的研究课题，探索“写什么”的问题。（二）通过分析日语学位论文的范文，学习日语论文的写作方法。（三）通过课堂讨论学生所写的小论文，明确论文的写作规范，发现写作过程中的问题。（四）学生课下收集、阅读文献资料，写出本科毕业论文的开题报告，基本明确毕业论文的写作思路。
Course Description
This course provides students with a detailed understanding of Japanese thesis writing. Topics focused include: what to write and how to write in a Japanese thesis. For undergraduate students this course has a substantial writing skills component such as use formal writing procedure to plan thesis writing, as well as the formats which determine the academic level of Japanese thesis expression. Contents of this course includes: 1） Explore the question of “what to write” by referring to the research topics of Japanese language and literature.2）Learn Japanese thesis writing method by analyzing the thesis writing model from Japanese degree.3）Review and discuss the students writing practices in peer to find out any problems and make a clearly understanding of academic writing standards.3）Collect and read additional document literature and write the proposal of undergraduate thesis. Students are expected to make explicit ideas of thesis through this course.
