课程简介：
因果推断是一门通过实验与非实验数据获得因果结论的学科。在这堂课上，我们会讨论因果推断领域最新的研究成果。包括这些研究成果的理论贡献以及局限性，以及与经济学、流行病学、医学、管理学以及计算机学科之间的关系。课程目标是让课程参与者有能力阅读因果推断领域最新的论文成果。
英文课程简介：
Causal inference is a science of drawing causal conclusions from experimental and non-experimental data. In this lecture, we will discuss most recent papers in the area of causal inference, including its theoretical advancement and limitations. By the end of this lecture participants are expected to be able to read and understand most of the recent publications in the area of causal inference. The entire course is splitted into two parts, in the first part, the instructor will introduce basic concepts and most recent progress in causal inference research; the second part is mostly based on student presentations of their relevant research projects or papers they are interested in.
