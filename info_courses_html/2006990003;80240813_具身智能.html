课程简介：
具身智能强调智能受脑、身体与环境协同影响，更侧重关注智能体与环境的“交互”，被誉为人工智能的终极形态，对推动智能机器人等领域的研究有极其重要的作用。在具身智能的研究中，智能体的物理形态与感知、学习、行为的关系起到至关重要的作用。本课程从这四个方面出发，系统梳理了具身智能的内涵与外延，建立了具身智能的体系结构，详细介绍了形态计算、形态控制、形态优化、视觉导航、主动感知、具身场景描述、具身学习与多智能体协同的相关原理与方法，并附有实例讲解。
英文课程简介：
Embodied intelligence highlights the impact of the interaction between the brain, body, and environment on intelligence. It is considered a pinnacle of artificial intelligence and is pivotal in advancing intelligent robotics research. This course explores the relationship between an agent's physical form and its perception, learning, and behavior. It systematically organizes the concepts of embodied intelligence, outlines its structure, and explains the principles and methods of morphological computing, control, optimization, visual navigation, active perception, scene description, learning, and multi-agent collaboration through examples.
