课程内容简介：
简介：本课程是无机非金属材料学科的一门专业课程，全面系统地介绍了结构陶瓷的发展历程、基本概念、种类、制备方法及理论、发展及应用等内容。希望同学们能够全面掌握结构陶瓷的基本特性、制备工艺、微观结构、性能和应用等相关知识，学会结构陶瓷材料科学及工程技术的研究方法和思路。
Course Description
This course is a specialized one for inorganic nonmetallic materials. It systematically introduces the history, basic concept, type, preparation method and theory, development and application of structural ceramics. The goal of this course is to help students to master the basic characteristics, preparation process, microstructure, performance and application of structural ceramics, and to learn the research method and thought for structrual ceramic materials science and engineering technology.
