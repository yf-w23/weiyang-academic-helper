课程内容简介：
《计算机原理与系统》是通用人工智能因材施教班基础课程。本课程以从程序到执行的阶段与流程为为线索，深入拓展学生对计算机原理与系统的深入理解。课程注重实践能力的培养，强化动手实践，强调对基本概念和基本方法的理解和灵活应用。课程提倡在课程中融入科学的方法论，要求学生养成多层次多角度开展计算性能分析的习惯，在实践环节综合运用理论技术开展独立工作的能力，对有余力的学生引入因材施教，开展研究性学习。
Course Description
Principles and systems of computer is a foundation course of General Artificial Intelligence Individualized Teaching Class. This course takes the stages and processes from sourcecodesto execution as clues to deeply expand students' in-depth understanding of computer principles and systems. The course focuses on the cultivation of practical ability, strengthens hands-on practice, and emphasizes the understanding and flexible application of basic concepts and basic methods. The curriculum advocates the integration of scientific methodology into it, requires students to develop the habit of carrying out computational performance analysis from multiple levels and multiple perspectives, comprehensively use theory and technology in practice to carry out independent work, and introduce students who have the ability to teach in accordance with their aptitude, and carry out research-based learning.
