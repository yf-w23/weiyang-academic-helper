课程内容简介：
生物统计学基础(Fundamentals of Biostatistic)是生物统计学的入门课，旨在基于医学和生命科学的数据，使学生掌握统计学基本概念与经典参数和非参数统计的方法。学习本课程，有助于阅读科学文献中的统计分析方法和结果以及在本专业中解决遇到的统计学问题。重点介绍基于z-分布、t-分布、卡方分布和F-分布所做的统计推论，其中线性回归与相关分析是本课程的主要考查内容。
Course Description
“Fundamentals of Biostatistics” is an introductory course of biostatistics, which aims to let students master the basic concepts of statistics and classical statistical parametric or non-parametric analysis methods based on medical and life science data. This course may be helpful for the students to rationally evaluate the analysis methods and results in scientific literature and to solve the statistical problems encountered in their scientific experiments. I will emphasis on the application of z-distribution, t-distribution, Chi-square distribution and F-distribution, in which regression and correlation analyses are the key content in the final examination of this course.
