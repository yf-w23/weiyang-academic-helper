材料科学前沿
学时：
16
学分：
1
课程简介：
专题讲授当今材料科学前沿领域的发展动向，以拓宽研究生的知识面，增强从事材料科学研究的事业心。讲座内容包括：新型的金属材料、功能陶瓷材料及应用、半导体材料与器件，有机光电功能材料，生物材料，新型太阳能材料，石墨烯材料，催化材料，超材料及应用，以及材料的微观结构分析表征方法与材料计算方法等。
英文课程简介：
The course contains a series of special lectures on the development trend of the frontier field of materials science, in order to broaden the knowledge of graduate students and enhance their dedication to materials science research. Lectures include: new metal materials, functional ceramic materials and applications, semiconductor materials and devices, organic photoelectric functional materials, biological materials, new solar materials, graphene materials, catalytic materials, metamaterials and applications, and materials microstructure analysis and characterization methods and materials calculation methods, etc.
