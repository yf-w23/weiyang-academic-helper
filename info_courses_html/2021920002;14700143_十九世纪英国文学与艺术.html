课程内容简介：
十九世纪(c. 1789-1914)对英国来说，大致从法国大革命开始，结束于一战前夕。这个时期的英帝国面积约占全球25%，国内社会秩序发生巨大变化，在政治、经济、文化、教育、宗教、科技、性别等问题上都产生了激烈的争论。十九世纪文学是英国文学研究中最活跃的领域之一，从浪漫主义到现代主义的诞生，为跨学科、跨文化研究提供了丰富的研究资料和思想资源。本课程将通过通过对一系列具体文本和历史语境的深入研读，帮助大家加深对历史和社会的理解，也对当代社会的复杂性有进一步了解。既希望提高大家对文学的品识力，也希望通过文学分析的写作，提升思辨能力和表达能力。
Course Description
Nineteenth-century (c. 1789-1914) British literature and culture is a rich field for interdisciplinary studies. This course spans a time of revolutions and changes. Writers of this time witnessed drastic social transformations: the development of public education systems, the transformation of publishing technology, newly defined social classes and barriers, etc. Literary writings of this period address very serious social issues of the rapidly modernizing nineteenth century. Over the next sixteen weeks, we will be discussing how nineteenth-century British writers challenged the social conventions of their times, and how they responded to pressing social issues when the British empire stretched itself to cover around 25% of the world’s land surface.
