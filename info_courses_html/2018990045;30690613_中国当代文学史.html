课程内容简介：
本课程主要讲授中国当代文学的发生、发展、演变规律，考察中国当代文学与其历史境遇的互动关系，探索这一时期主要的文学现象、潮流以及重要作家、作品的写作姿态、个性特征、现实和文化意含，帮助学生从整体上把握中国当代文学发展变化的脉络，较为深入地认识当代重要的文学现象、作家作品，在此基础上深入理解当代文学的性质，明了当代文学的分期及其依据，并掌握这一时期小说、诗歌、散文、戏剧等不同文体的基本美学特征及其演进。
Course Description
This course provides a crucial overview of the development and the patterns of contemporary Chinese literature, investigates the interactions between the contemporary Chinese literature and the historical reality of contemporary China, examines the important literary phenomena and the main streams since 1949, and presents the creative traits, the realistic and cultural meanings and the subjective positions of the famous writers and works in this period. This course will help students grasp the developing principles of contemporary Chinese literature, comprehend its important literary phenomena, its characters and historical stages, be acquainted its famous writers and works, and realize its main aesthetic characteristics of fiction, poetry and prose
