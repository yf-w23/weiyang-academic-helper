课程内容简介：
海洋气象水文学是应用水文与气象学知识，研究海洋-大气系统中水循环、能量平衡及耦合机理的交叉学科。主要内容包括：大气静力学与边界层过程、海洋-大气系统的水与能量循环、海气耦合机制、五大洋水循环特征、台风/飓风及ENSO（厄尔尼诺-南方涛动）等典型现象，并介绍中尺度气象模式与全球气候模式在海洋气象水文研究中的应用。通过本课程的学习，学生将深入理解海洋-大气系统的耦合规律，掌握相关理论方程与分析方法，能够认识极端天气气候事件的成因，并了解数值模式在海洋气象水文模拟与预测中的前沿应用。
Course Description
Marine Hydrometeorology is an interdisciplinary field that studies the water cycle, energy balance, and coupling mechanisms within the ocean-atmosphere system. The main content includes: atmospheric statics and boundary layer processes, the water and energy cycles of the ocean-atmosphere system, ocean-atmosphere coupling mechanisms, water cycle characteristics of the five major oceans, and typical phenomena such as typhoons/hurricanes and ENSO (El Niño-Southern Oscillation). The course also introduces the application of mesoscale meteorological models and global climate models in hydrometeorological research. Through this course, students will gain an in-depth understanding of the coupling principles of the ocean-atmosphere system, master relevant theoretical equations and analytical methods, comprehend the causes of extreme weather and climate events, and become familiar with cutting-edge applications of numerical models in the simulation and prediction of marine hydrometeorological processes.
