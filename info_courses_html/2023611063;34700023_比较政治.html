课程内容简介：
比较政治是政治学的一个重要分支，强调通过比较的视角去认识政治现象和政治过程。本课程将参考国内外文献，围绕国家、政体、政党、选举制度、立法制度、行政制度、司法体系和央地关系等主题，比较不同国家政治制度和治理能力的差别。在跨国比较中，我们也可以更好地理解中国政治的运作逻辑。本课程的目标是让学生初步掌握比较政治研究的知识体系，增强文献阅读和学术表达能力，加强对现实政治问题的分析能力。
Course Description
Comparative politics is a major branch of political science. This course will cover various topics on comparative politics such as states, regimes, political parties, electoral systems, legislative systems, executive systems, judicial systems, and subnational governments, discussing how political institutions and governance differ across countries. Through comparison, we can also better understand the logic of Chinese politics. This course aims to expand students’ knowledge of comparative politics, enhance their academic communication skills, and strengthen their capabilities of analyzing real-life political issues.
