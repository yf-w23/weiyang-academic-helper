课程内容简介：
《英语口译入门》属于本科公共基础课程。本课程以中英互译为教学内容，循序渐进，以实际工作生活翻译内容 为教授重点，从听力笔记、词汇选用、分析表达等方面入手，循序渐进地帮助学生了解并初步掌握翻译技巧的同 时，提升翻译术语收集管理和运用能力，培养良好的职业习惯。 本门课程采用综合视角，突出话题的全面性和时效性。授课内容包含文化、科技、经济、外交、教育、环保等广 泛领域，兼顾国际热点和国内话题。作为入门级课程，充分考虑难度的渐进性以及和本科公共外语必修课的衔接 性。从难度较小的对话口译逐渐拉长文字篇幅。话题从日常对话等难度较少的领域逐渐过渡到会谈磋商、演讲翻 译等有一定专业性难度的领域。 课堂教学方面借鉴外交部翻译司场景化口译培训方式，在课前设置“译前准备”环节，课后设置“术语归纳”环 节，提升口译学习的实战感，现场感。通过课上“英语新闻综述”环节，扩大口译学生急需的各领域背景知识，提 高外语表达能力、临场应急能力和专业自信心。课程拟邀请国家翻译机构权威语言专家、国内外翻译高校口译专 业教授、一线高级口译人员入校开展讲座、答疑等多形式交流，体现课程实践型特色。
Course Description
“Introduction to Interpreting" is a basic public language course for undergraduate students. The course focuses on interpreting between Chinese and English, and is designed for students a certain degree of familiarity with bilingual communication. The course takes real work settings as key lecturing content. Starting from analytical listening, note-taking, and vocabulary building, it gradually helps students understand and initially master translation skills while enhancing their abilities to collect, manage and use terminologies as required by the profession. The course is comprehensive in subjects. The content includes a wide range of fields such as culture, technology, economics, diplomacy, education, and environment, and encompasses both international hot topics and domestic issues. This introductory-level course fully considers the need for progressiveness in difficulty and its connection with compulsory public foreign language courses offered to undergraduate students. The length of the texts gradually increases from daily conversation interpreting to more sophisticated areas such as meeting and speech translation. In terms of classroom teaching, the course borrows the scenario-based interpreting training methods of the translation departments of the Chinese foreign ministry. It includes a "pre-translation preparation" segment and an after-class "terminology summary" segment to help students expand their learning experience. The in-class “news briefing” segment is designed to help students expand knowledge in various fields, and improve their language skills and professional confidence. As a course of practical nature, language experts from state translation institutions, renowned professors, and senior interpreters are invited to give lectures, answer questions, and engage in various forms of academic communication with students.
