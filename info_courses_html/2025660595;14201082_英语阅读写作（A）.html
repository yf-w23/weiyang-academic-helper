课程内容简介：
为适应大学生对英语和学术科研的需求，语言教学中心推出学术英语阅读写作系列课程。本课程为A级，侧重研究论文读写，面向英语分级考试三、四级学生，强调以项目为基础的研究论文的读写学习，帮助学生做好专业英语论文读写的准备。课程期间，学生通过分组调研，独立完成IMRD格式的研究论文写作。完成项目可以使学生在理解研究论文写作框架的同时跨越专业之间的界限共同合作，培养学生的团队意识。
Course Description
Reading & Writing for Academic Purposes (A) teaches undergraduates to read and write research papers in order to participate in intellectual communities at Tsinghua and beyond. In this course, we will study research papers that explore topics including academic integrity, digital technology, psychology, and writing processes. Through your reading, you will learn to critically analyze the form and content of research papers. In your writing, you will learn to conduct a research project and write a research paper based on your findings. This course is an effort to equip you with principled writing strategies and established research methods that you can adopt in the future to draft academic writing that matters.
进度安排：
This course employs a strong emphasis on the production of a research paper using a project-based approach. You will conduct a mini research project in group and finish individual writing assignments based on your group project.
  1. In phase I (week 1-3), we will introduce academic writing in general and you are expected to locate the scholarly problem your group is interested in. At the end of phase I, your group will design a Draft Research Plan. The research plan will not be graded but it will help to manage your research project. You are strongly encouraged to speak with me about the feasibility of your research project and revise your research plan.
  2. In phase II (week 4-7), we will discuss source-based writing and you are expected to conduct a literature review for your research project and establish the context and significance of your research in the Introduction section (20%).
  3. In phase III (week 8-12), we will discuss research methodology and you are expected to finish data collection and analysis. You will describe the procedure of your research project and the rationale behind in the Methods section (10%). Later, you will report and interpret the findings of your research project in the Results and the Discussion sections (20%).
  4. In phase IV (week 13-16), we will discuss research publication and you are expected to evaluate your research project. You will present your research project in group and receive peer feedback. Later, you will edit your research paper and submit a final version of the Research Paper (25%). As the key sections of the research paper have already been prepared throughout the semester, demands in the final week should be manageable.
考核方式：
Class participation: 10%； Project-based writing: 75%； Project presentation: 15%
