课程内容简介：
本课程面对较高英语语言水平的学生，旨在提高学生高级学术口头交流能力，包括（1） 充分准确理解正式及非正式的口头交流信息，（2）正式与非正式地口头陈述个人观点， 包括会议发言等，（3）提问及回答及、解释个人观点等。课程采取任务型教学法，通过 类真实的交流语境，为学生提供充足的习练机会。课程评估采取形成性评估方法，学生需 独立完成两个口语任务和小组合作完成一个口语任务。
Course Description
This is a learner-centered course at the high-intermediate to advanced level, in which students continue to develop their academic skills in listening and speaking. Half of the course will focus on Listening. Note- taking will be developed during listening; it further helps students to understand the principles of effective note-taking in order to create more focused, complete and organized notes. Speaking will be another half of the course. It focuses on building fluency, accuracy, and improving pronunciation. Meanwhile, the course helps students cultivate skills in leading discussions and individual presentations which are supportive to their future academic development in attending seminars or workshops.
