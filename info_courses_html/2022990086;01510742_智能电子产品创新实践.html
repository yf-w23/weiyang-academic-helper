课程内容简介：
本课程由智能电子产品的创意设计、先进材料及制造技术的认知与体验，以及智能电子产品创新实践项目综合训练两个板块组成。各部分主要课程内容包括： 一、智能电子产品的创意设计、先进材料及制造技术的认知与体验：该部分包括两个产品创意设计、先进材料及3D打印技术（含陶瓷3D打印、多彩3D打印、生物3D打印和超精密3D打印等）、外骨骼技术、脑机交互技术和柔性电子技术的认知与体验。培养学生建立科学的产品设计思维、掌握与智能电子产品制造相关的技术、促进学生深入理解创新思维与方法在智能产品制造中的运用。 该板块内容拟安排26学时，以课程讲授和认知体验为主。主要包括：(1) 课程概述、智能电子产品实践训练（3学时）；（2）产品设计思维与训练、产品造型设计与渲染、3D打印认知与体验、仿生设计产品设计与应用（12学时）；（3）外骨骼技术与脑机交互技术（2学时）；（4）柔性电子技术与智能电子产品，包括电子产品设计与元件贴装、纳米银制作柔性可穿戴电子手环、液态金属制作电子皮肤控制机械仿生手（7学时）；（5）人工智能认知与体验（2学时）。 二、智能电子产品创新实践项目综合训练：以医工交叉为主要创新应用方向，可结合课程提供的先进技术和产品，选取外骨骼、脑机交互和柔性电子等方向的课程内容，开展创意设计与实践训练。 该板块内容拟安排6学时，以综合实践训练等形式为主。主要包括：方案设计与研讨、项目路演等。
Course Description
This course consists of two parts: creative design, experience of advanced materials and manufacturing technology, and comprehensive training. The main course includes: 1、 Creative design, experience of advanced materials and manufacturing technology: This section includes two product creative designs, experience of advanced materials and 3D printing technology (including ceramic, colorful, biological, and ultra precision 3D printing), exoskeleton technology, brain computer interaction technology, and flexible electronic technology. Cultivate students to establish scientific product design thinking, master technologies related to intelligent electronic product manufacturing, and promote students to deeply understand the application of innovative thinking and methods in intelligent product manufacturing. The content is planned to be arranged for 26 class hours, mainly focusing on course teaching and cognitive experience. Mainly including: (1) Course overview, practical training (3); (2) Product design thinking and training, product modeling design and rendering, 3D printing experience, bionic design product design and application (12); (3) Exoskeleton technology and brain computer interaction technology (2); (4) Flexible electronic technology and intelligent electronic products (7); (5) Artificial Intelligence Cognition and Experience (2). 2、 Comprehensive Training for Intelligent Electronic Product Innovation Practice Project: Focusing on the intersection of medical and industrial fields as the main innovative application direction, courses in the fields of exoskeleton, brain computer interaction, and flexible electronics can be selected based on the advanced technologies and products provided in the course to carry out creative design and practical training. The content is planned to be arranged for 6, mainly in the form of comprehensive practical training, mainly including: scheme design and discussion, project roadshows.
