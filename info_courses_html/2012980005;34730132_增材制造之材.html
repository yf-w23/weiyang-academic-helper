课程内容简介：
本课程是面向未央书院本科生的必修课，主要采用桌面型增材制造（3D打印）机器作为课程 实验配套工具，并配合专门开发的讲义，课程主要包括：基本原理、3D打印设备结构认识、 软件使用、3D建模与打印、3D打印原材料制备等。其中结构认识是指学生通过自己动手拆装 机器，认识3D打印机内部结构，通过学习打印分层软件，掌握3D成型的基本原理；3D建模与 打印是指用基本建模软件（SOLIDWORKS、CREO、PHOTOSHOP等）建立三维立体模型，能独立 完 成模型的编辑、切层处理，并最终打印出学生自己设计的3D模型；利用虚拟现实平台引入模 型拆装，让学生体会3D设计的特点；学习3D打印材料的制备工艺，激发对材料研究的兴 趣。
Course Description
This course is mandatory for undergraduate students at Weiyang College, with the experimental basis of desktop additive manufacturing (3D printing) together with bespoke tutorials. The main structure of the course follows the fundamentals, the equipment layout of additive manufacturing, guidance for the software, modeling & printing, and raw material preparation. Among all, the equipment layout workshop will be a dismantle and reinstallation by the students themselves, which aims to help the students understand the inner structure of the printer. Then the layer-by-layer printing software will help the students grasp the basic principles of the in-situ forming of materials. Afterward, the modeling & printing module indicates the build of the 3D model via basic commercial CAD software (e.g. SOLIDWORKS, CREO, PHOTOSHOP, etc.). Students are expected to edit and layer the as-built model and finally print the 3D structure designed by themselves after the module. To conclude, the object of this course will be ① to introduce the characters of 3D printing via virtual-reality-based (VR) dismantle &reinstallation; ② to stimulate students’ interests in materials research via a series of workshops on additive manufacturing.
