课程简介：
生态系统碳汇评估和增汇技术课程以培养学生掌握生态系统碳汇产生的基本原理、碳汇的评估技术和增汇技术为主要目的，课程的主要内容包括：生态系统碳汇形成机制，认识生态系统碳循环及碳源汇形成机制；生态系统碳汇长期管理，碳汇长期的动态变化预测及管理措施；林业碳汇计量与增汇，森林和草地生态系统的碳汇计量方法与主要林业增汇措施；农业碳汇计量与增汇，农业生态系统的碳汇计量方法与主要增汇措施；湿地碳汇计量与增汇，湿地生态系统的碳汇计量方法与主要增汇措施；可再生能源-碳汇耦合，可再生能源生产与增汇耦合措施；碳汇遥感监测，生态系统碳汇遥感监测方法；生态系统碳汇交易，生态系统的碳汇国内外交易规则及最新市场动态。
英文课程简介：
The aim of the course, Techniques for Assessing and Improving the Ecosystem Carbon Sink, is to help students to understand the theory underlying the ecosystem carbon sink, its evaluation skill, and techniques for enhance the carbon sink. The main content of the course includes: the mechanism of ecosystem carbon sink, with a focus on the carbon cycle and formation of carbon sink in ecosystems; long-term management of ecosystem carbon sink, with a focus on projections of long-term dynamics and associated management measures; forestry carbon sink accounting and techniques for enhancing the sink, with a focus on accounting methods of forest and grassland ecosystem carbon sink and major carbon sink enhancing measures in the forestry sector; agricultural carbon sink accounting and techniques for enhancing the sink, with a focus on accounting methods of agricultural ecosystem carbon sinks and major carbon sink enhancing measures in the agricultural sector; wetland carbon sink accounting and techniques for enhancing the sink, with a focus on accounting methods of wetland ecosystem carbon sinks and major carbon sink enhancing measures for wetlands; coupling of renewable energy and carbon, with a focus on co-generation of renewable energy and carbon sinks; remote sensing of carbon sinks, with a focus on remote sensing techniques for monitoring carbon sinks; carbon trade, with a focus on domestic and international rules of carbon trade and the latest market trends.
