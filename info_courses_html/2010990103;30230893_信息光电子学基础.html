课程内容简介：
光电子学是光学与电子学的交叉科学。以光电子学为基础的现代信息获取、传输、处理、存储和显示等功能支撑了现代信息社会的高速发展。 光学频段可以利用的带宽远大于电子学所涉及的带宽范围，但光学信号产生、传输、处理和检测的方式和电子学有较大差异。如何充分利用这样大的带宽需要具备光电子学特有的基础知识体系。本课程面向光电子学在信息社会中的应用，讲授光的电磁本性，传播规律，产生机制和探测原理，帮助学生建立对信息光电子学及其思想方法的整体认识，为学生今后从事相关研究与工程应用工作打下良好的基础。
Course Description
Optoelectronics is the cross-discipline of optics and electronics. New methods of information acquisition, transmission, processing, storage and presentation based on optoelectronics have significantly changed the world. The bandwidth of optics is much higher than that of the electronics. In order to take advantage of such huge bandwidth, one has to acquire a new knowledge system of optoelectronics, since there are fundamental differences between photons and electons. This course introduces fundamentals knowledge of propagation characteristics of light as an electromagnetic wave, the mechanism of light and laser generation and the principle of light detection. The course will help students to establish an essential understanding of the information optoelectronics and provide them a solid body of knowledge for their further works on this field.
