课程简介：
宽带无线通信发展非常迅猛，无论是移动通信、广播还是微波传输。以移动通信为例，在短短十几年时间里，跨越了Kb/s Mb/s，向Gb/s发展。 上世纪末提出的MIMO技术，为人们突破信息论基本约束不断的提高传输速率找到了新的途径。另一方面，为了提高传输速率所用的信道带宽不断提高，信道带宽的增加有使得信道的时延扩展变得严重。OFDM等技术又被重新提出，以对抗信道的频率选择性衰落。本课程从信道模型出发，深入刻画宽带信道的特性，为展开各种频率选择性衰落技术提供理论基础。另一方面从信道容量角度出发，探讨MIMO技术在宽带无线通信中的作用。同时，每学年还会根据学术产业发展的趋势介绍3～4个专题，作为课程的补充。 为了使同学们对宽带无线通信有更全面的理解，课程会提供30个左右近年来宽带无线通信乃至无线通信研究的热点问题，同学们选择一个题目完成课程的project。
英文课程简介：
Wideband wireless digital communication, such as Mobile communication, Broadcast, Microwave, developed rapidly. Taking mobile communication as an example, it increased from Kb/s, Mb/s to Gb/s. MIMO was proposed at the end of last century, and it is a new path that people could break through the basic constraints of continuous improvement of transmission rate of information theory. On the other hand, to improve transmission rate，the channel bandwidth was constantly increase. OFDM is reintroduced for confronting the frequency-selective fading. This course starts from channel model, and will deeply study the features of wideband channel. It could be the theoretical basis of any the frequency selective fading scenarios. What’s more, we will discuss the broadband wireless communication from the channel capacity view.In order to make students own a broad understanding of the broadband wireless communication, this course provides about 30 hot topic in the field of broadband wireless communication and even wireless communication in recent years. Students could choose each of the 30 to complete this project of this course.
