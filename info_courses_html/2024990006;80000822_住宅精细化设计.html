课程简介：
开设背景： 1.我国住房市场迈入“新常态” 2.我国住宅建设走向“品质化” 3.我国人口结构加速“老龄化” 4.我国城市建设走向“智能化” 主要内容： 1. 我国住宅设计的发展历程与趋势展望 2. 老年人居住建筑的常见类型与设计要点 3. 住宅精细化设计的原理与方法 4. 居家适老化改造设计实践 课程目标： 1. 关注国家发展：了解国家政策对住宅设计的影响，关注国家、社会发展的大趋势 2. 强化专业能力：从居住者的使用需求入手，关注生活中相关的细节 3. 拓宽知识领域：学习发达国家住宅设计先进理念和方法，关注“建筑与人”的关系 4. 提高社会责任感：了解弱势群体的生活。为普通老百姓设计
英文课程简介：
Background: 1. China's housing market has entered a “new normal status.” 2. Housing construction in China is shifting toward “quality-oriented development.” 3. China's population structure is accelerating toward “aging.” 4. Urban development in China is moving toward “smart cities.” Contents: 1. Evolution and Future Trends in China's Housing Design 2. Common Types and Design Considerations for Senior Housing 3. Principles and Methods of Detailed Design for Dwelling. 4. Design Practice of Home Modification for the Elderly. Course Objectives: 1. Align with National Development: Understand how national policies influence housing design and stay attuned to broader societal trends. 2. Enhance Professional Competence: Focus on residents' functional needs and pay attention to relevant details in daily life. 3. Expand Knowledge Horizons: Learn advanced housing design concepts and methods from developed countries, emphasizing the relationship between architecture and people. 4. Strengthen Social Responsibility: Gain insight into the lives of vulnerable groups and design for ordinary citizens. Translated with DeepL.com (free version)
