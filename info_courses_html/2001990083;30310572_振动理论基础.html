课程内容简介：
本课程主要介绍机械振动中的线性振动理论基础和分析方法。内容包括：振动问题的数学描述、特征量以及相应的特点；振动过程的分析方法（直接法、影响系数法和能量法）和以及经典解法和相关的数值计算方法。
Course Description
This course is a fundamental course designed to provide the foundation to the subsequent courses, such as Nonlinear vibration, Random vibration, Fatigue Mechanics, Aeroelasticity, Theory of Shell and Plate, Aircraft Structure Design etc., and to solve the engineering problems. The content of the course consists of three parts: basic theory, systems with two or more degrees of freedom, continuous systems. Basic theory includes introduction, free vibration, forced vibration and transient vibration.Systems with two or more degrees of freedom includes more freedom systems, properties of vibration systems and computational methods. Continuous systems includes string vibration, longitudinal vibration of rods, torisonal vibration with rods and vibration of beams.
