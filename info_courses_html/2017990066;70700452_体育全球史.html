课程简介：
正在起步时期的体育全球史不论是在历史学还是体育学领域都属前沿，充分体现了跨学科研究的特征。在研究生阶段进行体育全球史学习，可以进一步增强学生的体育史学理论修养，更加灵活运用多国档案文献，从跨国视角发掘体育传播的深层历史动因。总而言之，是对以往体育史研究或者是体育历史知识的一种提升和补充。具体到课程内容，课程前半段旨在通过向学生介绍具体体育项目的全球传播历史，使学生对体育全球史有直观感受与初步认知，在课程学习中补充历史学、社会学、外交学、政治学相关理论，帮助学生构建全球史概念、体系。课程后半段旨在向学生介绍体育强国的历史形成过程，从国际视角审视外来因素对他们各自国家体育发展带来的影响，用史实论证这些国家完善的体育体制、丰富悠久的体育文化等特色并非自行生成，而是建立在充分的、频繁的、长时间段的对外交流的基础之上，破除文化中心论与文化例外论。通过整个课程的学习，旨在帮助学生通过体育认识人类社会发展进步的重要动因——交往。从历史学角度论证体育交往在人类社会命运共同体构建中的作用，不仅能够帮助学生形成和平共处的价值观，亦能提升学生对体育重要性的认知，实现超越体育看体育的教学目标。
英文课程简介：
In terms of the content of the course, the first half of the course aims to introduce the global communication history of specific sports items to students, so that students can have an intuitive feeling and know how global history of sports it is. In addition, relevant theories of history, sociology, diplomacy and political science are supplemented in the course to help students build the concept and system of global history. The second half of the course is designed to introduce students the big countries’ sports history, focusing on how international external factors hand impact on their sports development including their effective sports system, using rich historic fact to prove their successfulness is not generated by oneself, but basing on frequent, long time foreign exchange, and get rid of culture-centered theory and cultural exceptionalism thoughts. Through the study of the whole course, it aims to help students understand the important motivation of human social development and progress through sports -- communication. That the role of sports communication demonstrate can construct a community with a shared future in human society can not only help students form the values of peaceful coexistence, but also improve their realization of the importance of sports and achieve the teaching goal of looking at sports beyond sports.
