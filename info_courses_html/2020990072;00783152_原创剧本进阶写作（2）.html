课程内容简介：
通过新闻事件改编、小说改编两个阶段的写作练习，在对剧本不断进行修改的过程中，迅速 提升学生的台词写作能力以及对事件的把握能力。课程分为两个阶段：一、新闻事件改编， 通过对新闻事件的改编，提升学生对戏剧事件的掌控能力，训练学生在事件中展开人物情感 关系变化、塑造人物形象。二、小说改编，意在训练学生从素材中捕捉人物性格和人物关系 的敏锐度，从而具有独立编织人物关系、架构故事情节的能力。
Course Description
Through the writing practice of adapting news events and novels, and in the process of constantly revising scripts, students' writing ability and the ability to hold the incidence will be greatly strengthened. This course is divided into two phrases: first stage, training of adapting news events. Through the adaptation of news events, students' ability to control drama events and develop the relationship changes of characters will be improved. Second stage, training of adapting novels. The purpose is to train students' acuity to shape characteristic and emotional relationships from novels. Students will gain the ability to independently create characters' relationships and story plots.
