课程内容简介：
研究显示，以英语为母语的孩子在10岁时词汇量已达到两万左右，但是我国高中毕业生在平均学习英语10年后词汇量只有区区的3、4千左右（大部分地区的孩子从小学三年级开始学习英语）。这么小的词汇量远远不能帮助学生顺利实现高校大学生培养方案所提出的人才培养目标。如何快速提高学生的英语词汇量就是我国目前大学英语教学普遍面临的最严峻的挑战。 英语不同于汉语，是字母文字。尽管只有26个字母，但基于这26个字母所形成的英语词汇量保守估计有6、7十万，有人甚至认为英语单词有一百万之多。如此巨大的词汇量有一个显著特点，就是英语的单词经常有十数个词由同一词根派生而来。词源学就是通过学习词根、词缀（前缀和后缀）来了解他们的发展及演变历史以及由此派生出的大量的英语词汇。本门课程旨在通过词源学帮助学生迅速而有效地扩大其词汇量，以应对大学学习期间所面临的各种挑战。预计本门课程可以在一学期里帮助学生提高1500个左右的词汇量。
Course Description
Evidence points to the close relationship between vocabulary and personal, professional, and intellectual growth. Research findings show that the only common characteristic of successful people in the work world is an unusual grasp of the meanings of words, and that those people drawing down the highest salaries have the largest vocabularies. Why do large vocabularies characterize executive and possibly outstanding men and women in other fields? It is probably because words are the instruments by means of which men and women grasp the thoughts of others and with which they do much of their own thinking. Actually, it is not their large vocabularies that make these people successful and intelligent, but their knowledge. Knowledge, however, is acquired largely through words. Words are the symbols of ideas and understanding; words are the symbols of knowledge, the keys to accurate thinking; ultimately words are tools of thoughts. Indeed, a very important part of language teaching at the university level involves helping students learn vocabulary. Personally the best approach to new words is through etymology. Etymology deals with the origin or derivation of words. When one knows the meaning of a root, he can better understand, and more easily remember, all the words built on this root. Learn one root and one has the key that will unlock the meanings of up to ten or twenty words in which the root appears. And this course is designed to help students enlarge their vocabulary through etymology by about 1500 words throughout a semester.
