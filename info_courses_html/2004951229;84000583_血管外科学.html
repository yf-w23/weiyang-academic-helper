课程简介：
血管外科是一门悠久历史，而又充满新鲜活力的医学专科，其专科性强，涉及除心脑外的全身其他部位的血管。近年来，随着科学技术的进步，血管外科学由基础研究到临床实践都有了重大的突破。各种新型技术、材料、药物和设备如影像学技术、介入技术等的临床应用，使血管外科发展站在了崭新的历史舞台上。以器官、系统、疾病为中心的个体化治疗和微创治疗的模式深入人心，从根本上改变了对血管疾病的认识水平，从而提高了其诊断、治疗以及预后的能力，大大丰富了血管外科学的内容。本课程主要对血管外科基本技术及器材、药物应用及常见病这三方面内容进行介绍，旨在进行血管外科学的基本知识铺垫。
英文课程简介：
Vascular surgery, which has a long history, is an energetic discipline.It is extremely specialised and covers all the vessels in the body apart from cerebral and cardiac vessels.With the advancement of technology,many major breakthroughs have been made in the fields from basic research to clinical practices.The applications of all kinds of novel techniques,materials,medicines as well as appliances have promoted vascular surgery standing at a new historical stage. As the individualized therapy,which takes organs, systems,and disease as the center, and minimally invasive surgery are deeply rooted in the hearts of people,the cognition of vascular diseases has been changed fundamentally,which helps to improve the ability of diagnosis,treatment and prognosis,and enrich the contents of vascular surgery. This curriculum is going to introduce vasuclar surgery in the following three aspects:the basic technique and tools,medicine application and common vascular diseases，aiming to spread the basic knowledge of vascular surgery.
