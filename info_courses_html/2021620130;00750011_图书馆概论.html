课程内容简介：
本课程是本科低年级学生利用图书馆的入门教育课程，针对学生平时利用图书馆过程中遇到的问题进行讲授，使学生全面了解各种类型的图书馆，全面了解现代图书馆各种文献信息资源与服务，掌握图书、期刊、学位论文、会议论文、专利、报告、参考工具等各种资源的检索与利用基本方法，并了解相关的信息安全、道德、知识产权等问题，提高学生高效利用图书馆的意识和能力，引导启发进行批判式思维、创新型思维、探究式学习的能力，从而为个人学习、研究提供高效的信息保障。
Course Description
This course is an introductory education course for junior undergraduate students on the use of libraries. It is designed to address the problems encountered by students in the course of using libraries in general, to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of various types of libraries, a comprehensive understanding of various documentary and information resources and services in modern libraries, to master the basic methods of searching and using various resources such as books, journals, dissertations, conference papers, patents, reports, reference tools, etc., Furthermore, information security, ethics and intellectual property rights will be emphasized during the course, to enhance students' awareness and ability to make efficient use of libraries, as well as to inspire the ability of critical thinking, innovative thinking and inquiry-based learning, thus providing efficient information security for personal study and research.
