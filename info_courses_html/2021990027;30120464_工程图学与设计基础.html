课程内容简介：
《工程图学与设计基础》是工科基础课程，是学习产品设计和制造相关学科与技术的入门 课，旨在使学生掌握机械产品设计表达的基础知识和基本技能，是掌握工程语言、建立工程 思维、培养创新意识的有效载体，是学生认识工程、走进工程的桥梁。 课程的主要内容有：现代产品设计的基础知识、基本过程和支撑环境，相关领域的前沿技术 和工程标准；投影理论、形体的构建及表达、零件设计及表达、装配设计及表达，计算机二 维绘图和三维建模方法；两项专题研究实践：创新设计实践，典型产品的设计与表达实践。
Course Description
Introduction to the course: “Fundamentals of Engineering Graphics and Mechanical Design” is a fundamental course of engineering, and is an introductory course for related disciplines and technologies of both product design and manufacturing, which aims to equip students with the fundamental knowledge and skills indispensable for the design and representations of mechanical product. The course is a well-organized comprehensive carrier for mastering engineering language, establishing engineering thinking and cultivating innovative consciousness, which will act as a bridge for students to understand engineering and enter engineering. Main contents of the course: Basic knowledge, basic process and supporting environment of product design, related cutting-edge technologies and engineering standards; Theories of orthographic projection， Modeling and representation of shapes, Design and representation of parts, Design and representation of assembly, Computer 2D drafting and 3D solid modeling methods. Two projects: Innovative design project, Typical product design and representation project.
