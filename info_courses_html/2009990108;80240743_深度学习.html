课程简介：
深度学习是人工智能学科的一个重要组成部分，是近年来人工智能再次腾飞的主要推动力之一。本课程将介绍深度学习的概念和历史以及近期的进展，深入讲解其基本原理和典型模型，并讨论其研究前沿。具体内容包括多层感知机、卷积神经网络、自组织映射网络、Elman反馈神经网络、长短时记忆网络、Hofield网络、受限波尔兹曼机、深度信念网络、变分自编码器、生成对抗网络等模型等，以及他们在计算机视觉、语音处理和自然语言处理等领域的应用。课程还将简要讲授python编程基础和神经网络的工具软件Pytorch的使用方法。学期末学生需要针对一个实际的应用问题（自选+教师提供结合）构建自己的模型进行求解，撰写和提交课程报告，并通过海报展示。本课程是全英文课程。
英文课程简介：
Deep learning is an important part of artificial intelligence. It is the one of the major driving forces for the resurgence of artificial intelligence in recent years. In this course, you will learn the basic concepts and history of deep learning as well as recent developments. The theoretical foundation and typical models will be elaborated. The cutting-edge research about deep learning will also be discussed. Specifically, we will study the typical models including the multi-layer Perceptron, convolutional neural network, self-organizing map, Elman recurrent neural network, long short-term memory network, Hopfield network, restricted Boltzmann network, deep belief network, variational auto-encoder, generative adversarial network, etc. Interesting applications of these models will be discussed. Python, a simple programming language and Pytorch, a neural network software, will be briefly introduced. In the end of the course, you are required to design a deep learning model for solving a practical problem, which can be chosen from a set of problems provided by the teacher or proposed by yourself, submit a course project report, and present the results in a poster. English is the teaching language.
