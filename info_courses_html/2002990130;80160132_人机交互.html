课程简介：
本课讲解人机交互的基本理论，介绍人机交互的领域的前沿研究成果，将穿插许多实例。
英文课程简介：
The course is designed to widen students’ understanding in interaction design and familiarize students with the contemporary research topics in the field of human-computer interaction with a focus on social computing. Particular topics to be covered include: social computing, internet advertising and marketing, internet of things, accessibility, and intellectual property issues. It is highly recommended that students have already taken a basic course in human-computer interaction.
