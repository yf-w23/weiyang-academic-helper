课程内容简介：
“生理系统仿真与建模”课程立足生物医学工程学科特点，配合清华大学本科大类培养改革，致力于促进多学科交叉融合。该课程是生物医学工程系本科生的必修核心课程，也面向全校各院系对生物医学工程感兴趣的学生开放。 通过本课程的理论和案例教学，学生将掌握生物医学工程学科中生理系统的仿真与建模的基础理论和方法，课程将从生理系统仿真与建模的数学基础开始讲授，覆盖各个生理系统的仿真与建模方法，并进一步包含了作为当前研究热点的基于动态和复杂系统分析的仿真与建模方法，使学生掌握使用仿真与建模方法解决生物医学工程实际问题的思路和能力，满足新时代生物医学工程教育的需求。
Course Description
"Physiological System Simulation and Modeling" is based on the requirements of biomedical engineering and the reforms of the education system of Tsinghua University, which focus on the interdiscipline of multiple subjects. It is the core course for Biomedical engineering, but also open to all the students who interested in biomedical engineering. In this course, students will learn the basic theory and method of physiological system simulation and modeling in biomedical engineering. This course will start with the mathematics bases for physiological system simulation and modeling method, then the simulation and modeling of different physiological system will be introduced, finally the currently developing modeling method based on dynamic and complex system. Students will have the basic idea and ability to address problems or answer questions in biomedical engineering, fulfilling the requirements of the biomedical engineering education in this new era.
