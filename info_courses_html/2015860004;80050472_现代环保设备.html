课程简介：
本课程主要包括现代环保设备基础，污水处理设备，大气污染控制设备和固体废物污染控制设备四大部分内容。针对目前环境工程专业多数工程硕士课程出现的重工艺、轻设备等问题，本课程将系统阐述现代环境工程设备的基本原理、设计、应用和选型，通过视频教学环节，使选课同学对环保设备的构造和运行具有更为直观和深入的了解。同时考虑到国内环保产业的迅速发展以及对外交往的日益增强，本课程亦将注重介绍国内外知名品牌环保设备的性能、参数及其在实际工程中的应用。在课程中期和结束时，将开展两次阳光课堂活动环节，分别为“参观学习深圳市南山垃圾焚烧发电厂焚烧及除尘设备”和“参观学习深圳市罗芳污水厂污泥处置及臭气控制设备”。此外，将邀请环保工业界内的资深工程师进行一次主题式案例教学，以实现课堂知识和实际工程运行的有效对接。通过该课程的学习，可为学生在今后实际工作中参与环境工程设备研发，环境工程项目招投标、工程设计及其运营管理夯实基础，与智能制造交叉学科专业学位项目培养目标极为契合，且与环境学科其他课程内容形成有效互补，可在很大程度上完善选课同学的知识体系。
英文课程简介：
The course of Modern Environmental Protection Equipment includes four sections, i.e., an overview of modern environmental protection equipments, wastewater treatment equipments, air pollution control and treatment equipments as well as solid waste control and treatment equipments. Currently, many major courses in environmental science and engineering field mainly focus on the wastewater/air pollution/solid waste treatment process while paying less attention to the equipments, although they are crucial to guarantee the pollutants removal effect. Considering this practical situation, we will systematically introduce the fundamental, design, application and model selection of the modern environmental protection equipments. Video exhibition will be involved in the class and the students will get more familiar with the structure and operation of some equipments directly. In addition, we will spend much effort to introduce the function, performance, parameters and applications of some well-known domestic and overseas equipments. During the mid-term and end of the course, two field trip studies will be included. We will organize the students to visit the waste incineration power plant and municipal wastewater treatment plant in Shenzhen. In addition, we will also invite a senior engineer who has rich experience in the environmental protection instrument application and maintenance to give the students a seminar. This will aid the student to enhance their understanding and connection between the theoretical knowledge and the practical project application. Via the course study, our aims are to lay a sloid foundation and to improve the students’ ability of environment protection instrument development, environmental engineering project bidding, project design and management. Moreover, this course will act as an effective complementary part for the other courses of environmental science and engineering and could strength the professional knowledge body of the students.
