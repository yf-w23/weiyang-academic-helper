课程简介：
本课程主要包含数据挖掘和机器学习的基础理论、主要方法、典型应用案例。突出案例分析与应用，结合工程实例探讨数据挖掘和机器学习方法。理论部分包括数据挖掘和机器学习基础概念与范畴、数据分析基础、浅层与深度学习模型求解与优化等；主要方法包括数据准备、特征提取、时间与图像数据组织、时间序列分析方法、概率图模型与方法、深度学习、强化学习、迁移学习、无监督学习、推理与决策方法等；应用案例包括上述方法在能源动力工程中的典型应用，并介绍数据挖掘和机器学习的主流工具、平台和算法。
英文课程简介：
This course covers the principles of data mining and machine learning for energy and power engineering applications as well as the state-of-the-art methods, algorithms and application scenarios. The theoretical part consists of definition of cyber-physical systems, basics of data analytics, statistical learning, Markov process, Bayesian inference, shallow and deep learning models and solutions, etc. The methodology part contains the data preparation, feature extraction, data organization for time-series and images, probabilistic graphical modeling and approaches, deep learning, reinforcement learning, transfer learning, unsupervised learning, inference and decision making, etc. The case studies include typical applications of the aforementioned methods in energy and power engineering fields, and the popular tools, platforms, and algorithms for data mining and machine learning will also be covered.
