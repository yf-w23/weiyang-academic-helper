课程简介：
本课程将面向研究生介绍气候变化与碳中和的科学事实、影响、适应及应对的科学理论基础。课程将包括气候变化的科学认知与影响、气候变化的风险、全球及中国温室气体排放、全球及中国碳中和转型路径、非二氧化碳温室气体的减排技术与减排路径、碳中和的健康与环境效益、碳中和的经济学分析、气候变化国际谈判与碳中和国际治理、中国的减排行动与碳中和实践等内容组成。课程将覆盖地球系统科学、环境科学、经济学和管理学等多个领域，全面介绍目前对于气候变化与碳中和的最新科学认知和研究进展。
英文课程简介：
This course will introduce postgraduate students to the scientific facts,impacts,risk and the mitigation to climate change and carbon neutrality.The course will cover scientific knowledge on observation and impacts of climate change,climate change risk,greenhouse gas emissions,transition pathways towards carbon neutrality,non-CO2 greenhouse gas mitigation technologies and mitigation pathways,health and environmental benefits of carbon neutrality,economic analysis of carbon neutrality,international negotiations on climate change and international governance of carbon neutrality,and China's mitigation actions and carbon neutrality practices.The course will cover a wide range of fields including earth system science,environmental science,economics and management,and will provide a comprehensive introduction to the latest scientific understanding and research progress on climate change and carbon neutrality.
