课程简介：
本课程主要介绍对策论（也称博弈论）的基础理论及其应用：在基础理论方面，主要介绍非合作博弈的四种基本模型（完全信息静态博弈、完全信息动态博弈、不完全信息静态博弈、不完全信息动态博弈）和相应的均衡解概念及其理论性质和计算方法，以及合作对策论、行为对策论、演化对策论的基本思想；在应用方面，主要介绍对策论在机制设计、供应链管理等领域中的应用。
英文课程简介：
This course introduces the fundamentals of game theory and its applications. Four basic non-cooperative game-theoretic models (static game with complete information, dynamic game with complete information, static game with incomplete information, dynamic game with incomplete information) are analyzed, including the definition, the property and the computation methods for their corresponding equilibria. Cooperation games, evolutionary games and behavioral games are also discussed. Topics in applications include mechanism design and supply chain management etc.
