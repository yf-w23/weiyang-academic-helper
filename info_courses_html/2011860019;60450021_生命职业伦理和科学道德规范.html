课程简介：
生物医学研究现已成为全世界科技前沿最为活跃的领域，其中牵涉到一系列伦理问题。与其他领域的科学研究一样，也存在学术规范和科研诚信问题。本课程提过课堂讲授，介绍生命伦理学基本概念、生物医学研究中的伦理学问题、辅助生育的伦理与挑战、基因和细胞疗法现状及科学依据、动物权利、科学研究中的学术规范和科研诚信问题等等，并利用一些案例，进行讨论。希望通过本课程的学习，能让学生了解生命伦理学基本概念、生物医学研究中的基本伦理学和道德规范，建立正确的学术道德和科研诚信。
英文课程简介：
Biomedical research has become the most active field in the world's scientific and technological frontier, which involves a series of ethical issues. Like scientific research in other fields, there are also problems of academic norms and scientific research integrity. This course introduces the basic concepts of bioethics, the ethical issues in biomedical research, the ethics and challenges of assisted reproduction, the status quo and scientific basis of gene and cell therapy, animal rights, academic norms in scientific research and scientific research integrity, and so on. Some cases are used to discuss these issues. It is hoped that through the study of this course, students can understand the basic concepts of bioethics, basic ethics and moral norms in biomedical research, and establish correct academic ethics and scientific research integrity.
