课程简介：
薄膜材料与器件由于有很多优异的物理性能，因而在信息、新能源和表面防护等领域有广泛而重要的应用。本课程将阐述薄膜材料与器件的制备与应用中出现的基本物理现象、性质，在明晰物理概念的前提下，理论联系实际，介绍相关领域的最新发展，主要内容包括：薄膜材料与器件的制备技术，薄膜生长机制，薄膜力学性能，薄膜的光、电、热、磁、压电等物理性能，界面与量子效应，器件性质，以及利用这些特性制作器件的方法。
英文课程简介：
Thin film materials and devices have many excellent physical properties. They are able to be used in extended fields including information, new energy and surface protecting. This course will demonstrate elmentary physical phenomena and properties in fabricating and application of thin film materials and device. It will focus on physical concepts, device principles and the advanced developments. The main content include fabrication techniques of films and devices, growth mechanisms of thin films,mechanic properties, photonic,electronic, accoustic, thermal and magnetic physics of thin films, interface and quatum effect, device properties and device design and fabrication.
