多孔介质流体动力学及其应用

课程简介：
本课程重点讲授水分在土壤包气带、含水层中的运动、转化及相关的入渗、蒸散发、能量及溶质运移理论和研究方法，以深入理解大气水—地表水—土壤水—植物水—地下水系统各部分之间的相互作用及相互转化关系。主要内容包括以下两部分：(1) 土壤水动力学：主要包括土壤水的数量和能态，土壤水动动的基本理论与研究方法，入渗、蒸发条件下土壤水运动，土壤水盐运移，土壤水热耦合运移，SPAC水分运移。(2) 地下水动力学：主要内容包括地下水运动基本方程及其数值解，盆地地下水循环的基本问题，饱和—非饱和水耦合模拟，地表—地下水集成模拟，人类活动对地下水的影响，植被—地下水相互作用机理，地下水污染及其治理。
英文课程简介：
This course mainly focuses on theory and research method on water flow and transform in vadose zone and aquifer and related infiltration, evapotranspiration, heat and solute transport processes, so as to understand the interactions and transformations of water in the atmosphere-surface water-soil water- plant water-groundwater system. Main contents include: (1) Soil water dynamics, including soil water content and potential, theory and research method on soil water flow, water flow in infiltration and evaporation conditions, soil water flow and solute transport, soil water flow and heat transfer, water flow in the soil-plant-atmosphere continuum (SPAC); (2) Groundwater dynamics, including fundamental equations for groundwater flow and numerical method, groundwater cycle in basins, coupled simulation of saturated-unsaturated water flow, integrated modeling of surface water and groundwater, impact of human activity on groundwater, groundwater pollution and control.
