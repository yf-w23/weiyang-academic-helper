课程内容简介：
课程通过将讲授包装设计的基本概念、历史沿革与发展、基本理论和设计基本方法，包装设计的定义、分类、功能，包装设计基本程序与步骤，产品包装策划的市场调研、定位分析、消费心理、设计策略、美学法则、材料与工艺、包装与环保等内容；了解包装结构设计的基本理论和设计表现的基本规律与方法，包装结构造型设计成型基本规律与方法；包装容器设计的基本规律与方法。本课程包含包装结构设计和容器造型设计两部分重点基础内容，以及包装与品牌的策划开发设计，课程将结合实践课题，锻炼学生运用专业方法和手段进行独立实施包装策划与设计的实战训练，培养学生依据现实社会发展需求，消费形态和商业模式的变化，以及包装设计行业发展前沿动态，培养敏锐的专业前瞻视野与创新意识。
Course Description
The course will teach the basic concepts, historical evolution and development, basic theories and design methods of packaging design, the definition, classification, function, basic procedures and steps of packaging design, market research, positioning analysis, consumer psychology, design strategies, aesthetic principles, materials and processes, packaging and environmental protection in product packaging planning; Understand the basic theories of packaging structure design and the basic laws and methods of design expression, as well as the basic laws and methods of packaging structure shape design and formation; The basic rules and methods of packaging container design. This course includes two key basic contents: packaging structure design and container shape design, as well as packaging and brand planning and development design. The course will combine practical topics to train students in using professional methods and means to independently implement packaging planning and design in practical training. Students will be trained to adapt to the needs of real social development, changes in consumer forms and business models, as well as cutting-edge developments in the packaging design industry, and cultivate a sharp professional foresight and innovative consciousness.
进度安排：
1、包装设计历史发展、包装的主要概念与功能、包装结构设计概述。/讲授

3、特殊形态纸盒结构设计、纸盒包装结构成型特点和设计思维方法。/讲授
4、容器造型设计的基本创意与设计方法、设计实践。/讲授
5、包装设计与市场调研、定位分析、消费心理、设计策略。/讲授
6、包装设计中的色彩、字体、图形、材料与工艺。/讲授
7、包装设计与品牌策划、包装设计实践项目课题训练。/讲授
8、包装设计实践项目课题结课汇报与讲评。/研讨会
