课程简介：
创业活动是人类最重要的经济和社会行为之一，对创业行为及其生态系统的多学科研究是经济学和管理学领域的一个重点。本课程以多学科的创业研究方法论和历史观为背景，以重要理论问题为线索，通过精选的重要文献，采用研讨式教学方法，为博士生掌握具有国际水平的研究方法，具有前沿性和经典性的理论观点，培养发现问题、定位问题和分析问题的能力，提供全面的指导。 课程划分为六个模块，分别是：创业的起源、创业的组织、创业与产业发展、技术创业、创业金融和公司创业。 Entrepreneurial activities are one of the most important economic and social behaviors of the human beings. Interdisciplinary studies on entrepreneurial behavior and its ecosystem is a bonafide focus of economics and management. This course, which takes the interdisciplinary methodologies and historical outlooks as background, catches up the momentous theoretical issues, selects the classical literatures and uses seminar-style teaching method, provides the PhD students who selected the course with total guidance in mastering modern methodology, frontier and typical theories and upgrading the competences for discovering, positioning and analyzing the research issues. This course consists of six modules including origin of entrepreneurship, organizing entrepreneurship, entrepreneurship and industry development, technology entrepreneurship, entrepreneurial finance and corporate entrepreneurship (corporate ventures or new development ventures).
