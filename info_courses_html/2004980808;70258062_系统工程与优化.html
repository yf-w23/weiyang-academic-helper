课程简介：
本课程是面向自动化系工程管理专业硕士开设的领域基础课程，主要介绍如何用系统和优化的观点处理问题的基本理论和方法，使学生在解决大型工程管理问题时具有宏观战略的系统性思维和协调优化的思想，并掌握具体的系统建模、系统分析、系统决策、系统优化和系统评价的基本方法。具体包含以下主要内容：（1）系统工程与优化概述；（2）运筹学基础：主要介绍单纯性法、对偶理论和目标规划；（3）系统建模方法，包括解释性结构建模方法、黑箱建模方法；（4）系统分析方法：主要讲授主成分分析、因子分析和聚类分析等方法；（5）系统决策方法，包括决策树方法、风险决策理论、多目标决策方法、群决策方法等；（6）系统评价方法，主要讲授层次分析法。（7）项目管理概论。
英文课程简介：
Systems Engineering and Optimization, a course offered by Department of Automation, is one of the fundamental courses for the students of Master of Engineering Management, which aims at teaching basic theories and methodologies in the field of systems engineering and operation research. After learning this course, the students are expected to grasp the way of thinking with “system” and “optimization” and many concrete methods of systems engineering such as systems modeling, systems analysis, systems decision-making, systems optimization and systems evaluation. The course is composed of the follows seven parts. (1) Brief introduction on systems engineering and optimization; (2) Fundamentals on operation research, including linear programming and solution, simplex algorithm, duality principal and goal programming; (3) Systems modeling, including interpretive structural modeling (ISM) and black-box modeling etc.; (4) Systems Analysis, including principal component analysis (PCA), factor analysis and clustering analysis, etc.; (5) Systems decision-making, including decision tree, decision-making under risk; multi-objective decision making and group decision making, etc.; (6) Systems evaluation by analytic hierarchy process (AHP); (7) Introduction to project management.
