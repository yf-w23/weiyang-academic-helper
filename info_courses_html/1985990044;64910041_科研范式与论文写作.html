课程简介：
本课程属于科学研究与论文写作指导课程，采用讲授和讨论等方式，围绕创新领军工程博士学位论文写作过程中各关键环节的写作流程、科研方法、科学表达、学术规范、典型案例分析等内容开展教学，以实现创新领军人才培养目标、不断提升创新领军人才的科学研究能力与学术表达能力。
英文课程简介：
This course belongs to the guiding course of research paradigm and thesis writing. It will focus on the writing process, scientific research methods, scientific expression, academic norms and typical case analysis in the process of dissertation writing of Doctor of Engineering by means of lectures, flipped classroom, discussion and practice, so as to realize the training objectives and continuously improve the scientific research ability and academic expression ability of innovative leading talents.
