课程内容简介：
临床沟通能力是现代医务工作者应具备的重要能力之一，有效的临床沟通是建立良好医患沟通的主要途径。高级临床沟通课程内容由四部分组成：1）基础理论，2）基本原理；3）临床沟通原理、技能和方法；4）不同专科临床沟通的特点及经验等。 本课程注重理论联系实际、重视讨论式教学，在讲授理论课程基础上，采用多种教学方法。希望通过本课程学习,学生能够掌握医患关系的现状，理解并掌握临床沟通的基本理念和基本原理，掌握临床沟通的技巧，理解医生角色与患者角色各自的特点，了解各专业科室患者的特点，熟悉与各科室患者沟通的技巧，学会合理运用沟通技巧，增强在工作中应对各种沟通情况的能力。
Course Description
Clinical communication skills are one of the important abilities that modern medical workers should possess. Effective clinical communication is the main way to establish good doctor-patient communication. The advanced clinical communication course consists of four parts: 1) basic theory, 2) basic principles; 3) clinical communication principles, skills and methods; 4) characteristics and experience of clinical communication in different specialties. This course focuses on integrating theory with practice and emphasizes discussion-based teaching. Based on the theoretical curriculum, various teaching methods are adopted. It is hoped that through this course, students can grasp the current situation of doctor-patient relationship, understand and grasp the basic concepts and principles of clinical communication, master the skills of clinical communication, understand the characteristics of doctor and patient roles, understand the characteristics of patients in various professional departments, familiarize themselves with the skills of communicating with patients in various departments, learn to use communication skills reasonably, and enhance their ability to deal with various communication situations in their work.
