课程内容简介：
能源化学主要利用化学的理论与方法来研究能量的获取、存储、转换及传输等过程的规律和 探索新能源技术的实现途径，是动力工程及工程热物理学科与化学学科有机结合与知识交 叉。本课程将从物理化学角度深入介绍能量及其生产、转化、利用过程中机理与规律；分别 从能源物理化学、能源化学转化、能源材料化学、能源化学研究方法与测试技术、以及能源 化学系统工程与应用五个维度介绍相关基础及应用基础知识。课程注重基础知识传授及科研 技能传授，是面向高年级能源、化工、材料等专业本科生在本研贯通培养阶段的专业限选课 程之一。
Course Description
Energy chemistry mainly uses the theory and method of chemistry to study the law of energy acquisition, storage, conversion and transmission process and explore the realization way of new energy technology, which is an interdisciplinary combination between power engineering and engineering thermophysics and chemistry. This course will introduce the mechanism and rules of energy production, transformation and utilization from the perspective of physics and chemistry. The relevant basic and applied knowledge were introduced from five dimensions of energy physical chemistry, energy chemical conversion, energy material chemistry, energy chemical research methods and characterization technology, and energy chemical system engineering and application. This course focuses on the teaching of basic knowledge and scientific research skills, and is one of the restricted elective courses for senior undergraduates majoring in energy, chemical engineering, materials and other fields in preparing for the postgraduate stage.
