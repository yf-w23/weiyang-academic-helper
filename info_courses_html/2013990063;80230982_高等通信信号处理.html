课程简介：
本课程为电子系本研贯通专业课，主要讲授信息与通信工程一级学科中与信息传输有关的基础理论、基本方法和关键技术及其前沿发展。主要内容包括四个部分： 1）经典篇：通信信号与无线信道的表征、OFDM、信道估计与均衡、信号检测； 2）进阶篇：压缩感知理论与应用、大规模MIMO、毫米波通信、非正交多址接入等前沿通信信号处理理论与方法； 3）前沿篇：AI辅助通信、太赫兹通信、智能超表面、近场通信、电磁信息论。 4）实战篇：通信信号处理前沿研究实例的分析、讲解和点评。 通过本课程的学习，学生不仅可以在通信信号处理领域打下坚实的理论基础，提高从信号处理的角度严格设计和分析通信系统的能力，还可以熟悉本领域的前沿理论、方法和发展，提高学术文献的阅读、分析、理解和鉴赏能力，为进一步的科研工作奠定坚实的基础。
英文课程简介：
The course “Advanced Signal Processing for Communications” is oriented for both undergraduate and graduate students in the field of information and communication systems. This course mainly presents the basic theory, the basic methods, and key technologies for information transmission and their recent progress. Specifically, the main contents if this course include four parts: 1) Classic part: signal and channel models of wireless communications, expressions, OFDM, channel estimation, and multi-user detection. 2) Advanced part: compressive sensing and its applications, massive MIMO, millimeter-wave communications, and non-orthogonal multiple access. 3) Frontiers part: AI-assisted communications, terahertz communications, intelligent reconfigurable surfaces, near-field communications, and electromagnetic information theory. 4) Practical part: Analysis, presentation, and commentary on representative research papers in the general area of communications signal processing. Through the study of this course, students can not only lay a solid foundation in the field of communication theory and technology, but also understand the most advanced theory and technology in this field to prepare them well for the future research work.
