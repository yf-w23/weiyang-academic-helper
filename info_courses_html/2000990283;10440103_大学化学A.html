课程内容简介：
本课程专为非化学、化工专业理工科同学开设。课程将从新的角度，介绍化学基本理论与实际生活和未来科学研究的关系，拓展学术视野，提高用化学知识解决实际问题的能力。讲授内容主要包括：化学平衡原理(稀溶液依数性，缓冲溶液、沉淀反应、氧化还原与酸碱理论)，化学反应的方向与限度(化学热力学)，化学反应速度及调控（化学动力学），物质结构（原子结构，化学键，配合物化学和元素化学基础)，以及化学在材料、能源、信息及相关科学领域的应用。在重点强调化学学科体系中不同研究方向内在关系的基础上，深化和拓展化学与其他科学的交叉知识。 教学形式包括课堂讲授，课堂互动，课下自学，撰写小论文，完成作业，线上和线下答疑与讨论相结合等方式。课程考核将按照选课同学在以上环节中的综合表现来评定。考核标准与各部分所占比例分别为：期中占10%；期末占60%；作业占20%；课堂互动占10%。本课程强调学习过程的跟踪考核，特别提示：在整个学期，作业缺交次数达总次数的1/3（含）以上的选课同学，将被取消参加期末考试的资格，该同学本课程考核成绩等级将评定为F。鼓励学有余力的同学，根据自己的兴趣，志愿选择与课程进度相关的研讨专题，参加线上和线下专题讨论，积极参加课程研讨与综合表现突出者最高可获5分加分。
Course Description
Chemistry is at the heart of creating innovative energy solutions that power our daily lives and protect our environment, deciphering human genetic information to comprehend diseases and develop novel treatments, and even propelling the growth of AI and other cutting-edge technologies. As we understand it, chemistry is the exploration of matter, its properties, the transformations it undergoes, and the energy implicated in these changes. Comprehending the attributes of a substance and the changes it undergoes aligns us with the central tenet of chemistry: macroscopic properties and behaviors, those observable to us, are the outcomes of submicroscopic properties and behaviors, which remain unseen. General Chemistry A have been iteratively refined over time and continue to evolve even now. The course on the principles of general chemistry aspires to deliver a balanced fusion of experimental observations, mathematical models, and theoretical concepts. Learning chemistry is tantamount to acquiring problem-solving skills in the realm of chemistry - tackling challenges not only in exams or homework but also complex real-world problems in professional life and society. Engaging in this course will equip students with deep insights through applications, problem-solving, and visualization in chemistry and its associated fields.
