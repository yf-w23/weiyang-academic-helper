课程简介：
本课程是地球系统科学系生态学方向教师集体讲授的生态学前沿领域进展讲座系列研究生课程之一， 主要内容包括讲座内容包括生态学学科内涵和发展历史、生态学主要分支学科（包括植物生态学、动物生态学、微生物生态学、生态系统生态学、景观生态学、全球生态学、可持续生态学）和全球变化经济学等领域的研究内容、方法和进展及其研究案例。也会涉及大数据分析和前沿生态学研究问题及其成果转化应用等。通过本课程的教学与实践训练，使来自不同本科专业背景的研究生熟悉当前生态学和全球变化经济学等的发展趋势、最新研究方法和学科前沿，为进行相关领域毕业论文和未来研究提供创新学术思想与先进研究方法的系统训练。因此，设立此课程的目的就是要将这一科学基础和前沿知识介绍给生态学、大气科学、地理科学、环境科学、水文学、海洋科学、生命科学及其他相关学科的博士、硕士研究生，并指导他们对这些生态学及其相关领域研究进展和存在问题开展讨论、评论。
英文课程简介：
This course will be co-taught mainly by the faculty members of Ecology, in the Department of Earth System Science, via a seminar format to cover the basis and research frontiers of major ecology fields (including plant ecology, animal ecology, microbial ecology, ecosystem ecology, landscape ecology, global ecology, sustainability ecology) and global change economy, along with case study analysis. Big data analysis and frontier questions of ecological research will be also introduced. In addition, there will be two field trips to learn how organisms and typical ecosystems respond to global environmental changes. The graduate students with different undergraduate majors will have an opportunity to understand better the trends, new methodology and research frontiers of these fields, which will provide them with comprehensive trainings of innovative academic thinking and state-of-art research methods for their thesis and future research in selected fields. Thus, the aim of this course is to provide the Ph.D. and MSc. graduate students majoring in ecology, atmospheric science, geography, environmental science, hydrology, marine science, life science and other related fields with the basis and frontiers of ecology, and guide them to conduct discussion and review on specific topics of ecology and related disciplines.
