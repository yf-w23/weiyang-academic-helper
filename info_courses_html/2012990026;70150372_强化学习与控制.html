课程简介：
复杂工业对象的高安全、高准确、高实时需求，催生了全新一代的最优化控制手段。强化学习以及关联的近似动态规划，在大规模、高维度、非线性动态系统的序列决策及控制领域具有十分广泛的应用前景。课程拟讲授马尔科夫决策过程、Monte Carlo学习、时序差分学习、随机动态规划、值函数近似、策略梯度训练以及Q-learning、Sarsa、DQN、A3C、TD3、DDPG、SAC、DSAC算法等知识点。结合自动驾驶汽车或具身机器人遇到的决策和控制实例开展教学，培养学生分析被控对象、构造及求解强化学习问题的基本能力，为之后的科研工作和工程实践奠定基础。
英文课程简介：
The trends of safer, more accurate and high real-time industrial control pose increasing demands on next generation optimal control. Today, reinforcement learning (RL) as well as approximate dynamic programming (ADP) are widely used in sequential decision and control of complex, high-dimensional and nonlinear dynamic systems. In this course, mainstream RL/ADP theories and methods will be taught, including Markov decision process, Monte Carlo RL, temporal difference RL, RL with function approximation, and direct RL with policy gradients. Those RL algorithms will also be covered, including Q-learning, Sarsa, DQN, A3C, TD3, DDPG, SAC, DSAC, etc. Graduate students are able to build their abilities of analyzing, formulating and solving related decision and control problems with state-of-art RL methods, thus laying a solid foundation for their further research work and engineering practice.
