课程内容简介：
《人工智能与流程工业导论》课程是化学工程与工业生物工程专业（智能化工方向，简称 AI+ChE）本科培养方案中的一门核心课程。课程教学理念的核心，在于将人工智能技术与流 程工业的实际需求相结合，通过跨学科的知识融合，激发学生的创新思维和解决问题的能 力。课程内容不仅包括人工智能的基本原理和技术，如机器学习、深度学习等，还深入探讨 了这些技术在流程工业中的应用，如分子材料设计、智能制造、过程优化等。 在内容选择上，课程力求平衡理论与实践，注重基础知识的传授与前沿技术的探索。通过引 入实际工业案例分析、课程设计和小组项目，鼓励学生将理论知识应用于解决实际问题，从 而提高其实践能力和工程素养。同时，课程还特别强调团队协作、批判性思维和终身学习的 能力培养，以适应未来工业界不断变化的需求。 此外，课程还积极采用现代教育技术和方法，如在线学习资源、仿真软件、互动讨论等，以 增强学习的互动性和趣味性，提高教学效果。通过这些教学策略的实施，旨在培养学生的自 主学习能力，促进其全面发展，为他们成为能够适应未来社会发展需要的高素质人才打下坚 实的基础。
Course Description
The course "Introduction of Artificial Intelligence and Process Industry" is one of the core module in the undergraduate curriculum for Artificial Intelligent + Chemical Engineering (AI+ChE). The core teaching philosophy of the course is to combine AI technologies with the actual needs of the process industry, stimulating students' innovative thinking and problem-solving abilities through interdisciplinary knowledge integration. The course content not only includes the basic principles and technologies of computing and AI, such as machine learning and deep learning, but also explores the application of these technologies in the process industry, such as molecule and material design, intelligent manufacturing, and process optimization. In terms of content selection, the course strives to balance theory and practice, focusing on the transmission of basic knowledge and the exploration of cutting-edge technologies. By introducing actual industrial case analyses, course designs, and group projects, students are encouraged to apply theoretical knowledge to solve real-world problems, thereby enhancing their practical skills and engineering literacy. At the same time, the course emphasizes the cultivation of teamwork, critical thinking, and lifelong learning abilities to adapt to the ever-changing demands of the industry. Additionally, the course actively adopts modern educational technologies and methods, such as online learning resources, simulation software, and interactive discussions, to enhance the interactivity and enjoyment of learning and improve teaching effectiveness. Through the implementation of these teaching strategies, the course aims to cultivate students' autonomous learning abilities, promote their comprehensive development, and lay a solid foundation for them to become high-quality talents capable of meeting the needs of future societal development.
