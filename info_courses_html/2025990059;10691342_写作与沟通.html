课程内容简介：
课程采用小班制教学的方式，利用文本研读、资料分析、研讨交流、观点展示等各种环节训练同学的思维、阅读、写作与沟通能力。每个课堂都围绕“无专业门槛、有学理深度”的主题展开，引导来自不同专业、不同学科背景的学生遵循学术基本规范和文献检索方法开展有学理深度的研究，完成准学术性说理文的写作，形成突破学科壁垒的逻辑思维和批判性思维。教师将为选课学生提供一对一的写作指导，保证每位选课学生均得到全面、细致、有针对性的书面批改与口头反馈，个性化地提升学生的思维、写作与沟通能力。
Course Description
In terms of China's development and transformation, over the past century, the Chinese revolution blazed a new trail by "encircling the cities from the countryside," and the reforms and opening-up first achieved breakthroughs through adjustments to the rural production system. Since the beginning of the new century, the state has consistently released No. 1 Central Documents focusing on the "three rural issues" (agriculture, rural areas, and farmers), emphasizing the importance of the countryside. From poverty alleviation to rural revitalization, and from daily lifestyles to festive rituals, the transformation of rural areas is a microcosm of China's profound changes. Reading, thinking, and writing on the theme of rural China will be structured around the transformations of the countryside. Whether you have personal experience related to rural life or not, you can observe and understand the diverse and multifaceted world of rural China through case studies, field research, online observations, and historical reviews. The theme of rural China will guide students to delve into the depths of rural society, focusing on aspects such as rural sports, local arts, town markets, ancestral halls and genealogies, festivals and temple fairs, information technology, and healthcare. By exploring these details of rural life, students will raise questions that interest them, investigate the nuances of daily life and the spiritual world of the Chinese people amid epochal changes, and reflect critically to document a rural world that is both profound and captivating.
