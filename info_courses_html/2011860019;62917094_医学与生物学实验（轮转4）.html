课程简介：
医学和生物学实验课程是OPEN FIESTA Bio3项目特色课程系列的一个重要组成部分，是学生完成实验室轮转（属于轮转项目IV )和开展创新项目前的必修课程。本课程由不同学科专业的教师组成导师组，就医学和生物学前沿交叉领域相关实验技术，对学生团队提供系统和个性化的指导和专业实践。通过该课程，使学生在短时间内掌握医学和生物学交叉前沿方面的特定技术和关键方法，为医学和生物学科交叉和将来团队创新项目实施打下基础。
英文课程简介：
Medical and Biological Experimental Course is an important part of the OPEN FIESTA Bio3 project characteristic course series. It is a compulsory course before students complete the laboratory rotation (belonging to the rotation project IV) and carry out innovative projects. This course is composed of teachers from different disciplines and specialties. It provides systematic and individualized guidance and professional practice for students'teams on experimental techniques related to frontier areas of medicine and biology. Through this course, students can grasp the specific technology and key methods in the frontier of medicine and biology in a short time, and lay a foundation for the cross-disciplinary of medicine and biology and the implementation of team innovation projects in the future.

进度安排：
The course of Medical and Biological Experiments mainly focuses on introducing the frontier experimental techniques and related applications of medicine and biology. It includes three modules: molecular cell biology, experimental zoology and medical research. The course has a total of 64 hours, of which 16 are for theoretical courses and 48 are for individualized experimental guidance.
 
1、Theories (16 hours in total)
1) Introduction and planning of rotating courses: introduction of courses, exploration of innovative topics, grouping of rotating students and preliminary formulation of personalized project for 4 hours.
2) Molecular Cell Biology Experimental Theory Course: Focus on in vitro cell experiments, including but not limited to molecular cloning, gene expression, cell culture and other experimental techniques for 4 hours.
3) Animal Experimental Theory Course: Focus on in vivo animal experiments, including but not limited to SPF animal feeding and management, standardized operation and international management of animal experiments, animal model making, in vivo sample collection, experimental design and analysis for 4 hours.
4) Medical experimental theory course: focusing on clinical medical experiments, including but not limited to basic medical knowledge, medical experimental design, and medical data and bioinformatics analysis for 4 hours.
 
2、Personalized guidance (total 48 hours)
1） Individualized Guidance Group for Molecular Cell Biology Experiments (16 hours)
[1] Formulation of specific scheme for molecular cell biology experiment for 4 hours
[2] Guidance on experimental techniques of molecular cell biology for 4 hours
[3] Follow-up guidance on experimental techniques of molecular cell biology for 4 hours
[4] Preliminary summary of experimental results of molecular cell biology for 4 hours
2) Individualized Guidance Group for Zoological Experiments (16 hours)
[1] Four hours for formulation of specific programs for zoological experiments
[2] On-site guidance and training of animal experimental techniques for 4 hours
[3] Follow-up guidance on animal experimental techniques for 4 hours
[4] Preliminary summary of zoological experimental results for 4 hours
3) Individualized Guidance Group for Medical Experiments (16 hours)
[1] Four hours for the formulation of specific plans for medical experiments
[2] Guidance on medical experimental techniques for 4 hours
[3] Follow-up guidance on medical experimental techniques for 4 hours
[4] Preliminary summary of medical experimental results for 4 hours
 
3、Summary report
Students report results, and teachers grade students' personal performance and results. Students report results, PPT display, oral report; teachers score, in which students' performance and completion of ordinary experimental skills accounted for 60%, and results report accounted for 40%.
