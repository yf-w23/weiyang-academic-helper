课程简介：
消费心理学是应用心理学的重要分支，主要关注人们在消费活动中的心理现象和行为规律。本课侧重介绍消费心理学的概念、理论、研究方法，探讨消费者知觉、记忆、动机、态度及决策，并探索心理学实验方法和认知神经科学技术如何助力消费者行为研究。本课的预期目标包括：通过学习消费心理学，理解影响消费心理的个人因素、产品因素、情境因素，提高洞察能力和批判性思维能力，将心理学理论及研究成果与生活中的实际问题联系起来。
英文课程简介：
Consumer psychology refers to the study of how people relate to the products and services they use in daily life. Consumer psychology provides opportunities to examine which factors influence people's decisions to buy a product, how people determine the value of a service, and why advertisements can convince people to try something new for the first time. Consumer Psychologists not only seek to describe and explain consumer behavior, but also try to predict or influence customers' decisions. This course not only introduces the basic concepts, theories, and research methods of consumer psychology, but also discusses consumer perception, memory, motivation, attitude, and decision-making.
