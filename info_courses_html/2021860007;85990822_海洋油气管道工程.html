课程简介：
本课程是海洋技术与工程(土木水利)、海洋能源工程(土木水利) 方向、土木工程、水利工程的专业课，总计32学时。海洋油气管道是海上油气田开发过程中的重要设施。它可以实现海上油、气、水的输运，具有不受风浪流环境因素的影响和运行成本低等特点。近年来，国内外油气田开发工程中不断有距离更长、水深更大、口径更粗的海洋油气管道建成。随着我国海洋油气开发逐步走向深海，也将对国内海洋油气管道工程的设计、施工技术、科学实验研究、工程建设检验法规等方面提出了更高的要求。通过本课程的学习，学生将熟练掌握海洋油气管道设计的基本原理和基本方法，了解海洋油气管道安装与施工方法，提高学生解决工程实际问题的能力。
英文课程简介：
This is a specialised module for students majoring in Marine Technology and Engineering (Civil and Hydraulic Engineering), Marine Energy Engineering (Civil and Hydraulic Engineering), Civil Engineering and Hydraulic Engineering with a total of 32 credit hours. Offshore oil and gas pipelines are important facilities in the development of offshore oil and gas fields. They can realise the transportation of oil, gas and water at sea and have the characteristics of being unaffected by the marine environmental factors (wind, wave and current) with low operating costs. In recent years, offshore oil and gas pipelines with longer distances, larger water depths and larger diameters have been built in offshore oil and gas exploitation projects. As offshore oil and gas activities gradually move to deeper waters, higher requirements are imposed on the design, construction, research as well as inspection of offshore oil and gas pipelines. Through the study of this module, students will grasp the basic principles and methods for the design the offshore oil and gas pipelines, understand the installation and construction methods and improve their ability of solving practical engineering problems.
