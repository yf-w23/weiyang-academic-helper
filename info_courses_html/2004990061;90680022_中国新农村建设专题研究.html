中国新农村建设专题研究

课程简介：
“三农”问题是关系国计民生的根本性问题，党中央始终把解决好“三农”问题作为全党工作的重中之重。党的二十大报告指出：“全面建设社会主义现代化国家，最艰巨最繁重的任务仍然在农村。”习近平曾在多种场合强调：“从中华民族伟大复兴战略全局看，民族要复兴，乡村必振兴。”“只有深刻理解了‘三农’问题，才能更好理解我们这个党、这个国家、这个民族。” 本课程以专题研讨方式，以老师亲自调研的国内外一手资料，对研究生关注的相关重大理论与现实问题进行深度剖析，旨在加强研究生这一高层次人才对中国党情国情农情的正确把握和深刻认识，坚定加快“建设农业强国，实现中国式现代化”的信念！
英文课程简介：
Issues concerning agriculture, rural areas and farmers are fundamental issues concerning the national economy and people's livelihood. The CPC Central Committee has always made solving issues related to agriculture, rural areas and farmers a top priority in the work of the whole Party. The party's 20th report pointed out: " To build a modern socialist country in an all-round way, the most arduous task of building the country is still in rural areas." Xi Jinping has stressed on various occasions:" From the overall perspective of the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation, the nation, the countryside will be revitalized."" Only with a deep understanding of the issues related to agriculture, rural areas and farmers can we better understand our Party, our country and our nation." This course in workshops way, to the teacher personally research information at home and abroad, the graduate attention related important theoretical and practical problems, to strengthen the graduate students the high-level talent of China party conditions of the correct grasp and deep understanding, determined to speed up the" construction of agricultural power, realize Chinese modernization " faith!
