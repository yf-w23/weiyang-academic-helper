课程内容简介：
我们将仔细研读早期现代西方哲学中的几个重要哲学家：笛卡尔，马勒布朗士，莱布尼茨，洛克，贝克莱，休谟。讨论的核心是形而上学和认识论。
Course Description
We will carefully read and discuss some key philosophers in the Early Modern period of Western philosophy: Descartes, Malebranche, Leibniz, Locke, Berkeley, and Hume. The central topics will be in metaphysics and epistemology.
