课程简介：
本课程是面向材料学专业硕士和博士研究生的专业技术理论课，主要讲授功能陶瓷材料与器件的电磁功能测试原理和技术，包括介电谱、阻抗谱、微波测试、压电铁电测试、磁学测试以及微区电磁表征技术等，重点讲授物理参量的精确测试方法与技巧、数据分析、图谱解析及其物理信息的提取等，注重基础性、实用性，通过一些实例分析，让学生掌握陶瓷功能测试的基本原理与常用技术，熟悉功能陶瓷材料与器件的实验研究方法。
英文课程简介：
This course is for graduate students of materials science and engineering. The teaching content of the course includes the theories and techniques of the electro-magnetic measurements for electronic ceramics and devices, especially involves dielectric spectroscopy, impedance spectroscopy, microwave measurements, ferroelectric and piezoelectric measurements, and magnetic measurement techniques. The measurement methods and skills, data and spectroscopy analysis, and physical information obtained from the measurements will be taught primarily. Through this course, students can acquire the knowledge about the measurement techniques and experiment research methods for electronic ceramics and devices.
