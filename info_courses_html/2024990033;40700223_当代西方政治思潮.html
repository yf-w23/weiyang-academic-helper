课程内容简介：
这门课旨在帮助学生批判性地认识当代西方的主要政治思潮，了解其理论逻辑、代表人物和政策偏好，锻炼批判性思维，提升全球胜任力。当代西方政治思潮的一个突出特点，就是自由主义正在受到其他各种思潮的挑战。因此，课程将围绕“自由主义及其批评者”这一线索展开。课程内容包括三部分：首先，我们将厘清自由主义的概念、历史和现状，关注自由主义的国家理论、经济政策和文化态度；接下来，我们将分析保守主义、另类右翼和民粹主义对自由主义的批评，这些来自右翼的不满构成了当前自由主义所面临的最大挑战；最后，我们将考虑社群主义、反殖民主义、女性主义等左翼思潮的理论逻辑和现实意义。课程对每种思潮的分析，都将围绕特定的核心文本来展开，学生应在课前完成必要的阅读。
Course Description
This course aims to help students critically understand the theoretical logic, representative figures, and policy preferences of several main political ideologies in the contemporary West, thereby improving their critical thinking skills and global competence. A major characteristic of contemporary Western politics is that liberalism is being challenged by many other ideological trends. Therefore, this course is framed as an introduction to liberalism and its critics, and it contains three sections. We will first clarify the concept, history, and current situation of liberalism, focusing on its state theory, economic policy, and cultural attitudes. Then, we will analyze the greatest challenge that liberalism is currently faced with, i.e., right-wing critiques of liberalism by conservatism, the alt-right, populism, and nationalism. Finally, we will consider the theoretical logic and practical significance of several left-wing ideologies, not least communitarianism, anti-colonialism, and feminism. The analysis of each ideology will be based on certain core texts, which you are supposed to read before every lecture.
