课程内容简介：
本课程主要关注国际经济的理论阐述。内容覆盖李嘉图贸易模型、新古典贸易理论等关于国际贸易的理论。同时，要素移动的原因和经济效果、贸易政策的形式和作用、国际收支、汇率决定、以及开放经济下的宏观政策也是课程讨论的重要内容。
Course Description
This course aims to provide students with a survey of fundamentals in international economics, in both theory and empirics. The course consists of two parts: international trade and international finance, with an emphasis on the former. The first part includes topics on why countries trade, what they trade, the benefits and costs of trade, and the motivations for and the effects of government trade policies. The second part contains topics on how exchange rates are determined and the effects of global imbalance.
