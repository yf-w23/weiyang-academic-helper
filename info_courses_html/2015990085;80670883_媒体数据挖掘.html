课程简介：
本课程围绕媒体数据挖掘展开讨论，从媒体数据的获取、清洗、分析、重构、呈现乃至评价入手，完整了解媒体数据挖掘的多个环节。其中包含数据、内容、网络互动等诸多要素的结合。完成本课程之后，对媒体转型、新媒体发展乃至智能设备的未来生态将会有一定程度的清晰了解
英文课程简介：
This course starts the discussion from media data mining. From the aspects of access, cleaning, analysis, reconstruction, rendering and evaluation of media data, the whole process of multimedia data mining is going to be discussed through the whole semester. Additionally, the key elements in the process of data mining containing data, content, network interaction as well as their conflation will be well understood. Upon completion of this course, there will be a clear understanding of media transformation, new media development, and even the future of intelligent equipment ecosystem.
