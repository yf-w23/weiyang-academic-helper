课程内容简介：
中国文明课内容，从历史与比较的视野，讨论中华民族独特发展道路，形成的制度、组织、思想特色，以及个人、家庭与社会的价值体系。教学过程中，有原典资料分析，也有形象性视频展示，力图深入浅出地讨论中国文明的发展历程、现实影响以及对今天的启示。
Course Description
The history of Chinese civilization includes the outline of Chinese history, political management system, economic and financial system, social and cultural thoughts, as well as the comparison between China and the West.In the course of teaching, we will discuss the development course, characteristics and influence of Chinese civilization on today in a simple and profound way by using the original classic materials and video materials.
