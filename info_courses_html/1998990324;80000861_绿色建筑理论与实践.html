课程简介：
本课程共八周，每周一大节课。由不同教师分头讲授： 其中，第一、二、三讲，由我校客座教授，慕尼黑工业大学建筑系前系主任Thomas Herzog教授讲授，重点在与国际前沿理论和实践，以及太阳能在城市规划和建筑设计中的应用等。 第四讲由秦佑国教授主讲，内容为"当代中国国情的绿色建筑"，第五讲由朱颖心教授主讲，内容为"建筑环境学认知"，第六讲由宋晔皓副教授主讲，内容为"中国农村生态住宅设计实践，以张家港生态农宅设计为例"，第七讲由周正楠副教授主讲，内容为"中国城市绿色建筑设计分析，以清华大学超低能耗楼为例"，第八讲由林波荣副教授主讲，内容为"绿色建筑设计的计算机辅助工具"。 课程考核以考察方式进行，结合Seminar和最终的论文学生需提交一篇课程论文。 The course lasts eight weeks and consists of eight lectures. The first three lectures will be given by Prof. Thomas Herzog, former dean of Department of Architecture in TUM. He will concentrate on the using of solar energy in architecture and urban planning. Besides, he will introduce briefly the new trends of green building design and research. The forth lecture will be given by Prof. Qin Youguo, former dean of School of Architecture, Tsinghua University, he will show the students the overall view of the development of green building design in China. The fifth one will be given by Prof. Zhu Yingxin, which is about the recognition of the built environment. The sixth one will be given by Associate Prof. Song Yehao, which concentrates on green building design for farmers in the rural area in China. The seventh one will be given by Associate Prof. Zhou Zhengnan, which concentrates on the green building design in the urban area. The last one will be given by Associate Prof. Lin Borong, which concentrates on computer simulation in green building design., There are several seminars in the course and one paper is asked to each student at the end of course.
