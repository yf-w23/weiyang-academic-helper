课程内容简介：
深度学习是人工智能学科的一个重要组成部分，是近年来人工智能再次腾飞的主要推动力之一。深度学习也正在快速的融入经济金融学科的应用之中，尤其在最近几年，以深度学习作为研究工具顶级研究论文开始涌现。本课程将介绍深度学习的概念和历史，深入讲解其基本原理和典型模型，并讨论其研究前沿。具体内容包括多层感知机、卷积神经网络、Elman反馈神经网络、长短时记忆网络、transformer、生成对抗网络等模型，在兼顾计算机视觉、自然语言处理等领域的应用的同时，结合实证公司金融与资产定价的具体理论，介绍这些模型在金融和经济数据分析中的应用。课程还将简要介绍python编程基础和神经网络的工具软件Pytorch的使用方法。学期末学生需要针对一个实际的应用问题（自选+教师提供结合）构建自己的模型进行求解，撰写和提交课程报告，并在课堂展示。
Course Description
Deep learning is an important part of artificial intelligence. It is the one of the major driving forces for the resurgence of artificial intelligence in recent years. In addition, in recent years (since 2016), a surging trend with a combination of deep learning and finance emerges in top finance and economic journals. In this course, you will learn the basic concepts and history of deep learning. The theoretical foundation and typical models will be elaborated. The cutting-edge research about deep learning will also be discussed. Specifically, we will study the typical models including the multi-layer Perceptron, convolutional neural network, Elman recurrent neural network, long short-term memory network, transformer, generative adversarial network, etc. On one hand, selective applications of these models in computer vision and natural language processing will be covered. On the other hand, applications in finance and economics will also be covered. Python, a programming language and Pytorch, a neural network software, will be briefly introduced. In the end of the course, you are required to design a deep learning model for solving a practical problem, which can be chosen from a set of problems provided by the teacher or proposed by yourself, submit a course project report, and present the results in class.
