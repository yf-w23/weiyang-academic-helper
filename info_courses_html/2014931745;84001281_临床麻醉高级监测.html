课程简介：
讲授术中心电监测和常见心律失常（包括窦性心动过速、窦性心动过缓、早搏、心房纤颤、心房扑动、室上性心动过速、室性心动过速、心室纤颤、房室传导阻滞、预激综合征、心肌缺血、心梗、以及常见电解质紊乱的心电图改变，围绕病因、表现、治疗）、血气分析与内环境监测、呼吸功能监测、术中凝血功能监测、麻醉深度及脑氧饱和度监测、神经肌肉传递和肌松监测、血流动力学监测和中心静脉置管以及经食道超声心动图监测等内容。
英文课程简介：
Contents of Specialty Courses including: Intraoperative ECG Monitoring & Arrhythmia (Sinus Tachycardia, Sinus Bradycardia, Premature Beat, AF, Af, Supraventricular Tachycardia, Ventricular Tachycardia, Vf, AVB, Preexcitation Syndrome, Myocardial Ischemia, MI, Electrolytes Disorder-Induced Arrhythmia ), ABG Analysis & Internal Environment Monitoring, Invasive Hemodynamics Monitoring, Pulmonary Function Test, Intraoperative Coagulation Function Monitoring, Anesthesia Depth Monitoring, Cerebral Oxygen Saturation Monitoring, Neuromuscular Transmission & Blockade Monitoring, TEE
