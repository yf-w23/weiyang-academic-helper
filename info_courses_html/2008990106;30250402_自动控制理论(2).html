课程内容简介：
反馈控制是复杂工程系统中最重要的设计原理之一。课程将系统讲授基于反馈机制的进阶控制理论，主要内容包括多输入系统设计、最优控制、采样系统与预测控制等。 多输入系统部分主要包括抗干扰控制和解耦控制。最优控制部分，主要介绍最优控制问题举例与数学描述、泛函与变分法基本原理、基于变分法求解最优控制问题、线性系统二次型指标的最优控制和极大值原理简介等。采样系统部分主要介绍采样控制、离散时间系统以及相应的最优控制理论。在此基础上，预测控制部分将介绍预测控制基本原理、基于非参数化模型的动态矩阵控制、基于参数化模型的状态反馈预测控制、预测控制的稳定性分析和预测控制的应用案例。
Course Description
Feedback control is one of the most important design principles in complex engineering systems. The course will systematically teach advanced control theory that roots on feedback mechanism, including multi-input system design, optimal control, sampled-data system and predictive control. The multi-input system part includes the disturbance rejection control and decoupling control. The optimal control part will introduce the paradigms and mathematical descriptions of optimal control problems, the fundamental principles of functional and variational calculus, the solution of optimal control problems based on variational method, and the linear quadratic regulation of linear systems and the maximum principle. The sampled-data part focuses on the sampled-data and discrete-time systems, as well as their optimal control. The part of predictive control will introduce the basic principles of predictive control, dynamic matrix control based on non-parametric model, state-feedback predictive control based on parametric model, stability analysis of predictive control and application paradigms of predictive control.
