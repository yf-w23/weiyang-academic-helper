课程内容简介：
本课程围绕视觉信息获取与显示相关的科学问题、前沿进展和广泛应用展开教学，讲授人工智能技术驱动下的计算摄像学、光场理论、立体视频采集与显示、计算全息等前沿技术进展，介绍人工智能驱动的视觉计算方法、类脑智能与具身智能等研究方向，讨论若干具有探索意义的重大科学问题。同时，课程将穿插介绍该领域的国际学术前沿热点、代表性研究机构以及“图诺问题”等重大科学议题，帮助学生建立问题导向意识与全球视野，激发科研兴趣，增强其对相关基础课程的学习动力，引导其学以致用，为未来从事相关研究工作奠定基础。此外，课程设置大作业环节，对学生的文献调研能力、学术表达能力以及团队协作能力进行系统训练。
Course Description
Visual signal is the most important way of carrying and transmitting information, and it has been studied for a long time in academia and widely applied in industrial fields. However, conventional acquisition, processing and display are all largely limited, so researchers have proposed many emerging theories and technologies in both capture and display of visual information, including computational photography, light field theory, stereoscopic acquisition and display, etc. This course is mainly taught via classroom instruction, and consists of 16 class hours. On the one hand, we will teach the emerging theories and technologies in capture and display of visual signal. On the other hand, we will introduce the academic frontiers and well know groups, broaden the views of students, stimulate their interest in scientific research, motivate them to study the fundamental course of science, and instruct them to learn for practice and help them lay foundations for the future researches.
