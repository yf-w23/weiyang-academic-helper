课程内容简介：
医学是适应人们战胜疾病的需要而产生、发展起来的；解剖学在一定程度上，主要是适应医学发展的需要而发展并完善起来的。《人体奥秘》从运动系统、神经系统、感觉器、内分泌系统、脉管系统、消化系统、呼吸系统、泌尿系统、生殖系统开始学习，结构和功能统一，理论与实际统一。了解这些结构的功能甚至结构出现异常，将导致何种疾病，个体生活行为在此过程中有何作用。授课分为理论课和实验课。理论课是按系统介绍人体的基本结构及功能，各系统器官常见疾病的表现与常用检查方法。实验课是在教师的指导下，学生以自学为主，通过观察标本、模型及自身的结构，巩固学习的知识。
Course Description
Medicine is developed to meet the needs of people to overcome the disease; anatomy, to some extent, mainly to meet the needs of medical development and improvement. Mysteries of Human Body begins with the learning of the motor system, the nervous system, the sensory organs, the endocrine system, the vasculature, the digestive system, the respiratory system, the urinary system, the reproductive system, based on the unification of structure and function, the unification of theory and practice. Understand what kind of disease will be cause if the function or structure is abnormal, and what is the role of individual life behavior in this process. Teaching is divided into theoretical and experimental class. Theory class is introduced by the system of the basic structure and function of the human body, the performance of various systems of common diseases and common inspection methods. Under the guidance of teachers, experimental classes are mainly self-learning. They observe the specimens, models and their own structure to consolidate the learning knowledge.
