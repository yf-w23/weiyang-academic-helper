课程内容简介：
本课程结合材料学基础理论及工程材料的实际应用，通过课堂讲授、教学实验和课外调研，使学生了解常用工程材料以及它们的应用特点，理解材料的成分、工艺、组织、性能及应用间的相互影响，掌握进行材料选择和工艺路线制定的基本方法。 课程首先介绍材料科学基础知识，包括原子的结合键、晶体的特征、典型晶体结构、晶向与晶面指数、晶体的塑性变形及缺陷等。然后讲授扩散原理及合金相图，包括金属的结晶、相图、相律及杠杆原理，重点分析了铁碳相图。接下来对典型工程材料的化学成分、组织结构、加工工艺、材料性能和典型应用进行了整体介绍，涉及钢铁材料、有色金属、陶瓷、高分子和复合材料，重点是典型材料的成分、组织、工艺及性能的关系。最后对机械零件的失效形式及失效分析方法进行了介绍，加深学生对机械零件选材原则的理解，以及对于规范和标准的把握，帮助学生掌握在先进制造、重大装备和重要工程领域进行合理选材的基本能力。
Course Description
This course combines the fundamentals of engineering materials with their applications. By means of lectures, discussion, and lab exercises, students can recognize commonly used engineering materials and their application characteristics, understand the relationships among compositions, processes, microstructures and performances, and master the basic methods of material selection and processing route formulation. We will first introduce the basic knowledge of material science, including the chemical bonds, the characteristics of crystals, typical crystal structures, Miller indices, the plastic deformation and defects of crystals, etc. Then the principle of diffusion and phase diagrams will be introduced, including the crystallization, phase diagrams, phase equilibrium, etc., focusing on the analysis of Fe-Fe3C phase diagram. We will then introduce the chemical composition, microstructure, processing processes, material performances and applications of typical engineering materials, mainly involving steel, non-ferrous metals, ceramics, polymer and composite materials. The key knowledge is the relationship among compositions, microstructures, processing and properties. Finally, the failure form and analysis methods of mechanical parts are explained to deepen students' understanding of the principles of material selection and standards, which also help them master the basic capabilities of reasonable material selections in advanced manufacturing, major equipment and other important engineering fields.
