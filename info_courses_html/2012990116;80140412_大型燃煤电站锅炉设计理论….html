大型燃煤电站锅炉设计理论与应用

课程简介：
本课程的主要内容包括：大型煤粉锅炉和流化床锅炉技术应用现状，锅炉工作原理与燃烧技术；锅炉的热力计算与整体布置，锅炉关键参数的选择方法；水循环与水动力；燃烧方式和燃烧设备的选择方法，现代火力发电燃烧设备的设计方法，包括流化床燃烧设备、煤粉炉燃烧设备；根据特定条件进行的案例研究（统一案例12个；单独案例即没有重复每个学生一个），研究深度达到可以发表的水平。
英文课程简介：
The main contents of this course include: application status of large pulverized coal boiler and fluidized bed boiler technology, working principle and combustion technology of boiler; thermal calculation and overall layout of boiler; selection method of key parameters of boiler; water circulation and hydrodynamic; selection method of combustion mode and combustion equipment; design method of combustion equipment for modern thermal power generation, including fluidized bed combustion equipment, overall layout of boiler, selection method of boiler key parameters, and design method of combustion equipment of modern thermal power generation Coal fired boiler combustion equipment; case studies based on specific conditions (12 unified cases; single case, i.e. no repetition of one for each student), and the research depth reached the level that can be published.

进度安排：

本课程共32个学时：

前16个学时主要讲述燃煤锅炉基本知识及大型煤粉锅炉的设计及结构特点，其中包括4个学时的调研与讨论；

后16个学时主要是大型循环流化床锅炉的结构特点和运行技术，其中也包括4个学时的试验室运行操作。
