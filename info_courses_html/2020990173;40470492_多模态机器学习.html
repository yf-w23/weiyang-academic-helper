课程内容简介：
随着互联网的发展，多媒体数据已经变得唾手可得，如图像，音频，视频，文本等；近十年的人工神经网络的进 展（如多模态大模型GPT4）也让这些数据的融合交叉变成了大趋势。本课程涉及的应用领域包括图像和视频处 理/生成、声音和语音处理/生成、自然语言处理/生成等。课程旨在介绍人工智能领域中常见的媒体的信号处理 和机器学习方法，如数据表征，数据压缩，序列模型，数据合成，多模态融合等。课程通过理论教学与项目实 践，让学生了解不同数据与信号的特性，处理方式，以及共通与交叉融合之处。该课程可作为计算机视觉，自然 语言处理等课程的后续课程。
Course Description
With the development of the Internet, multimedia data such as images, audio, video, and text has become readily available. Advances in artificial neural networks over the past decade (e.g., the multimodal large model GPT‑4) have also made the integration and fusion of these data a major trend. This course covers application areas including image and video processing/generation, audio and speech processing/generation, natural language processing/generation, and so on. It aims to introduce common media signal processing and machine learning methods in artificial intelligence, such as data representation, data compression, sequence models, data synthesis, multimodal fusion, etc. Through theoretical instruction and project practice, the course enables students to understand the characteristics and processing methods of different data and signals, as well as their commonalities and intersections. This course can serve as a follow‑up to courses such as computer vision and natural language processing.
