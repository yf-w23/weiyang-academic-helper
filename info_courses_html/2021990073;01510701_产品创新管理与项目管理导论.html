课程内容简介：
新时代对于项目管理能力，创新能力，沟通交流和协作的能力，国际化工作的能力，提出了较高的要求。这门课程可以帮助学生通过16个学时的学习后初步掌握这些能力，能够在国际化的环境里面开展创新和项目管理等工作。 该课程介绍了项目管理，创新管理和产品开发的关键概念，并支持学生有效地参与创造和实现商机。学生将能够获得与项目管理，创新管理和产品开发相关的知识和技能，此外，还可以通过产品开发项目获得实践经验。通过课堂讨论和课程活动，学生将能够获得额外的国际经验和曝光率，并提高他们的英语语言能力。课程将会使用多种教学方法：从术语和概念的基本解释，练习，案例研究工作来呈现知识应用的个人工作以及项目的团队合作。除了对理论讲座的描述之外，重点将放在实际工作上，学生将计划和管理一个项目。 主讲教师朱峰担任iCenter智能制造实验室主任，拥有多年高科技行业的研发和管理经验，拥有多项国际专利，在多个国家生活和工作过，曾经在阿里巴巴，亚马逊，三星电子，和爱立信等世界知名高科技公司担任工程师，产品经理与总监等综合管理职务，具有丰富的创新管理和项目管理的经验。 朱峰老师获得2022年清华大学年度教学奖，清华大学青年教师教学比赛（工科组）二等奖。毕业于清华大学自动化系，获得博士学位和优秀博士毕业生，并且在美国康奈尔大学获得MBA。朱峰老师重视和同学们的互动式交流，会分享自己的工作与人生经历，让学生学有所悟，学有所成。 课程特点： 1. 小组研讨，师生互动，享受学习与创新 2. 产业案例学习 3. 投资相关经验分享
Course Description
 Do you particularly want to know how product innovations are implemented and managed to influence human society?  Do you want to learn and practice how to innovate with teachers and classmates from different countries in an international environment?  Do you want to turn your and your classmates' ideas into products and then find investors to go to market on a large scale?  Do you know the relationship between product innovation management and project management? If you answered the above questions in the affirmative, then you are welcome to join the course: Introduction to Project Management and Product Innovation Development. This course is an English seminar course conducted by Feng Zhu, a teacher from iCenter. You will study project management and product innovation management in groups with students from different departments in different countries, and exercise your communication and writing skills. Through the study, discussion and practice, you can initially establish the students' engineering project management ability, innovation ability, communication and collaboration ability, international learning and work ability, and finally be able to carry out innovation and project management in an international environment. Feng Zhu is a senior lecturer at iCenter with varied international experience on engineering and business. His research interests including Artificial Intelligence, Intelligent Manufacturing, Internet of Things, Industrial Internet, 5G/6G communication, E-Commerce, and Digitalization Business where he published 20+ international papers and patents. He worked at Ericsson, Amazon, Samsung, and Alibaba on different positions in different functions from engineer, manager to director. Course features: 1. Group discussion, teacher-student interaction, learning and innovation 2. Industrial case study 3. Investment related experience sharing
