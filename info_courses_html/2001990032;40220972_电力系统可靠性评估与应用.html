课程内容简介：
本课程是电机系本科生专业选修课。通过本课程的学习使学生掌握电力系统可靠性评估方法基础，培养不确定性的思维模式和概率性评估方法，学会并能应用可靠性评估软件及其他同类电气分析软件开展实际的科学研究工作。主要学习成效包括： 1. 能够掌握随机过程及概率性评估方法，掌握可靠性评估理论模型、方法及应用，并结合实际问题，建立相应问题的评估模型。本学习成效对应电气工程及其自动化专业培养方案中的培养成效1-2，AE级。 2.能够针对电力系统面临的实际问题，应用可靠性评估的思路，通过提出与实际问题相关的概率性评估模型，开展实际的科学研究工作。本学习成效对应电气工程及其自动化专业培养方案中的培养成效4，E级。 3. 能够结合实际的工作需要，进行模型参数设置，并应用可靠性评估软件及其他同类电气评估软件，分析、研究评估结果。本学习成效对应电气工程及其自动化专业培养方案中的培养成效5，E级。 4. 建立团队合作意识，培养沟通协调能力，通过课程讨论、展示等环节，加强学生总结、交流能力。本学习成效对应电气工程及其自动化专业培养方案中的培养成效9-10，C级。 5. 培养学生探索科学研究问题和自主开展科研工作的能力，通过对交叉学科的了解，保持科学研究热情。本学习成效对应电气工程及其自动化专业培养方案中的培养成效12，E级。
Course Description
This course is an elective course for undergraduate students. Through this course, students will be able to master the basis of power system reliability assessment method， cultivate students’ thinking mode of uncertainty and probability assessment method, learn and apply reliability assessment software and other similar electrical analysis software to carry out practical scientific research work. 1. Able to master the random process and probability assessment method, master the theoretical model, method and application of reliability evaluation. Combined with practical problems, to establish the corresponding problem assessment model. The effectiveness of this study corresponds to the training programs in the training results 1-2, AE. 2. To solve the practical problems faced by the power system, apply the idea of reliability evaluation, carry out the actual scientific research work by putting forward the probabilistic evaluation model related to the practical problems. The effectiveness of this study corresponds to the training programs in the training results 4, E. 3. Students can combine the actual work needs, set the model parameters, and master the reliability assessment software and other similar electrical evaluation software, able to analyze and study evaluation results. The effectiveness of this study corresponds to the training programs in the training results 5, C. 4. To establish teamwork awareness, develop communication and coordination ability. Through course discussion, display and other learning parts, to strengthen students’ ability of conclusion, summary and communication. The effectiveness of this study corresponds to the training programs in the training results 9-10, C.
进度安排：
    课程分授课、小组讨论、可靠性评估云平台应用三个环节。其中，

This course contains taught, group discussion and the application of the software platform of reliability assessment.

（一）授课环节包括10讲，共26学时

Taught, including 10 sessions, 26 learning hours

1、绪论，电力系统可靠性的基本概念及其研究进展；

Introduction, basic ideas and research development of the power system reliability evaluation

2、概率论与随机过程；

Probability theory and stochastic process

3、元件可靠性分析方法；

Component reliability analysis method

4、系统可靠性分析方法；

System reliability analysis method

5、发电系统可靠性分析方法；

Generation system reliability analysis method

6、发输电系统可靠性分析方法；

Composite system reliability analysis method

7、配电系统可靠性分析方法；

Distribution system reliability analysis method

8、全电压等级可靠性评估方法；

Over-all system reliability analysis method

9、可靠性经济性协调；

Reliability and economy coordination

10、电力系统运行可靠性分析。

Power system operational reliability analysis

掌握工程系统可靠性分析的数学基础及基本分析方法，掌握电力系统可靠性分析的模型和方法，了解电力系统可靠性的研究前沿。

Master the mathematical basis and basic analysis method of reliability analysis of engineering systems, master the model and method of power system reliability analysis, understand the frontier of power system reliability.

（二）讨论课，6学时

Group discussion, 6 learning hours

学生自由分组，完成选题内容，并进行课堂展示、分享，选题内容包括可靠性评估算法实现与校验，可靠性理论研究文献综述两大部分。

Students are encouraged to group, complete the topic content, share and present research work during the classroom display. Topics including the implementation and verification of the reliability assessment algorithm, reliability theory research literature review two parts.

（三）可靠性评估云平台应用

The application of the software platform of reliability assessment

配合课堂教学与学生讨论课，学生可以在云平台中实现对发电系统、发输电系统、配电系统、主接线的可靠性评估工作，掌握一类电力系统分析评估软件使用方法。

        Combined with taught and discussion, students can complete the power generation system, power transmission system, power distribution system, the substation system reliability assessment based on the reliability assessment software cloud platform, and master a type of power system analysis and evaluation software.
