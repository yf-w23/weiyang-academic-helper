课程简介：
本课程提出“基础表达层→学术映射层→功能单元层→论文本体层”的递进式“四层级写作法”。通过引入“学术化表达四步提升法”“研究结果经纬度地图”等十余个创新概念，将原本机械的写作知识学习升华为学术思考与表达能力共进的训练，从而提升论文结构设计、研究分析与学术表达的能力。
英文课程简介：
This course proposes a progressive "four-level writing methodology": "foundational expression layer → academic mapping layer → functional unit layer → dissertation entity layer." By introducing over ten innovative concepts such as the "four-step enhancement method for academic expression" and the "latitude-longitude map of research findings," it transforms the originally mechanical learning of writing knowledge into a training process that simultaneously advances academic thinking and expressive skills. Consequently, it enhances abilities in dissertation structure design, research analysis, and academic expression.
