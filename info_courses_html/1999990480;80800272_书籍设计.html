课程简介：
书籍设计是涵盖了文字、图形、符号、版面、插图以及多元素的系统工程 。课程前期主要讲授书籍概论、东西方书籍设计的发展文脉、中国现代书籍出版系统的形成与发展、未来书籍的媒介的转换与观念的更新、书籍整体设计原理等基础知识；中期从理论向实践过渡，邀请印刷、纸张、出版编辑等相关专家来到课堂中进行经验分享；后期结合实际案例，辅导学生课程作业并为学生作品落地提供帮助。 每位学生发挥自己的专业特点和兴趣爱好，自编自导自演，经历一本书籍设计的全过程。涵盖了主题的选定、文本的编辑梳理、字体字号的选择、版式的设计、图片插画的绘制、手段风格的确立，以及图文之间所呈现视线流的韵律关系和后期纸张印刷工艺的选择等等。书籍页数不少于40页，并为书籍设计对应的宣传海报。在课程结课的成果汇报中分享设计过程、交流设计心得、积累经验教训、展示实物成品。
英文课程简介：
Book design is a system project that covers text, graphics, symbols, layout, illustration and multi-elements. In the early stage of the course, we mainly teach basic knowledge such as introduction to books, the development of eastern and western book design, the formation and development of China's modern book publishing system, the transformation of the book medium and the renewal of ideas, and the overall design principle of books. In the medium term, we will make a transition from theory to practice, and invite relevant experts such as printing, paper, publishing and editing to come to the classroom to share their experience. In the later stage, combined with practical cases, we’ll tutor students' course and provide help for the making of students' works. Each student gives full play to his professional characteristics and interests, and experiences the whole process of designing a book. It covers the selection of themes, the editing and sorting of texts, the selection of fonts and sizes, the design of layouts, the drawing of illustrations, the establishment of means and styles, as well as the rhythmic relationship of line of sight between texts and words, and the selection of later paper and printing technology, etc. The number of pages of the book shall not be less than 40, and design a poster for the book. Share the design process, exchange design experience, accumulate experience and lessons, and display physical products in the result report of the course conclusion.
