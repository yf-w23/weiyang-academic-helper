课程简介：
机器学习和人工智能发展迅猛，给统计学的研究提出了很多新的挑战。热点问题包括高维统计学习，迁移学习的理论和方法，对抗学习的理论和方法等。本课程将讲授和探讨理解这些问题的理论基础工具，并进一步介绍这些热点问题研究上的最新理论进展。
英文课程简介：
Rapid developments of machine learning and AI have prompted challenging research issues in statistics and data science. Hot topics include high-dimensional statistical learning, transfer learning, adversarial learning, etc. In this course, we will learn foundational tools needed to understand these issues, and present new results in the areas.

Content of the course:

 

1.     Review of basics for high-dimensional statistics and machine learning

a.     Concentration inequalities

b.     Metric entropy and gaussian complexity

c.     Empirical process theory

2.     Information theory and connections to statistical learning

a.     Coding theory

b.     Data compression and function estimation

c.     Adaptive estimation and universal coding

d.     Aggregation theory

3.     Classification

a.     VC theory

b.     Risk bounds for empirical risk minimization

4.     Model selection and model averaging

a.     Model selection methods and properties

b.     Model averaging methods and properties

c.     Localized model selection/averaging

d.     Mixture of experts

5.     Minimax optimal learning

a.     Methods for minimax lower bounding

b.     Minimax rates for nonparametric function estimation

c.     Minimax rates for adversarial learning

d.     Minimax rates for transfer learning
