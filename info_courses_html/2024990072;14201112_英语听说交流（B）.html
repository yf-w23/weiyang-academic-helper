课程内容简介：
本课程面向中级英语语言水平的学生（英语分级考试成绩为二级），聚焦发音训练、听力理解、日常口头对话、演说及学术话题讨论能力的培养, 鼓励学生提升个人表达自信度和准确度，培养团队合作意识。课程设计基于“输出驱动、输入促成”的产出导向法教学理念，讲练内容包括：（1）较为准确地理解语速为100-140词/分钟的听力材料；（2）对英语元音、辅音、词重音、句重音、连读等准确发音；（3）口头陈述个人观点，如课堂讨论、公众演讲等；（4）口头转述、总结、分析与评价他人观点。 本课程除听力和口语内容训练以外，还将借助语音学专业知识和专业软件，深入剖析语音发音机制，科学指导和个性化辅导英语发音，将为同学们揭开英语语音科学的奥秘。
Course Description
This course is designed for students with an intermediate level of English proficiency (Grade 2 on the English proficiency test). It focuses on developing skills in pronunciation, listening comprehension, everyday conversation, public speaking, and academic discussions. The course is built around an "output-driven, input-enhanced" teaching approach, and aims to boost students' confidence and accuracy in self-expression, while also encouraging a sense of teamwork. Key areas of focus include: (1) accurately understanding listening materials spoken at a speed of 100-140 words per minute; (2) pronouncing English vowels, consonants, stress patterns, etc. correctly; (3) presenting personal viewpoints orally, such as in class discussions or public speeches; and (4) paraphrasing, summarizing, analyzing, and evaluating others' opinions. The instructor Xin Li holds a Ph.D. in phonetics from Utrecht University. She previously taught in the English Department at Fudan University and joined the Language Center at Tsinghua University in the fall of 2024. Dr.Xin Li has received numerous teaching awards, including the First Prize in the Shanghai Higher Education Teaching Innovation Competition, the Special Prize in the Fudan University Teaching Innovation Competition, and the First Prize in the Tsinghua University Young Teacher Teaching Competition. With extensive experience in both English phonetics research and teaching, she brings valuable expertise to the course. In addition to listening and speaking training, the course will use advanced phonetic knowledge and specialized software to analyze English pronunciation mechanism in depth, providing scientific guidance and personalized coaching, making it especially beneficial for students with pronunciation issues and interesting for students curious about speech sciences.
