课程简介：
本课程将围绕组织管理学中的两大基本视角（个人层面和团队层面）来选取主题进行展开研讨。个体层面的研究主题包括特质理论、特质与情境的交互作用理论、多元化、情绪与心境、感知和决策、个体动机和激励理论、以及这些机制对如下行为的影响：包括员工的工作绩效、组织公民行为、反生产和不道德的行为、建言行为、离职行为、创造力和创新行为等。团队层面的研究主题包括团队研究的理论和方法入门、团队的结构组成和团队的分类、团队开发、适应力和团队动力学、团队成员互动过程和心理状态、领导力在团队中的作用、团队绩效和团队创新、以及虚拟团队和多团队系统的特点等。本课程将平衡传统的经典理论和前沿的学术发表成果，旨在起到扩展视野和启发兴趣的作用和效果。
英文课程简介：
This course examines behavioral management research and select topics for discussion from two fundamental levels or angles, namely, the individual level and the team level. Research topics at the individual level include trait theories, trait-context interactionists' perspectives, diversity, emotions and moods, perception and decision making, individual motivation theories, and the effects of these mechanisms on such behaviors as employee performance, organization citizenship behavior (OCB), counter-productive and unethical behavior, voice and turnover, creativity and innovation, etc. The research topics at the team level include the introduction of team research theories and methodology, team composition and the classification of the teams, team development, adaptability, and dynamics, team interaction process and emergent state of team members, the role of leadership in teams, team effectiveness and team innovation, and the characteristics of virtual teams and multi-team systems. This course tries to strike a balance between classic behavioral management theories and up-to-date empirical publications. This course aims at expanding students’ horizons by exposing them to a variety of organizational management topics, and inspiring their research interests. If interested in a specific topic, students are encouraged to study more in-depth and take initiatives to continue exploring that topic with the instructor during and after this course.
