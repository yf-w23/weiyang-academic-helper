课程简介：
本课程为秋季学期《电子显微学》理论课对应的实验课程，于每年春季学期前八周开设。通过系统的32学时的上机教学培训、以及学生课后自行预约上机练习相结合的方式，使同学们比较全面地掌握透射电子显微学的实验方法，包括电子衍射与衍射衬度、相位衬度与高分辨透射电子显微像、扫描透射电子显微像、X-射线能量色散谱和电子能量损失谱等谱学分析方法，从而能够在材料研究中选择或创新性地设计合适的方法分析材料的结构。学生通过最后的上机考核后可以取得独立上机操作资格。
英文课程简介：
This is an Laboratory excerscie course on tranmission electron microscopy and spectroscopy, including the basic instrument operation of TEM, electron diffraction, imaging (phase contrast and high-resolution TEM, scanning transmission electron microscopy and aberration-corrected TEM), and spectroscopy (X-ray energy dispersive spectroscopy and electrons energy loss spectroscopy). The course consists of 32 hour in-class TEM demo, plus additional out-of-class TEM practices under the guidance of experienced engineers. Students who passed the final examination can independently operate the TEM in their thesis studies.
