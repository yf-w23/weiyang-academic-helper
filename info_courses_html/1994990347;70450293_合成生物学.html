课程简介：
合成生物学是跨生物学、计算机科学、工程学、数学等多种学科的综合性学科。近年来，基因测序技术的更新换代以及DNA合成速率和成本的降低为合成生物学的快速发展奠定了坚实的基础，各个分支领域都取得了飞速的进展，未来将在研究方法、研究内容和产品开发、产品应用等方面迎来更大的突破。 合成生物学建立在系统生物学的基础上，结合工程学和基因组学技术，开发生物元件和模块，构建在生命体内发挥特殊功能的分子线路，实现现有生命体改造，甚至创造新的生命体。过去十年，合成生物学理论和技术飞速发展，并开始迅速向应用领域延伸，对医药健康、生物制造、环境监控和农业生产等领域产生了深远的影响。合成生物学被称为继DNA双螺旋发现所催生的分子生物学革命和“人类基因组计划”实施所催生的基因组学革命之后的第三次生物技术革命，是本世纪一项“颠覆性技术”，合成生物学将对人类社会产生渐进性、渗透性及革命性的影响。 本课程预计48学时，面向全校研究生，采用全英文授课，由清华大学合成与系统生物学中心主任陈国强教授总负责，主讲人为来自清华大学合成与系统生物学中心的17位专家教授。本课程将围绕生物元器件设计与挖掘、合成生物学关键技术及合成生物学的应用进展三方面开展系统讲解。
英文课程简介：
Synthetic biology is based on Systems Biology, which combines Engineering and Genomics Technology to develop biological components and modules, to build molecular circuits that play special functions in the body of life, to optimize the existing life bodies, or even to create new life bodies. In the past decade, the theory and technology of Synthetic Biology have developed rapidly, and began to extend rapidly to the application field, which has a profound impact on the fields of medical health, biological manufacturing, environmental monitoring and agricultural production. Synthetic biology is known as the third biotechnology revolution after the molecular biology revolution caused by the discovery of DNA double helix and the genomics revolution caused by the implementation of "Human Genome Project". It is a "Subversive Technology" in this century. Synthetic biology will have a gradual, penetrating and revolutionary impact on human society. This course will be offered to graduate students throughout the school. It is expected to take 48 class hours and teach in English. Professor CHEN Guo-Qiang, director of the Center for Synthetic and Systems Biology of Tsinghua University, is in charge of the course. This course will focus on the design and mining of biological components, key technologies of synthetic biology and application progress of synthetic biology.
