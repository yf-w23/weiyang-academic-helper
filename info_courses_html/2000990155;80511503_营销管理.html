课程简介：
在全球化和数字化的大环境中，面对日益激烈的市场竞争，企业越来越需要通过制定前瞻性的营销战略，建立高效的营销体系以及卓越管理执行过程来获得长期的竞争优势。本课程围绕这个主题，通过课堂讲授、案例讨论与解剖、应用导向的课程报告等形式实现以下3个教学目标：1，使MBA学生树立市场营销的观念，发展营销分析和决策技巧；2，使MBA学生充分认识市场营销在企业中的角色地位，了解市场营销与其它管理职能的关系；3，使MBA学生系统了解市场营销的理论和方法以及前沿实践，掌握建立高绩效的市场营销体系的步骤。 本课程首先讨论营销观念的演化过程以及新环境下营销模式的进化，并讨论建立顾客满意、顾客价值和顾客关系的重要性。其次，讨论建立市场导向的营销战略计划的内容和步骤，并主要从顾客行为分析和竞争者行为分析来探讨营销环境分析的方法和步骤。第三，从市场细分、目标市场和市场定位的角度讨论开发营销战略的内容、方法和步骤。第四，讨论在营销战略的指导下如何建立有效的4P组合(产品、定价、分销和促销)策略。最后，讨论如何建立高效营销体系的方法和步骤。
英文课程简介：
To survive and develop in competitive marketplace, marketing managers need to scan marketing environment, formulate effective marketing strategy tailored to the specifics of the market and establish efficient marketing system. This program, by theory introduction and case study analysis, is to achieve the following objectives: 1) enabling the participants to the core marketing concepts, build up integrated marketing analysis framework, and develop their skills and ability in marketing decision-making. 2) enabling the participants to identify the role of marketing in the firms, understand the relationship between marketing and other functions of firms. 3) enabling the participants to understand the leading theories and practices of marketing, be able to make high-performance program of marketing. and 4) enabling the participants to be familiar with and comply with the moral rules The program will first analyze opportunities and environment in the market and explore customers value, including customer needs forecast, customer behavior analysis and competitor behavior analysis of the market. It will then discuss marketing strategy for market development in terms of market segments, target market and market positioning, so as to identify and choose customer value. In the end, the program will discuss how to establish effective 4P portfolio (products, pricing, place and promotion) strategy and marketing system in line with the marketing strategy, with an aim to create, provide and communicate customer value.
