课程内容简介：
本课程介绍适合于计算机使用的、求解各种常用数学问题的数值计算方法，研究各种数学问题求解的数值计算方法，讲解如何用计算机解决实际数学问题的方法。主要内本课程介绍求解各种常见数学问题的数值计算方法、以及对应的算法设计与实现，强调各种算法的特点及适用范围。主要内容包括数值计算基础与误差分析，非线性方程的数值解法， 线性方程组的解法（包括直接法与迭代法），矩阵特征值与特征向量的计算，函数插值，函数逼近及线性最小二乘法，数值积分与数值微分，以及常微分方程初值问题的解法。这些既是数值计算领域最基础、最重要的部分，又为后续学习最优化方法、大数据分析与机器学习等领域的高级算法提供基础。
Course Description
The course Numerical Analysis introduces the numerical methods for solving various common mathematical problems, and the techniques for the corresponding algorithm design and implementation. It emphasizes the characteristics of various numerical algorithms and their distinct scopes of application. The main content of this course includes the basics of numerical computing and error analysis, numerical solution of nonlinear equation, solution of linear equation system (including direct method and iterative method), computation of matrix eigenvalues and eigenvectors, function interpolation, function approximation and linear least squares, numerical quadrature and numerical differentiation, and solution of the initial value problem of ordinary differential equation. They are the most fundamental and the most important parts in the area of numerical computing. Moreover, they provide a foundation for learning the follow-up courses on optimization method, and the advanced algorithms for big data analytics and machine learning, etc.
