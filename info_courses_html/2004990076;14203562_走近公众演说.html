课程内容简介：
公众演说是充分体现社会责任和价值传递的复杂社会行为，高度融合了读、写、听、说、观察、思考、现场表现等多方面的能力。本课程引导不同学科背景的学生研习公众演说的经典理论，探究公众演说的传统及其对个人发展和社会进步的深刻意义，领悟语言之美、演说之道。在本课程完备的英文演说实践模型中，选课学生还将学习克服紧张情绪、提升英语演说能力，主动探究、深入思考自身的理想信念、学科的特色与多样性、学科间的交叉融合，在跨学科、跨文化交流中互鉴互学。
Course Description
Public speaking is a complex social activity that embodies social responsibility and promotes social values. It integrates multiple abilities such as reading, writing, listening, speaking, observing, thinking, and delivery. This course guides students from different disciplines to study the classic theories of public speaking, explore its traditions and its significance for personal development and social progress, and appreciate the beauty of language and the art of oration. The pedagogical model in this course helps the students overcome nervousness, improve their English speaking skills, and reflect on disciplinary dynamics as well as their own beliefs. Additionally, the interdisciplinary and cross-cultural context of the course fosters greater understanding and empathy among the students.
