课程内容简介：
本课程旨在帮助学生提升心理素质与健康水平，主要内容包括：自我认识、情绪管理、挫折承受力提升、人际沟通、压力应对与管理、创造性智商与创造力的培养、自我调适以及生命教育等。通过这些主题，课程旨在有效帮助学生在学习中开发潜能，建立自信，维护身心健康，明确人生发展方向，珍视生命、欣赏生命，成为一个健康、自强、有使命感的人。  课程目标是提升学员的自我认知与情绪管理能力，培养团队协作精神、领导力以及抗压心理韧性，从而更好地应对未来的挑战与机遇。
Course Description
This course aims to enhance students’ psychological resilience and overall well-being. It covers key topics such as self-awareness, emotional management, resilience in the face of adversity, interpersonal communication, stress management and coping strategies, the cultivation of creative intelligence and creativity, self-adjustment, and life education. Through these themes, the course seeks to effectively assist students in unlocking their potential, building self-confidence, maintaining physical and mental health, clarifying their life direction, cherishing and appreciating life, and becoming healthy, self-reliant, and mission-driven individuals.  The course objectives include enhancing students' self-awareness and emotional management skills, as well as fostering teamwork, leadership, and psychological resilience under pressure, thereby better preparing them to face future challenges and opportunities.
