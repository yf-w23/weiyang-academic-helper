毛泽东思想和中国特色社会主义理论体系概论

总学分：

课程内容简介：
（一）引导学生从总体上把握毛泽东思想、邓小平理论、“三个代表”重要思想和科学发展观形成发展、主要内容和历史地位。 （二）引导学生科学认识和正确把握什么是马克思主义、怎样对待马克思主义？什么是社会主义、怎样建设社会主义？什么是中国特色社会主义、怎样建设中国特色社会主义？建设一个什么样的党、怎样建设党？实现什么样的发展、怎样发展？ （三）通过课堂教学和作业重在解决学生三大关键性问题：一是思想（解决学生的思想困惑）；二是政治（培养学生树立起正确的三观，并能够娴熟运用马克思主义理论的立场、观点和方法分析问题，为其奠定一生成长的基础）；三是理论（通过讲深讲透理论来实现思想、政治目标）。
Course Description
(1) Guide students to grasp from the whole Mao Zedong thought, Deng Xiaoping theory, the important thought of "Three Represents" and the scientific outlook on development, the main content and historical position. (2) Guide students to scientifically understand and correctly grasp what is Marxism, how to treat Marxism? What is socialism and how to build socialism? What is socialism with Chinese characteristics, and how do we build it? What kind of party should we build and how should we build it? What kind of development and how? (3), solving three key problems of students through classroom teaching and homework: first, thought (solving students' ideological confusion); second, politics (cultivating students to establish correct three views, and can skillfully analyze problems from the standpoint, viewpoint and method of Marxist theory, and lay a foundation for their lifelong growth); third, theory (by speaking deep and thorough theory to achieve ideological and political goals).
