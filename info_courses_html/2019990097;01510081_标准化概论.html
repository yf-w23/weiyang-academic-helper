课程内容简介：
课程内容分为标准化素养、产业讲座与调研实践三部分。标准化素养部分系统讲授标准化基本概念、原理及发展脉络，涉及国际与我国的标准分类体系、标准化过程管理及核心原理，强化标准化与质量管理、知识产权保护的关联性认知。 产业讲座部分聚焦行业标准化应用，通过汽车产业国际法规博弈、欧盟WP.29技术法规体系等案例，剖析标准在技术创新与市场准入中的作用；解析团体标准制定流程与团体标准转化为国家标准的实践路径，探讨标准化在供应链管理、绿色低碳等新兴领域的战略价值。 标准调研实践部分设置企业标准体系诊断、标准文本编写等环节，通过标准比对分析、技术性贸易壁垒应对等场景训练，提升标准实施监督与跨文化协调能力。
Course Description
The course content is structured into three components: Standardization Literacy, Industry Lectures, and Standardization Research & Practice. Standardization Literacy​ This module systematically covers fundamental concepts, principles, and developmental trajectories of standardization. It explores international and Chinese standard classification systems, standardization process management, and core methodologies. Emphasis is placed on strengthening the cognitive connection between standardization and quality management, intellectual property protection, and regulatory compliance. Industry Lectures​ Focusing on applied standardization in industries, this section analyzes case studies such as international regulatory in automotive sectors and the EU WP.29 technical regulations system. It examines how standards facilitate technological innovation and market access while addressing challenges in supply chain management and green transitions. The lectures also explore practical pathways for converting industry consortium standards into national standards, emphasizing strategic value in emerging fields like sustainable development. Standardization Research & Practice​ This hands-on segment incorporates enterprise standard system diagnostics, standard document drafting, and scenario-based training in standard comparison analysis and technical trade barrier mitigation. Through real-world simulations, participants enhance skills in standard implementation oversight, cross-cultural coordination, and compliance auditing.
