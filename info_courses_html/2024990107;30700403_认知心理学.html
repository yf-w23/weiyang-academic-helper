课程内容简介：
本课程为认知心理学的基础入门课程，旨在介绍认知心理学的基本理论、研究方法和核心主题。课程涵盖心智的研究历史、认知神经科学的基础知识、知觉、注意、记忆、概念、语言、问题解决和决策等领域，通过丰富的案例和经典实验帮助学生理解人类认知过程的复杂性。本课程特别适合对认知科学和心理学感兴趣的学生，为进一步学习认知心理学进阶课程，以及在人工智能研究中应用人类认知理论打下坚实基础。
Course Description
This course serves as an introductory foundation in cognitive psychology, aiming to present its fundamental theories, research methods, and core topics. It covers the history of mind studies, the basics of cognitive neuroscience, perception, attention, memory, concepts, language, problem-solving, and decision-making. Through a wealth of case studies and classic experiments, students will gain an understanding of the complexity of human cognitive processes. This course is particularly suited for students interested in cognitive science and psychology, laying a solid foundation for advanced cognitive psychology studies and the application of human cognitive theories in artificial intelligence research.
