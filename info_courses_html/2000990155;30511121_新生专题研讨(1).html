课程内容简介：
现代社会，品牌与人们的生活息息相关。人们通过品牌识别产品与服务，通过品牌判断产品质量和价格，通过品牌表达自己的价值观和生活方式，通过品牌维系社会网络。总之，品牌渗入到人们社会生活的方方面面。然而，品牌是什么？ 品牌对消费者具有什么价值，对企业又具有什么价值？为什么有些品牌默默无闻、而有些品牌让人趋之若鹜？背后的机理是什么？品牌的魅力在哪里？ 本课程将引导同学们通过选择和分析现实生活中的品牌来认识品牌的力量把握品牌的精髓，在此基础上，使同学们初步了解和熟悉品牌营销管理的基础理论，主要方法和分析框架，并促进同学们思辨性思维能力的提升。 本课程主要教学内容包括品牌定义与品牌管理分析框架、基于顾客的品牌资产、客户洞察与品牌策略、品牌定位与顾客价值、品牌营销的四步法等。本课程拟遵循理论与实践相结合的教学理念，采用研讨教学形式来实现教学目标：每次课将涉及品牌营销管理的一个方面，集中探讨某个品牌营销主题。结合主题提出问题、引导学生思考，安排小组做品牌分析报告并课堂汇报、老师点评分析以及全员参与讨论。
Course Description
Brand is closely related to people's life in modern society. People depend on brands to identify products and services, evaluate product quality and price level, express their values and lifestyle, and maintain social networks. In short, the brand has penetrated into people's life. However, what is the brand? What value does the brand give for consumers and for enterprises? Why are some brands unknown, while others attract people? What is the charm of the brand? This course will enable students to understand the power of the brand, understand the leading theories and practices of brand management, build up integrated brand analysis framework, and develop their skills and ability in branding decision-making. This course also will enable students to promote students' critical thinking ability and be familiar with and comply with the moral rules The main contents of this course include brand and brand management, customer based brand equity and brand positioning, customer insight and brand strategy, four steps of brand marketing, growing and sustaining brand equities. This course will combine theory and practice and adopt the participatory teaching method to achieve the teaching objectives.
