课程内容简介：
统计计算是数据科学得以流行的关键，计算思维是其核心，统计学理论是其基石。通过该课程的学习，使学生掌 握统计计算的基本概念、基本理论、常用方法和技能，熟悉统计软件的操作使用，具有进行定量分析所必需的计 算机、软件和算法等知识，为分析研究科研实践中出现的计算问题提供坚实的统计基础和实用的工具帮助。 本课程的主要内容包括：统计软件R介绍和讲解，数值线性代数、优化问题、随机数生成、Bootstrap方法、 Markov Chains Monte Carlo方法等。
Course Description
Statistical computation is the key to the popularity of data science, computational thinking is its core, and statistical theory is its cornerstone. Through the study of this course, students will master the basic concepts, fundamental theories, commonly used methods and skills of statistical computation, become familiar with the operation and use of statistical software, and acquire the knowledge of computers, software, algorithms, etc. that are necessary for quantitative analysis, providing a solid statistical foundation and practical tool support for analyzing and studying computational problems that arise in scientific research practice. The main contents of this course include: introduction and explanation of the statistical software R, numerical linear algebra, optimization, random number generation, the Bootstrap method, the Markov Chains Monte Carlo method, etc.
