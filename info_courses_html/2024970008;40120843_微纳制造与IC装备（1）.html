课程内容简介：
本课程《微纳制造与IC装备（1）》为机械工程方向探索式学习前沿课程，授课对象为已接受两年基础课程和专业基础课程教育的大三秋季学期学生。本课程通过理论教学和专题实践相结合的方式开展，首先介绍微纳制造的内涵、分类、提出背景、发展历程以及应用领域，使学生对机械工程专业的前沿有更为清晰的认知，提高专业认同感，充分激发对微纳制造技术的兴趣；然后系统讲授多种微纳制造工艺原理、工艺流程、工艺参数、应用领域及相应IC制造装备，为解决实际工艺问题、理解IC装备面临的挑战奠定理论基础；最后通过专题实践环节，加深学生对微纳制造工艺和装备的理解，培养学生利用微纳制造技术分析解决问题的能力。 《超精密光栅尺无限速高细分信号处理技术研究》作为《微纳制造与IC装备（1）》课程的重要专题实践，聚焦于超精密光栅位移测量领域中的关键技术挑战。学生将在教师指导下，深入研究高速测量中细分率受限与低速测量中高阶周期非线性误差等实际问题，系统掌握从误差建模、算法设计、MATLAB仿真到FPGA硬件实现的完整工程路径。实践内容涵盖相位恢复算法开发、高阶误差抑制方法设计、软硬件协同优化等，旨在提升学生面向实际工程的分析、解决问题能力。
Course Description
The course of "Micro/nano fabrication and IC equipment (1)" is aiming at the exploring of the frontier in Mechanical Engineering field, which is facing to the third-year undergraduate students, including both the lectures and the lab experiments. This course introduces the general concepts, background, overall developments and applications of micro/nano manufacturing to help students have a clear understanding of the Mechanical Engineering, and inspire the interests in this field. The core of this course includes main micro/nano manufacturing methods with the principle, characteristics, applications and the corresponding IC equipment. Moreover, experiments on special topics will be performed by students in order to assist a better understanding on the processing technologies and equipment but also improve the ability in solving problems in practical micro/nano manufacturing technology. The project of “Research on Unlimited-Speed and High-Subdivision Signal Processing Techniques for Ultra-Precision Grating Scales” is an important topic-based experiment under the course of “Micro/nano fabrication and IC equipment (1)”, focusing on key technical challenges in the field of ultra-precision grating displacement measurement. Under the guidance of instructors, students will explore practical issues such as limited subdivision capability in high-speed measurements and high-order periodic nonlinear errors in low-speed conditions. Through this project, students will systematically develop a complete engineering workflow from error modeling, algorithm design, and MATLAB simulation to FPGA-based hardware implementation. The practice includes phase recovery algorithm development, high-order error suppression techniques, and hardware-software co-optimization, aiming to improve students’ abilities in analyzing and solving problems in real engineering scenarios.
