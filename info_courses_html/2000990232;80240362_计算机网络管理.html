课程简介：
互联网已成为现代社会最重要的基础设施之一，互联网迅猛发展的同时也带来了新的问题和挑战，其中网络管理就是一个重大挑战，保障网络的服务质量、应对网络的安全攻击、维护千万个网络设备和千万条网络线路的健康和畅通是互联网可持续发展的前提。课程将结合大规模互联网的实际运行管理工作，通过案例介绍和分析讨论，展现真实的互联网及其管理问题，学习学术界和产业界对相关问题的看法和解决思路。
英文课程简介：
The Internet has become one of the most important infrastructures in modern society. With the rapid development of the Internet, it has also brought new problems and challenges, among which network management is a major challenge. Ensuring the service quality of the network, responding to network security attacks and maintaining the health and smoothness of thousands of network devices and lines are the prerequisites for the sustainable development of the Internet.Based on the real-world operation and management of large-scale Internet, the course will show the real Internet and its management problems through case study and discussion, and learn the views and solutions of academia and industry on related problems.
