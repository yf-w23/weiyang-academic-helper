课程内容简介：
本课程以老师授课、学生参与讨论为主，将读写、听说等各方面基础技能有机地结合起 来。授 课内容以教材为主，并辅以中国文化、艺术等通识内容的课外教学材料。课程重点：1.基 础阅 读技巧训练着重阅读速度、词汇拓展和中心思想理解，为学生提供提高领悟理解力、词汇 学习 能力的实践机会。2.写作技能重点在于句式结构、主题句以及篇章结构。3.听说技能重点 为词 汇拓展、跨文化交际实际运用以及如何做学术讲座笔记、参加讨论等。
Course Description
Based on teacher's lecture and students'discussion, the course integrates the basic language skills including reading, writing and translating together as an organic whole. The teaching content centers on text books with additional materials of general education on Chinese culture and art.This course focuses on：A. the introduction of basic reading skills with an emphasis on speed, vocabulary expansion, and comprehension of ideas. It provides intensive practice in improving visual and perceptual skills, comprehension skills, and vocabulary acquisition. B. the introduction of basic writing skills with an emphasis on sentence patterns, topic sentence and general structure.C.the introduction of basic listening and speaking skills with an emphasis on vocabulary expansion, cross-cultural context and pragmatic use in general communication for daily life as well as note-taking and discussion in attending lectures.
