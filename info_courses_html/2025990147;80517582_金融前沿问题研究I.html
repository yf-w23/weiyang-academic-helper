课程简介：
这门课程将为学生系统地介绍连续时间企业金融（corporate finance）的相关文献。主要内容包括：不确定性下的投资决策、战略性试验、动态资本结构以及动态契约设计等主题。 在课程结束时，成功完成课程的学生将能够： 掌握求解连续时间企业金融模型所需的数学与编程工具； 理解该领域文献的发展脉络与演进逻辑； 培养识别并评估高质量研究论文的学术品味； 能够将理论结论与实证研究相联系，并识别支持实证分析的合适模型； 将所学概念应用于撰写毕业论文的相关章节.
英文课程简介：
This course provides students with a comprehensive introduction to the continuous-time corporate finance literature. Topics include investment under uncertainty, strategic experimentation, dynamic capital structure, and dynamic contracting. By the end of this course, successful students will be able to: Acquire the mathematical and coding tools necessary to solve continuous-time corporate finance models. Gain insights into how this literature evolves and develops. Cultivate a taste for identifying and evaluating high-quality research papers. Link theoretical implications to empirical findings and identify suitable models to support empirical analysis. Apply the concepts learned to develop chapters of their graduate theses.

进度安排：
Week 01 Introduction and Preliminaries
    Why do we study dynamic corporate models?
    Math preliminaries
    Main readings: Dixit and Pindyck 1994 (Chapter 3-4)
 
Week 02 Investment Under Uncertainty 
    Option value to invest
    Smooth pasting condition
    Entry, exit, lay-up and scrapping
    Exercise time as screening/signaling
    Main readings: McDonald and Siegel 1986, Dixit 1989, Grenadier and Wang 2005, Garlappi et al. 2022, Dixit and Pindyck 1994 (Chapter 4-7)
 
Week 03 Strategic Experimentation
    Multi-armed bandit problems 
    The filtering problem
    Main readings: Bolton and Harris 1999, Keller et al. 2005, Strulovici 2010 
 
Week 04 Capital Structure I
    Trade-off models with commitment
    Main readings: Leland 1994, Leland and Toft 1996, Goldstein Ju, and Leland 2001 
 
Week 05 Capital Structure II
    Trade-off models without commitment
    Commitment mechanisms
    Main readings:  Admati et al. 2019, DeMarzo and He 2021, Benzoni et al. 2022, Malenko and Tsoy 2025, Hu et al. 2025
 
Week 06 Q-theory of Investment and Liquidity Management, Numerical Methods
    Q-theory and liquidity management
    Super contact condition
    Numerical Methods: finite difference, built-in ODE/PDE packages, deep-learning
    Main readings: Bolton, Chen, and Wang 2011, Lecture notes from Ben Moll, Sauzet 2025
 
Week 07 Dynamic Contracting I and Student Presentations
    Dynamic contracting without hidden savings
    The first-order approach
    Main readings: DeMarzo and Sannikov 2006, Biais et al. 2007, He 2009, DeMarzo et al. 2012, Piskorski and Westerfield 2016, Malenko 2019
 
Week 08 Dynamic Contracting II and Student Presentations
    Dynamic contracting with hidden savings
    Why first-order approach does not work
    Main readings: Kocherlakota 2004, He 2012, He 2011, Di Tella and Sannikov 2021
