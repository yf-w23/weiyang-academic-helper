课程名：
社会学研究方法专题－定性研究
学时：
48
学分：
3
课程简介：
通过课程讲授、经典阅读、实际操作等不同方式，深入了解定性研究的基本知识，仔细体会经典研究的内在脉络，能够进行定性研究的实际操作，并完成一个完整的定性研究论文。
英文课程简介：
Through different methods such as course teaching, classic reading, and practical operation, students can gain an in-depth understanding of the basic knowledge of qualitative research, carefully understand the internal context of classic research, and be able to carry out the practical operation of qualitative research and complete a complete qualitative research paper.
