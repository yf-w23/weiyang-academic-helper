课程简介：
该课程侧重介绍在产品预测、生产制造及供应链管理等场景中大数据分析和人工智能技术的应用。课程主要内容包括： -产品需求预测：利用数据分析技术、大模型等人工智能技术，分析客户反馈、使用数据和市场趋势，预测客户需求和市场趋势。 -生产计划与控制：利用统计学习、神经网络、机器视觉、大模型等技术，实现数据驱动的生产决策优化，并实时监控生产数据，如设备状态、生产速度、质量指标等，以便及时调整生产参数，提高效率和质量。 -质量管理：利用历史和实时数据，基于人工智能方法，对产品质量进行监控 -供应链管理：综合利用多种人工智能技术，对供应链数据进行清洗增强，分析供应链数据，优化库存管理，预测需求变化，预警供应链风险，降低供应链成本并提高响应速度。
英文课程简介：
This course focuses on introducing the application of big data analysis and artificial intelligence technologies in scenarios such as product forecasting, manufacturing, and supply chain management. The main contents of the course include: -Product Demand Forecasting: Using data analysis technologies, large models, and other artificial intelligence technologies to analyze customer feedback, usage data, and market trends, so as to predict customer demand and market trends. -Production Planning and Control: Leveraging technologies such as statistical learning, neural networks, machine vision, and large models to achieve data-driven optimization of production decisions, and real-time monitoring of production data (e.g., equipment status, production speed, quality indicators, etc.) to adjust production parameters in a timely manner, improving efficiency and quality. -Quality Management: Using historical and real-time data, based on artificial intelligence methods, to monitor product quality. - Supply Chain Management: Comprehensively applying various artificial intelligence technologies to clean and enhance supply chain data, analyze supply chain data, optimize inventory management, predict demand changes, warn of supply chain risks, reduce supply chain costs, and improve response speed.
