课程简介：
此门课程集中听、说、读、写、译各项语言技能的训练，是一门语言综合课程。本课程包括一系列反映当代中国社会生活和中国民族文化特点的多种题材、体裁、语体、风格的文章，旨在使学生学习内容丰富、语言结构复杂的汉语原文，准确地理解文章的原义和内涵，提高使用汉语的能力。本课程通过词汇扩展、语法讲练、偏误纠正、语篇分析、阅读理解、成段表达等课堂形式，针对学生较难掌握的汉语知识点和技能，展开全面的讲解和训练。
英文课程简介：
This course is open to all international graduate students and is specialised in offering a Chinese Language curriculum at an advanced level. The course focuses on each of the language skills listening, speaking, reading, writing and translation, it is a comprehensive language course. The course includes a series of subjects, genres and literary styles that reflect the characteristics of contemporary Chinese social life and ethnic culture. Its purpose is to enable students to study a diverse range of subjects, rich content and complex language structures in the original Chinese text. In addition, it will help students understand the original meaning and connotations of texts and increase their ability to use Chinese. This course through use of extensive vocabulary, grammar practice, correction of writing errors, analysis of texts, reading comprehension and other classroom exercises aims at comprehensively explaining and training the skills and elements of Chinese Language that students typically find difficult to grasp.
