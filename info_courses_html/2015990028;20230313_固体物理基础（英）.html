课程内容简介：
固体物理是研究固体的微观结构以及组成粒子（原子、离子、电子等）之间相互作用与运动规律的科学，是近代物理学的重要组成部分之一，是包含电子科学与技术在内的诸多学科的基础。本课程运用包括经典力学、电动力学、量子力学和统计力学的基本物理原理，使学生在掌握原子结合、晶体结构、晶格振动、能带理论等固体物理学核心概念和理论的基础上，深刻理解固体的电学、磁学、热学、光学特性，及其在各种器件与系统中的应用。
Course Description
Solid-state physics studies the foundations of our world --- solid materials. We start with the introduction of materials science and crystal structures, and analyse the electronic, optical, thermal and magnetic behaviors of solids, based on theories of classical physics, electrodynamics, quantum and statistical mechanics. Emerging applications such as semiconductor diodes, photonic devices and superconductors will also be briefly covered.
进度安排：

1. Introduction (Week 1)

       - Overview, history, applications

       - Preliminary knowledge

2. Materials and Crystal Structures (Week 2–3)

       - Crystal structures, lattices

       - Reciprocal space, Brillouin zones

       - Materials Characterization: Wave diffraction, the Bragg law

3. Electronic Properties (Week 4–12)

       - Free electrons (the Drude and Sommerfeld models)

       - Bloch's Theorem

       - The near-free electron model, the tight-binding model

       - Electronic band

       - Metals, insulators, semiconductors

       - Devices: junctions, diodes, transistors

4. Thermal Properties (Week 13)

       - Crystal vibration, phonon band

       - Thermal conductivity and capacity

5. Optical Properties (Week 14)

       - Origin of Dielectric constant and Refractive index 

       - Optical absorption, reflection, refraction, emission

6. Magnetic Properties (Week 15)

       - Origin of Magnetics

       - Diamagnetism, Paramagnetism, Ferromagnetism

       - Superconductivity
