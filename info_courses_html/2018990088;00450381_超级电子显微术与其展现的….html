课程内容简介：
“眼见为实”，“一目了然”是科学探索中发现新现象，解答疑团和证明真伪的基本哲学和方法。光学显微 镜和电子显微镜等革命性显微技术被发明后立即投入到生命科学研究，并打开了从未被看见的微观生物世界。 然而在科学史上，它们所揭示的微观生物世界一开始饱受质疑，经历近百年才获得大众认可并奠基分子生物 学。 本课程以电子显微镜和结构生物学互相推动的发展历程为索引，梳理电子显微镜的发展历史，并将百万放 大倍数下魅力无穷的生物世界呈现给学生，展示生物个体、器官、组织、细胞、病毒等复杂生命体系是如何通 过蛋白、核酸、生物膜等基础生命单元的互相协同完成复杂功能的。此外，课程也将讲解新型电子显微技术所 开启的原位结构生物学新篇章，并介绍结构生物学是如何影响疫苗、抗体和药物研发的。 课程安排（每次课2课时）： 第一次课：“眼见为实”对科学探究的影响 第二次课：电子显微术的发展历程 第三次课：电镜简介 第四次课：从生物样本到电镜结构的过程 第五次课：分辨率，样品尺度和原位性 第六次课：电子显微摄影（实验课） 第七次课：百万放大倍数下的生命世界1 第八次课：百万放大倍数下的生命世界2
Course Description
“Seeing is believing,” “it is immediately obvious at a glance” is the fundamental philosophy and method in scientific exploration for discovering new phenomena, resolving doubts, and proving truths and falsities. Revolutionary microscopy technologies such as optical microscopes and electron microscopes were immediately employed in life science research after their invention, opening up an unseen microscopic biological world. However, in the history of science, the microscopic biological world they revealed was initially met with skepticism and took nearly a century to gain widespread recognition, laying the foundation for molecular biology. This course uses the interwoven development of electron microscopy and structural biology as a guide to comb through the history of electron microscopy development. It presents to students the enchanting biological world under millions of magnifications, showcasing how complex life systems such as organisms, organs, tissues, cells, and viruses achieve complex functions through the synergy of basic life units like proteins, nucleic acids, and biomembranes. Additionally, the course will explain the new chapter of in situ structural biology opened by emerging electron microscopy techniques and introduce how structural biology impacts the development of vaccines, antibodies, and drugs.
