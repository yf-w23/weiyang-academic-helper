课程内容简介：
《电磁场与波》是电路、光电子学、微波系统等的核心基础理论，是理解、分析、解决电磁问题的最重要的工具和方法之一。本课程是电子系十门专业核心课之一，将为后续专业核心课和专业选修课打下电路基础。《电磁场与波》的具体内容包括：麦克斯韦方程组、洛伦兹力、矢量分析基本方法、静电场、静磁场、时变电磁场与电磁波的传播、电磁波的辐射。本课程主要阐述电电磁场基本原理，同时提供了包括电路、波导、微波天线、医学及生物学中的电磁测量等相关案例，使得学生对电磁场与波的认识不仅停留在理论上，还能够和实际生活以及科研工作相结合。
Course Description
Electromagnetic field and wave is the theoretical foundation for the studies of electrical circuits, wave optics and optoelectronics, microwave systems, and provides the basic methods and tools for understanding, analyzing, and solving problems involving electromagnetism. The course will introduce vector analysis, Maxwell's equations, Lorentz force, electrostatics and magnetostatics, electrodynamcis, propagation of EM waves, and radiation. Beside basic principles, the course will introduce a number of examples including electrical circuits, optical and RF waveguides, antenna, and electrical measurement in biomedical applications, such that the students can implement the theory to solve real-world problems.
