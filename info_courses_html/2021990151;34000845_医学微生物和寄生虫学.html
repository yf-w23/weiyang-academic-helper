课程内容简介：
病原微生物和寄生虫学是生命科学及医学的一个重要的分支学科，是人类在长期对传染性疾病的病原性质的认识和防治过程中总结出的一门学科，主要研究与人类疾病有关的病原微生物的生物学特性、致病性及免疫性、微生物检查方法、特异性预防和治疗原则等。以及寄生在人体的寄生虫形态、生活史、生理、寄生虫与宿主之间的相互关系、寄生虫病的流行以及防治原则。本课程是医学专业本科生的基础课程，将系统地讲授这些病原学的基础知识。本课程主要的教学形式为课堂讲授、实验课程、病理病例讨论、以及全新的在线预习形式。课堂讲授部分包括病原细菌学、病毒学两大部分。
Course Description
Microbiology and Parasitology are important branches of life science and medicine. They represent a discipline that has evolved over time as a result of humanity's long-term understanding and efforts to combat infectious diseases. These fields primarily focus on the biological characteristics, pathogenicity, immunology, microbial examination methods, specific prevention, and treatment principles related to pathogenic microorganisms associated with human diseases. They also delve into the morphology, life history, physiology, the relationship between parasites and their hosts, the epidemiology of parasitic diseases, and principles of prevention and treatment in the context of parasitic organisms in the human body. This course serves as a foundational subject for undergraduate medical students, systematically imparting fundamental knowledge in these areas. The primary modes of instruction in this course include classroom lectures, laboratory sessions, discussions of pathological cases, and a newly introduced online self-learning format. The classroom lectures are divided into two major parts, Bacteriology and Virology.
