课程内容简介：
相对于过去，突然地，我们发现数据不是太少而是太多，信息不是匮乏而是繁杂，新一代人的重要能力是“鉴别”和“挖掘”。我们将教授生物信息学方面的专业技能，这些技能不是单纯地运行软件的能力，而是让大家拥有探索各种真实数据的自由。 生物信息学是采用数学、统计学、计算机等方法分析生物医学数据的一种交叉性学科。该课程的教学目的是为了让学生对生物信息的基础知识有所了解和掌握，同时让学生能够掌握实用的生物信息学实用技能和编程技术。课程设计是让学生逐步学习生物信息基础知识和练习基础计算机技能，能够对基本的生物信息学技术有所掌握。课程将让学生从了解和练习基本的Linux操作开始，通过生物信息所需的计算语言（如Bash, R, Python等）的编程训练，逐步掌握生物信息学在新一代高通量测序数据上的基本分析方法。 本课程除了理论上的教授，非常重视实践的训练环节，强调学生对生物信息学实际应用的实践学习和上机练习。
Course Description
Compared with the past, suddenly, we find that the data is not too little but too much, the information is not lacking but complicated, and the important ability of the new generation is "discrimination" and "mining". We teach professional skills in bioinformatics. These skills are not just running software. They will give you the freedom of exploring various real data. Bioinformatics is an interdisciplinary subject that analyzes biomedical data using methods such as mathematics, statistics, and computers. The teaching purpose of this course is to give students an understanding and mastery of the basic knowledge of bioinformatics, while enabling students to acquire practical bioinformatics practical skills and programming techniques. The curriculum is designed to allow students to gradually learn the basic knowledge of bioinformatics and practice basic computer skills, so that they can master basic bioinformatics techniques. The course will allow students to understand and practice basic Linux operations, and master the basic analysis methods of bioinformatics on next-generation high-throughput sequencing data through programming training in computing languages required for bioinformatics. In addition to theoretical teaching, this course strengthens and supplements the training links of practical exercises, and emphasizes students' practical learning and computer-based practice of the practical application of bioinformatics, which increases the challenge and practicability of the course.
