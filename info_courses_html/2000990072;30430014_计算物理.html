课程内容简介：
讲授物理学研究中基本的计算机数值模拟方法，讨论其理论基础和实际应用，着重于解决实际物理问题的基本数值方法。目标是使学生对物理学中的主要计算技术有一个全面的了解，并初步具有利用计算机进行数值计算解决复杂体系物理问题的能力。课程的具体内容包括：基本的数值计算方法（数值积分和微分、优化方法、有限差分、常微分方程和偏微分方程）、本征值问题的求解、Monte Carlo方法的理论基础及其在物理学各个领域中的应用、分子动力学方法（包含各种系综）等。
Course Description
This course focuses on theoretical foundation and practical usage of computer numerical simulations with a special emphasis on solving physics problems. After the course, the students are supposed to have a comprehensive understanding of computing techniques in physics and a preliminary capability of solving problems of complex physical systems with them. This course covers basics of numerical calculation methods (numerical integration and differentiation, optimization, finite difference, ordinary and partial differential equations), solution of eigenvalue problem, Monte Carlo method and its usage in different areas of physics, molecular dynamics method under different ensembles, etc.
