课程内容简介：
本课程内容包括三部分：直流电阻电路，动态电路的暂态分析和动态电路的正弦稳态分析。 直流电阻电路部分包括各种典型的电路元件的u-i特性、各种等效变换方法、电阻分析的系统化方法（节点电压法和回路电流法）、线性电路的若干重要定理（叠加、替代、戴维南、诺顿）、非线性电阻电路的分段线性法和小信号分析方法。 动态电路的暂态分析包括一阶电路的经典解法及三要素法、二阶电路的经典解法、高阶电路列写状态方程、冲激响应与卷积积分。 动态电路的正弦稳态分析包括相量法的基本概念及步骤方法、谐振、含互感电路的分析、三相电路和周期非正弦电路的分析方法。 本课程知识点很多，同时体系也很完整清晰，是电类专业的第一门专业基础课，帮助大家学习知识的同时，逐步掌握用所学理论知识分析并解决工程实际问题的能力，并逐渐建立工程思维模式。
Course Description
The course contents include three parts: DC resistive circuits, transient analysis of dynamic circuits, and sinusoidal steady-state analysis of dynamic circuits. The DC resistive circuits part includes the u-i characteristics of various typical circuit elements, various equivalent transformation methods, systematic methods of resistance analysis (node voltage method and mesh current method), several important theorems for linear circuits (superposition, replacement, Thevenin, Norton), the segmental linear method and the small-signal analysis methodfor nonlinear resistive circuits. The transient analysis of dynamic circuits includes the classical solution method and three-element method for first-order circuits, the classical solution method for second-order circuits, the method of writing equations in terms of state variables for high-order circuits, impulse response and convolution integration. The sinusoidal steady-state analysis of dynamic circuits includes the basic concepts and steps of phasor method, resonance, analysis methods of circuits with mutual inductance, three-phase circuits and periodic non-sinusoidal circuits. This course covers a lot of knowledge points, and the system is also complete and clear. It is the first professional foundemental course for electrical engineering majors, helping students learn knowledge while gradually acquiring the ability to analyze and solve engineering practical problems using the theoretical knowledge they have learned, and gradually establishing an engineering thinking mode.
进度安排：

本课程以讲授为主，充分运用雨课程开展混合式教学。课程包含19节理论课（38学时）、8节习题课（16学时）和4节应用课（6学时），有4学时会因各种放假被放掉。

电阻电路部分是全课程的基础，包括9讲理论课、3节习题课、2节应用课，共26学时；

动态电路的暂态分析部分是课程的难点所在，包括4讲理论课、2节习题课、1节应用课，共14学时；

动态电路的正弦稳态分析包括6讲理论课、3节习题课、1节应用课，共20学时。
