课程简介：
本课程共48学时。学习完该门课程，学生将在以下几方面有所进步。 1.学生能掌握拼音的声母、韵母和声调，以及拼音书写的基本规则及使用拼音认读。 2.学生还要学习汉字的基本结构，包括笔画、笔顺、偏旁、部首等，学会如何正确书写汉字，并在此基础上提高书写速度。 3.学习生词及基本语法结构，操练所学生词及语法。 4.学习日常交际话题，并能流利与他人就这些问题进行交流。 5.阅读主要由所学词汇和语法构成的汉语文章，在操练中力争逐步做到能正确理解文章的主要内容。 6.学习运用所学词语书写短文，内容涉及人物介绍、家庭介绍、兴趣爱好等话题。
英文课程简介：
This course has a total of 48 hours. After completing this course, students will make progress in the following areas. 1. Students can master the initial consonants, vowels, and tones of Pinyin, as well as the basic rules of Pinyin writing and the use of Pinyin recognition. 2. Students also need to learn the basic structure of Chinese characters, including stroke, stroke order, radicals, etc., to learn how to write Chinese characters correctly, and to improve writing speed on this basis. 3. Learn new words and basic grammar structures, and practice the students' vocabulary and grammar. 4. Learn daily communication topics and be able to communicate fluently with others on these issues. 5. Read Chinese articles mainly composed of learned vocabulary and grammar, and strive to gradually understand the main content of the article correctly during practice. 6.Learn to use the vocabulary learned to write short essays, covering topics such as character introductions, family introductions, interests, and hobbies.
