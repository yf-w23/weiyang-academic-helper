课程内容简介：
几何学系列课程包括两门：微分几何 I 与微分几何 II。 本课程“微分几何 I”（DG I）介绍微分几何的基本概念与工具，主要内容涵盖： 光滑流形：切丛与向量场；子流形；浸入与淹没；弗罗贝尼乌斯定理。 微分形式：流形上的积分；斯托克斯定理；德·拉姆上同调。 ℝⁿ中子流形的局部与整体理论：第一与第二基本形式；平均曲率；高斯–博内定理。
Course Description
Course Syllabus: Differential Geometry I 1. Course Name: Differential Geometry I Instructor: Zhu Bo 2. Class Time: Monday: 13:30–15:05 Tuesday: 19:20–21:45 3. Course Description: The geometry sequence consists of two courses: Differential Geometry I and Differential Geometry II. This course, Differential Geometry I (DG I), introduces the fundamental concepts and tools of differential geometry. The main topics covered include: Smooth manifolds: Tangent bundles and vector fields; submanifolds; immersions and submersions; the Frobenius theorem. Differential forms: Integration on manifolds; Stokes’ theorem; de Rham cohomology. Local and global theory of submanifolds in ℝⁿ: The first and second fundamental forms; mean curvature; the Gauss–Bonnet theorem. 4. Prerequisites: Students are expected to have a solid background in: Multivariable calculus Linear algebra Basic ordinary differential equations Basic point-set topology 5. Course Textbook References: The course material primarily follows the textbooks listed below. Students are encouraged to consult the instructor for additional references in differential geometry. Berger, Marcel, and Bernard Gostiaux. Differential Geometry: Manifolds, Curves, and Surfaces. Springer, New York, 2012. Dover Publications, 2016. (English e‑edition available.) 陈维桓 (Chen Weihuan). 微分几何引论 (Introduction to Differential Geometry), 2013. Higher Education Press. (This book is more suitable for beginners than Chen’s 微分流形初步.)
