课程内容简介：
《舞蹈审美与创作实践》是面向普通大学生的舞蹈美育课程，既不同于艺术院校强调技能学习的职业舞蹈教育，也并非职业舞蹈教育课程在普通高校的照搬，而是面向普通高校学生打造的“创造性舞蹈”美育课程。以创造力为核心的“创造性舞蹈”课程，以舞蹈作为手段，以象征的方式表达自我，强调创造作为人对自我存在的体验，通过富有创造性的活动不仅能开发人的天资潜能，培育创新能力，同时能推动人内在自我整合、自我发展。
Course Description
We propose to create an aesthetic creation curriculum of quality-oriented dancing for regular college students to develop their artistic education. The class neither copies from vocational dance schools’ curriculum, nor does it present strict rules on technique as they would in these schools. This class is offered and made of novelty for the students who are new to the concept of dance, to elevate the artistic side and thrive to develop harmony in the body, mind, and soul. It is widely known that there is a significant difference between professional dance education and aesthetic education for dancing, and the latter is essentially important for the complete growth as a person. Therefore, while offering a systematic and comprehensive curriculum, the class guides and cultivates students in all levels, especially with morals, creativity, aesthetics, and many more.
