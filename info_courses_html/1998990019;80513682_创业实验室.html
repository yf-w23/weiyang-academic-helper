课程简介：
创业实验室是一门与创业者及其创业企业合作的项目式工商管理实践课程：选修的研究生组成若干个团队,每个团队在创业实践导师的指导下完成一个实际创业项目研究和计划制订。这是一个强调“干中学”的实践课程，参与的学生应当准备花大量的时间于客户调查、专家访谈和产品（服务）改进方案设计等实践活动之中。项目的类型可能各种各样，但典型的项目应当包括调查潜在市场需求、评估产品对目标客户的价值、改进产品设计等等内容。配合项目的进展,该课程还有讲座和课堂讨论。
英文课程简介：
Entrepreneurship Lab is a project-based business management practice course that cooperates with entrepreneurs and their entrepreneurial companies: elective graduate students form several teams, and each team completes a practical entrepreneurial project research and plan formulation under the guidance of an entrepreneurial practice instructor. This is a practical course that emphasizes "learning by doing". Participating students should be prepared to spend a lot of time in practical activities such as customer surveys, expert interviews, and product (service) improvement program design. There may be various types of projects, but a typical project should include investigating potential market needs, evaluating the value of the product to target customers, improving product design, and so on. In line with the progress of the project, the course also includes lectures and classroom discussions.
