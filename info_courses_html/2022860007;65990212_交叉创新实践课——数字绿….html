课程简介：
《数字绿色深圳》课程是一门研究生公共课程，以绿色技术为核心，以数字技术为支撑，以深圳市为载体，结合现代信息和环境工程技术，研究解决现实社会问题的技术方案，开展科学研究和实践应用。本课程主要包括以下方面的内容：第一，介绍数字绿色概念和基本背景，结合深圳市现实需求分析和探讨数字绿色技术的发展趋势；第二，介绍数字绿色技术的基本原理和平台技术，探讨数字技术在绿色工程中的应用，如虚拟仿真、数值模拟和算法、计算机辅助设计等；第三，结合数字绿色前沿专题讲座和实际应用案例，探讨绿色技术在深圳市环境、双碳、建筑、能源、交通等领域的发展趋势，培养学生的创新思维和创新能力；最后，通过学科交叉和创新性的训练，将其专业知识背景和交叉研究兴趣有机融合，通过实践实验，提升学生综合运用数字绿色技术解决实际问题的能力。本课程以学科交叉和创新性为特色，是一门具有重要意义的研究生课程。
英文课程简介：
The course "Digital Green Shenzhen" is a postgraduate public course combined with modern information technology and environmental engineering technology. This course will integrate green technology as the core and digital technology as support, and will be conducted exclusively in the area of Shenzhen. This course is aimed at exploring technical solutions to solve real social problems, to carry out scientific research closely related with practical application. This course mainly includes the following aspects: First, introduce the basic background and concepts of digital green technology, analyze and discuss the development trend of green technology considering practical needs with respect to Shenzhen City; second, introduce the basic principles and platform technologies of digital green technology, and discuss in depth the application of “digi-tech” in green technology, such as virtual simulation, numerical simulation and algorithm, computer-aided design, etc.; thirdly, cover a series of frontier lectures related with “digital-green” and practical application cases, discussing the progresses in application of green technology for Shenzhen City, in the fields of environment, “double-carbon target”, energy, architecture, cultivate students' innovative thinking and innovative ability; finally, through interdisciplinary and innovative training, integrate their professional knowledge background and interdisciplinary research interests with practical experiments. Improve students' comprehensive use of digital green technology ability to solve practical problems. This course is characterized by interdisciplinary and innovative features, and is a postgraduate course of great significance.
