Course Description
Dear international students, it is strongly recommended that you only enroll in this course if you can independently understand the Chinese instructions above ————————————————————————— In the era of information explosion and false news, what is the most valuable ability? Critical thinking! Critical thinking means not being credulous and not following blindly, which is the "independent thinking" advocated by many experts and scholars. It helps us keenly detect the suspiciousness of certain information and "knowledge", and there are ways to verify those unreliable information and improve those flawed "knowledge". Moreover, unlike some people's understanding of critical thinking, we are not only good at questioning and discovering problems, but also able to provide better solutions to solve problems. During the university study period, no matter what courses and lectures you listen to, critical thinking can help you grasp the key links and ask high-level questions. At the same time, he can perform well in various research activities and assignments. After entering the workplace in the future, critical thinking can help you show your ability and wisdom in various situations. Let you dare to accept new tasks and challenges that others dare not face. Because your mind is full of weapons for analyzing and solving problems, and you know how to use them to deal with new problems. Which professions are most in need of critical thinking? Excellent leaders, decision makers, excellent doctors and legal professionals, excellent media professionals, experts and scholars, and ordinary people. For others, critical thinking helps us identify the various methods of sophists and manipulator, and identify rumors and fake news. Discover the truth about complex events. It can also help us make wise choices when making various decisions and avoid paying IQ taxes. If you are ready, let us start this wonderful journey of exploring the world with thinking.
进度安排：

一、如何判断经验性陈述的可靠性：A“事实-观点”区分、B复杂陈述的真伪判断

二、如何扫除判断陈述真伪的障碍：识别和澄清模糊词语、识别“假新闻”的基础练习

三、如何识别语言陷阱：通过了解语义和语用预设

四、如何做到逻辑清晰——首先消除定义和分类中的常见谬误

五、如何识别和消除归纳推理中的谬误

六、如何识别和消除类比论证时的谬误、如何公正的评价？

七、如何识别演绎推理的无效形式？如何避免演绎时最常见的偏差？

八、问题解决过程中常见的谬误和应对方法

九、如何避免使用论证方法时的常见谬误？如何改进基础的演绎论证(图尔敏模型)?

十、如何识别和消除复杂论证中的偏差(图尔敏模型的运用)

十一、决策时常见谬误和应对方法

十二、如何识别和消除论证中形形色色的非正当理由和操纵手段

十三、如何识别和消除1因果解释中的几种谬误、2 “把相关当因果谬误”的解决之道

十四、如何在使用“穆勒五法”提出原因假设时避免谬误？

十五、A社会生活领域提出原因假设的思路和归因偏误的应对方法。B科学研究中提出原因假设的思路、筛选和佐证假设的方法。

十六、（补充）统计方法的相关谬误——各类取样偏差、以及来自被试、研究者的心理谬误
