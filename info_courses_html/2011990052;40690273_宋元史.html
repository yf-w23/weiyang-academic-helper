课程内容简介：
宋元史在中国历史上占有独特的地位，唐宋时期的社会转型至宋代已经基本完成，中国的历史发展呈现出一个新的面貌。与此同时，几个政权的林立与对峙又使这一时期的政治、军事及思想文化具有多元化的色彩，特别是这一时期的制度与文化发展对于明清历史有深远的影响，而蒙古最后的统一又使中国历史的大一统格局发展到一个新的阶段。本课在充分吸收学术界的相关研究基础上，对于宋元历史的发展变化予以揭示，从而丰富学生对于相关历史的认识，为其从事进一步的研究打好基础。
Course Description
The period from the Song to the Yuan dynasties is unique in Chinese history. The social transformation, which initiated during the Tang dynasty, had totally restructured Chinese society as a whole. The coexistence and confrontation of different regimes led to the more eclectic nature of politics, military affairs, and culture at the time. The institutional and cultural development of this period had profound influence on the Ming and Qing dynasties, and the eventual reunification by the Mongol brought China into a new phase. With a solid basis in relevant scholarship, the course reveals the development and change of the Song-Yuan period. The goal is to enrich students’ understanding of the Song and Yuan China and to prepare them for more advanced study.
