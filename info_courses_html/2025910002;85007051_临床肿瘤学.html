课程简介：
《临床肿瘤学》是研究恶性实体瘤（癌症）发生、进展规律、诊断预测技术、整合治疗措施、预后预防策略的实践医学。本课程作为清华大学培养“卓越型医师”的专业课程，重点讲解临床肿瘤学的基本生物学原理、核心防治技术和代表性疾病。 临床肿瘤学的核心科学原理是肿瘤生物学，以洞悉各种实体瘤临床病理过程的基本规律。临床肿瘤学的主要诊断技术措施是基于临床病史和症状体征的实验室检查和影像学检查，以实现肿瘤的定性、定位和定量诊断。临床肿瘤学的主要治疗原则是“以手术为核心的整合治疗”，以此为纲，一体化设计手术治疗、放射治疗、化学治疗、靶向治疗、免疫治疗、巩固康复等一系列技术措施，目的是在实现整体状态稳定的前提下，有效控制或消灭原发肿瘤，显著提高治愈率，维持、改善和提高生活质量，恢复肿瘤病人的自我职能、家庭职能、社会职能，实现以病人为中心的全生命周期健康管理和生活提高。 在上述基础上，本课程以我国最常见的实体瘤为经典案例，重点讲解呼吸系统、消化系统、泌尿生殖系统、内分泌系统最常见恶性肿瘤，通过PBL教学法，使学生掌握这类肿瘤的治疗原则和方法。
英文课程简介：
Clinical Oncology is a branch of practical medicine to study the biological principles underlying cancer initiation and promotion, to develop cancer diagnosis and prediction technologies, to devise integrated therapeutic approaches, and to implement cancer prognosis and prevention strategies. As a common clinical course designed for “The Outstanding Clinician Scholar Program” at Tsinghua University, Clinical Oncology focuses on the fundamental cancer biology, key prevention and treatment technologies, and typical cancers. The key scientific rationale underlying Clinical Oncology is tumor biology. Major diagnostic technologies are tailored laboratory and/or medical imaging examinations based the medical history, clinical signs and symptoms, in order to make accurate cancer diagnosis with precise location, quality and quantity. The cardinal therapeutic principle in Clinical Oncology is the “Integrated Treatment with Surgery as the Core”, which requires top-level design of holistic treatment strategies integrating surgery, radiotherapy, chemotherapy, targeted therapy, immunotherapy, rehabilitation, and consolidation therapies. The goal of such integrated treatment is, on the condition of maintaining the physiological and psychological well-being of whole body, to effectively control and eliminate the primary tumor, to increase the cure rate, to maintain, improve and enhance the quality of life, and ultimately realize patient-centered whole-life health management and life improvement. Building on these foundations, this course focuses on the principles of diagnosis and treatment for malignant tumors. Using comprehensive, surgery-centered management of common solid tumors and rare, complex metastatic tumors as the main teaching cases, and combining problem-based learning (PBL) with hands-on clinical practice, the course enables students to master the core principles and practical methods for managing these types of cancers.
