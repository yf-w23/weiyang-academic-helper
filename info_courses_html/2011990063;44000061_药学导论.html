课程内容简介：
本课程主要对药学学科、药学内容及药学规律加以介绍和讨论，并围绕多样性药物“安全、有效、可控”的特点，设置如下主要内容进行教学，包括：1）药学开篇：介绍药学学科的基本内容，发展历史、学科范畴、基本概念、基本研究方法、主要成就以及尚未解决的问题；2）药物药理和安全：介绍抗体药物、化学药物药理学和药代动力学，并探讨药物安全性对药物的影响以及前沿性问题和热点；3）药物药学：介绍药物化学、药物制剂以及药物质量控制的学科内容及前沿问题和热点；4）药物实践：实验观察药物研究的基本过程；5）药物实例：介绍初学者较为熟知药物的研发过程，及其成功的关键内容。 药学学科的特点是多学科交叉，对从事相关研究的人具有极高的挑战性。课程教学内容设计由浅入深，并对当前最热门的药物加以介绍。通过本课程的学习，学生可以对药物研究以及相关学科加以初步的了解。课程采取课堂教学与讲座相结合的形式进行，并辅以相关实例的讨论，启发学生对药学的认识和兴趣。通过良性互动，使同学们最大限度地参与教学并取得收获。
Course Description
This course provides an in-depth introduction and discussion of the discipline of pharmaceutical sciences, its content, and governing principles. Based on the key characteristics of diverse drugs being "safe, effective, and controllable," the following core components are included in the curriculum: 1) Introduction to Pharmaceutical sciences covers the fundamental aspects, historical development, disciplinary scope, basic concepts, research methodologies, major accomplishments, and unresolved challenges within the field of pharmacy; 2) Drug Pharmacology and Safety explores the pharmacological properties and pharmacokinetics of antibody-based and chemical drugs while addressing drug safety considerations, as well as emerging issues and current trends; 3) Pharmaceutical Sciences delves into the disciplines of medicinal chemistry, pharmaceutical formulation, and quality control, along with their frontier topics and hotspots; 4) Practical Drug Research: Students will observe the foundational processes of drug research through experimental observation; 5) Case Studies: This section examines the development process of well-known drugs familiar to beginners, highlighting the critical factors contributing to their success. A defining feature of the pharmaceutical discipline is its interdisciplinary nature, which presents significant challenges for researchers in this domain. The course content is structured progressively, from foundational concepts to advanced topics, with a focus on introducing currently trending drugs. Through this course, students will gain a foundational understanding of drug research and related disciplines. The teaching approach combines classroom instruction with specialized lectures, supplemented by case discussions designed to stimulate students' interest and comprehension of pharmaceutical sciences. Active interaction and discussion between instructors and students ensure maximum engagement and learning outcomes.
