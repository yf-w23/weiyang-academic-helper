课程内容简介：
《物理化学2》主要讲授统计热力学、化学动力学、电化学、表面和胶体化学。本部分将从统计热力学和化学动力学的微观本质出发，将化学热力学和化学动力学以及物质传递的知识相结合，介绍物理化学在表面、胶体和电化学等体系中的应用。《物理化学2》的主要内容包括： 1. 分子动力学和统计热力学原理 2. 化学动力学理论 3. 化学动力学应用 4. 表面化学 5. 胶体化学 6. 电化学。通过上述内容学习，本科生重点掌握统计热力学、化学动力学、表面和胶体化学、电化学的基本概念、重要理论、研究方法和实际应用。上述内容将利用48个课内学时进行课堂讲授，学生需要利用96个课外学时完成复习相关概念的理解与习题练习。
Course Description
Physical Chemistry 2 teaches statistical thermodynamics, chemical kinetics, surface and colloid chemistry, and electrochemistry. Starting from the microscopic nature of statistical thermodynamics and chemical kinetics, this part will combine the knowledge of chemical thermodynamics and chemical kinetics and material transport, and introduce the application of physical chemistry in surface, colloidal and electrochemical systems. The main contents of "Physical Chemistry 2" include: 1. Principles of Molecular Dynamics and Statistical Thermodynamics 2. Chemical Kinetics Theory 3. Chemical kinetics applications 4. Surface Chemistry 5. Colloid Chemistry 6. Electrochemistry. Through the above content, undergraduates focus on mastering the basic concepts, important theories, research methods and practical applications of statistical thermodynamics, chemical kinetics, surface and colloid chemistry, and electrochemistry. The above content will be taught in class using 48 in-class hours, and students will need to use 96 extracurricular hours to complete the understanding and exercise of reviewing related concepts.
