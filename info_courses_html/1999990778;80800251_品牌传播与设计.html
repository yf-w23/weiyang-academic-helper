课程简介：
这是一个信息化的时代，市场营销与大众消费趋向以及信息传播方式都发生了很大的变化，基于品牌传播定位下的品牌视觉计划和设计就显得更加重要。为品牌传播、市场营销而进行的视觉开发设计较之以前更加强调品牌定位对设计计划的引领，使得视觉设计符合传播的需求，并通过有效的传播，建立品牌传播的符号化传播系统，最终构筑和提升品牌的形象价值。因此，品牌定位先行下的视觉计划设计是当今品牌传播设计的有效设计方法，也是目前世界品牌设计比较推崇的设计开发程序。 课程内容包括：品牌认知、品牌定位、品牌传播、整合营销传播、品牌视觉计划、品牌设计体验。
英文课程简介：
In the era of informationization, marketing and mass consumption tendency, and great changes have taken place in the information dissemination way. Brand vision planning and design based on brand communication positioning is even more important. The visual development design for brand communication and marketing emphasizes the lead of brand positioning on the design plan, which makes the visual design meet the needs of communication, and through effective communication, establishes the symbolic communication system of brand communication, and finally constructs and enhance the image value of the brand. Therefore, the visual planning design under the brand positioning first is an effective design method for brand communication design, and it is also a design development program that is highly respected by the world brand design. Course content includes: brand recognition, brand positioning, brand communication, integrated marketing communication, brand vision planning, and brand design experience.
