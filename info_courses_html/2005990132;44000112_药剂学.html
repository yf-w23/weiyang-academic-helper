课程内容简介：
本课程为清华大学精品课、课程思政示范课。 药剂学是研究药物制剂的基本理论、配方设计、制备工艺、质量控制与合理用药以及新型药物递送系统的理论与技术的综合性技术科学，是药学专业核心专业课程之一。 本课程的教学目的和任务：学生通过学习，（1）掌握药物制剂的基本理论，制剂处方设计，各种剂型的原理与制备方法及质量控制，以及新型药物递送系统的新理论与新技术等；（2）了解各剂型相关新理念、新材料、新技术的国内外前沿进展，明确相关领域研究热点与挑战。通过专业素养、科学精神与职业责任的培养，为今后从事药物制剂学理论研究、新药开发、生产技术管理、质量控制、政策监管以及交叉性研究等打下坚实的基础。
Course Description
Pharmaceutics is one of the core pharmaceutical disciplines that deals with all facets of the basic theory, dosage form design, manufacure, quality control, rational clinical application as well as novel drug delivery systems. The purpose and task of this course: through the study, students can (1) master the basic theoretical knowledge, formulation design, manufacure and quality control of different dosage forms, as well as novel theories and technologies of drug delivery systems, and (2) understand the latest developments and frontiers in nover theories, materials, and technologies related to different dosage forms both domestically and internationally, and know the current research hotspots and challenges in related fields. Based on the cultivation of professional qualities, scientific spirit and professional responsibility, this course can lay a solid foundation for the students for future research in dosage form theory, new drug development, manufacture technology, product quality control, policy supervision, and interdisciplinary research.
