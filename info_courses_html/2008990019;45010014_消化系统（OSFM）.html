课程内容简介：
《消化系统(OSFM)》课程整合了消化系统,包括消化管（食管、胃、肠）及消化腺（肝胆 胰）的大体解剖结构和位置，正常组织结构，生理功能，病理结构及病理生理机制，药物治 疗及与临床疾病之间的相互关系等医学知识。涵盖解剖学、组织胚胎学、生理学、病理生理 学、 病理学、和药理学中与消化系统相关部分的基础医学内容，同时与基础医学总论衔 接，将临床病例导入教学模式，融合PBL教学手段和以器官为中心教学模式相结合、课堂教 学与自主学习相结合、理论教学与实验教学相结合，提高医学生综合思维能力、沟通交流能 力、动手操作能力和解决问题能力，为临床学科的学习打下坚实基础。
Course Description
The Digestive System (OSFM) course integrates medical knowledge of the digestive system, including digestive tract (esophagus, stomach, intestine) and digestive glands (liver, gallbladder, pancreas), their anatomical structures and positions, normal tissue structure, physiological functions, pathological structures and pathophysiological mechanisms, pathogen infections and pathogenic mechanisms, medication therapies, and their interrelations with clinical diseases. It covers basic medical content related to the digestive system in anatomy, histology and embryology, physiology, pathophysiology, pathology, and pharmacology, while also connecting with general medical principles. Clinical cases are incorporated into the teaching methodology, combining Problem-Based Learning (PBL) approaches with an organ-centered teaching model, integrating classroom instruction with self-directed learning, as well as theoretical and experimental teaching. The course aims to enhance medical students' comprehensive thinking abilities, communication skills, practical skills, and problem-solving abilities, laying a solid foundation for the study of clinical disciplines.
