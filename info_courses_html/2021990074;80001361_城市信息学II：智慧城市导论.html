课程简介：
本课程以城市信息学为理论基础，介绍了全球智慧城市的发展脉络，相关理论以及当前城市数据计算和数据科学分析在城市规划、设计、治理中的应用实践。讲座介绍了城市信息学在解决城市问题的原理、技术、应用以及城市数据计算的独特性，例如多源而异构的数据类型、城市数据如何驱动决策，公众参与以及涉及数据伦理、公平和隐私的社会技术考量因素。在理论和案例讲座之后，学生将由设计、规划、科技等多维角度完成一个智慧城市案例的分析研究，探讨如何利用城市科技产生更加积极的社会影响。
英文课程简介：
This course builds upon the foundation of urban information （Urban Informatics I: Applied Urban Analytics）and introduces the concept and development of smart cities in a global context. With an introduction of major theories related to urban systems and urban data science, the course first overview the rich urban data landscape and various applications utilizing data for better urban planning, design, and governance. The following lectures introduce the principles, technologies, applications of urban informatics in solving urban problems, as well as the uniqueness of urban data computing, such as multi-source and heterogeneous data types, data-driven decision-making, public participation, and social-technical considerations involving fairness, privacy, and ethical controversies. After the lectures and in-class practice, students will complete a smart city case analysis as a research project from the multi-dimensional perspectives of design, planning, and technology. Ultimately, students will gain training in data-driven problem-solving skills and develop critical thinking capacity by exploring how to use urban technology to produce more positive impacts in cities.

进度安排：

1. 智慧城市的历史演进 (THE EVOLUTION OF SMART CITIES)

2. 城市系统的监测与量化 (INSTRUMENTATION OF URBAN SYSTEMS)

3. 城市作为网络社会系统 (CITIES AS CYBER-SOCIAL SYSTEMS)

4. 数据驱动的城市决策和运营 (DATA-DRIVEN URBAN DECISION-MAKING AND OPERATIONS)

5. 智慧城市与当前热点议题 (HOT TOPICS IN SMART CITIES)

6. 智慧城市中的公众互动 (PUBLIC ENGAGEMENT IN SMART CITIES) 

7. 城市数据应用中的冲突和争议 (CONFLICTS AND CONTROVERSIES OF URBAN DATA APPLICATION)

8. 案例汇报与讨论讲评（CASE STUDY PRESENTATION & DISCUSSION）
