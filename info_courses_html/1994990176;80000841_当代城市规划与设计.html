课程简介：
在城市社会背景下，本课程旨在通过系列讲座和讨论，运用比较研究和案例研究方法，从中国和世界两个视角，论述当代城市发展和城市规划的发展趋势与核心议题，帮助学生建立起对于城市化发展、城市规划学科和城市规划实践的系统认知。
英文课程简介：
This course aims at, through lectures and discussion, as well as comparative approach and case studies, helping the students to build up the knowledge on the urbanization development of China and the world, the academic discipline of urban planning and design, and the theories and practice of urban planning and design in the contemporary time, with particular focus on the urban development of China. It is composed of seven themed lectures and one seminar discussion. The seven lectures will concern the following aspects: 1) City development and urbanization 2) From strategies to action: The role of urban planning 3) Beijing metropolitan development: Integrity of history and modernity 4) New town development: A tool of planned urban growth 5) Rural development and planning in China 6) Build the city on the city: Sustainable urban growth through brownfield redevelopment 7) Cultural industry and urban development The seminar discussion will focus on the topic of how to shape a city or a metropolis through planning and design.
