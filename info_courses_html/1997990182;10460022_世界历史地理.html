课程内容简介：
历史地理学是现代地理学的一个组成部分，其主要研究对象是人类历史时期地理环境的变化，这种变化主要是由于人的活动和影响而产生的。《世界历史地理》着重研究世界人类历史上的地理现象及其演变规律。它研究的是历史上的世界地理，即从人类旧石器文化至近现代几千年间全球的地理状况。本课程详细梳理了人类发展史上重要的古代文明疆域界限的地理变化、经济地理演变、古代文明间的文化与经济交流、世界古代和近现代军事与交通地理、世界历史时期的气候演变与植被分布、世界人口与移民等内容。本课程阐明了当前世界主要国家地理环境的形成和特点，注重引导学生寻找人类文明历史地理发展演变的规律。
Course Description
Historical geography is an important part of modern geography. Its main research object is the change of geographical environment in human history period, which is mainly caused by human activities and its influences. This course focuses on the study of geographical phenomena and their evolution in the world's human history. This course makes a detailed analysis of the geographical changes in the boundaries of ancient civilizations, the evolution of economic geography, the cultural and economic exchanges among ancient civilizations, the geography of military and transportation in ancient and modern times, the climate evolution and vegetation distribution in world history, and the world population and migration. This course guides students to look for the laws of the evolution of human civilization, history and geography, and to clarify the formation and characteristics of the geographical environment of the major countries in the world.
