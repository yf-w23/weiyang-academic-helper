课程内容简介：
本课程是代表学科特点的核心专业基础课，面向对象是建筑环境与能源应用工程专业本科生。内容涉及热学、流体力学、物理学、心理学、生理学、劳动卫生学、城市气象学、房屋建筑学、建筑物理等学科知识，展示了人工环境的内在特征、变化规律与理论知识，反映了本学科的跨学科特点。本课程介绍了建筑外环境、室内热湿环境、室内空气质量环境、空气流动，以及声、光环境，同时还从人的生理和心理角度出发，分析介绍了人的健康舒适要求与室内、外环境质量的关系，为创造适宜的建筑室内环境与室外微环境提供理论依据。通过学习《建筑环境学》，我们要完成这样的任务：（1）了解人类生活和生产过程需要什么样的室内、外环境；（2）了解各种内外部因素是如何影响人工环境的；（3）掌握改变或控制人工环境的基本方法和原理。
Course Description
Built Environment is a core basic theoretical course for the undergraduate students majoring in Built Environment and Building Energy Engineering (HVAC or Building Services Engineering). The content covers several fields include thermal science, hydrodynamics, physics, psychology, physiology, labor hygiene, urban meteorology, architecture, building physics, and so on. It describes the inherent characteristics, change rules and theoretical knowledge of the built environment, and is an interdisciplinary subject. This course introduces the outdoor environment, indoor thermal environment, indoor air quality, indoor air flow, acoustic and luminous environment. From the physical and psychological point of view, relationship between occupant's demand on health-comfort and the indoor/outdoor environment quality is discussed. It is important theory basis for creating suitable built environment. The purpose of this course is to help the students (1) to understand what indoor or outdoor environments are needed for human healthy/comfortable life as well as the production processes; (2) to understand how various internal and external factors affect the built environment; (3) to master the basic methods and principles of changing or controlling the built environment.
