课程内容简介：
这门课通过阅读代表性的小说和相关研究性文章，介绍小说这一体裁的历史、流派、不同时期的特点、思想特征，并介绍分析小说的基本分析方法。阅读的作品集中在18-20世纪，涵盖成长小说、现实主义、自然主义、现代主义等基本流派，我们将通过阅读不同时期的小说文本，对其历史语境、美学特征和思想内涵有一个基本的把握，并结合相关的批评性文章，初步理解叙事学分析、女性主义、结构主义、后殖民主义、文化唯物主义等分析方法。
Course Description
This course aims to introduce students to the history, styles, characteristics of fictions and the critical view of how to read a fiction. We will read representative fictions in 18-20th century, covering the major schools of Bildungsroman, realism, naturalism, modernism. Through reading the fictional text, we will get a grasp of the historical context, aesthetic characteristics and political implications of these novels. We will also read some criticism and theories, and get a basic idea of different schools of criticism, such as narratology, feminist, structuralist, post-colonial and Marxist criticism.
