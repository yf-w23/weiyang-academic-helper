课程简介：
计算机辅助几何造型有着广泛而重要的应用，是当前卫星、火箭、航天飞船、轮船、汽车等重要设备和装备设计必不可少的基础手段。本课程讲解面向制造业的工业级计算机辅助几何造型基础和前沿理论与技术，主要包括：参数曲线曲面的微分几何特性、参数曲线曲面设计、参数曲线曲面质量评价、等距曲线曲面、曲线与曲面的重新参数化、在曲面上的曲线设计、裁剪参数曲线与曲面、实体表示、面向几何造型的方程和方程组求解、曲线曲面距离计算、曲线曲面求交、实体布尔运算、几何约束求解、特征造型、基于约束构造的几何造型等内容，引导学生掌握现有知识，并进行前沿探索。本课程力图使得软件理论、软件工程、算法设计、高等数学、高等代数、微分几何等多学科在计算机辅助几何造型层面得到交融与统一，引导学生展开计算机辅助几何造型创新实践。通过本课程的学习，希望学生能够掌握编写计算机辅助几何造型程序，通过计算机辅助几何造型解决现实问题，构筑和优化解决方案，了解计算机辅助几何造型前沿动态，掌握计算机辅助几何造型基本研究技巧，甚至展开创新实践，推动计算机辅助几何造型的发展。
英文课程简介：
Computer aided geometric modeling has a lot of comprehensive and important applications. The current equipments for satellites, rockets, space vehicles, ships, and automobiles cannot be designed with the tools of computer aided geometric modeling. This course introduces the fundamental and advanced theories and technologies of computer aided geometric modeling for manufacturing industry, mainly including differential geometric properties of the parametric curves and surfaces, designing parametric curves and surfaces, quality evaluation of parametric curves and surfaces, offset of curves and surfaces, reparameterization of curves and surfaces, designing curves on the surface, trimmed parametric curves and surfaces, representation of solid bodies, solving equation or equations for geometric modeling, curve and surface distance computation, curves and surfaces intersection, Boolean operations of solid bodies, solving geometric constraints, feature modeling. Hope that the students are able to understand the knowledge and carry out some advanced research. Some other courses such as software theories, software engineering, algorithm design, mathematics, algebra and differential geometry will be integrated into computer aided geometric modeling. Thus, the students will make some practice to achieve some innovation in computer aided geometric modeling. In summary, via this course, the students are expected able to write computer aided geometric modelling programs to solve some practical problems, or to optimize some solutions. Anyway, the students will have chances to master the basic research skills, to catch the advanced knowledge, and to carry out some research, which may promote the development of the computer aided geometric modeling.
