课程内容简介：
通过专题讲授、史料分析结合的方式讲授秦汉时期的基本情况，包括史料、研究状况、自然环境、人口、聚落；政治发展、对外关系、主要制度、经济、统治思想与儒学、祭祀、信仰与观念、社会的构成、日常生活等等，帮助同学对早期帝国的形态与运作获得比较系统的认识，为进一步把握中国王朝时期的历史奠定基础。同时，也希望同学们能透过这些往事，思考中国与世界，思考现在与未来。
Course Description
Through thematic lectures and historical analysis, students are taught the basic facts of the Qin and Han dynasties, including historical materials, the state of research, the natural environment, population and settlements of the time; the threads of political development, foreign relations, major institutions, economy, ruling ideology and Confucianism, rituals, beliefs and concepts, the composition of society, and daily life, to help students gain a more systematic understanding of the shape and functioning of the early empire, the economy and ideology, and the life of the people, and to lay the foundation for further grasp of the history of the Chinese dynastic period.
