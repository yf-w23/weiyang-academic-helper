课程内容简介：
现代世界经济繁荣的原因和动力何在？人人自利的市场交换是否必然导致道德退步？物质丰裕的商业社会必然是一个幸福的社会吗？这门课通过文本精读、课堂讲授与小班讨论相结合的方式，带领学生走入亚当·斯密的思想世界。斯密通常被看作“经济学之父”和自由放任经济政策的先驱，但这些刻板印象并不准确，因为它们忽视了斯密对道德哲学、政治理论、社会理论、自然法理学等领域的重大贡献。在这门课上，我们将在十八世纪的语境中细致研读斯密的文本，发现一个真实、复杂而有趣的斯密，认识斯密对人文学科与社会科学的贡献，并思考斯密思想与当代社会的关联。
Course Description
What are the causes and dynamics of modern economic prosperity? Does self-interested market exchange necessarily result in moral degradation? Is our materially abundant commercial society necessarily a happy society? Through a combination of close reading, lectures, and seminars, this course will introduce students to Adam Smith’s intellectual world. Although Smith is known as “the father of economics” and a pioneer of laissez-faire policy, these stereotypical depictions are inaccurate as they neglect Smith’s profound contributions to many other fields, not least moral philosophy, political theory, social theory, and natural jurisprudence. Throughout this course, we will peruse Smith’s texts in its eighteenth-century context, aiming to uncover a real, complex, and interesting Adam Smith, understand his contributions to humanities and social sciences, and think about how Smith’s ideas remain relevant in our own time.
