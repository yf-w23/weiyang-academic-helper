课程简介：
本课程以最小二乘法、神经网络等为基础，系统地介绍线性或非线性系统模型辩识和参数估计中的有关理论问题及实际应用方法。主要内容包括：动态系统的建模方法，最小二乘法理论和方法实现，参数估计方法，非线性系统辩识方法等。 课程中还安排一次课程实践，结合课题研究进行系统辨识及参数估计的案例实践，完成实验和数据分析，并撰写小论文。
英文课程简介：
This course mainly teaches students in the concept of least square algorithm and its extending methodology. The theoretic problems and practical methods for carrying out the system identification are mainly concerned. The main contents of this course are comprised of: modeling of linear dynamic systems, theory and method of least square algorithm, parameter approximation, nonlinear system identification, and etc. Lecture, class discussion, literature reading and practice are integrated to give students a versatile experience.
