课程简介：
《脑影像数据分析》课程旨在为学生提供全面的脑影像数据处理与分析的理论基础和实践技能。课程涵盖功能磁共振成像（fMRI）、结构磁共振成像（sMRI）、弥散加权成像（DWI/DTI）等多种脑影像技术的原理与应用。学生将深入学习数据预处理、统计分析、皮层分析、白质纤维重建、脑网络分析等关键内容，并通过严超赣教授自主开发的DPARSF、DPABI、DPABISurf、DPABIFiber、DPABINet等常用工具进行实际操作训练。此外，课程还涉及多中心大数据的标准化分析（DPABIHarmonization）与脑影像深度学习（BrainImageNet），帮助学生掌握大规模数据集的处理与分析技能。通过理论讲授与实操结合，学生将具备独立开展脑影像数据分析的能力，并在课程结束时完成项目汇报考核，展示学习成果。
英文课程简介：
The course "Brain Imaging Data Analysis" aims to provide students with a comprehensive foundation in both the theoretical and practical aspects of brain imaging data processing and analysis. The course covers the principles and applications of various brain imaging techniques, including functional magnetic resonance imaging (fMRI), structural magnetic resonance imaging (sMRI), and diffusion-weighted imaging (DWI/DTI). Students will delve into essential topics such as data preprocessing, statistical analysis, cortical analysis, white matter fiber reconstruction, and brain network analysis. Practical training will be conducted using commonly used tools developed by the instructor, including DPARSF, DPABI, DPABISurf, DPABIFiber, and DPABINet. In addition, the course addresses standardized analysis of multi-center big data (DPABI Harmonization) and deep learning in brain imaging (BrainImageNet), helping students master the skills necessary for handling and analyzing large-scale datasets. Through a combination of theoretical lectures and hands-on practice, students will develop the ability to independently conduct brain imaging data analysis, culminating in a project report assessment at the end of the course to showcase their learning achievements.
