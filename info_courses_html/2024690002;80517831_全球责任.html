课程简介：
在本课程结束时，我们希望共同实现以下目标： 1. 全球责任的基础：我们应该学会理解和尊重全球负责任领导力的原则和伦理。我们将增强对环境保护、社会正义和经济可持续性的意识。 2. 伦理决策和道德领导：我们将了解如何在复杂的全球商业环境和数字技术时代中做出伦理和道德决策。这包括理解和处理伦理困境，以及指导组织维持高标准的伦理和正直。 3. 社会影响、包容性和参与：我们将学会评估和制定商业运营的社会影响战略，专注于创造对社会和环境有益的价值。 4. 多样性、公平性和包容性：我们将深入了解工作场所中多样性、公平性和包容性的重要性，以及这些原则如何有助于创建更加投入和高效的工作力量。 5. 环境管理和治理：我们将理解环境管理的概念以及治理在促进可持续商业实践中的作用。 6. 战略思维和行动规划：我们将学会有效地领导变革，激励和动员他人共同迈向可持续和公平的未来。我们将发展战略思维和行动规划技能，专注于领导努力的长期影响和韧性。
英文课程简介：
By the end of this course, we hope to achieve the following goals together: 1. Foundations of Global Responsible Leadership: We should learn to understand and respect the principles and ethics of global responsible leadership. We will have increased awareness of environmental protection, social justice, and economic sustainability. 2. Ethical Decision-Making and Moral Leadership: We will understand how to make ethical and moral decisions in a complex global business environment and in an era of digital technology. This includes understanding and dealing with ethical dilemmas, as well as guiding organizations to maintain high standards of ethics and integrity. 3. Social Impact, Inclusion, and Engagement: We will learn to assess and strategize for social impact through business operations, focusing on creating value that benefits society and the environment. 4. Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion. We will gain insights into the importance of diversity, equity, and inclusion in the workplace, and how these principles contribute to a more engaged and productive workforce. 5. Environmental Stewardship and Governance: We will Understand the concepts of environmental stewardship and the role of governance in promoting sustainable business practices. 6. Strategic Thinking and Action Planning: We will learn to lead change effectively, inspiring and mobilizing others towards a shared vision for a sustainable and equitable future. We will develop strategic thinking and action planning skills, focusing on long-term impact and resilience in leadership efforts.
