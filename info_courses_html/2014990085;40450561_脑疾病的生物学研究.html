课程内容简介：
神经精神疾病和神经退行疾病的深入研究是人类社会经济与健康的发展对神经科学提出的要求，是神经科学发展的主流趋势。研究大脑疾病的发生、发展与治疗是在理解神经系统的结构与功能的基础上的必然方向，是生命科学的前沿学科之一,也是本世纪神经科学最具发展潜力的学科。关于脑疾病的课程是世界范围内神经生物学专业本科生的专业课，也是与生命科学、医学、药学、心理学和其它相关专业学生的选修课。本课程讲授主要的神经精神疾病和神经退行疾病的基础知识，包括自闭症、精神分裂、抑郁、老年痴呆、帕金森病、ADHD和Down syndrome等经典的神经系统疾病。本课程将基础知识、转化应用和临床研究结合起来，涵盖脑疾病的各个研究层面，包括遗传、发育、分子、细胞和社会学层面等各层次的研究，为生命科学、医学、药学和心理相关社会科学专业本科生学习目前主要的神经疾病提供经典理论知识和前沿研究进展，使学生对脑疾病有一定的理解。
Course Description
Research into neurodegenerative diseases and neuropsychiatric disorders has been an important task for neuroscientists due to the development of economy and health of human society, and thus is one of the most important fields of neuroscience research. Exploring the occurrence, development and treatment of neuronal disorders is the goal for neuroscientists based on current knowledge about the structure and function of the nervous system. Hence, studying neurodegenerative and neurodevelopmental disorders not only is one of frontier life sciences, but it is also one of the most promising neuroscience topics. Biology of Brain Disorders is a worldwide neurobiology undergraduate course, and is also an elective course for students of other majors related to neuroscience and biomedicine. In this course, the students will learn the basic knowledge of neurodegenerative diseases and neurodevelopmental disorders, including autism, schizophrenia, depression, Alzheimer's disease, Parkinson's disease, ADHD, Down syndrome, and so on. The teacher will teach the knowledge of basic research, clinical study and translational application, which covers mechanisms of genetics, development, molecular and cellular biology, and sociology. The goal of this course is to provide classical theory knowledge and cutting-edge research progress of neuronal disorders to the undergraduate students.
