课程内容简介：
生物化学工程简称生化工程，是应用化学工程和生物技术的原理和方法，研究解决有生物体或生物活性物质参与的生产过程中的基础理论及工程放大技术问题的工程学科。本课程拟通过基础理论分析和案例讲解，使学生掌握生化反应过程和生物分离过程中的共性工程技术原理，进一步培养学生利用理论知识分析和解决科研成果向工业化生产转化过程中实际工程问题的能力。课程内容主要包括生物化学工程的上游、中游和下游操作的基本原理和方法，重点介绍微生物的基本知识、灭菌过程、生物代谢过程的计量学、微生物培养动力学、酶促反应动力学、生物反应过程的操作模式、反应器的设计和放大，以及典型的生物分离技术，如絮凝、细胞破碎、层析以及电泳技术的基本原理，并引入大量的工业实践案例进行讲解。本课程是生物技术的一门工程性技术基础课，主要面向化学工程系高年级学生，亦可作为生物系、环境系等高年级学生和研究生的选修课。
Course Description
Biochemical engineering is an engineering discipline that applies the principles and methods of Chemical Engineering and Biotechnology to study and solve the basic theories and engineering problems in the production process involving organisms or bioactive substances such as enzymes. Through basic theoretical analysis and case study, this course aims to enable students to master the common engineering technology principles in the process of biochemical reaction and biological separation processes, and further cultivate students' ability to analyze and solve practical engineering problems in the process of transforming scientific research results into industrial production. The course contents mainly cover the basic principles and methods of upstream, midstream and downstream operations of biochemical engineering, with emphasis on the basic knowledge of microorganisms, sterilization process, metrology of biological metabolic process, microbial culture kinetics, enzymatic reaction kinetics, operation mode of biological reaction process, reactor design and scale-up. The basic principles for typical biological separation process such as flocculation, cell fragmentation, chromatography and electrophoresis techniques will also be included. A large number of industrial practice cases will be also introduced.
