课程内容简介：
本课程将有13章节，以微流控及其相关技术为研究手段，细胞分析为主要研究对象，着重讲授：1）微流控芯片技术及细胞分析的最新进展；2）微流控芯片的设计、研制和应用；3）微流控芯片上的细胞分选；4）微流控芯片上细胞共培养与形态分析；5）微流控芯片-质谱联系细胞代谢物分析；6）微流控芯片上的器官模拟研究及代谢物分析；7）微流控芯片上细胞分析方法的具体应用实例等。全书13章，大多数的内容为林金明教授实验室的研究成果以及国内外最新研究报道。课程教材为授课讲师林金明教授主编的Cell Analysis on Microfluidics(Springer 2017)及其中文版《微流控芯片细胞分析》（科学出版社，2018），将系统地讲授微流控芯片上细胞分析这一最新研究内容的相关新知识、新理论和新方法。
Course Description
This course will have 13 different lectures and 6-8 discussions. The lectures will mainly focus on, 1) A review of the development of microfluidic and cell analysis; 2) A detailed introduction and discussion over the design, formatting,application and development of microfluidic chip related with cell biology research; 3) Cell isolation on microfluidics; 4) Cell culture and observation on microfluidic; 5) Combination of microfluidic device with mass spectrometry for cell analysis; 6) Organ on a microfluidic chip and analysis of metabolites; 7) Same application areas of methods of cell analysis on microfluidics. The text will be the book entitled “Cell analysis on microfluidics”（English, Springer 2017）and Chinese version (Science Press, 2018) edited by Jin-Ming Lin, which will be published in Springer. Serving as both elementary introduction and advanced guidebook, this lecture and the book should interest and inspire the students who will be or are currently under the study or wish to study microfluidics based cell analysis methods.
