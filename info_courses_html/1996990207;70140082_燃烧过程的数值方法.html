课程简介：
教学过程主要针对实际应用中比较常用的一些数值计算方法进行讲解。具体内容有： 1.燃烧过程建模思想与方法：在课程讲解过程中，考虑到燃烧与湍流流动的密切关系，重点介绍有关湍流的研究情况，以及工程上常用模型与求解方法 2.液体燃料的燃烧模型：重点讲述颗粒运动模型及SIMPLE计算方法 3.气体燃料的燃烧过程数值计算：重点讲述多组分湍流燃烧数值计算方法 4.两相流动燃烧问题：重点讲述两相流动前沿问题-相间相互作用机理及模型 5、CFD软件讲解与应用
英文课程简介：
The teaching process mainly focuses on some numerical calculation methods commonly used in practical application. The main contents are as follows: 1. Combustion process modeling ideas and methods: in the course, considering the close relationship between combustion and turbulent flow, it focuses on the research of turbulence, as well as the commonly used models and solving methods in engineering 2. Combustion model of liquid fuel: focus on particle motion model and SIMPLE calculation method 3. Numerical calculation of gas fuel combustion process: focus on the numerical calculation method of multi-component turbulent combustion 4. Two-phase flow and combustion: focus on the front problem of two-phase flow - mechanism and model of interaction between phases 5. Application of CFD software
