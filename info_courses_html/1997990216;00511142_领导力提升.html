课程内容简介：
领导力是现代社会高素质人才必备的能力。领导力虽然有天赋和经验的成分，但完全可以通过学习提升。本课程面向有意锻炼并提升自身领导力的本科生，通过阅读思考、教师讲解、案例讨论、课堂练习、课后交流、业界导师辅导、观察、亲身实践等多种教学方式，帮助学生了解领导力的本质、领导的基本理论以及领导者如何激励人、如何沟通、如何领导团队、如何建设组织文化、如何引领变革等，从而帮助学生达到以下目标：系统掌握领导的基本理论和知识；加深自我认知，寻找符合自身的领导之道；改变日常生活中和他人的相处之道；提升学生在组织中的领导力；将知识初步转化为习惯、能力、品格。
Course Description
Leadership is essential for high-quality talents in modern society. Although talent and experience are helpful for leadership, leadership can be improved through learning. This course is for undergraduate students who are interested in improving their leadership. Through reading, thinking, lectures, case discussion, in class exercises, after-class communication, mentoring, observation, and personal practice, the students will learn about the nature of leadership, basic leadership theories, how leaders build organizational culture, motivate people, communicate, lead teams, make decisions, and lead change, so as to help students achieve the following goals: systematically grasping the basic theory and knowledge of leadership; deepening self-awareness and finding their own ways of leadership; change the way to get along with others in daily life; improve their leadership in the organization; initially transform knowledge into habits, abilities, and characters.
