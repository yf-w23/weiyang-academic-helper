高性能钢材与钢结构

课程简介：
本课程是土木工程专业的研究生专业课，主要讲授高性能钢材与钢结构的应用、材料力学性能、基本构件的受力特征与原理等研究前沿内容与最近进展，具体包括高强钢、不锈钢、低屈服点钢、耐火耐候钢、复合钢材、可拆卸钢结构体系等。课程以课堂讲授和专题讨论相结合的形式开展。
英文课程简介：
This course is one of specialised courses for postgraduates majoring in civil engineering. It mainly introduces application and principles of high-performance steel and steel structures in terms of materials and typcial members, with latest research advances being involved. More specifically, high-strength steel, stainless steel, low-yield point steel, fire-resistant and weather-resistant steel, clad steel as well as deconstructable steel structures are incoporated. Both lectures and topic discussions are employed to conduct this course.
