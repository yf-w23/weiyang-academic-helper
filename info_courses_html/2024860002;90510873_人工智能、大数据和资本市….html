课程简介：
本课程首先帮助学生梳理人工智能、大数据和资本市场领域的研究脉络和基本理论，通过阅读和分析资本市场、机器学习、人工智能、大数据，以及文本分析等五个方面的前沿论文，思考下面的问题：（1）文章的研究问题是什么？（2）文章的研究意义是什么？（3）文章的实证研究方案是如何设计的？（4）文章的模型设定是如何的？（5）作者为什么选择这样的研究样本？（6）文章的结果和统计推断是什么？（7）文章有哪些局限性？（8）文章的贡献是什么？基于对这些问题的回答，本课程帮助学生掌握从事人工智能、大数据和资本市场领域研究的逻辑分析方法、研究方法选择与设计。
英文课程简介：
This seminar focuses on applying machine learning techniques to finance and accounting research. Topics include supervised and unsupervised learning, deep learning, natural language processing, and time series analysis, with applications in asset pricing, financial statement analysis, credit risk assessment, and fraud detection. The course will also address the challenges and ethical considerations of using machine learning in finance and accounting. Conducted in a seminar format, the course will blend lectures, discussions, and student presentations. Students are expected to complete assigned readings before each class, actively participate in discussions, and deliver one or more presentations on research topics related to the course.
