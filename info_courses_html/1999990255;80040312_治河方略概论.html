课程简介：
本课程内容包括我国历史上的治河方略、新中国成立以来的治河思路与方略的变化，以及对大江大河治理的经验与教训反思。课程中还介绍和评述世界其他国家河流治理中的理念、方法与实例。课程在讲解治河原理与思想的同时，启发学生从历史角度理解古今治河方略的演变，探讨现代治河（水）研究的热点问题，对比分析国内外河流治理思路的异同，启发学生辩证分析河流治理成败的原因。本课程共32学时，本课程采用课堂讲授、分组讨论、课外实践等方式开展教学，其中，课堂讲授24学时，分组讨论4学时，课外实践4学时。
英文课程简介：
This course covers the strategies for river management in the history of our country, the evolution of river management thinking and strategies since the establishment of the People's Republic of China, as well as reflections on the experiences and lessons learned from the governance of major rivers. The course also introduces and evaluates the concepts, methods, and examples of river management in other countries worldwide. While explaining the principles and ideologies of river management, the course inspires students to understand the evolution of ancient and modern river management strategies from a historical perspective. It explores the current hot topics in modern river (water) research, compares and analyzes the similarities and differences in domestic and international river management approaches, and encourages students to dialectically analyze the reasons for the success or failure of river management. The course consists of 32 class hours, utilizing various teaching methods such as classroom lectures, group discussions, and extracurricular practices. Specifically, there are 24 class hours for lectures, 4 class hours for group discussions, and 4 class hours for extracurricular practices.

选课指导：
本课程面向（但不限于）水利工程研究生开设的选修课。通过讲解国内外治河思想的历史变迁和治河工程的历史沿革，从历史的角度理解古今治河方略的演变，对比分析国内外治河思路异同，辩证分析河流治理成败的原因。
