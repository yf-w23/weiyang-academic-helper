课程简介：
海洋生态毒理学是研究海洋环境中多种环境压力（包括富营养化、化学污染物等）影响的新兴学科。本课程将通过十章内容（污染物、统计方法、污染物吸收利用、生物累积与生物监测、生物标志物、水柱毒性试验、沉积物毒性试验、中宇宙系统与实地试验、生态风险和全球变化）进行讲解。课程在介绍基本理论与例证基础上，突出介绍国内外最新研究进展，强化学生对知识点的掌握与对学科前沿的了解。
英文课程简介：
Marine Ecotoxicology will cover issues related to contamination, responses, and testing techniques of seawater from a toxicological perspective. Divided into 10 chapters, including contaminants, statistics, contaminant modeling, bioaccumulation and biomonitoring, biomarkers, water column toxicity tests, sediment toxicity tests, mesocosm and field tests, ecological risk, and global environmental change, Marine Ecotoxicology will communicate core concepts with subjects ranging from molecular to global issues. As human interaction with marine environment increases, understanding of the pollutants and toxins introduced into the oceans becomes ever more critical, Marine Ecotoxicology builds a foundation of knowledge to assist students in studying, monitoring, and making decisions that affect marine ecosystem and, even the human health.
