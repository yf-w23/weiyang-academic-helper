课程内容简介：
本课程面向信息通信网络，以网络的核心概念（接入、交换、路由、流量控制、移动性管理等）为主线展开，通过对信息网络的代表性技术及其原理的穿插讲授，并辅以不同网络形式下的应用举例，内容主要涵盖：网络分层模型与可靠传输、网络延时分析（排队论基础）、多址和随机接入、交换原理与技术、路由与移动性管理、流量控制与资源分配，最终展望信息网络的最新进展，如软件定义网络，网络功能虚拟化等。作业以理论题、论文阅读评述和仿真实验为主。
Course Description
This course aims at providing a big picture and design methodologies of communications networks through their fundamental principles, elements and technologies. Specifically, the course will first introduce the basic and enhanced services and their QoS (Quality-of-Services) requirements (chapter 2), which lays foundations of the network architecture. Then, the building blocks of the communications networks, including (but not limited to) multiplexing and multiple access technologies (chapter 3), circuit- and packet-switching technologies (chapter 4), routing protocols (chapter 5), resource allocation and traffic control technologies (chapter 6), and mobility management technologies (chapter 7), are introduced in details through real examples from practical networks such as telecommunication networks, the Internet, local area networks, sensor networks, mobile networks, optical networks, etc. Through the above-mentioned fundamentals of communication networks, the students are expected to master the key principles, technologies and their design methodologies so that they can adapt themselves to the dynamic and varying future of the networks.
