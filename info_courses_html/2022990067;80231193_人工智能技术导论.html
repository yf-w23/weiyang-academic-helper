课程简介：
本课程面向电子信息类学生开设，主要包括工智能领域的发展及现状，人工智能的基本概念、原理、方法与技术，人工智能方法应用（如机器视觉、语音识别与分析、文本认知计算等），人工智能硬件等。本课程充分考虑电子信息类学生的学科基础和课程体系，学生更容易进行知识的迁移和学科脉络的形成。 这门课是一门人工智能领域通识课程，介绍人工智能领域的全景，同学学了这门课程之后，可以根据研究方向、兴趣去选择更加特定领域方向的课程。
英文课程简介：
This course is designed for students in the field of electronic information, covering the development and current state of the field of artificial intelligence, as well as the fundamental concepts, principles, methods, and techniques of artificial intelligence. The course will also cover the application of artificial intelligence methods, such as machine vision, speech recognition and analysis, text cognition computing, and artificial intelligence hardware. The course takes into account the disciplinary foundation and curriculum system of students in the field of electronic information, making it easier for them to transfer knowledge and form disciplinary connections.
