课程简介：
课程内容为水利系及其它工科系许多专业研究生所需的基本理论及基础知识，且与本科生教材《水力学》相应章节相街接，加强了原理、公式证明部分和量纲分析与相似原理关系，保持了体系的完整性。除原理、公式内容阐述，加强在工程实践中的应用介绍，使数学推导运算在模型实验和专题研究中获得实际运用，增加学习兴趣与获得解决工程实际问题有力工具的信心。模型试验与数值模型二者对比与相互关系内容，使学生了解数值模型特点及两种方法相辅相成解决实际问题的前景。根据教学内容应反映科研新成果要求、扩大同学的知识面，选择水流模型试验实例等加以介绍，并附有文献资料，可使同学进一步提高。既涵盖原课程的核心内容，又增添学科发展的新内容。课程体系更为清晰、课程内容更完整。主要内容有：（1）相似概述；（2）量纲分析；（3）流体运动相似的基本理论；（4）河渠及水工建筑物的水流模型；（5）挟沙水流的动床模型；（6）海工建筑物的水力模型；（7）水力机械模型。
英文课程简介：
Dimensionless parameters significantly deepen students’ understanding of fluid-flow phenomena in a way which is analogous to the case of a hydraulic jack, where the ratio of piston diameters determines the mechanical advantage, a dimensionless number which is independent of the overall size of the jack. They permit limited experimental results to be applied to situations involving different physical dimensions and often different fluid properties. The concepts of dimensional analysis introduced in this course plus an understanding of the mechanics of the type of flow under study make this generalization of experimental data possible. The consequence of such generalization is manifold, since one is now able to describe the phenomenon in its entirety and is not restricted to discussing the specialized experiment that was performed. Many of the dimensionless parameters can be viewed as a ratio of a pair of fluid forces, the relative magnitude indicating the relative importance of one of the forces with respect to the other. If some forces in a particular flow situation are very much larger than a few others, it is often possible to neglect the effect determined by the major forces. This means that simpler, although not necessarily easy, mathematical and experimental procedures can be used to solve the problem. For situations with several forces of the same magnitude, such as inertial, viscous, and gravitational forces, special techniques are required. After a discussion of dimensions, dimensional analysis, and dimensionless parameters, dynamic similitude and model studies are presented.
