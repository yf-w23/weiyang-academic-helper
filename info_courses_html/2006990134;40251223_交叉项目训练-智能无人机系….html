课程内容简介：
课程以培养选课同学参加国际空中机器人比赛(IARC)、中国机器人奥林匹克大赛空中机器人 组等赛事为重要目标之一，主要目标为系统学习和实践先进智能无人机所涉及的理论和相关 知识。课程分为三部分：理论课教学、实验和实践。理论课以讲座为主，主要介绍无人机和 空中机器人研究的最新进展；实验环节包括无人机编队控制、无人机智能任务规划；实践环 节着重学习和研究解决智能无人机系统的若干核心问题，为分组完成，目前的分组包括飞控 系统1（基于自研嵌入式系统）、飞控系统2（基于开源飞控系统）、单目视觉跟踪与识别系 统、立体视觉导航与避障系统、无人机虚实融合仿真系统、微型无人机视觉导航与避障技 术、微型无人机视觉编队与任务规划技术等。
Course Description
The target of this course is to teach and train students theory and technology related to advanced intelligent UAV, as well as participate the important competition, such as International Aerial Robotics Competition, Chinese Olympic Robotics Competition. The course can be separated as three parts as follow, 1. Lecture, introducing the latest theory and technology of UAV. 2. Experiment, including UAV swarm control and UAV intelligent mission planning. 3. Practice and design, which is team research and development work, aiming at studying and researching some important problem in intelligent UAV, including Autopilot System Design Group 1 based on embedded MCU, Autopilot System Design Group 2 based on open source FCU, Mono Vision Recognition and Tracking System, Guidance and Obstacle Avoidance System Based on Stereo Vision, Simulation System of Mixing Virtual and Actual Reality for UAV, Visual Guidance and Obstacle Avoidance Technology Based on Nano UAV, Vision Swarm and Mission Planning Technology for Nano UAV, to name just a few.
