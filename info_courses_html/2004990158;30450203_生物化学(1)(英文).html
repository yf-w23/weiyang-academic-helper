课程内容简介：
《生物化学》(I)采用国际上通用的原版英文教材，《Lehninger Principles of Biochemistry》（第6版），全英文讲授。课程内容主要包括：1. 细胞结构与功能简介；2. 生物化学相关的重要概念；3. 四大类生物分子（蛋白质、脂类、糖类、核酸）等的化学组成和结构；4. 生物膜和跨膜运输；5. 细胞信号转导机理。本课程的目标是让同学们除了能全面掌握有关生物化学方面的专业知识外，还能获得用英文直接进行科学文献的阅读及思考问题的能力。
Course Description
The contents of 《Biochemistry》(1) include: 1) Introduction of the structure and function of the cell; 2) The important chemistry concepts that are related to biochemistry; 3) The chemical composition and structure of four major biomolecules (Protein, nucleic acids, lipids and carbohydrates); 4) Biomembranes and solute transport; 5) Biosignaling. The whole course will be taught in English using the original English textbook, 《Lehninger Principles of Biochemistry》(The sixth edition). The aim of this course is to let students understand all the basic concepts in biochemistry, and at same time, develop their ability of reading scientific literature directly in English and critical thinking.
