课程内容简介：
本课程旨在为中、高年级本科学生提供当今粒子物理最新最前沿的介绍。课程内容包括大量基本粒子物理研究的新进展，并力图在实验与理论方面的内容教学中保持平衡。重点强调唯象方面的近似，以及基本理论概念，而不是复杂的数学推导。对本领域的一些关键性实验也会做简单的介绍，并侧重在这些实验结果如何影响我们的思考。尽管本课程绝大部分内容都是有关夸克与轻子的标准模型内容，但是关于该模型的不足以及之外的新物理也会有所讨论。该课程适合于大三与大四年级学生选修，需要微积分和普通物理的基础知识，有量子力学的基础概念最佳。
Course Description
This course aims to provide intermediate and senior undergraduate students with an introduction to the latest and most advanced aspects of particle physics. The course covers many recent developments in elementary particle physics and tries to maintain a balance between experimental and theoretical content. Emphasis is placed on phenomenological approximations and basic theoretical concepts rather than complex mathematical derivations. Some of the key experiments in the field will also be briefly introduced, emphasizing how the results of these experiments affect our thinking. Although most of the course is focused on the standard model of quarks and leptons, there will also be discussions of its shortcomings as well as new physics. This course is for the 3rd and 4th-year students and requires the knowledge of calculus and general physics and the basic concepts of quantum mechanics.
