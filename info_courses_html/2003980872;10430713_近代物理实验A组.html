课程内容简介：
本课程以著名的近代物理实验和现代实验技术为主要内容，涉及量子物理与技术（量子纠缠源测试、多种量子计算机、量子通讯等）、凝聚态物理与技术（材料制备与多样化表征和测试）、现代光学（激光原理与技术、光信息学、光镊、各类前沿应用光谱方法等），建立了基础、综合、研究型实验相结合的分层次实验教学体系，实施自由预约、以人为本、因材施教的个性化、多模式研究型实验教学方法，强调物理思想与新技术、新方法的结合，重视学生实验能力和创新能力的养成和展现。 学生可依据个人兴趣选择不同学术技术方向的实验开展学习，每人须完成6个实验，并提交实验报告。
Course Description
This course focuses on renowned modern physics experiments and cutting-edge experimental technologies, covering quantum physics and technology (quantum entanglement source testing, various quantum computers, quantum communication, etc.), condensed matter physics and technology (material preparation, diversified characterization and testing), and modern optics (laser principles and technology, optical informatics, optical tweezers, various advanced applied spectroscopic methods, etc.). It has established a hierarchical experimental teaching system integrating basic, comprehensive, and research-oriented experiments, implementing a personalized and multi-modal research-based experimental teaching method featuring free appointment, student-centered approach, and individualized instruction. The course emphasizes the integration of physical concepts with new technologies and methods, and attaches great importance to the cultivation and demonstration of students' experimental capabilities and innovative thinking. Students can select experiments from different academic and technical directions based on their interests. Each student is required to complete 6 experiments and submit experimental reports.
进度安排：

 第一周绪论课要求全体学生参加。
