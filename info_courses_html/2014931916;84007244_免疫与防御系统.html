课程简介：
本课程主要介绍由于免疫防御机制引起的皮肤、关节、肌肉、血管和累及上述组织器官的系统性常见和/或重要疾病，包括疾病的免疫/炎症机制、临床表现、诊断和治疗原则。本课程的重点为由失调或不适当的免疫反应导致的自身免疫性疾病和过敏性疾病，以及先天性/获得性免疫缺陷（医源性和感染性）的原因和表现，还包括包括皮肤病学和风湿病学的其他临床重要方面，例如皮肤癌和骨关节炎等。
英文课程简介：
This course focuses on commonly encountered and/or highly instructive diseases of the skin, joints, muscles and blood vessels, systemic diseases that involve these tissues, and on the mechanisms and manifestations of autoimmune and allergic diseases. The integrating theme of this course is immune/inflammatory mechanisms of disease, with a focus on dysregulated or inappropriately targeted immune responses that give rise to autoimmune and allergic diseases. Causes and manifestations of congenital or acquired immunodeficiency (both iatrogenic and infectious) are also covered. Other clinically important aspects of Dermatology and Rheumatology that do not readily associate with the immune/inflammatory theme, such as skin cancer, and osteoarthritis are included.
