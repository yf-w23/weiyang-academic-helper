课程内容简介：
本课程以马克思《资本论》为学习、研究对象，它主要探讨资本的生产过程、资本的流通过程、以及作为二者统一的资本生产总过程。在内容体系上，它包括商品理论、货币理论和资本理论三部分，分别构成马克思的劳动价值论和剩余价值学说。在马克思主义理论体系中，没有劳动价值论就没有剩余价值学说，而没有唯物史观和剩余价值学说，社会主义就不可能从空想变为科学，因此，《资本论》是学习和掌握马克思主义理论的核心文本，也是马克思主义理论的百科全书。在学习和研究方法上，本课程贯彻精读与泛读相结合、文本研究与历史研究相结合、学术问题与现实问题相结合等原则，在马克思主义与其他流派的比较中，使学生获得对马克思主义经济学基本概念、原理及其现实意义的初步理解和把握。
Course Description
This course takes Marx's "Capital" as the object of study and research. It mainly explores the production process of capital, the circulation process of capital, and the overall process of capital production as the unity of the two. In terms of content system, it includes three parts: commodity theory, monetary theory, and capital theory, which respectively constitute Marx's labor theory of value and surplus value theory. In the Marxist theoretical system, without the theory of labor value, there would be no theory of surplus value, and without the materialist conception of history and the theory of surplus value, socialism would not be able to transform from utopia to science. Therefore, "Capital" is the core text for learning and mastering Marxist theory, and also an encyclopedia of Marxist theory. In terms of learning and research methods, this course implements the principles of combining intensive reading with extensive reading, text research with historical research, and academic and practical issues. Through comparing Marxism with other schools of thought, students gain a preliminary understanding and grasp of the basic concepts, principles, and practical significance of Marxist economics.
