课程简介：
本课程为基础理论课，采用课堂授课与讨论结合的方式授课。授课老师来自临床医学相关专业，并致力于医工结合人才培养的师资。通过本课程，研究生将能够理解在医疗行为中的两大重要措施的“治疗学”相关方法、技术。授课教师将采用新的视角对研究生进行临床基本知识、治疗技术前沿和治疗方法学的发展概况知识传授，通过讨论和案例使研究生分析现有治疗药物和技术的产生过程、设计思路，为未来培养医工结合研究生在医学治疗领域的创新思维和创新方法奠定基础。本课程的授课教师均来自本校附属医院，课程的设计由负责人组织讨论形成。
英文课程简介：
his course is a fundamental course for the students who devoted to combination of medical and engineering. The method of teaching are combination of the lectures and discussion. The teachers of this course are all from clinical department of related majors, and are committed to the combination of medical and industrial personnel training. Through this course, the students will be able to understand the "treatment" in medical services and get to know the research and development of some treatment mothed and medications. The lecturer will adopt a new perspective to teach the basic clinical knowledge, treatment technology frontier and development overview of treatment methodology to the postgraduates. The discussion course can give the students opportunity to analyze the production process and design ideas of existing treatment drugs and technologies, so as to lay a foundation for the cultivation of innovative thinking and methods in medical treatment field in the future. The teachers of this course are from the Affiliated Hospital of our school, and the design of the course is organized and discussed by the person in charge.
