课程内容简介：
本课程将古琴演奏技法讲授与古琴作品赏析融合为一体，多角度、多元素、多技能循序渐进展开教学。课程设计以讲授古琴文化知识和古琴基础演奏技法为重点，注重引领学生从专业角度赏析古琴经典作品。课程既讲授古琴基础知识、古琴减字谱、古琴流派及风格特点等知识，又通过教授学生熟练掌握左右手基本指法，带领学生达到演奏《沧海一声笑》、《秋风词》等琴曲的水平。同时，通过剖析作品背景、音乐结构、曲用技巧等方面，从专业角度系统讲授赏析古琴作品的方法，提高学生音乐素养。为进一步扩展学生知识面，本课程还将带领学生探索古琴表演形式的创新，如古琴与其他乐器的合作、移植中外经典音乐等。
Course Description
The course will teach students playing techniques and appreciation of Guqin music. A multi-faceted, step-by-step teaching approach will be used.This course is designed to teach the cultural knowledge and playing techniques of Guqin and will focus on giving students an appreciation of the Guqin in a professional way. The course not only teaches basic knowledge, abbreviated character tablature (jian zi pu), the genre of Guqin and similar areas, but also guides students to master the basic fingering of the left and right hands, and teaches them to play music (such as Qiu Feng Ci, or similar level pieces). At the same time, by analyzing the background of different works, musical structure, and performing skills, the course systematically teaches the method of appreciating and analyzing Guqin works in a professional way, so as to improve the students' musicianship. To further expand students' knowledge, this course will also lead students to explore the innovation of guqin performance forms, such as the cooperation between guqin and other instruments and the transplantation of Chinese and foreign classical music.
