课程内容简介：
本课程探讨原社会主义国家集群地区、当今世界最大转型地区、“丝路经济带”主干地区——原苏联东欧地区的政治、经济与外交变迁规律，思考这些国家的所谓社会主义道路发展道路缘何遭遇挫折以及向何处去，培养学生进行转型地区与国别研究的基本能力。
Course Description
This course aims to explore the political, economic and diplomatic changes in the former Soviet union and eastern Europe, the world's largest transition region and the backbone of the "silk road economic belt".It is designed to develop students' basic skills in conducting research in transition regions and countries.
