课程内容简介：
本课程是国际政治、国际事务等社会科学专业本科生的专业基础课，旨在从分析视角讨论与战争和安全相关的问题。例如，战争的原因是什么？战争的影响有哪些？如何解释国际和国内争端解决中武力（暴力）的使用？所谓的“自由秩序”是否能促进和平与稳定？世界权力分配的变化和转移有何影响？在什么情况下，威胁是可信的？第三方干涉能否减少暴力，促进地区和平？核武器是否让世界更加安全与和平？如何解释恐怖主义的发生？如何理解性别权力平等与安全之间的关系？如何理解气候变化、公共卫生问题对国际安全的影响等。本课程将结合理论讨论与政策辩论，旨为学生提供理解当今世界纷繁复杂的安全问题的理论训练和方法思维，向学生介绍如何从国际关系学科的视角理解当今的世界安全问题。课程将结合教师讲授与学生研讨，学生需要批判性地阅读国际安全领域的经典和前沿文献，并进行写作练习，熟悉国际关系理论和政策研究文章的风格。
Course Description
This course intends to provide an overview of the foundational literature in international security for undergraduate students majoring in international politics and global affairs. It tackles questions related to war and security from an analytical perspective. What are the causes and consequences of war? What explains the use of violence for the resolution of inter and intra-state disputes? Does the so-called "liberal order" promote peace and stability? What are the implications of changes and shifts in the distribution of power in the world? What renders the threats of force credible? Can third-party intervention in civil war reduce violence and promote peace? Do nuclear weapons make the world safer and more peaceful? What explains the occurrence of terrorism? How do we understand the relationship between gender equality and security? What are the impacts of climate change and global pandemic on international security? This course will combine theoretical discussions and policy debates to provide students with theoretical training and methodological skills to understand the complex security issues in the contemporary world and to introduce students to how to understand current security issues from the perspective of the discipline of international relations. The course will combine lectures and student discussions. Students need to critically read both the classical and cutting-edge literature in international security and write intensively to be familiar with the style of international relations and policy research articles.
