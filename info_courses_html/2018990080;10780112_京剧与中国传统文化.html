课程内容简介：
课程从中国传统文化的角度，讲解京剧艺术的审美形态和艺术特征，让学生系统全面认识和鉴赏 京剧艺术。 从京剧艺术的“形”（符号与表意）与审美范式，“类”（行当与程式）与审美法 则，“赏”（角色与流派）与审美品格，“情”（高雅与通俗）与审美意境四个维度，以老生、 武生、小生、青衣、花旦、老旦、武旦、净行、丑行九个行当为依托，逐一赏析每一个行当中的 传统文化元素和不同的审美风格。 注：课程进度会依照学生整体学习和消化程度适当调整，学习内容有增加或减少的可能性
Course Description
From the perspective of traditional Chinese culture, the course explains the aesthetic forms and artistic features of Beijing Opera art, and enables students to systematically and comprehensively understand and appreciate Beijing Opera art. The relationship between Beijing Opera and traditional Confucian culture， explains the reference and expression of Beijing Opera art to Confucian cultural elements. The relationship between Beijing Opera and Taoist traditional culture， explains the reference and meaning of the art of Beijing Opera to Taoist cultural elements. The relationship between Beijing Opera and religious traditional culture， explains the reference and performance of Beijing Opera art to religious cultural concepts. The relationship between Beijing Opera and traditional folk culture， explains the influence of folk culture on the art of Beijing Opera. From the "shape" of the Beijing Opera art (symbol and meaning)， the aesthetic paradigm of "class" (business and program) and the aesthetic rules of "play" (role and genres) and aesthetic character, "taste" (highbrow and popular) aesthetic artistic conception with four dimensions，rely on nine trades, namely laosheng-role, wusheng-role, xiaosheng-role, qingyi-role, huadan-role,laodan-role, wudan-role, jinghang-role and chouhang-role, as the backing,appreciate the traditional cultural elements and different aesthetic styles in each industry one by one. （The course schedule will be adjusted according to the students' overall learning and digestion level, and the learning content may be increased or decreased.）
