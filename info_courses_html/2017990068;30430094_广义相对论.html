课程内容简介：
本课程旨在介绍广义相对论的基本原理。课程将从广义相对论所需要的基础的数学工具出发，逐步对广义相对论进行讨论。课程的最后将介绍一些现代引力理论的发展及其在宇宙学和天体物理学中的应用。
Course Description
进度安排：

Ch. 1 Review of special relativity (1st week)

Ch. 2 Mathematical tools, the Inertial force, the principle of equivalence, and a first look of a new gravitational Theory (1st and 2nd week) 

Ch. 3 Einstein's field equation and some basic examples (3nd week)

Ch. 4 Black holes (introdution) (4th - 5th week)

Ch. 5 Rotating black holes (5th week)

Ch. 6 More about black holes (6th week)

Ch. 7 Gravitational waves (7th week)

Ch. 8 Action principle (8th week)

Ch. 9 Review of quantum mechanics and basic introduction to quantum field theory (9th week)

Ch. 10 Unrul effect and Hawking Radition (10th week)

Ch. 11 Introduction to cosmology (11th - 12th week)

Ch. 12 Quantum field theory in expanding universe (13 week)

Ch. 13 Gravitation waves as fundamental particles. (13th week)
