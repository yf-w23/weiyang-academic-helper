课程内容简介：
《英语阅读写作-A》，即《研究论文读写》是语言中心开设的学术必修系列课之一。旨在满足当代大学生运用英语进行学术科研的需求，培养学生的英语学术阅读和写作能力以及批判性思维。这门课程将从知识、能力和实践方面学生准备好学习各种学术领域所需要的研究体裁。 本课程以项目驱动。课上对社科类研究论文进行阅读和文本分析，培养和练习略读、扫读、记笔记等基本阅读技能，课下学生以4人为一组的团队形式，通过检索文献，确定研究课题，设计并进行项目研究。期末到课堂上轮流进行陈述演示汇报，并在此基础上写出IMRD（介绍、方法、结果和讨论）格式的研究论文。学生在培养基本学术能力、锻炼研究论文读写的同时，还能跨越专业之间的界限共同合作，培养团队意识。
Course Description
Reading & Writing for Research Papers is designed to develop students' academic reading and writing competencies as well as critical thinking. The course will prepare students for the research genre which is required in various academic fields. Through reading research papers, students can practise and develop such essential reading skills as skimming, scanning, and note-taking; and meanwhile students can cultivate an awareness of different styles of English language, academic and colloquial, so to speak. A considerable emphasis is put on students’ production of a research paper with a mini-project-based approach. The project involves limited original research but provides the adequate vehicle for applying the aforementioned skills and knowledge acquired through reading to writing an experimental research article follows the four-part IMRAD format (Introduction, Method, Results, and Discussion). Using an idealized format for the genre allows students to address writing issues through authentic tasks without the barrier of field-specific content. It also allows students to employ the general framework of the research article to cultivate the awareness of disciplinary deviations.
