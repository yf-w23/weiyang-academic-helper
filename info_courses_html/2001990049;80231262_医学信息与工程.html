课程简介：
本课程内容包括： 1. 信息的采集方法 (1) 心电/脑电检测的原理与分析，电刺激调控的原理与分析； (2) 生物组织的光学成像，包括显微光学和组织光学。 2. 信息的处理方法 (1) 信息处理基础，包括数据分析的发展历视，机器学习的概念，数据挖掘的概念，以及人工智能技术的起源和发展； (2) 医学图像信息处理，包括传统处理方法和深度学习方法的介绍，病灶检测、分类和分割的前沿医工交叉应用； (3) 医学文本信息处理，包括常用自然语言处理方法和前沿医工交叉应用； (4) 医工交叉研究方法，重点介绍医工交叉项目设计过程中的设计思路和原则;
英文课程简介：
This course covers: 1. Information collection method (1) The principle and analysis of ECG/EEG detection, the principle and analysis of electrical stimulation changes. (2) Optical imaging of biological tissues, including microscopic optics and tissue optics. 2. Information processing method (1) Information processing foundation, including the development history of data analysis, the concept of machine learning, the concept of data mining, and the origin and development of artificial intelligence technology. (2) Medical image information processing, including the introduction of traditional processing methods and deep learning methods, and the lesion detection, classification and segmentation. (3) Medical text information processing, including common natural language processing methods and the cutting-edge applications. (4) Medical-industrial crossover research methods, focusing on the experiment design and principles, and some frequently encountered problems. (5) Problems and prospects of medical artificial intelligence.
