课程简介：
陆面过程模型是地球系统模型的基本组成单元，反映地表各圈层（岩石圈、生物圈、冰冻圈和水圈）与大气的相互作用过程与机理。本课程讲述陆地和大气之间的水、热、碳和动量交换的基本过程和过程参数化，探讨陆气相互作用以及在生态、新能源等可持续发展领域研究中的应用。课程内容分为三部分： 第一部分，讲授基本陆面过程和模型发展的基本原理。（1）陆面过程基本理论，包括：土壤的水热传输过程，Monin-Obhukov近地层相似理论，植被冠层辐射传输过程，植被冠层蒸散发和碳交换的耦合。（2）卫星遥感数据在陆面模型中应用的挑战，包括获取与特定陆面过程模型匹配的陆面参数，发展模型驱动数据和模拟验证数据所面临的挑战。 第二部分，学科研究实践和前沿的讨论。邀请观测和模型发展领域的专家作为嘉宾，讨论如何加深过程理解，发展参数化和应用卫星数据推进陆面过程相关研究等前沿问题。可能的主体包括陆面过程尺度问题、陆面过程分析方法、陆气相互作用典型案例、卫星数据应用等。 第三部分，选择一个综合陆面过程模型(Noah-MP)进行学习和训练。
英文课程简介：
A land surface process model (LSM) is a basic component of climate system models / earth system models. LSMs describe the interactions between the earth\'s surface (including lithosphere, biosphere, cryosphere, and hydrosphere) and atmosphere. This course covers the fundamental processes and parameterizations of water, heat, carbon, and momentum exchange between land and atmosphere. It explores land-atmosphere interactions and briefs their applications in sustainable development fields such as ecology and renewable energy research. The course content consists of the following three parts: Part 1: Fundamental Land Surface Processes and Model Development Principles (1) Basic theories of land surface processes, including: • Soil water and heat transfer processes • Monin-Obukhov similarity theory for the surface layer • Radiative transfer processes in vegetation canopies • Coupling mechanisms of evapotranspiration and carbon exchange in vegetation canopies (2) Challenges in applying satellite remote sensing data to land surface models, including: • Acquiring land surface parameters compatible with specific process-based models • Challenges in developing model input datasets and validation data for simulations Part 2: Research Practices and Frontier Discussions Experts in observational and modeling fields will be invited as guest speakers to discuss cutting-edge topics such as: • Enhancing process understanding • Improving parameterization schemes • Advancing land surface process research through satellite data applications Part 3: Training with an Integrated Land Surface Model (Noah-MP) This section involves learning and practical exercises using the Noah-MP (Multi-Physics) model, a comprehensive land surface process model, to apply theoretical knowledge in simulations and analysis.
