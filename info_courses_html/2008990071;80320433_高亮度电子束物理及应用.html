课程简介：
本课程致力于系统的介绍有关高亮度电子束的关键物理和技术，及其在当代科学研究中的重要应用，主要包含四部分内容。首先在绪论部分介绍什么是高亮度电子束以及电子束发射度的概念，并简介高亮度电子束的若干典型应用；第二大部分内容围绕高亮度电子束产生的物理和技术，介绍光阴极的光电发射模型，光阴极微波电子枪系统，束流空间电荷力和传输动力学，为优化电子束发射度而进行的超快激光的纵向、横向整形原理及其传输，高亮度电子束产生中的同步控制原理，以及高亮度电子束串的产生技术；第三部分内容为高亮度电子束关键参数的诊断，包括发射度和纵向分布测量；第四部分涉及高亮度电子束的典型应用，包括高亮度电子束在超快电子显微镜、相干渡越辐射、逆康普顿散射和自由电子激光等方面的应用。
英文课程简介：
This course is devoted to teaching the key physics/technologies of high-brightness electron beams (HBEB) and their important applications in modern scientific research. It consists of four major parts. First, what HBEB is and the concepts of emittance are introduced. The second part focuses on the physics and technologies of HBEB production. It includes photoelectron emission model of photocathodes, the RF gun system, space charge and beam transport dynamics, the basics of ultrafast lasers, the principles of longitudinal and transverse laser shaping for emittance optimization, the synchronization and timing principles for HBEB production, and the approaches for high-brightness electron bunch train production. The third part is about how to characterize the key performance of HBEB, mainly for the diagnostics of electron beam emittance and longitudinal distribution. In the last part, several important applications of HBEB in modern science will be introduced, which includes ultrafast electron microscopy (UEM), coherent transition radiation (CTR), inverse Compton scattering (ICS) and free electron lasers(FEL).
