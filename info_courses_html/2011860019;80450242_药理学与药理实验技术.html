课程简介：
目前生物活性前沿基础研究与临床实际应用的研究有些脱轨，为了促进生物学科研成果在健康领域的转化，有必要引进生物学基础研究与临床研究的桥梁课程。而药理学正是生物学与临床医学转化方面的桥梁课程，这是一门交叉学科，以生物化学和生理学为基础，主要研究生物活性物质在人体的作用与作用规律。本课程除介绍药理学基本理论外，还结合生物学研究生的基本情况，还开展药理动物模型与相关实验的技能培训与提高，为以后生物学研究生开展生物活性物质在体内体外活性测试以及分子机制的研究奠定坚实的基础。 本课程教学内容分为药理学总论、重大疾病专题论述，药理学新方法新技术讲座，药理学实验动物技术课，药理学社会活动实践，学生口头总结报告等。教学方式有教师基本理论和前沿研究讲座，具体实验操作，企业具体研发案例实践等。成绩考核以平时上课表现以及学生最终口头报告总结与书面材料为参考。所以教学内容和方式方面，是从书本走向实验台，走向社会，让学生体验和掌握生物活性药物或产品实际研发的全过程，对于生物学其他科研产品的研发也具有借鉴意义，这是其他大多数课程教学主要限于课本理论教学所没有的。
英文课程简介：
At present, there are some derailments in the research of Frontier basic research and clinical application of biological activity. In order to promote the transformation of biological research achievements in the field of health, it is necessary to introduce the bridge course of basic research and clinical research of biology. Pharmacology is a bridge course in the transformation of biology and clinical medicine. It is an interdisciplinary subject. Based on Biochemistry and physiology, it mainly studies the action and law of bioactive substances in human body. In addition to introducing the basic theory of pharmacology, this course also combines the basic situation of biology graduate students, and carries out the training and improvement of the skills of pharmacological animal models and related experiments, which lays a solid foundation for future biology graduate students to carry out in vivo and in vitro bioactivity testing and molecular mechanism research. The content of this course is divided into general pharmacology, special discussion on major diseases, lectures on new methods and technologies of pharmacology, pharmacological laboratory animal technology course, pharmacological social activities practice, students'oral summary reports, etc. Teaching methods include teachers'basic theory and advanced research lectures, specific experimental operations, enterprise specific R&D case practice, etc.
