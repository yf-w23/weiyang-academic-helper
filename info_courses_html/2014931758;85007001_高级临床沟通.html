课程简介：
临床沟通能力是现代医务工作者应具备的重要能力之一，有效的临床沟通是建立良好医患沟通的主要途径。高级临床沟通课程内容由四部分组成：1）基础理论，2）基本原理；3）临床沟通原理、技能和方法；4）不同专科临床沟通的特点及经验等,共16学时。 本课程注重理论联系实际、重视讨论式教学，在讲授理论课程基础上，采用多种教学方法。希望通过本课程学习,学生能够掌握医患关系的现状，理解并掌握临床沟通的基本理念和基本原理，掌握临床沟通的技巧，理解医﻿生角色与患者角色各自的特点，了解各专业科室患者的特点，熟悉与各科室患者沟通的技巧，学会合理运用沟通技巧，增强在工作中应对各种沟通情况的能力。
英文课程简介：
Clinical communication skills are one of the essential competencies for modern healthcare professionals, and effective clinical communication serves as the primary pathway to establishing a positive doctor-patient relationship. The advanced clinical communication course consists of four components: 1) foundational theories, 2) fundamental principles, 3) principles, skills, and methods of clinical communication, and 4) characteristics and experiences of clinical communication across different specialties, totaling 16 instructional hours. This course emphasizes the integration of theory with practice and prioritizes interactive, discussion-based teaching. In addition to theoretical lectures, a variety of teaching methods are employed. Upon completing this course, students are expected to: Grasp the current state of doctor-patient relationships. Understand and master the fundamental concepts and principles of clinical communication. Acquire clinical communication techniques. Comprehend the characteristics of both the physician and patient roles. Learn about the unique features of patients in various specialized departments. Become familiar with communication techniques tailored to different patient populations. Develop the ability to apply communication skills appropriately and enhance their capacity to handle diverse communication scenarios in professional practice.
