课程简介：
《发动机数字仿真与光学诊断》是“动力工程及工程热物理”和“航空宇航科学与技术”一级学科发动机研究方向研究生的一门专业课。主要围绕车用和航空发动机共性的流动、喷雾、燃烧和排放等主题，对其中涉及到的湍流、两相流和化学反应动力学等基本理论和前沿技术，以及AI数智仿真和光学可视化等研究方法开展教学，通过课堂授课、案例教学、CFD软件实操和光学试验获得发动机基础理论和研究方法的创新素材，掌握CFD数智仿真和光学可视化两大现代科研方法，培养研究生的创新意识和AI应用科研能力，为研究生从事动力相关的科学研究奠定坚实的数理与技术基础。
英文课程简介：
Engine Digital Simulation and Optical Diagnosis is a specialized postgraduate course for students majoring in engine research under the first-level disciplines of Power Engineering and Engineering Thermophysics and Aeronautical and Astronautical Science and Technology.Focusing on common topics of automotive and aero-engines including flow, spray, combustion and emissions, this course teaches fundamental theories and cutting-edge technologies such as turbulence, two-phase flow and chemical reaction kinetics, as well as research methods including AI-driven intelligent simulation and optical visualization.Through classroom lectures, case studies, hands-on CFD software practice and optical experiments, students will acquire innovative materials for engine fundamental theories and research methods, master two modern scientific research tools: CFD intelligent simulation and optical visualization. The course aims to cultivate postgraduates’ innovative awareness and AI-applied research capabilities, laying a solid mathematical, physical and technical foundation for their future scientific research in power-related fields.
