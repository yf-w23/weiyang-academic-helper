课程内容简介：
理解设计思维并能够运用设计思维完成产品原型的设计与制作。意将设计理论生活化、生产化、具象化、创新化。多角度实践融合操作是课程的具体途径。 内容主要包括: 设计的意涵、形式美法则、设计中名词与动词的思考、设计思维。着重强调设计思维对于产业与创新的重要性。在综合构成环节结合设计原理、技术、加工工艺、材料等要素共同理解设计的完整性和系统性，通过具体项目和实践体会设计的统筹力和协调力。不同领域资深专家随堂讲座交流，结合企业实地参观考察，让学生切实感受制造业中的设计思维如何实现，创新行业中设计如何激发脑力。帮助学生完成完整的设计作品原型，完成大多数学生的人生第一件设计作品！
Course Description
Understand design thinking and be able to use it to complete the design and production of product prototypes. The design theory is intended to be life oriented, productive, concrete and innovative. Multi angle practice and integration operation is the specific way of the course. The content mainly includes: the meaning of design, the rules of formal beauty, the thinking of nouns and verbs in design, and design thinking. Emphasize the importance of design thinking for industry and innovation. Comprehend the integrity and systematicness of design in combination with design principles, technology, processing technology, materials and other elements in the comprehensive composition link, and experience the overall planning and coordination of design through specific projects and practices. Senior experts in different fields give lectures and exchanges in the classroom, and in combination with enterprise field visits, so that students can really feel how to realize design thinking in the manufacturing industry and how to stimulate brain power in the innovative industry. Help students complete the prototype of a complete design work, and complete the first design work in most students' lives!
