凝聚态物理的光学性质和超快动力学

课程简介：
本课程将衔接传统的凝聚态物理课与材料系统中光与物质相互作用及超快现象最前沿的研究领域。课程首先介绍超快超强激光及回顾光在材料中的基本性质。然后将覆盖大量材料研究中常用的基础及先进的光学实验手段及前沿研究话题。主要包括：光吸收、激子、金属及半导体的超快动力学、自旋电子学、（二维）半导体材料的自旋和能谷物理、磁光效应、超快磁学、太赫兹光的产生探测及在凝聚态物理中的应用、二次谐波产生及在凝聚态物理中的应用。
英文课程简介：
This course aims to bridge the gap between standard condensed matter physics course and frontier research in light-matter interaction and ultrafast phenomena in condensed matter physics. The course will start with an introduction to ultrafast and ultrastrong lasers and a review of fundamentals of optical properties. Then it will not only cover a variety of both basic and advanced experimental optical techniques that are commonly used to study materials, but also discuss hot research topics, including optical absorption, excitons, luminescence, ultrafast dynamics in metals and semiconductors, spintronics, spin and valley physics in (2D) semiconductors, magneto-optical effects, ultrafast magnetism, terahertz wave generation and detection and its application in condensed matter systems, second harmonic generation and its application in condensed matter physics.
