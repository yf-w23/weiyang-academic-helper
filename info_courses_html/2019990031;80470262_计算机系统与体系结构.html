课程简介：
本课程将深入探讨现代计算机系统中的先进架构技术。课程将涵盖典型计算机系统中的多个主要组成部分，如多核处理器，缓存，内存，及重要的系统设计与优化思想，如计算并行、数据局部性、投机执行、专用定制设计等。通过本课程的学习，学生将从计算机整体系统的角度，深入理解系统组成原理与方法，性能与能耗间的复杂权衡关系，以及硬件层与软件层接口间的丰富交互。 本课程的安排如下。课程的前2/3为讲授内容，涵盖的主题包括：乱序执行设计，前端指令获取技术，缓存一致性，非均匀缓存访问（NUCA）技术、新型内存技术、及大规模数据中心。课程的后1/3为互动论文讨论，研讨计算机体系结构国际顶级会议上发表的最新论文。
英文课程简介：
This course focuses on in-depth discussion of advanced architectural techniques in modern computer systems. It covers the major components in typical computer systems, including multi-core processors, caches, and memories, as well as important design and optimization ideas, such as parallelism, locality, speculation, specialization, and more. Upon completion of the course, students will better understand the system organization principles and methodologies, the complex system tradeoffs between performance and energy, as well as the rich interaction between hardware and software layers from a system perspective. The course is organized as follows. The first two thirds of this course will be lectures covering topics including: out-of-order execution, front-end instruction fetch, cache coherence and memory consistency, non-uniform cache architectures, new memory technologies, and large-scale datacenters. The last one third of this course will be interactive paper discussion sessions that study recent research papers published at top-tier computer architecture conferences.
