课程内容简介：
《电子电路与系统基础》的具体内容包括：电路基本定律和定理、基本电路元件（电源、电阻、电容、电感）的伏安特性及分析方法、运算放大器及其分析方法、电路的时域分析方法、电路的频域分析方法、pn结与二极管、BJT管的原理与分析、MOSFET管的原理与分析、CMOS数字电路原理、振荡电路原理与分析
Course Description
The course curriculum covers: 1. Fundamental circuit laws and theorems. 2. Current-voltage (I-V) characteristics and analysis methods for basic circuit elements (sources, resistors, capacitors, and inductors). 3. Operational amplifiers (Op-Amps) and their analysis. 4. Time-domain and frequency-domain circuit analysis. 5. PN junctions and diodes. 6. Principles and analysis of BJTs and MOSFETs. 7. Principles of CMOS digital circuits. 8. Theory and analysis of oscillator circuits.
