课程内容简介：
《名公书判清明集》是一部汇集诉讼判决、官府公文的南宋文献，自1980年代上海图书馆藏明刻本被发现以来，掀起了海内外40年来学术研究的热潮，是研究法制史、社会史等的珍贵材料。众所周知，传统中国法被认为“以刑为主”，婚姻、田土、债负等均是“细故”，而《名公书判清明集》保留了大量的“细故”案件的判决，是管窥传统中国民事法秩序的重要窗口。本门课程以《名公书判清明集》之“户婚门”、“人伦门”为研读对象，择取其中有关田土交易、亲属关系、财产继承等判决文书，先由任课教师讲授基本的背景知识、演示案例剖析的方法，再由学生依照示范的方法领读其他案例，师生共同参与讨论。
Course Description
The Minggong Shupan Qingming Ji (“Collection of Enlightened Judgments by Famous Officials”) is a compendium of the Southern-Song court decisions and official documents. Ever since the Ming-dynasty blockprint in the Shanghai Library was rediscovered in the 1980s, this book has ignited a trend of the Song studies in China and abroad, and has become an invaluable source for the study of Chinese legal and social history. Traditional Chinese law is commonly described as a penal code, with the disputes of marriage, land transaction, and debt relegated to the realm of petty affairs. Yet the Qingming ji preserves a wealth of judgments on precisely these petty affairs, offering a unique window onto the civil legal order of imperial China. This course focuses on the two sections named Marriage and Human Relations in this book. We will select decisions concerning land transaction, kinship, and property inheritance. After the instructor supplies essential background and models the method of case analysis, students will lead discussion of other cases following the same approach.
