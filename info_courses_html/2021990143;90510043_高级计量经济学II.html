课程简介：
本课程面向经济学、金融学、管理学等专业研究生，主要介绍现代计量经济学的基础理论与方法。目标是使博士生能够在相关领域恰当地使用计量方法开展实证研究，并为其学习其他高深计量专题打下基础。课程将介绍平稳与非平稳时间序列模型，经典面板数据回归模型（固定效应与随机效应），以及各种非线性模型（离散选择、删失与截断数据回归、样本选择、分位数回归、处理效应分析等）。先修课程高级计量经济学I所涵盖的截面数据经典线性回归是本课程的学习基础。非参数/半参数分析、因果推断与项目评估等高深专题在本课中略有涉及，更多讨论留待其他专题课程。
英文课程简介：
This course is designed for graduate students in economics, finance, and management who want to take a solid course in advanced econometrics and lay down the foundation for theoretical and applied research in these disciplines. It offers a systematic training in modern econometrics techniques, with particular focus on stationary and non-stationary time series data, panel data (fixed effects and random effects models), discrete choice models, censoring, sample selection, quantile regression, and treatment effects analysis. Classical regression analysis with cross-sectional data is covered in Advanced Econometrics I and is prerequisite knowledge for studying this class. More advanced topics such as non-/semi-parametric analysis, causal inference and program evaluation will be covered in subsequent electives.
