课程简介：
语言是人类进化的重要成果，同时作为最重要的交流工具，对于人类文明的进步和发展起到了不可替代的作用。让机器能够理解人类的语言，能够利用人类的语言和人类进行交流，可以让人们日常生活更加方便，也能够帮助人们更有效率的工作，从而促进人类文明继续发展。因此长期以来，自然语言处理一直是信息科学与人工智能领域中的重要研究方向之一。 自然语言理解已经成为人工智能领域最有标志意义的难题。尽管当前的计算机还不能完成自然语言理解的任务，但是经过几十年来众多学者的不断努力，在这个方向上已经取得了很多重要的进展。计算机已经给予人们巨大的帮助，特别是在面对海量文本数据的时候。计算机所拥有的可以无限扩展的存储能力和计算能力，极大地弥补人类处理能力有限的短板。文本数据挖掘是数据挖掘技术和自然语言处理技术结合，用于处理海量文本数据所形成的交叉学科方向。本课程将集中介绍自然语言处理和文本数据挖掘方面的基本知识和主要应用。
英文课程简介：
Language is one of the most important achievements of human evolution, furthermore, as the most widely used communication tool, language also plays a crucial role in the development of civilization. If a machine can understand human languages, and communicate with people with human languages, people’s daily life will be more convenient, and the work can be more efficient. In this way, human civilization can move forward. Therefore, for a long time, natural language processing is one of the more important research areas in the domain of information science and artificial intelligence. Natural language processing has become a landmark topic of artificial intelligence. Although computers cannot really understand human language，there are already many important achievements in this field with great effort in these several decades. When we face large amounts of text, computers provide great help with almost unlimited ability of storage and computing, which greatly increase human capabilities. Text mining, as a interdiscipline subject, try to handle massive text data combined with data mining and natural language processing technologies. In this course, we will focus on the fundamental knowledge and main applications on natural language processing and text mining.
