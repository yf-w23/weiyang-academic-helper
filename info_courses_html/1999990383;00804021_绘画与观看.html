课程内容简介：
该课程向清华大学各学科同学开放，从绘画窗口了解社会在历史不同时期所呈现的系统性生长、系统性开放或系统性坍塌，认识艺术发展的周期性规律，进而理解中西不同的观看方式在文明进程中的重要作用。 课程旨在打通学科界限，重点讲解艺术与科技的内在关联，注重理论与实践相结合，包含校园写生实践和艺术博物馆观展，提升同学的审美判断力和表达能力，增强自身的文化自信。
Course Description
This course is open to students from all disciplines of Tsinghua University. From the painting perspective, students will understand the systematic growth, opening or collapse of society in different eras，the periodicity of the development of art, and the important role of different ways of viewing the progress of civilization in China and the West. The course aims to break through disciplinary boundaries, focus on explaining the internal relationship between art, science, and technology, emphasize on the combination of theory and practice, including sketching practice in campus and visiting art museum exhibitions, to improve students' aesthetic judgment and ability of expression, and thus enhance their own cultural confidence.
