课程内容简介：
本课程为2学分的课程，授课时间16周，目的在于通过不同体裁的写作，提高同学们的英语写作能力，为日后学术英语写作做好准备。多采用讨论、学生互评、AI润色修改等教学方法体现愉快学习、逐步提高的过程化教学理念。 pleasant, challenging, but rewarding.
Course Description
LEFL shall be a blessing, not a burden. The process and product shall be pleasant. This course is called Happy English Writing, to a large extent, for it’s plan, process and products: -- It is to help college students to learn how to write, in English, narration, exposition, argumentation (book/movie reviews, abstracts, etc.); -- It will be carried out in a pleasant process in which active involvement and cooperation (AI, students) would be encouraged; -- The products will be pleasant: know how to communicate in the academic field.
