课程内容简介：
课程以艺术品创新制作为切入点，以团队协作进行艺术品创新制作的方式开展对陶艺和精 密铸造的系统体验，使学生切身体验先进的制造技术（如逆向工程、3D打印等），并通过 主题调研和思辨交流环节梳理艺术品的历史、现状和发展趋势，引导学生思考艺术品背后 的文化内涵。以期增强学生的动手操作能力和团队精神，提高学生的工程素养和创新意 识，同时提升学生的审美意识和对中国传统文化的认同感，并增强科学强国的学术志向。
Course Description
The course takes the innovative production of artworks as the starting point, and carries out a systematic experience of ceramic art and precision casting through team collaboration, enabling students to experience advanced manufacturing technologies (such as reverse engineering, 3D printing, etc.) firsthand. Through thematic research and speculative communication, the history, current situation, and development trends of artworks are sorted out, guiding students to think about the cultural connotations behind artworks. In order to enhance students' hands-on operation ability and team spirit, improve their engineering literacy and innovation awareness, enhance their aesthetic awareness and identification with traditional Chinese culture, and enhance their academic aspirations to become a strong country in science.
