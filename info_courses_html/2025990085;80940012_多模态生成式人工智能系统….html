课程简介：
本课程系统讲授生成式人工智能系统的核心原理与工程方法，重点围绕多模态大模型的训练机制、模型结构设计、数据组织策略、生成控制技术与部署优化流程展开。课程内容从大语言模型基础（如 Transformer、对齐机制、RLHF）出发，延伸至多模态对齐预训练、控制式生成、安全性处理、推理加速、系统评测等关键技术，并结合 GPT-4V、Emu2、LLaVA 等代表性系统进行案例拆解与前沿进展讨论。课程采用“理论讲授 + 案例分析 + 文献阅读与讨论”的教学形式，旨在帮助学生建立从底层机制到系统实现的整体认知框架。期末考核以文献综述报告为主，引导学生围绕某一具体子方向开展深入调研与分析。本课程既为从事多模态生成模型研究的学生奠定理论基础，也为未来参与大模型系统设计与优化的学习者提供系统化的知识结构与工程思维。
英文课程简介：
This course provides a systematic introduction to Generative Multimodal AI Systems, focusing on the training paradigms, architectures, data organization strategies, generation mechanisms, and deployment optimizations of Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs). Beginning with foundational concepts of large language models such as Transformer architectures and RLHF, the course extends to vision–language alignment, multimodal prompting, controlled generation, hallucination mitigation, memory-enhanced modeling, and inference acceleration. Lectures are complemented by case studies and guided paper readings, with a final literature review project in which students conduct an in-depth survey on a chosen sub-topic rather than implementing a full system. By the end of the course, students will gain a comprehensive understanding of modern MLLM architectures, current research frontiers, and system-level engineering practices, preparing them for advanced research or industry roles involving generative or multimodal foundation models.
