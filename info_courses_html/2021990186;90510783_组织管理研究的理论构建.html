组织管理研究的理论构建

课程简介：
本课程旨在培养学生在组织管理研究领域的理论构建能力。理论构建能力是组织管理研究学者的一项重要技能，该能力的高低很大程度上决定了学者的研究能力。坚实的理论构建能力是开展一流学术研究的基础。课程设计旨在加强参与者的以下能力： • 提升组织管理研究的综合素养，能够判断区分一流研究的特点和内在规律 • 掌握常见管理理论构建的方法和流程 • 提升基于理论推导论证的能力 • 培养对文献梳理和整合的能力 • 培养提出一流研究问题的能力，并对该研究问题进行理论论述 • 能够将某些具体的管理实践进行理论论述 • 能够流畅严谨的进行学术写作
英文课程简介：
This course aims to develop students' theoretical building skills in the field of organizational research. Theoretical building ability is a fundamental skill for organizational scholars, and the level of this ability largely determines whether scholars can produce high quality research. The course requirements are designed to strengthen the students’ following skills: • Improve overall organizational research skills, and be able to understand the key features of high-quality research • Understand basic approaches to develop theories • Be able to develop theoretical based argument • Develop skills to synthesize relevant literatures • Be able to identify and develop important research questions and develop the research questions into theoretical models • Be able to develop theories based on practical phenomena • Be able to write well and clearly communicate academic ideas
