课程内容简介：
现代生活方式下师生普遍存在“躯体亚健康、生理亚健康、心理亚健康”。通过课程学习，学生可以掌握识别与改善亚健康的方法，引导学生身心健康的生活方式。课程围绕上述三维度亚健康的调护与治理展开，课程内容涉及西医、中医及数智管理等多方面的健康调护理论、技术、方法。中医健康调护与治理以“天人合一”、“阴阳平衡”“形神一体”等理念为核心，融合了“道、儒、释”国学及历代医家健康调护与治理的思想与经验，阐述中医调摄身心、养护生命、预防疾病的基本理论和方法。同时，课程突出了AI技术在协同中西医综合健康管理中的优势与作用。通过理论授课、小班研讨和实践等教学方式，使学生能够深入理解并掌握健康调护与治理方法，应用到实际学习生活中去，改善现代生活节奏下导致的亚健康状态。
Course Description
Under modern lifestyle, teachers and students generally have "physical sub-health, physiological sub-health, and psychological sub-health". Through course learning, students can master methods for identifying and improving sub-health, and guide them towards a healthy lifestyle both physically and mentally. The course revolves around the maintenance and management of sub-health in the aforementioned three-dimensional dimensions, covering various theories, techniques, and methods of health maintenance in Western medicine, traditional Chinese medicine, and digital management. Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) focuses on the concepts of "unity of heaven and man," "balance of yin and yang," and "unity of form and spirit." It integrates the traditional Chinese culture of "Dao, Confucianism, and Buddhism," as well as the ideas and experiences of medical practitioners throughout history, to expound the basic theories and methods of TCM in regulating the body and mind, maintaining life, and preventing diseases. At the same time, the course highlights the advantages and roles of AI technology in collaborative integrated health management of traditional Chinese and Western medicine. Through theoretical lectures, small class discussions, and practical teaching methods, students are able to deeply understand and master health care and management methods, apply them to practical learning and life, and improve sub-health status caused by the modern pace of life.
