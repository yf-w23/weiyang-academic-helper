课程内容简介：
本课程是面向非医学和医学专业、对脑科学研究感兴趣的同学开设的一门跨学科通识课。课程涉及认识大脑、理解大脑到控制大脑等所涉及的基础脑科学知识、心智现象的脑科学解释、先进技术在脑科学的应用、脑科学研究与临床转化、数字智能临床神经科学、脑机接口、神经调控等，还包括大脑3D打印、无创脑机接口体验和尝试脑科学项目开发。课程结合医院实地考察，聚焦前沿脑科学技术如何走向临床诊疗一线，强调跨学科表达与协作能力培养，帮助未来工程师理解医学问题本质，协助未来医生了解工程解决方案原理与边界。本课程适合对脑科学感兴趣的本科生，也适合从事医工交叉的研究生。
Course Description
This course is an interdisciplinary general education course designed for both medical and non-medical students who are interested in neuroscience. The course introduces fundamental knowledge related to understanding the brain, interpreting the mind, and modulating brain function, covering topics such as basic neuroscience, neuroscientific explanations of cognitive and mental phenomena, applications of advanced technologies in neuroscience research, translational neuroscience from laboratory to clinical practice, digital intelligent clinical neuroscience, brain–computer interfaces, and neuromodulation.In addition, the course provides hands-on learning experiences, including 3D brain printing demonstrations, non-invasive brain–computer interface experiments, and opportunities to develop neuroscience-related projects, allowing students to explore both the scientific foundations and innovative applications of modern neuroscience.
