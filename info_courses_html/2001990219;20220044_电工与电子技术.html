课程内容简介：
本课程主要介绍电工、电子技术的基本概念，电工技术部分包括电路的基本分析方法、工业电气设备的使用与控制方法。电子部分包括模拟电路与数字电路的基本构成与分析方法。
Course Description
This course is designed to serve as a basic technical course for non-EE majors, which is divided into circuit analysis and electronics. In the circuit analysis it includes the following topics: fundamental conceptions and basic method of electric circuit analysis, AC network analysis, three-phase circuit, transient analysis, electric machines and rely control system. Next topics are included in the electronics part: semiconductor devices, basic bjt amplifiers, feedback in amplifier circuits, operational amplifier and its applications, diode circuits and DC power supply, digital gates, combinational circuits and sequential circuits.
