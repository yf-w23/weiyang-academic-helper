课程简介：
针对大湾区方向的学生特点，本课程从实践背景切口，以问题导向驱动，激发学生对湾区发展中的关键问题的分析兴趣；基于区域治理理论的学习，提高学生对湾区治理的理论素养和解决方案构建能力，扩宽学生对湾区发展未来前瞻性视角。 课程将采用传统课上教学、小组课程讨论和专家访谈几种方式，在深刻理解湾区发展的全球背景形势基础上，学习湾区发展的基本理论。从产业演进、城市规划、科技创新、能源环境、社会治理等不同维度理解国际四个知名湾区的发展规律、主要成效和机遇挑战；采用多案例比较方法识别国际四个湾区发展的规律性特征和异质性。研讨全球湾区发展的未来远景和挑战。本课程拟邀请来自政府部门、高校和智库的专家学者、业界精英参与授课和专题研讨。
英文课程简介：
In view of the teaching characteristics of the Greater Bay Area direction, this course is driven by problem orientation from the practical background to stimulate students' interest in analyzing the key issues in the Bay Area development; based on the study of regional governance theories, it improves students' theoretical literacy in Bay Area governance and their ability to construct solutions, and broadens students' prospective perspectives on the future of the Bay Area development. The course will use traditional classroom teaching, group discussion and expert interviews to learn the basic theories of Bay Area development based on a deep understanding of the global context of Bay Area development. Understand the development pattern, main achievements and opportunities and challenges of the four well-known international Bay Areas from different dimensions, such as industrial evolution, urban planning, scientific and technological innovation, energy and environment, social governance, etc.; adopt multi-case comparative methods to identify the regular characteristics and heterogeneity of the development of the four international Bay Areas. The course will also discuss the future vision and challenges of global bay area development. Experts and scholars from government departments, universities and think tanks will be invited to participate in the lectures and seminars.
