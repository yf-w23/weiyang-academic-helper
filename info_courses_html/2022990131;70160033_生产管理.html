课程简介：
本课程的课堂讲授包括以下方面：战略与竞争，产能规划，需求预测，综合计划，面向已知需求的库存控制，面向不确定性需求的库存控制，物料需求计划，作业调度，项目调度，设施布置与选址，精益生产系统，约束理论。将在实验室中进行一系列实验：（1）装配生产系统分析与设计；（2）数字化多品种混流装配生产。
英文课程简介：
The course provide lectures on the following topics: strategy and competition, capacity planning, forecasting demand, aggregate planning, inventory control subject to known demand, inventory control subject to uncertain demand, material requirement planning, operations scheduling, project scheduling, facilities layout and location, lean production system, theory of constraints. A series of experiments will be performed in laboratory: (1) analysis and design of assembly production system; (2) digital model-mixed mass assembly production.
