课程内容简介：
本课程为2学分的课程，授课时间16周，目的在于通过不同体裁的写作，提高同学们的英语写作能力，为日后学术英语写作做好准备。根据这种课程设置和教学要求，采用“单周写作，双周评议，期间作业批改”过程化施教方案。
Course Description
This course aims to improve students' writing proficiency through eight writing tasks of different genres which include exposition, arguementation, application letters, abstracts and short stories. Formative assessment and multiple feedback will be adopted in teaching so that students will better understand the task-specific requirements or assessment criteria and develop their reflective learning/writing ability.
进度安排：

Weeks 1-2： Free Writing

Weeks 4 & 5： Writing application letters

Weeks 6-7：Writing argumentations

Weeks 8-9： Writing summaries

Weeks 10-11:  Writing reviews

Weeks 12：Writing short stories

Weeks 13-14： Writing expositions

Week 15： Writing abstracts

Week 16： Peer review & Happy ceremony

 
考核方式：
