课程内容简介：
内容包括： 一．理论篇: 学习正投影基本理论，包括抽象几何元素(点、线、面)的投影及其相对位置关系。 二．基础篇: 学习几何形体的二维视图表达方法，包括基本体及组合体的三视图表达、以机械为背景的机件图样表达方法、尺寸标注等。 三．应用篇: 学习标准件及常用件的画法；学习工程图样的基本画法，包括：零件图的视图方案选择、零件图的画图与读图、零件的尺寸和技术要求标注等；装配体的视图表达方案选择、简单装配体的画图与读图等。 四．现代篇: 学习计算机二维绘图基本方法；学习计算机三维实体建模方法。 以投影理论为基础、图形表达为核心、形象思维为主线，培养学生工程设计表达的基本能力。引进现代设计技术，把手工绘图与计算机绘图、二维视图和三维实体建模结合起来，培养学生现代工程设计表达的基本能力。
Course Description
1. Theory Learn the basic theory of projection, including the projection of abstract geometric elements (points, lines and surfaces) and their relative positional relationships. 2. Fundamentals Learning three view expression of the geometry object, including the basic object and the combined object and the part. Learning the method of dimension, etc. 3. Application articles Learning drawing method of standard parts and common parts, including thread and thread fasteners , keys, gears, bearings, spring, etc. Learning drawing method of engineering drawings, including expression of part drawing, reading and drafting of the part drawing, the dimension of the parts and the technical requirements, etc. Learning the expression of the assembly drawing, learning the method of drawing and reading on the simple assembly. 4. Modern articles Learning computer two-dimensional drawing basic method, learning computer three-dimensional solid modeling method.
