课程内容简介：
讲述经典流体力学，由浅入深。 奠定流体力学基本概念和分析问题方法 内容包括：流体的物理性质、静力学，运动学、流体动力学基础、理想及真实流动特性、气体高速流动、相似及模型实验方法。 从力学理论和工程相结合的角度，讲述经典流体力学基本理论，并结合工程实际。
Course Description
Teaching classical fluid mechanics from the easy to the difficult and complicated. The first five chapters mainly focus on the foundation of thetheory and analysis of methods， the after sixth chapters focus on the different fluid flow characteristics. The contents include: physical properties of fluid, kinematics, fluid dynamics, ideal and real flow characteristics, gas flow at high speed, similarity and model experiment. From the point of view of mechanical theory and engineering, the basic principles and engineering practice are discussed.
