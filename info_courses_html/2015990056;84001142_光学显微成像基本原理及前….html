课程简介：
光学显微成像技术和荧光标记方法革新了现代生物学的研究，特别是过去二十年来光学显微成像技术有了突飞猛进的发展，并在神经科学和细胞生物学等领域得到广泛应用，这使得研究者甚至可以对清醒动物的大脑进行长时间的成像。本课程通过讲解，使学生们了解光学成像的基本原理；通过拆解并组装显微镜，使学生们深入了解显微镜的各部件，理解各部件的功能和作用；通过使用自组装的显微镜进行荧光成像、动物行为追踪、光遗传调控等实验，大大加深学生对光学成像技术的理解。 具体内容包括共轭面的概念、柯勒照明的条件、探测器的基本特征和测量、光的衍射及分辨率、荧光显微镜及荧光分子、各种显微镜的比较（宽场荧光显微镜、激光扫描共聚焦显微镜、转盘共聚焦显微镜、线扫描共聚焦显微镜、爱里扫描共聚焦显微镜、TIRF荧光显微镜、多光子显微镜）、活细胞成像技术、双光子显微镜及其在神经科学中的应用、超分辨显微镜、膨胀显微成像、光片显微镜、全脑快速成像、（大）数据处理等。
英文课程简介：
Optical imaging and fluorescent labeling techniques have revolutionized the research in modern biology. For example, the “father of modern neuroscience” Ramón y Cajal used light microscopy and the Golgi staining method to investigate the nervous system and proposed the “neuron dorctrine”. In the past two decades, there was a huge advancement in light imaging techniques. Nowadays, it is even possible to do long time imaging in awake behaving animals. This class will lecture the basic principles of light microscopy, and will deepen the understanding of basic parts of modern microscope through dissembling and assembling microscopes. The class will also encourage students to use the simple, student-assembled microscope to do a series of experiments, such as fluorescence imaging, animal behavior tracking, optogenetics, et al. This will firmly deepen the understanding of light microscopy. The class will cover the following topics: the concept of conjugate plane, koehler illumination, detectors and their characteristics, light diffraction and imaging resolution, fluorescence microscopy and molecules, comparison of different microscopes, live imaging techniques, two photon imaging and its application in neuroscience, super resolution imaging, expansion microscopy, light sheet microscopy, whole brain fast imaging, (big) data processing et al.
