课程内容简介：
本课程为新生研讨课，采用实验为主的形式，通过典型的实验（体现机械的多学科交叉性，包括机械的设计与制造、机械电子、成形与装备、摩擦学等），增强新生对机械工程学科的研究方向及前沿领域的直观认识和兴趣。具体如下： 1）摩擦学：表界面中的力学效应（表面力与超疏水等）、表界面中的磁光电效应（纳米发电机等）、零摩擦与润滑控制（超滑探索）、冰面摩擦规律等。 2）机械设计：橡塑动密封工作机理探究、机械振动与故障诊断、脑机接口康复机器人等。 3）机械制造：数控切削自动编程、自动生产线的布局实验等。 4）机械电子：智能车机电系统的组装与系统集成、仿人机器人系统等。 5）材料成形：超快激光微纳精密制造、搅拌摩擦焊接与加工、先进材料连接技术等。 6）成形装备：增材制造与生物制造（生物器官）、微流体和微流控芯片技术等。
Course Description
The course mainly adopts the form of experiment, through typical experiments (reflecting the interdisciplinary nature of machinery, including the design and manufacturing of machinery, mechatronics, forming and equipment, tribology, etc.), it can enhance freshmen's intuitive understanding and interest in the research direction and frontier fields of mechanical engineering. The details are as follows: 1) Tribology: mechanical effects at surface interface (surface force and superhydrophobic, etc.), Magnetic photoelectric effect at the surface interface (nano-generator, etc.), Zero friction and lubrication control (super-lubricity exploration), etc. 2) Mechanical design: Mechanism of rubber and plastic dynamic seal, Mechanical vibration and fault diagnosis, Brain computer interface rehabilitation robot, etc. 3) Mechanical manufacturing: CNC cutting automatic programming, Automatic production line layout experiments, etc. 4) Mechatronics assembly and system integration of the electromechanical system of intelligent vehicle, Programming to realize automatic ground finding control of unmanned vehicle, Humanoid robotetcs, etc. 5) Material forming: Ultra-fast laser micro-nano manufacturing, Friction stir welding and processing, Advanced material connection technology, etc. 6) Material forming equipment: Additive manufacturing and biological manufacturing (biological organs), Microfluids and microfluidic chip technology, etc.
