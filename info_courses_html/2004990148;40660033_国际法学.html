课程内容简介：
国际法（这里主要是国际公法）是国家在相互交往中形成的、主要调整国家间关系的有法律拘束力的原则、规则和规章制度的总和，包括条约法、海洋法、国际环境法、国际组织法、国际人权法、外交和领事关系法、争端解决和使用武力等内容。本课主要讲述国际公法在和平时期的解释与适用， 包括基本原则和理论。对上述二级领域不作深入的讲解，同学可以选择有关这些领域开出的选修课（还包括人道法、国际刑法）。另外，国际经济法另有相关课程安排。
Course Description
International Law (here mainly public international law) consists of rules and principles accumulated through inter-state relations. It includes the subjects of treaties, the law of the sea, environmental law, human rights law, diplomatic and consular relations, dispute settlement and resort to force, etc. This course introduces the interpretation and application of this body of law in time of peace, including basic principles and doctrines. But it does not delve deeply into the aforementioned subjects, which (plus humanitarian law and international criminal law) will be suitable as elective courses. Besides, international economic law has its own range of courses.
