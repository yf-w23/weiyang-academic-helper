课程简介：
本课程《能源材料化学》定位为研究生任选课，主要讲授能源储存材料，能源催化转化材料，分离与吸附材料，离子输运材料，光电与热电装换材料等新能源领域中关键材料的化学原理，材料设计以及面临的前沿挑战。解决未来能源问题的关键之一是开发可持续发展的新能源。新能源的开发、装换和储运具有高效性、环保性和安全性，而这些过程和目标的实现离不开材料化学的发展及其新技术的突破。本课程将从化学和材料学的角度，对能源、环境领域中的关键材料进行介绍；同时将结合化学领域研究前沿，金属有机框架材料和共价有机框架材料，探讨其在能源/环境领域的应用。本课程除要求学生了解能源/环境面临的挑战，及其对应的材料化学发展概况之外，还需要学生针对某一能源材料化学领域，能结合自身的知识或兴趣，提出自己的见解并在课程末总结成一份简要的材料设计或研究计划。
英文课程简介：
"Energy Material Chemistry" is an optional course for graduate students. The major contents of this course are the principles, material design, and frontier challenges of energy materials, including energy storage materials, energy catalytic/conversion materials, separation and adsorption materials, ion transport materials, photoelectric/thermoelectric materials, etc. One key to overcome the future energy challenge is to develop sustainable new energy sources. The development, replacement, storage, and transportation of new energy are highly efficient, environmentally friendly and safe; the realization of these processes and goals is inseparable from the development of material chemistry and breakthroughs in new technologies. This course will introduce key materials in energy and environmental science, from the perspective of chemistry and materials science. At the same time, it will combine research frontiers in the field of chemistry —— metal-organic framework materials and covalent organic framework materials, and explore their applications in the energy/environmental field. This course will not only require students to understand the challenges faced by various new energy technologies, but also learn their origins in basic science. And finally, And finally, students will be able to make a brief material design or research plan, according to their knowledge or interests.
