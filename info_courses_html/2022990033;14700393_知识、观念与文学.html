课程内容简介：
文学作品的生成与传播与社会的整体构造密不可分。我们今天所熟知的文学经典经历了漫长的历史过程，是不同时代的作者、读者、编者共同努力的结果，他们的写作、评议和编辑活动，集中呈现了从文学延发而来的关于道德、政制、个人、审美等一系列观念。本课通过精读中国文学经典作品和经典研究，从文学的构造形式及其与社会、历史的辩证关系出发，系统探讨中国文学传统中的四个重要主题：礼乐、书写与传唱，神话、历史与演绎，世情与社会，以及现代性的转型。本课程将围绕以下核心问题展开：以文学作为对象和途径，作者、评论家、编者和研究者试图在各自的知识视野下表达什么样的政治、哲学和社会思想？20世纪的现代学术在中西比较的视野和“传统-现代”转型的框架下，如何整理中国古代的文学传统？在现代科学的认识论影响下，中国学者如何接续讨论有关文学和历史、社会之关系的一系列重要议题？
Course Description
The birth and circulation of literature deeply engages with the whole structure of society. The masterpieces of literature, as what we have well known today, were created through a long historical process. Readers, writers, and editors from various periods contributed to the formation of these canonic works. Their practice of writing, commenting, and editing reflected a series of ideas such as those related to morality, politics, individual, and aesthetics. Through a critical reading of selected Chinese literature and a careful analysis on important studies, this class aims to investigate the formulation of literature and its dialectic relation to society and history. It systematically discusses four significant themes in the field of literary studies: ritual, writing, and orality; myth, history, and fiction; worldly affairs and society; and the modern transformation. This class is organized based on following questions: how did authors, commentors, editors, and scholars frame literature in their own perspective of knowledge and express their ideas on politics, philosophy and society? In the twentieth century, from comparative perspectives between China and the West and between “tradition” and “modernity”, how did modern scholarship adapt the premodern literary tradition? And under the influence of modern scientific epistemology, how did Chinese intellectuals continue the discussions on the relations among literature, history, and society?
进度安排：

 第1周：导论 （多重视野中的中国文学经典）

第2-5周：礼乐、传唱与书写 （主题包括：《诗经》与诗教；赋、文学与政制；诗歌的传唱与书写；歌舞、宗教与民间）

第6-8周：神话、历史与演绎 （主题包括：“正统”与“治乱”视角下的历史小说；神话与古史/历史/当代史；神话（志怪）与社会）

第9-11周：世情与社会 （主题包括：消闲品鉴文化；“才子佳人”与女性文学；儒者世界）

第12-15周：现代文学的兴起 （主题包括：找寻“白话”；找寻“真实”；找寻“科学”；文学与革命）

第16周：总论
