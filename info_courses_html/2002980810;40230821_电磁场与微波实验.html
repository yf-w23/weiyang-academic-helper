课程内容简介：
本课程是在《电磁场与波》/《电动力学》的基础上，为促进学生深入理解和掌握电磁场相关原理知识的实验课程，它将为学生今后从事相关领域的学习和科研工作打下坚实的基础。 课程针对电磁场“看不见、摸不着”的特点，以动画演示的方式，为同学们讲解电磁场在各类传输线、微波元器件、自由空间中的传播特性，包括电磁场的分布、时域特性、频域特性、辐射特性等。 操作内容在三套实验系统上完成，电磁波综合测试仪、测量线系统、矢量网络分析仪。电磁波综合测试仪上涉及电磁场的内容，包括电磁波的传播特性、反射折射特性、极化特性等；测量线系统的内容有利于学生建立场的驻波分布概念，从而进一步理解场的分布和传播理论；矢量网络分析仪是应用于射频和微波频率的先进测试仪器，利用这个仪器，学生可以进行关于各类微波元器件的阻抗、匹配、传输参数等特性的测量研究。 课程还通过微波仿真软件ADS（Advanced Design System）的熟悉和使用来掌握微带线的基本原理和特性，建立了更加完善的电磁场与微波实验教学体系内容。
Course Description
This course is based on the courses-"Electromagnetic Fields and Waves" and "Electrodynamics", in order to promote students' in-depth understanding and mastering of knowledge. The course will help the students lay the solid foundation for their future study and scientific research in related fields. Because of the invisible and untouchable electromagnetic field, this course uses the animated demonstration to explain the electromagnetic field propagation characteristics in various transmission lines, microwave components and free space, including the distribution of electromagnetic fields, time-domain , frequency-domain and radiation characteristics. Three sets of instruments will be used, which are Electromagnetic Wave Tester, Slotted Line Measure System, Vector Network Analyzer. Electromagnetic Wave Tester is used to study propagation characteristics, such as refraction and refraction properties, polarization characteristics. Introducing the frontier knowledge of scientific research to understand the characteristics of frequency selection. Slotted Line Measure System is used to establish the concept of standing wave distribution in order to further understand the field distribution and propagation theory. Vector Network Analyzer is an advanced test instrument for RF and microwave frequencies. With VNA, students can measure various microwave components’ impedance, match the components, and study their transmission characteristics. The course also uses the microwave simulation software-ADS to let the students grasp the basic principles and characteristics of microstrip line. The course helps students to master the microwave measurement methods, and learn to use common measuring instruments and software. These will fully develop students' professional abilities.
