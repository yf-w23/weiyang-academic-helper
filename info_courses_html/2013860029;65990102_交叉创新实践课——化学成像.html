课程简介：
化学成像是近年来出现的前沿领域，与传统的将研究对象视为均相体系的化学研究不同，运用最新的成像测量技术，获得化学反应众多参数的空间分布和时间变化，为基础研究和工程实践都提供了全新的表征和测试手段。 本课程以化学成像这一领域的前沿成果出发，引导学生进行团队的探索性研究实验。这一真实完整的科学研究流程，将提高学生的科学素养、交叉学科视野和团队合作能力。
英文课程简介：
Chemical imaging is a frontier field emerged in recent years. Different from traditional chemical research that regards the research object as a homogeneous system, chemical imaging used the latest imaging technology to obtain the spatial distribution and temporal variation of multiple chemical reaction parameters, providing new means of characterization and testing for both fundamental research and engineering practice. Starting from the cutting-edge achievements in the field of chemical imaging, this course will guide students to conduct team exploratory experiments. This real and complete scientific research process will improve students' scientific literacy, interdisciplinary vision and teamwork ability.
