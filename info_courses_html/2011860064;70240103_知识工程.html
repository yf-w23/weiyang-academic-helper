课程简介：
本课程主要介绍知识工程中有关知识表示、知识推理、机器学习和智能化系统的理论和算法。结合当前网络环境下大规模数据和知识处理和网络技术的发展，介绍Web知识发现，以及当今IT领域里受到各界重视的"语义Web"、"Web 服务"等主流技术
英文课程简介：
Knowledge engineering is an important field of artificial intelligence. The course will introduce the key technologies about knowledge engineering on the Web. Combining the development of big data, knowledge graph and Semantic Web, we will systematically introduce the major theories and algorithms on knowledge representation, knowledge acquisition, semantic integration, knowledge sharing and intelligent services in the Internet.
