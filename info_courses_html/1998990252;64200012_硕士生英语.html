课程简介：
本课程通过介绍英美等国风俗习惯、文化背景等知识进行若干听力训练项目和实用性口语训练项目，突出了多种情形下的英语调研采访活动，培养学生进行学术英语演讲的能力。同时，本课程通过对学术文本的分析帮助学生掌握英语论文写作规范，并能完成学术论文相关项目。该课程选材新颖、题材广泛、语言规范。
英文课程简介：
This course aims to improve the abilities to 1）understand highly-informative spoken discourse 2）evaluate the speakers’ opinions, stance, attitude, intentions 3）identify weaknesses in the opposing argument and refute promptly 4）summarize main ideas 5）describe events /issues /books /movies /technology… 6）express personal opinions on academic and global issues 7）express main ideas and supporting details of arguments 8）produce clear, fluent, accurate and intelligible speeches and written pieces 9）read widely in one’s field

进度安排：

 

  1. Instruction for Task A: Listening comprehension (15%)

Some listening assignments are to be issued and the answers should be submitted to the E-classroom（网络学堂）as the course processes. (No chance to make up.)

 

  1. Instruction for Task B: Watching & discussion on Ted talks (10%)

Team members watch and discuss on the talks from the TED talk list provided by the teacher (or you may choose a talk from www.ted.com ). Then the team choose ONE of the talks to generate positive or negative arguments on the topic and the communication skills. Each team member makes a 5-minute solo presentation on discussion arguments. Afterwards the whole class may join them to discuss. This discussion section will be made in Week 3 --10 in class.

 

  1. Instruction for Task C: Team presentation (incl. ppt) (20%)

Every student is required to participate in team presentation in about 25 minutes on a contemporary academic topic, in Week 6 -14. The listeners should try to ask questions or make comments after the presentation or speech. The speaker should be able to answer the questions or respond to the comments. The form can be:

-    a debate                                         -- a news talk show on a formal topic

-    a panel discussion                         -- a survey 

-    program promotion                      -- a conference

(Be creative with the presentation form and team ppt is required and submitted after the show by the team leader.)

 

  1. Instruction for Task D: Reading comprehension (15%)

 

  1. Instruction for Task E: Reading academic materials & writing report / review (25%)
