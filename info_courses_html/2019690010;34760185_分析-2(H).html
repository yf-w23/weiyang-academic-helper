Course Description
This course covers the theory of differentiation and integration of multivariable functions. It starts from the definitions of continuity and derivative of a multivariable function and continues through applications of Fourier series. Topics include partial derivatives and differentials, the inverse and implicit function theorems, the tangent space and normal vector of a hypersurface, differential and integral calculus of submanifolds, Stokes’ theorem, calculation of integrals on submanifolds, the divergence theorem and Green’s theorem, and Fourier series and their applications.
