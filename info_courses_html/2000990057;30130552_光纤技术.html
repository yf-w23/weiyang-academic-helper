课程内容简介：
本课程讲授内容全面，重点突出。从光的电磁理论出发，全面地论述光在光纤中传输和传感的基本特性及技术。第一部分为光纤中光传输和传感的基本理论，是本课程的重点内容，主要包括光纤传输的模式理论和模耦合理论，光纤的损耗、色散和偏振特性以及光纤传感的原理。第二部分为各类光纤和光纤处理工艺，全面地介绍特种和新型光纤结构和光纤处理方法，第三部分为光纤的应用，深入地介绍由光纤构成的各种有源和无源器件以及各种光纤传感器件。 本课程侧重基础，注重对基本概念和理论分析结果的物理意义的阐述；本课程学以致用，较全面地介绍光纤技术和应用，包括应用的基本原理，光纤器件和制作工艺以及一些应用实例和典型参数。 本课程安排了实验内容，通过使用光纤商用软件，帮助学生理解和掌握光纤中的模式以及模式间耦合的基本特点和规律；通过线偏振环形光纤激光器制备和性能测试实验，使同学们深入理解光纤激光器工作原理，了解各种光纤器件性能和使用方法，学会高质量光纤端面处理和光纤连接方法，以及光纤激光器输出性能测试方法。
Course Description
This course has comprehensive contents and prominent key points. Starting with the electromagnetic theory of light, the course mainly introduces the characteristics and technology of light propagating and sensing in the optical fiber. In the first section of the course, the basic theory about the propagation and sensing in the optical fiber is introduced, which is the key content of this course. This section mainly includes the theory about mode and mode coupling, the loss, dispersion and polarization of the optical fiber as well as the principle of fiber sensing. The second section of this course consists of various kinds of optical fibers and fiber processing including the structures of special fibers and novel fibers as well as the fiber processing techniques. The last section, which is the application of optical fibers, introduces a variety of active and passive devices made of optical fibers and all sorts of fiber sensors. This course focuses on teaching of the basic concepts and the physical explanation of theoretical analyses. This course also values the application by comprehensively introducing the principle of optical fiber application, the manufacturing techniques of fiber and fiber devices, application examples and typical parameters. Experiments are included in this course. Through using commercial optical fiber software, students will obtain a better understanding of the basic features and rules of mode and mode coupling in the optical fiber. Through the experiment of linearly polarized ring fiber laser, students can deeply understand the working principle of the fiber laser, learn to use various fiber optic devices. The experiment will help students master the common methods of fiber end processing and splicing.
