课程内容简介：
本课程是以美国对外政策及其国内政治根源为主要授课内容的概论性课程。 课程的教学目标是（1）使学生初步掌握有关美国当代政治、外交、安全以及中美关系的基本的知识性常识；（2）初步了解学术界对相关问题的不同观点及各自理据，理解国际关系以及社会科学的复杂性；（3）初步具有政策研究意识，了解政策研究的基本问题、方法路径与工具，锻炼从事政策研究分析的综合能力。 本课程在兼顾理论与历史的基础上，将努力突出美国政治与外交的政策研究和动态研究视角，并努力培养学生对美国政治与外交的复杂思维习惯。 本课程主要内容分为四个单元：（1）美国政治与外交的基本制度设计；（2）美国政治与外交思想传统；（3）美国外交的制定过程；（4）美国外交中的主要议题。
Course Description
This course is an introductory course that focuses on U.S. foreign policy and its domestic political roots. The objectives of the course are: (1) to provide students with a basic knowledge of contemporary U.S. politics, foreign policy, security, and Sino-U.S. relations; (2) to gain an understanding of the different views on related issues and the respective rationales behind each view, so to understand the complexity of international relations and social sciences; (3) to gain a preliminary exposure to policy research, understand the basic issues, method and approaches of policy research, and develop the skill to engage in policy research and analysis. Taking into account both theory and history, this course will strive to highlight the policy research perspectives of American politics and foreign policy, and endeavor to cultivate students' habits of complex thinking about American politics and foreign policy. The main content of this course is divided into four units: (1) institutional design of American politics and foreign policy; (2) the tradition and thoughts of American political and foreign policy; (3) the process of making American foreign policy; and (4) the major issues in American foreign policy.
