课程内容简介：
本课程是统计学核心分支课程，以无分布假设的统计推断与建模为主线，系统讲授非参数统计的理论基石、经典方法与现代技术。课程从次序统计量、经验分布、置换检验、二项检验等基础理论出发，层层递进讲授单样本 / 配对样本、两独立样本、多样本非参数检验与估计，覆盖符号检验、Wilcoxon 符号秩检验、Mann‑Whitney U 检验等经典秩检验，深入讲解渐近相对效率（ARE）与方法比较；拓展秩相关分析（Spearman/Kendall）、分类数据推断、稳健估计等实用内容，并引入核密度估计（KDE）、非参数回归等现代非参数建模技术。 课程坚持理论与实操并重，配套 R 语言案例教学，实现各类非参数统计分析，强化方法理解与工程实现能力。通过本课程学习，学生将掌握不依赖分布假定的稳健统计工具，能够独立完成数据探索、统计推断与非参数建模，真正理解非参数统计的思想本质与应用价值，为实证研究与数据分析打下坚实专业基础。
Course Description
This course is a core branch of statistics, centered on distribution-free statistical inference and modeling. It systematically covers the theoretical foundations, classical methods, and modern techniques of nonparametric statistics. Starting with fundamental theories including order statistics, empirical distribution, permutation tests, and binomial tests, the course progresses to nonparametric tests and estimation for one-sample, paired-sample, two-independent-sample, and multi-sample settings. It encompasses classical rank tests such as sign test, Wilcoxon signed-rank test, and Mann‑Whitney U test, and elaborates on asymptotic relative efficiency (ARE) and comparisons among testing methods. It further extends to practical topics including rank correlation analysis (Spearman/Kendall), categorical data inference, and robust estimation, as well as modern nonparametric modeling techniques such as kernel density estimation (KDE) and nonparametric regression. The course emphasizes the integration of theory and practice, supported by case studies using the R programming language to implement various nonparametric statistical analyses, enhancing both methodological understanding and computational skills. Upon completion, students will master robust statistical tools free from distributional assumptions, be capable of independently conducting data exploration, statistical inference, and nonparametric modeling, and gain a deep understanding of the essence and applications of nonparametric statistics, laying a solid professional foundation for empirical research and data analysis.
