课程简介：
本课是为经济学以及其他社会科学学科研究生设计的。课程从理论与实践出发，重点讲授参数、半参数及非参数方法在因果推断和项目评估中的应用。课程目标是使学生掌握应用微观经济学实证研究的经典设计，为理解其他前沿微观计量方法打下基础，从而能够在经济学、金融学及其他社会科学领域的实证问题中使用恰当的计量方法开展研究。
英文课程简介：
This course is designed for graduate students in economics and other social science disciplines who want to take a solid course in applied microeconometrics. It mainly focuses on the methodological and practical aspects of parametric, semiparametric and nonparametric techniques for causal inference and program evaluation. The goal is to introduce canonical research designs in empirical microeconomics and provide a foundation for understanding more sophisticated cutting-edge econometric methods, which enables Ph.D. students in economics, finance and other social science disciplines to apply appropriate quantitative methods in their independent research.
