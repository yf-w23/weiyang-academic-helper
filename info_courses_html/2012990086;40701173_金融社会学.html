课程内容简介：
金融是当今社会重要的组成部分和社会制度，其社会属性和社会后果需要从社会学的视角进行全面而深入的考察和研究。本课从金融的本质出发，着重探讨其自然、经济、政治和社会的多重属性以及不同属性之间的关系，围绕金融与创新创业、金融与收入财富分配、金融与社会信任、金融产品和技术的社会影响、金融风险与金融监管、金融与社会结构、金融与社会公平等议题进行讲解和讨论。
Course Description
Finance is an important societal component and institution in modern society. This course aims to examine and reflect upon the social characteristics and social consequences of the financial world. The course explores the essences of finance and examines its natural, economic, political and social characteristics with a focus on the following topics, including finance and innovation & entrepreneurship, finance and income & wealth distribution, finance and social trust, financial products and technologies, financial risks and governance, finance and social structure, and finance and social justice and equity, etc.
