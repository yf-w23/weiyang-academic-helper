课程内容简介：
选取西方思想史上最主要的经典，采取经史结合的方式进行讨论与讲解，在阐释这些思想经典的基本思想与核心精神的同时，阐述这些思想与精神如何影响、塑造西方的历史和社会，特别将阐述中古与近代西方思想经典如何与古希腊文明与希伯莱文明的核心精神相融合，开创出一个新的思想时代，并影响和规定了现代社会。
Course Description
The human history can be divided into ancient society and modern society, that correspond to different stage of human thought. The evolution of society is accompanied by the evolution of human thought, and even guided by the change of their principles. Ancient society is founded on five principles: principle of finality, principle of primacy of the Good, principle of duty and obedience, collective priority over individual, legitimacy of power based on transcendence. Since the other principles must be applied through the principle of duty, it is on the principle of duty that social order and human relationship are formed in ancient society. However, Enlightenment awakens the consciousness of liberty by providing solid arguments, and establishes new principles: priority of liberty, individual priority over collective, parallelism of right and duty, legitimacy of power based on the agreement of people. The manifestation of these principles is the principle of right, which is also the foundation of modern social order. This course will show this evolution by discussing the following questions: How did human thought establish the principles of ancient society? Why did it abandon them? How were the principle of modern society formed? Why does human have duty and right?
进度安排：

第一讲讨论何谓现代性社会？现代性社会与古代社会的根本区别。

第二讲至第六讲，讨论古希腊神话的命运观，希腊哲学对命运观的否定，以及希腊哲学如何确立起“科学思维“。

第七讲至第十讲讨论中古时期基督教哲学如何克服希腊哲学遗留下来的理论困境，同时给哲学贡献了哪些新问题与新思想。

第十一讲至第十五讲讨论近代哲学如何确立起一系列构建现代性社会秩序之基础的原则体系。从哲学上澄清古今之别。

主要讨论霍布斯、洛克、卢梭到康德的核心思想。
