课程内容简介：
课程内容将围绕力学和航空航天中的科研探索和工程应用问题，讲授近代力学和工程动力学等领域的测量方法和技术的基本原理、实验设备和操作过程，并通过大量的实验培养学生的动手能力、数据分析能力以及理论和实践相结合的分析能力。课程包括课堂教学和专业实验两个环节。课堂教学将涵盖固体力学、流体力学、结构动力学等领域的前沿测量和分析技术，包括数据处理与误差分析、相似理论和量 纲分析、电阻应变测量技术、光测力学；风洞实验、测压及测速，涡量、流线、雷诺应力及 涡脱落频率；振动传感器、动态信号处理等。在专业实验环 节，包括基本的图像和信号分析与处理实验、电阻应变测量、材料力学行为测量、复杂结构的应变测量、全息干涉位移测量、散斑动静态变形测量、云纹应变测量、光弹性应力分析、 流体内力测量、圆柱尾流测量、平板边界层测量、振动传感器标定、多种传感器简谐振动基 本参数测量、振动信号基本性能分析、DSA主要分析功能的应用等。
Course Description
The course content focus on scientific research exploration and engineering application issues in mechanics and aerospace, teaching the basic principles, experimental equipment, and operational processes of modern mechanics and engineering dynamics. Through a large number of experiments, students will be trained in hands-on ability, data analysis ability, and analytical ability that combines theory and practice. Classroom teaching will cover cutting-edge measurement and analysis technologies in fields such as solid mechanics, fluid mechanics, and structural dynamics, including, electrical resistance strain gages, photomechanics, wind tunnel test, pressure and velocity, vorticity, streamline, Reynolds stress and vortex shedding; vibration sensors, dynamic signal processing. In the professional experimental stage, including basic image and signal analysis and processing experiments, resistance strain measurement, material mechanics behavior measurement, strain measurement of complex structures, holographic interferometric measurement, speckle and moiré strain measurement, photoelastic stress analysis; fluid internal force, cylindrical wake, and plate boundary layer measurements; vibration sensor calibration, and measurement of basic parameters of multiple sensors for harmonic vibration Basic performance analysis of vibration signals, and application of DSA main analysis functions.
