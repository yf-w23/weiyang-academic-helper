课程简介：
本课程的目的是介绍政府与市场经济学最新研究成果。 政府与市场经济学是经济学的新分支，其产生直接背景是改革开放以来中国经济的快速发展。政府与市场经济学最有可能成为中国经济思想与实践创造具有普遍意义的经济学新知的典范，类似于英国工业革命后的资本主义凝练出马克思政治经济学理论。政府与市场经济学更为久远的背景是工业革命以后尤其是后工业时代，政府事实已经成为经济体中为重要的行为主体，一般经济体中三分之一以上的经济活动是直接或间接经过政府的运作而进行的，政府的作用直接决定了一个经济体运行表现。长期以来，经济学研究政府的作用极为简化而无法准确分析现实，这些研究或者假设政府是社会福利的代表者，全心全意为所有人服务的，或者假设政府追逐个人利益。政府与市场经济学基于大量的实证研究，分析政府机构的行为、激励、作用及其相关的制度设计，把政府机构的行为作为研究对象，从而更好的理解政府在市场经济中的作用，进而提供改进政府行为和激励的机制设计，终极目标改善经济的运行效率和社会福祉。
英文课程简介：
The purpose of the course is to introduce the latest research in government and economics, which is an emerging new branch of economics. Government and economics as a new branch of economics is directly motivated by the rapid emergence of the Chinese economy and other emerging market economies, in which the role of the government has been particularly prominent. The larger background is that government, in reality, has become the single largest and most influential entity in all economies after the industrial revolution and in all of the post-industrial economies, accounting for easily over one third of all economic activities. Government and economics may well become the area of new knowledge that economics learnt from the rapid development of the Chinese economy, just like the British economy after industrial revolution gave rise to theories of Marxist political economy. Until now, economics mostly has assumed that the government is a social welfare maximizer or a self-interested agent. In government and economics, the role, the incentives, and the behavior of the government are the subjects of study. This way, government and economics not only better understands the operations of the government in the economy but also help propose alternative institutions of government in the economy and therefore more desirable economic performance.
