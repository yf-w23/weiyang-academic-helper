课程简介：
调度是一类解决稀缺资源合理利用的优化问题，在生产和服务系统中具有极其重要的作用，也是目前以智能互联为特点的智能系统中的核心功能之一。在生产系统中，它是根据生产计划、作业计划、技术工艺文件以及市场变化的情况，在生产过程中落实、监督、协调生产计划中的各项指标，保证按时完成生产任务的决策。本课程以讲授生产调度的基本理论及其应用为主，介绍与调度相关的经典模型建模、基本算法设计、性能评估、计算复杂度证明的方法，以及调度领域当前的最新研究成果和在实际中的应用。本课程作为研究生课程，力图使其理解并掌握调度的基本理论、方法和研究范式，为之后的相关理论和应用研究奠定较为扎实的基础。
英文课程简介：
Scheduling is a kind of optimization problem to solve the rational utilization of scarce resources. It plays an extremely important role in production and service systems. It is also one of the core functions of intelligent systems characterized by intelligent interconnection. In the production system, it is a decision to implement, supervise and coordinate various indicators in the production plan in the production process according to the production plan, operation plan, technical process documents and market changes, so as to ensure the completion of production tasks on time. This course focuses on the basic theory and application of production scheduling, and introduces the classical model modeling, basic algorithm design, performance evaluation, computational complexity proof methods related to scheduling, as well as the latest research results and practical applications in the field of scheduling. As a graduate course, this course tries to make it understand and master the basic theory, method and research paradigm of scheduling, and construct a foundation for the subsequent related theory and application research.
