课程内容简介：
本课程将采用案例、研讨、能源系统设计实战相结合的教学方法，旨在使学生深入理解能源技术如何服务于人类社会发展，同时又受限于社会发展。进而尝试运用所获得理念系统创新性地设计与制造适用于特定应用场景的能源系统。 课程内容分为三个部分，包括： 1. 能源技术基础：重点介绍能源系统转化、利用及储存的理论基础及前沿发展，辅以案例和研讨，拟安排23学时，以讲授和研讨为主； 2. 能源技术小实践：以生活中常见的能源装置制作为初步的实践项目，拟安排3学时，以实践为主； 3. 能源与社会场景搭建：设置不同选题，通过文献调研确定能源系统组合方案，搭建适用于特定场景的发电、储能系统与装置，拟安排6学时，以小组合作实践为主。
Course Description
This course will adopt the teaching method of combining case study, discussion and energy system design, aiming to make students deeply understand how energy technology can serve the development of human society, but also limited by social development. Then try to use the obtained ideas to design and manufacture the energy system suitable for specific application scenarios. The course is divided into three parts 1. Basis of energy technology: it focuses on the theoretical basis and frontier development of energy system transformation, utilization and storage, supplemented by cases and discussion. It is planned to arrange 14 class hours, mainly teaching and discussion; 2. Small practice of energy technology: Taking the production of common energy devices in daily life as a preliminary practice project, it is planned to arrange 6 class hours; 3. Construction of energy and social scenarios: set up different topics, determine the combination scheme of energy system through literature research, and build power generation and energy storage systems and devices suitable for specific scenarios. It is planned to arrange 12 class hours.
