课程简介：
算法设计与分析是计算机科学领域最基础最重要的组成部分。本课程向学生介绍高级算法设计与分析，侧重于大数据应用。主题涵盖了随机算法、近似算法、并行算法和基于人工智能的算法设计。更具体而言，课程将会涉及到哈希表算法，在线算法，线型规划和半定规划，网络算法，随机漫步，字符串算法和数据结构，近邻搜索的许多有用的例子。
英文课程简介：
Algorithm design and analysis are fundamental parts of computer science. This course introduces advanced techniques for algorithm design and analysis, with an emphasis on big data applications. The topics cover string algorithms, hash functions, locality-sensitive hashing, approximation algorithms, randomized algorithms, parallel algorithms, and AI-based algorithms. Students will gain a deeper understanding of the design, analysis, and implementation of efficient algorithms for a wide range of applications. Prerequisite: One undergraduate or graduate course in algorithms
