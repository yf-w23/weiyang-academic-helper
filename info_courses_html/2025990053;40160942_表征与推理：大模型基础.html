课程内容简介：
本课程以大语言模型为核心对象，围绕“表征与推理”这一人工智能的基本问题展开系统讲授。课程重点介绍大模型的表征机制，包括词嵌入这一基础表示方式、依赖上下文的动态语义表征，以及深层网络中逐步涌现的复杂知识结构；同时讨论大模型的推理能力，如链式思维、检索增强与工具使用，并引入近年来有关大模型的最新研究进展。通过连续性的编程作业，学生将亲手实现一个小型语言模型，从构建分词器和嵌入层出发，逐步完成模型结构、预训练与后训练流程，全面理解大模型的原理、能力与局限。
Course Description
This course takes large language models (LLMs) as its central focus and provides a systematic exploration of the fundamental AI problem of “representation and reasoning.” It emphasizes the mechanisms of representation in LLMs, including basic word embeddings, context-dependent dynamic semantic representations, and the complex knowledge structures that emerge in deep networks. At the same time, it examines the reasoning capabilities of LLMs, such as chain-of-thought reasoning, retrieval-augmented generation, and tool use, while also incorporating recent advances in LLM research. Through a sequence of programming assignments, students will implement a toy language model from scratch, starting with the construction of a tokenizer and embedding layer, and progressively completing model architecture, pre-training, and post-training. By the end of the course, students will have a comprehensive understanding of the principles, capabilities, and limitations of large language models.
