课程内容简介：
本课程以实际科研过程中所需要的能力和面临的科学本身及专业知识以外的各种挑战为中心，增进学生对科研过程需要的能力以及作为科研人员或工程师的多方面认识。以讲授、学术界和工业界邀请演讲、科研实践、讨论互动、以及模拟会议报告等形式，为学生提供与科研相关的方法、经验分享、不同性质的前沿课题研究、科研及工程师的专业伦理、安全规范相关的内容。
Course Description
This course aims to develop students’ capability to perform scientific research as well as other challenges being considered during the process of conducting research and technology development. Course content includes basic methodologies of scientific research, invited talks from both industry and academia, revisit of recent research problems, methods, and analyses, introduction to professional ethics in scientific research and engineering, safety and regulations for conducting scientific research. The course will be offered in the forms of lectures, group discussion and interactions, invited talks, conducting actual research problems, and mock procedures and presentations of international conferences for evaluation of final projects.

选课指导：
基于能源动力工程烽火班的课程安排及人才培养目标，本课程针对掌握了基础能源动力的专业知识的大三本科生，为曾经或正在从事本科SRT研究、毕业设计以及未来继续从事研究工作奠定较良好的基础。
