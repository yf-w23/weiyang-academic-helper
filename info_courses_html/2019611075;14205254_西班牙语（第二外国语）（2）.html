课程内容简介：
该课程为基础西班牙语教学，面向修完二外西班牙语1和具有同等西班牙语基础的学生开 设。遵照“听说领先”和“循序渐进”的原则，在课前、课上和课下以学生为主体，目的 是强健学生自身的语言创造机制，使其通过一学期的西班牙语学习，在听、说、读、写方 面达到欧洲共同语言参考框架（Common European Framework of Reference for Languages,CEFR西班牙语B1-B2级水平，可以使用西班牙语进行基本交流，掌握正确的发音 规则，能够流利朗读西班牙语文章，同时掌握西班牙语动词陈述式一般现在时、虚拟式现 在时、肯定命令式、否定命令式的变位规则，掌握宾格代词和与格代词、自复被动句与无 人称句、副动词与过去分词、直接引语与间接引语、定语从句与宾语从句、主要前置词等 内容的基本用法。此外，本课寓文化教育于语言教学之中，使学生通过语言学习，从各个 方面了解并热爱西班牙语国家文化。旨在开拓学生视野，丰富人文知识，了解新的思维方 式，同时帮助学生拓宽继续深造学习和就业的选择面，进一步提高学生的综合竞争能力。
Course Description
The course is based on Spanish language teaching and is designed for students who have completed Spanish (Second Foreign Language) (1) or students with an equivalent Spanish level. In accordance with the principle of “listening and speaking come first” and “learning step by step”, students are the leading role before class, in class and after class. The purpose is to strengthen the students' own language using capability, so that they can reach B1-B2 level of Spanish（Common European Framework of Reference for Languages, CEFR, in listening, speaking, reading and writing through one semester of Spanish learning. In addition, this course brings cultural education to language teaching, enabling students to learn and love Spanish-speaking culture from all aspects through language learning. It aims to broaden students' horizons, enrich human knowledge, understand new ways of thinking, and help students to broaden their choices for further study and employment, and further improve students' comprehensive competitiveness.
