课程内容简介：
预测是对尚未发生或目前还不明确的事物进行预先的估计和推测。对于电网企业，预测是其进行规划、计划、营销（用电）、市场交易、调度运行等部门的重要工作之一；对于发电企业，预测也是其电源规划、发电计划、购煤计划、报价决策等工作的依据。 1） 本课程主要讲授应用于电力系统的预测方法和技术，包括中长期电力需求预测、短期负荷预测、母线负荷预测、电力市场电价预测、可再生能源预测（如风电场风速预测、光伏出力预测等）。 2） 本课程还将讲授智能用电大数据、机器学习、深度学习等相关知识，并讲授R语言、Python等工具的使用。 3） 通过本课程的学习，系统地掌握预测的基本原则和具体方法，对学生将来从事相关工作大有裨益。 每学期可推举一名同学获得国际预测学会International Institute of Forecaster (IIF) - Student Award
Course Description
Forecasting is the process of estimation and inference on something not yet happened. For power grid enterprise, forecasting is closely related to planning, marketing and operation. For power generation enterprise, forecasting is essential to generation planning, coal preparation planning, and optimal bidding. 1） This course focuses on power system forecasting methods and technologies, including medium to long term power demand forecasting, short term load forecasting, ultra-short term load forecasting, bus load forecasting, electricity market price forecasting, and renewable energy forecasting (e.g. wind speed forecasting and PV output forecasting). 2） This course intends to teach smart meter big data, machine learning, R and Python languages. 3） Students will benefit from the basic forecasting principal and method in their future work.
