课程内容简介：
本课程以金融基本理论为基础，介绍金融市场的基本结构、运行逻辑、主要参与主体、主要工具，帮助学生理解现代金融市场的运行规律与金融机构的运作方式。除了覆盖成熟金融市场的相关内容之外，课程还将广泛涉及中国金融市场的多项议题。 课程主要内容包括：（1）金融理论基础；（2）金融市场基本运行机制，包括金融市场机制、中央银行、货币政策、金融监管；（3）金融机构的运行与管理，包括各类金融机构的性质、运作及发展展望；（4）金融工具的设计与应用；（5）中国金融市场热点问题。 课程以课堂讲授与讨论为主，同时也将采用案例分析与汇报、专家讲座等多种授课方式。
Course Description
This introductory course at the beginner’s level starts with the principles of finance and related market practices, to help students understand the dynamics of the modern financial system. The course also covers a wide range of hot topics related to the Chinese financial system. The course covers topics on asset pricing theory, corporate finance theory, financial markets, and Chinese financial issues. Upon completion, students would understand the principles of finance and the operation of markets. They would be able to analyze financial problems and develop the ability to use financial tools. The course consists of classroom lectures and discussions, case studies, industry expert lectures, among other teaching methods.
