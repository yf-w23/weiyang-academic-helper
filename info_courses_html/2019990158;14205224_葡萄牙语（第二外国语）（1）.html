课程内容简介：
该课程为葡萄牙语第一阶段的基础学习课程，目的在于使学生熟练掌握发音规则以及实用语法基础知识，具备基本的日常语言情境中的交流能力，并具备初步的中葡跨文化交流能力。课程内容主要包括以下四个方面：1.教授葡萄牙语发音规则；2. 讲解名词和形容词的性和数、冠词、主格人称代词、指示代词、物主代词、动词陈述式现在时、陈述式现在进行时、前置词、副词、形容词和副词的比较级、句法结构等基本语法知识；3. 讲解国籍、职业、家庭关系、婚姻状况、外貌、性格、爱好、数字、颜色、购物、服饰、饮食、钟点时刻、日常作息、处所方位等方面的词汇和表达；4. 讲授葡萄牙文化知识和中葡跨文化交际知识，对比和分析两国文化异同。
Course Description
As the basis of Portuguese language learning in the initial stage, the course will play a key role in providing the students with correct pronunciation practices and proper use of basic grammar, ensuring them basic communication ability in daily life, as well as basic intercultural communicative competence between Chinese and Portuguese cultures. The course mainly consists of the following four aspects: 1. Study of Portuguese pronunciation rules; 2. Study of basic grammar mainly regarding the gender and number of nouns and adjectives, articles, nominative pronouns, demonstrative pronouns, possessive pronouns, present indicative, present continuous tense, prepositions, adverbs, comparative adjectives and adverbs, and syntactic structure; 3. Study of vocabulary and expressions regarding nationality, occupation, family relationship, marital status, appearance, personality, hobbies, number, color, shopping, clothes, gastronomy, time, daily routines, as well as location; 4. Study of Portuguese Culture and knowledges about intercultural communication between Chinese and Portuguese cultures, with comparation and analysis of the similarities and differences of the two cultures.
