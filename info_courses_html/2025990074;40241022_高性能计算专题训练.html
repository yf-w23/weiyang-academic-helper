课程内容简介：
这门课是“高性能计算导论”的进阶课程，旨在培养学生在高性能计算领域的理论基础与实践技能，重点培养应用 性能分析、优化技术解决实际问题的能力。学生在本课程中将学习高性能计算在科学、工程领域的实际应用，并 实践使用高性能计算技术解决真实、复杂、大规模的科学与工程问题。通过以上目标的达成，学生将全面提升在 高性能计算领域的综合素养，为未来深入研究或从事相关职业奠定坚实基础。
Course Description
This course is an advanced level following the “Introduction to High-Performance Computing”, aiming to cultivate students' theoretical foundations and practical skills in the field of high-performance computing. The emphasis is on developing the ability to apply performance analysis and optimization techniques to address real-world problems. In this course, students will learn about the real applications of high- performance computing in scientific and engineering domains. They will engage in hands-on practices utilizing high-performance computing technologies to solve real, complex, and large-scale scientific and engineering problems. By achieving these objectives, students will comprehensively enhance their overall competency in the field of high-performance computing, laying a solid foundation for future in-depth research or careers in related fields.
