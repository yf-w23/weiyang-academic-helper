课程内容简介：
来到五道口体校，一定要感受一下不一样的运动项目。也许你曾经玩过篮球、足球、乒乓球、羽毛球、网球，也许你打过跆拳道、空手道、甚至是混元太极五连鞭，但是垒球你恐怕没有尝试过吧？Come on~ 来到五道口体校，一定要感受一下门槛最低的项目。也许身高不是你在篮球、排球场上的优势，也许速度限制了你在足球、田径场上的发挥，千万别灰心！对身体条件没有要求的垒球能帮你发现自己的运动才能！从门外汉到教学比赛与跨班级对抗，只需要一个学期。 来到五道口体校，一定要感受一下清华最传统的项目。垒球由清华最传统的运动项目——“棒球”发展而来。两者在规则上几乎完全一样。清华为什么要自建校伊始就建立棒球校队？为什么要鼓励女生参加垒球？为什么要在五道口建设北京高校唯一的一块正规棒垒球场？这项运动在中国社会虽然并不大众，但为什么有如此魅力，让清华大学如此重视让清华人如此着迷？ 完成这一学期的课程，你就会明白。
Course Description
Softball was introduced to Tsinghua, as the equivalent of baseball, which is the sport for the boys, shortly after its establishment in 1911. As one of the sport with the longest history in Tsinghua, softball and baseball have been traditionally organised and emphasised in the university even in the days when students and faculties were moved to Chongqing for protection during the Second World War. It is undeniable that this American pastime enjoys more popularity in Tsinghua than in Chinese society at the moment. As a result, our softball module is a course focusing at beginner level, starting with teaching students how to throw a ball and swing the bat. Students will ultimately be encouraged and organised to play games at the end of semester, however, if you are an experienced player and are looking for something at advanced level, you are more than welcome to join the varsity team. You can find us at the baseball field right behind, or as a Beijinger would say just to the north of, the natatorium. The training hour is Thursday and Sunday afternoon from 1330.
