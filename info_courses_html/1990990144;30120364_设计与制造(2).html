课程内容简介：
本课程主要介绍制造技术与制造工程的基本原理与方法，重点阐述制造过程与系统的物理本质和随机特性，以及它们对质量、效率、成本和柔性的影响。内容包括材料加工和金属切削的基本原理，机床刀具和夹具的设计，加工精度和表面质量，制造工艺设计与生产组织。本课程包括两个专题研究，一个典型产品原型零件的数控编程和加工以及产品的装配，另一个是激光加工原理的探索与实验研究。
Course Description
This course covers the basics of manufacturing technologies and engineering. Emphasis is on the physics and stochastic nature of manufacturing processes and systems, and their effects on quality, rate, cost and flexibility. Specific topics include fundamentals of materials processing and metal cutting, design principle of machine tool, cutting tool and fixture, machining accuracy and surface quality, and manufacturing process planning and organization. Two group projects are required: 1) NC programming and machining of parts and assembly methods to produce a typical product prototype, and 2) laser processing principle exploration and experimental research.
