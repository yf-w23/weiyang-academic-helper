课程内容简介：
本课程从宏观和微观两个层面介绍发展经济学的历史、基本理论及其分析方法，并简要介绍发展经济学主要研究专题的内容和成果。专题包括增长模型、健康、教育回报、性别歧视、金融与信用市场、风险、制度等。每个专题都由理论分析和实证案例组成。在课程学习的过程中逐渐掌握发展经济学的分析思路并共同思考（尤其是中国的）发展问题。
Course Description
This course explores the micro and macro foundations of development economics, covering its history of thought, basic theories and principles, and the analysis method. The course provides a concise introduction of topics in development economics, including productivity effects of health, returns to education, gender discrimination, market for finance and credit, risk, institution, etc. For each topic, we will study several concrete examples chosen from around the world. While studying each of these topics, we try to grasp the way of thinking in the field of development, and eventually, raise and answer relevant (especially to China) questions.
进度安排：

1 发展简介：贫困、经济增长、发展陷阱、发展不均 2 劳动力专题：人口、家庭、性别、移民 3 人力资本专题：健康、营养、教育 4 财经专题：风险、信用、金融、土地 5 政治经济学专题：制度、文化、冲突 6 中国发展专题讨论
