课程内容简介：
本课程为材料科学与工程专业基础课，适合材料科学与工程学科中各专业方向以及其它相近学 科和交叉学科的本科生。主要内容包括材料的电学性能（电导、介电）、磁学性能、光学性 能、热学性能等。系统介绍各种物理性能基本参数的物理意义、机理与基本理论；材料物理性 能参数测量原理、方法及应用；材料物理性能与材料的组成、结构、工艺的相关性、相互联 系、转换与变化规律。通过本课程的学习为新材料设计和材料改性奠定基础。
Course Description
This is a fundamental course for the undergraduate program of materials science and engineering. It is designed for students majoring in materials science and engineering and other related disciplines. This course mainly covers electrical properties (including conductance and dielectric), magnetic properties, optical properties, thermal properties and the like. In this course, there is a systematic introduction to the physical meaning of various basic parameters of the physics properties, mechanisms and the fundamental theories. The general principles, methods and applications of material physical property parameters are also involved. Moreover, the dependency, interrelation, conversion and transformation laws between material physical properties and the composition, structure and technology of the material will also be introduced in this course. Upon completion of the course, students will be expected to have a basic understanding and are ready to study new material design and material modification.
