课程内容简介：
本课程为流体机械及工程学科本科生专业基础课。课程内容主要包括：流体机械的分类与应用；叶片式流体机械的工作过程、结构、主要性能参数；叶片式流体机械的工作原理及基本方程；有限叶片数的影响；机组高压侧过流部件的作用原理；导流部件的作用原理；机组低压侧过流部件的作用原理；流体机械内的能量损失及效率；流体机械的相似理论和计算；比转速与过流部件几何尺寸及性能的关系；流体机械的空化理论及空蚀；轴流式流体机械叶轮设计的升力法等内容。通过本课程的学习，使学生掌握流体机械的基本原理和过流部件的基本设计理论和设计方法，为进一步在本学科领域深造和从事与本学科有关的科研、技术工作打下良好的基础。
Course Description
This course is the professional foundation for undergraduates of fluid machinery and engineering. The course content mainly includes classification and application of fluid machinery; working process, structure and main performance parameters of vaned fluid machinery; working principle and basic equations of vaned fluid machinery; the influence of limited blade number; the working principle of the high-pressure side in the unit’s flow-through component; the working principle of the guide component; the working principle of the low-pressure side in the unit’s flow-through component; the energy loss and efficiency in the fluid machinery; the similarity theory and calculation of the fluid machinery; the relationship between the specific speed and the geometry and performance of the flow-through component; cavitation theory of fluid machinery; lift method and singularity method of axial-flow fluid machinery impeller design, etc. Through the course study, students can master the basic principles of fluid machinery, the basic design theories and design methods of flow-through components. Furthermore, the course can provide a good foundation for those who needs advanced study in the field or who plan to undertake scientific research and technical work related to this discipline.
