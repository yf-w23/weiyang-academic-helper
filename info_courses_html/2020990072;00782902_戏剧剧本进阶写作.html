课程内容简介：
戏剧剧本进阶写作是培养学生复杂事件小品的写作能力，让学生掌握戏剧小品的理论知识 和写作技 巧，能够娴熟的独立完成不同风格戏剧小品写作。该课程是戏剧剧本基础写作在教学上的 延伸，大致 分为复杂事件小品、小说改编小品、多种风格小品写作三个阶段。复杂事件小品讲授小品 题材选择、 事件开掘、冲突设计、结构设计等技巧，训练学生对复杂人物关系和矛盾冲突的驾驭能 力。小说改编 阶段通过借助中外中短篇小说简化小品写作在内容和主题上复杂程度，专注提升学生的编 剧技巧。第 三阶段训练学生对悲剧、喜剧、正剧小品的写作，进一步专门训练学生对不同风格小品的 写作能力。
Course Description
This course is to cultivate students' writing ability of complex stories, so that students can master the theories and skills of playwriting, and be able to skillfully complete the writing of different styles of drama. This course is an extension of the basic writing of drama script, which can be roughly divided into three stages: complex event, novel adaptation, and various styles of playwriting. The complex event stage focus on writing skills, such as selecting subjects, extracting events, creating conflicts, building structure, and focus on training students' ability to building complex character relations and conflicts. In the stage of novel adaptation, by using Chinese and foreign novellas we can simplify the complexity of writing in terms of content and theme and let students focus on improving their writing skills. The third stage is to train students' imagination of pictures and enhance their writing ability of different styles.
