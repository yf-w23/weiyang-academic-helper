课程名：
俄罗斯外交思想与实践
学时：
32
学分：
2
课程简介：
通过前期讲授，勾勒出自基辅罗斯以来的俄罗斯外交的千年历史脉络，理解不同时期的俄罗斯外交思想及其对当代俄罗斯对外政策的影响，进而通过课堂讨论解析地理、宗教、文化、民族性、领袖、能源、利益集团和地方主体等要素如何影响外交决策。
英文课程简介：
By being taught, early sketch from Kievan Rus Russia's diplomatic history in one thousand, since the understanding in different periods of Russia's diplomatic thoughts and its influence on contemporary Russia's foreign policy, and then parse geography, religion, culture, nationality, leader, energy, interest groups and local main elements such as how to influence the Russian diplomacy.
