课程内容简介：
教育心理学是心理学理论与教育教学实践相结合的交叉学科，主要研究教育情境中教与学的心理过程和原理，包括学生的认知与能力发展、学习信念与自我概念、学业情绪与动机、教学方法的有效性等。本课程主要介绍教育心理学领域中的重要概念、基本原则和经典理论在学习和教学过程中的有效应用，引导学生从教育心理学视角分析学生的学习和发展问题并思考改善策略，学习了解教育心理学研究的基本方法和范式。作为教育学辅修项目课程，本课程面向不同专业本科生同学开放，旨在帮助学生深入理解教与学的心理学基础与机制，并能将所思所学应用在作为教育工作者或终身学习者的生涯中。
Course Description
Educational psychology is an inter-discipline that integrates psychological theories and educational practices. It is concerned with psychological processes and principles in teaching and learning, including students’ cognitive and aptitude development, learning beliefs and self-concepts, academic emotion and motivation, effectiveness of teaching approaches, and so forth. This course mainly introduces important concepts, basic principles, and classical theories in the field of educational psychology, as well as their applications in the processes of teaching and learning. The course also guides students, from the lens of educational psychology, to analyze school students’ learning and development issues and think about strategies to improve them. Moreover, basic methodologies and paradigms for conducting relevant educational research would be introduced. As a part of undergraduate minor program in education, this course is open to undergraduates of different majors, and aims to help students develop an understanding of the psychological underpinnings and mechanisms of learning and teaching, which could be applied to their future careers as educational practitioners, researchers, or life-long learners.
