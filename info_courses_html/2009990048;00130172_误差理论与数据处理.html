课程内容简介：
“科学始于测量”，但“测量”并不仅限于数据的采集，更包括数据分析与评估的完整过程。唯有对测量数据进行全面的分析与评估，明确其不确定度和可信度，才能得出真正有意义的结论，从而推动科学发现与技术应用迈向更高水平。 本课程旨在系统讲授科学实验和工程实践中测量数据分析与处理的基本理论与方法。课程内容涵盖测量误差理论、测量不确定度及数据统计分析的基本概念与方法，并结合多学科的实际案例进行剖析，帮助学生将理论知识应用于解决实际问题。通过学习，学生将全面提升在具体测量实践中评估测量结果的能力，支撑后续实验课程学习及科研实践探索。
Course Description
"Science begins with measurement," but measurement is not limited to the collection of data; it also encompasses the comprehensive process of data analysis and evaluation. Only through thorough analysis and assessment of measurement data, with a clear understanding of its uncertainty and reliability, can meaningful conclusions be drawn, advancing scientific discovery and technological application to new heights. This course is specifically designed for students in science and engineering disciplines, aiming to systematically teach the fundamental theories and methods of measurement data analysis and processing in scientific experiments and engineering practices. The curriculum covers core topics such as measurement error theory, uncertainty analysis, and basic concepts and techniques in statistical data analysis. It integrates real-world multidisciplinary case studies to help students apply theoretical knowledge to practical problem-solving. Through this course, students will significantly enhance their ability to evaluate measurement results in practical contexts, laying a solid foundation for future research and technological development.
进度安排：

第一部分：绪论 

第二部分：测量误差的基本理论（误差的来源、分类、基本特征；不同类型误差的处理方法、误差的传递、合成与分配等）

第三部分：测量不确定度（测量不确定度概念、GUM测量不确定度评定法、基于蒙特卡洛方法的测量不确定度评定、扩展不确定度、测量不确定度评定案例等）

第四部分：测量数据分析（最小二乘分析、经验公式拟合和回归分析等）
