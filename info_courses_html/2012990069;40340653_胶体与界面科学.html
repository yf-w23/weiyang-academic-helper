课程内容简介：
课程从分子间相互作用研究的发展历史入手，分阶段讲授分子间基本作用力、分子间二体势、分子自我能等基本物理概念，通过玻尔兹曼分布将分子间相互作用能与物质的宏观性质建立联系，深入讲解静电相互作用和范德华相互作用，推导诱导、取向、色散力表达式，介绍分子间斥力和氢键的作用特征。在分子间相互作用的基础上，介绍分子相互作用与颗粒和表面之间的相互作用的相似性和区别，系统讲述颗粒和表面间范德华相互作用和电解质溶液体系的作用能关系，分析流体界面张力和接触角的本质原因，简介宏观界面和自组装现象等内容，加深对于气体、溶液、胶体颗粒和界面现象中微观现象理解，认识胶体和表界面体系宏观特性产生的本质原因。
Course Description
Starting from the development history of intermolecular interaction research, the course teaches basic physical concepts such as intermolecular basic force, intermolecular two-body potential, and molecular self-energy in stages. It teaches how to establish a relationship between intermolecular interaction energy and the macroscopic properties of matter through Boltzmann distribution, deeply explains electrostatic interaction and van der Waals interaction, and derives the expression of induction, orientation, and dispersion force, introduces the interaction characteristics of intermolecular repulsion and hydrogen bond. On the basis of intermolecular interaction, this course introduces the similarities and differences between molecular interaction and the interaction between particles and surfaces, systematically describes the van der Waals interaction between particles and surfaces and the interaction energy relationship between the electrolyte solution system, analyzes the essential reasons of fluid interface tension and contact angle, briefly introduces the macroscopic interface and self-assembly phenomenon, deepens the understanding of gas, solution, shows the microscopic phenomena in colloidal particles and interface phenomena, and introduces the essential reasons for the macroscopic characteristics of colloidal particles and surface interface systems.
