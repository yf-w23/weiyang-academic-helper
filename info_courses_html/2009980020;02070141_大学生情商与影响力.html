课程内容简介：
该课程为有志于提高情商和影响力的学生而设计。情商（Emotional Quotient, EQ）又称情绪智力，是心理学家们提出的与智力和智商相对应的概念。它主要是指人在情绪、情感、意志、耐受挫折等方面的品质。影响力是指一个人对他人心理与行为施加改变的能力。 该课引进国际前沿，美国宇航局（NASA）的科技精英团队所采用的卓越4D领导力训练课程。该课程的理论体系由NASA前天体物理部主任查理•佩勒林设计，理论内容逻辑性强，8项提高情商和的行为训练简单实用。该训练课程已经提升了NASA超过500个团队的绩效。该课程采用了适合清华学生的教学环节与案例选择的本土化设计。 在课程结束时，你将： （一）通过参与知识讲授、问卷测试评估、角色扮演等环节，掌握4D卓越团队理论知识。 （二）通过参与课堂讨论、实际团队领导力行动项目实施，感激欣赏、共同利益找寻等8项领导力行为训练，提升自我认知、情绪管理、沟通表达、团队背景管理和冲突解决5大能力。 （三）通过反思和自我评估等元认知练习，深深认同成为未来领导者所需具备的价值观念——主动承担社会责任、追求卓越、尊重、包容、自律。
Course Description
The Introduction to Emotional Intelligence and the Power of Influencing EQ is also known as emotional intelligence. This concept is proposed by many psychologists which is corresponding to the notion of intelligence. It mainly refers to the characteristics of people’s mood, emotion, willpower and their resilience towards frustration. The power of influencing describes how an individual can influence other people’s mind and behaviors. In general, EQ and influence are not genetically determined and it is closely related to the nurturing environment. After learning this course, you will: 1)Master the theory of MBTI Leadership style and 4-D System Team building; 2)Acquire the five main abilities of EQ and influence, including Self- cognition, emotion management, communication, team performance promotion and conflict resolution; 3)Develop a sense of social responsibility as a leader and an spirit of pursuit of excellence. This course will: 1) Use lectures, questionnaire test evaluation, role play to obtain theoretical knowledge; 2)Improve the five main abilities through problem-based learning process and practical training, such as leadership case simulation and 8 types of leadership actions; 3)Apply inter-group evaluation and teacher’s feedback during the learning process, finally achieving the sense of social responsibility and spirit of pursuit of excellence.
