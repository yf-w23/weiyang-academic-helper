课程简介：
本课程介绍卫生医疗服务科学研究架构和方法，旨在培养学生的科学方法理论，提高实践能力。首先，运用HSR理论框架形成完整的思维架构；其次，经由学习假说的建立来训练学生了解科学研究的逻辑观念；类实验研究设计和调查研究法将是本课程的核心内容。同时，应用医疗服务研究计划的执行过程和研究结果将训练学生的实践能力。
英文课程简介：
The course will introduce scientific study framework and methods in healthcare services research. The training is critical to students to frame their theses in scientific way. The course first introduce the framework of HSR constructs and the linkage between the constructs. Assumptions development will be introduced after the steps of conducting a HSR. Quasi-experimental study design and survey research is will be the core elements of the course. The applications of the methods will be reviewed and discussed in the class to inspire students to learn in a practical way.
