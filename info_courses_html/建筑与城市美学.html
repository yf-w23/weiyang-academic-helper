课程内容简介：
在国家城乡发展日新月异的今天，如何总结其中的经验和教训、深入探讨研究建筑与城市审 美文化具有十分重要的意义。本课程试图剖析讲解建筑与城市美学的基本概念、构成要素与 深层机制，并以此尝试构建具有中国特色的建筑与城市美学思想理论，课程的内容特色具体 体现在： 1 美学与建筑城市形象研究相结合跨学科解析理论问题 课程借鉴美学、哲学、心理学等学科理论方法，跨学科搭建研究平台探索建筑与城市学科核 心领域，针对城市品质塑造的难点问题建筑城市形象审美与评价进行探讨。 2 历史研究与现实问题相结合指导设计实践 课程希望能以当代中国建筑与城市形象建设问题为导向，试图把握其中的美学观念及问题的 演化脉络，并从中国传统建筑城市美学文化中寻求启示。 3 西方与中国比较研究相结合探索中国特色命题 课程试图从中西文化比较的角度探索建筑与城市美学的中国特色命题，探讨体现中国文化的 中国建筑与城市美学思想。 从上述三个方面出发，本课程内容包括：1建筑与城市美学概述，2建筑美学的发展历史与主 要特征，3建筑美学的原理与内容，4中国传统建筑美学精神与范畴,5城市美学的发展历史与 主要特征,6城市美学的原理与内容，7中国传统城市美学思想与方法，8结论与讨论：当代中 国建筑与城市美学的再建构。
Course Description
At present, China is experiencing large-scale, high-speed urbanization and urban landscape changes with each passing day. However, the current situation of urban built environment in China is not satisfactory. The problem of "same appearance of thousand cities" is prominent. And it remains a question of how to keep the Chinese building culture in urban renewal. In contrast to the vast building and construction practice, the basic research and teaching on architectural and urban aesthetics are still relatively weak. Based on the above consideration, this course aims to explain the basic concepts, elements and mechanism of architectural and urban aesthetics, and to build the theory of Chinese architectural and urban aesthetics. The contents of the curriculum are illustrated as follows: 1 combining the study of aesthetics and the issue of urban image, solving theoretical issues with interdisciplinary methods 2 combining historical research with practical problems to guide design practice. 3 exploring the Chinese architectural and urban aesthetics with Western and Chinese comparative studies From the three aspects above, this course comprises eight lectures, including Lecture 1 for overview, Lecture 2 to 4 on architectural aesthetics, Lecture 5 to 7 on urban aesthetics, and Lecture 8 for summary and discussion. The specific contents of the eight lectures are as follows: 1 the overview of architectural and urban aesthetics. 2 the development and main characteristics of architectural aesthetics. 3 the principles and contents of architectural aesthetics. 4 the aesthetic spirit and contents of Chinese traditional architecture 5 the development and main characteristics of urban aesthetics. 6 the principles and contents of urban aesthetics 7 the aesthetic ideas and methodologies of Chinese traditional cities. 8 summary and discussion: reconstruction of contemporary Chinese architecture and Urban Aesthetics
