课程简介：
该课程面向车辆、自动化、计算机、电子、人工智能、软件、航空航天等院系的研究生和学有余力的本科生，系统讲授以智能汽车、特种车辆、飞行汽车、无人机、机器狗、移动机器人为代表的泛机器人系统智能决策规划理论知识和前沿算法。教学内容包括三个模块：（1）经典运动规划（离散规划、采样/优化方法、微分约束规划、非完整系统等）；（2）经典决策方法（博弈论、概率图模型、马尔可夫决策过程等）；（3）学习型决策规划（强化学习、模仿学习、端到端规划、Transformer及大语言模型赋能的决策规划、生成式规划、多智能体规划、可信人工智能等）。除课堂讲授，还包括（1）运动规划实车挑战赛，开发实际的轨迹规划功能软件并完成部署调试，通过实战开发培养工程能力；（2）企业邀请报告2-3次（每次1学时），了解汽车业界典型技术路线和发展状态；（3）智能决策前沿技术研讨，通过分组多样性深入调研，以研讨方式增强对前沿技术发展趋势的了解。课程从多个学科角度系统深入地讲授智能决策规划相关理论和实际应用，以三个结合（理论与科研结合、理论与实践结合、多学科结合）为特色，助力同学夯实理论基础和工程开发能力。
英文课程简介：
This course is designed for graduate students and senior undergraduates with extra learning capacity from departments such as automotive engineering, automation, computer science, electronics, artificial intelligence, software engineering, and aerospace engineering. It systematically presents theoretical knowledge and cutting-edge algorithms for intelligent decision-making and planning of robot systems such as intelligent cars, special vehicles, flying cars, and drones. The course content includes three modules: (1) Classical motion planning (discrete programming, sampling/optimization based methods, differential constraint planning, nonholonomic systems, etc.); (2) Classical decision-making methods (game theory, probabilistic graphical models, Markov decision processes, etc.); (3) Learning-based decision-making and planning (reinforcement learning, imitation learning, end-to-end planning, decision-making and planning empowered by Transformer and large language models , generative planning, multi-agent planning, trustworthy artificial intelligence, etc.). In addition to classroom lectures, the course also includes (1) a real-vehicle motion planning challenge, where participants develop and deploy actual trajectory planning software, cultivating engineering skills through hands-on development; (2) 2-3 invited reports from enterprises (1 class hour each), providing insights into typical technical routes and development status in the automotive industry; and (3) a seminar on cutting-edge technologies for intelligent decision-making, conducting in-depth research through diverse group discussions to enhance understanding of cutting-edge technology trends.

进度安排：
第一部分（经典运动规划，1-3周）
第1周 绪论及离散规划；第2周 基于采样的运动规划；第3周 基于优化的微分约束运动规划。
第二部分（经典决策方法，4-7周）
第4周 博弈决策理论；第5周 概率图模型； 第6周 路径规划实车挑战赛；第7周 马尔可夫决策过程（MDP）
第三部分（学习型决策规划，8-16周）
第8/9周 强化学习（RL）；第10周 模仿学习；第11周 端到端决策方法；第12/13周 Transformer和大语言模型及其在决策规划中的应用；第14周 生成式轨迹规划；第15周 多智能体规划；第16周智能决策规划前沿技术研讨汇报
