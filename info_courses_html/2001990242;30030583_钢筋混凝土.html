课程内容简介：
课程内容主要为钢筋混凝土基本构件的设计理论及应用，包括：钢筋混凝土结构的材料和力学性能（包括粘结）、设计计算原理和实用表达式、基本构件（弯、剪、拉、压、扭等）的承载力极限状态计算和正常使用极限状态验算等。还要讲述初步的预应力混凝土构件设计（集中在轴心受拉构件）及钢筋混凝土结构抗震设计两个专题。 教材采用四校合编的《水工钢筋混凝土结构学》，其设计表达式以水利行业DL/T5057-2009规范为主线；条件允许下，安排一次校内工地现场参观。
Course Description
The course mainly covers the design theory and application of reinforced concrete basic components, including material and mechanical properties of reinforced concrete structure (including bonding), design calculation principle and practical expression, ultimate bearing capacity calculation of basic components (bending, shearing, tension, compression, torsion, etc.) and servicability limit state checking calculation. It also introduces two special topics: preliminary design of prestressed concrete members (concentrated on axial tension members) and seismic design of reinforced concrete structures.
