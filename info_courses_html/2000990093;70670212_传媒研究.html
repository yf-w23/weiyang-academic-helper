课程简介：
本课程为信息艺术设计交叉学科专业的研究生，尤其是非传播相关专业背景的同学讲授传播学理论的基础知识与研究方法，以及媒介环境迅速变化的移动互联网时代因传播方式改变而出现的新问题，关注传媒研究前沿，为交叉学科背景研究生提供更为广阔的研究视野。本课程的特色在于把传播学与信息科学、互联网研究等前沿学科相联系贯通；把理论教学与体验学习相结合；把课堂教学与自主学习的MOOC网络课程教学相互贯通，开展网络教学；把课内教学与课外实践相结合，邀请业内人士介绍传媒创新前沿，并通过小组研究的方式让学生基于课程所介绍的研究方法展开研究。
英文课程简介：
This course is designed for graduate students majoring in Informational Art Design, especially those with non-communication backgrounds, to teach the basic knowledge and research methods of communication theory, as well as new problems arising from changes in communication methods in the rapidly changing media environment under the era of mobile internet. The course pays attention to the frontiers of media research and provides a broader research vision for students with interdisciplinary backgrounds. The characteristic of this course is to connect communication studies with information science, internet research and other cutting-edge subjects, combining theoretical teaching with experiential learning. The course integrates in-class teaching and independent learning through MOOC teaching. Insiders from media industry are invited to introduce frontiers of media innovation. Students are encouraged to conduct research based on the methods taught in the course through group study.
