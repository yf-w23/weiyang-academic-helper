课程简介：
《恐怖主义及其治理》是一门面向硕博研究生的课程，旨在研究恐怖主义的逻辑、生成机理、活动规律，探讨恐怖主义与国际反恐斗争对国际政治的影响以及国际恐怖主义的治理之策。授课语言为汉语，阅读材料包括中文和英文两种。
英文课程简介：
"Terrorism and its governance" is a course for master and doctoral students, aiming to study the logic, generation mechanism and activity rules of terrorism, and to discuss the influence of terrorism and international counter-terrorism struggle on international politics， and to explore the governance strategy of international terrorism. The language of instruction is Chinese, and the readings are in both Chinese and English.
