课程内容简介：
社会医学是一门致力于研究社会因素对健康影响的新兴学科，旨在从宏观的社会层面理解健康与疾病的关系。该课程涵盖社会医学的基础概念、医学模式的演变、生物-心理-社会模式、健康的社会决定因素、卫生服务体系、社区卫生服务与健康促进、环境与健康等内容。通过理论讲授、案例分析、小组讨论，学生将学习并掌握社会医学的方法，具备从社会角度分析健康问题的能力。本课程强调临床和科研的早期接触，以增强学生的实践应用能力，培养其在公共卫生与医疗政策中的批判性思维。通过学习，学生能够理解社会、经济、文化、环境等多种因素如何影响健康，并在医学教育的框架内应用这些知识，为他们未来在医学研究和医疗服务中的多样化发展打下坚实基础。
Course Description
Social medicine is an emerging discipline dedicated to studying the impact of social factors on health. It aims to understand the relationship between health and disease from a broader societal perspective. This course covers fundamental concepts in social medicine, the evolution of medical models, the biopsychosocial model, social determinants of health, healthcare systems, community health services and health promotion, and environment and health. Through lectures, case studies, and group discussions, students will learn and master the methods of social medicine and develop the ability to analyze health issues from a social perspective. This course emphasizes early exposure to clinical and research settings to enhance students' practical application skills and cultivate critical thinking in public health and healthcare policy. By the end of the course, students will be able to understand how social, economic, cultural, and environmental factors influence health, and apply this knowledge within the framework of medical education, laying a solid foundation for their future diverse development in medical research and healthcare services.
