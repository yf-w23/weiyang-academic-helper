课程简介：
本课程的目的是培养学生的英文期刊论文的写作能力，为此，本课程会采用课堂授课、讨论、同伴互评、课堂点评及一对一点评等不同教学方式，期望学生大量阅读文献、辩证思考、不断修改自己的习作，以期期末能写出一篇合乎期刊要求的学术论文。
英文课程简介：
Purpose: To help you prepare and refine journal research papers and theses. Method: Lecturing, group discussion, peer review, review and discussion will be the main means of our teaching and learning. 1) Process-writing and project-based writing are emphasized. 2) Peer review is encouraged

进度安排：

Week 1: Introduction of the course

Week 2: Overview of research methods

Week 6: Results and discussion

Week 9: Review of assignment 2

Week 12: Paper submission

Week 13: Review of assignment 4

考核方式：
