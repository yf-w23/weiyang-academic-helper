课程内容简介：
“光电综合系统专题实验”是电子信息类专业相关的综合系统实验课程。以真实的应用系统作为实验课上的研究内容，培养学生综合的跨专业领域的实践与创新能力。课程中首先通过1次讲授与2次基础实验学习光电子方向基本技术与方法。在此基础上学生3人一小组组队设计实现一个以光电子技术为核心的综合系统，涉及的技术可延展到信号的采集、调制、传输探测、解调、数据采集、信号处理、统计运算、机器学习等跨学科融合的部分技术范畴。 本实验课以项目的设计、实现与研讨为主要教学形式。包括项目选择、系统方案设计、开题、系统实现、验收演示、答辩各个环节。实验内容包括且不限于如下专题，学生也可以自主提出研究内容和目标，通过和老师讨论确定方案。光量子信息实验探究；无透镜光卷积计算；可见光空间光通信系统设计；基于光散射的空气中微粒检测；激光诱导击穿光谱检测；光纤光栅传感系统设计实验等。 通过学习和实践，学生可以获得光电子系统设计的基本技能，同时对电路、通信、信号、图像、光电、网络等系列课程的部分知识融会贯通和综合应用。了解工程实际的前沿发展，掌握系统设计的思维方法、系统分解与集成 的基本思路，以及完整系统的评价方法。从而提高电子信息工程应用、系统设计和创新能力。
Course Description
"Special Experiments of Optoelectronic Comprehensive System " is a comprehensive system experimental course related to electronic information major. Take the real application system as the research content of the experimental class to cultivate students' comprehensive practice and innovation ability in interdisciplinary fields. In the course, we first learn the basic technologies and methods of Optoelectronics through lecture and practice. With that, we design and implement an integrated system with Optoelectronics Technology. The technologies involved can be extended to some interdisciplinary integration technologies such as signal acquisition, modulation/demodulation, transmission, detection, data acquisition, signal processing, statistical operation, machine learning and so on. This experimental course takes the design, implementation and discussion of projects as the main teaching form, including project selection, system design scheme, topic opening, implementation, acceptance demonstration, and presentation. The experimental contents include but are not limited to the following topics. Students can also put forward research contents and objectives independently and determine the scheme through discussion with teachers. Experimental exploration of light quantum information; Design of visible light space optical communication system; Particle detection in air based on light scattering; Laser induced breakdown spectroscopy; Grating sensing system design experiment, etc.. Students can acquire the basic skills of optoelectronic system design, and integrate and comprehensively apply some knowledge of circuit, communication, signal, image, optoelectronics, network and other courses. Understand the frontier development of engineering practice, master the thinking method of system design, the basic idea of system decomposition and integration, and the evaluation method of complete system.
