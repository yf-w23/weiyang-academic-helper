课程简介：
本课程将是脑和认知科学的进阶课程，重点讲授面向现代神经科学研究的知识体系和研究能力的培养，突出当今神经科学研究的热点与前沿。课堂以专题讲授为主，结合讨论，内容涵盖神经系统，包括神经发育、突触组装、环路运行、行为认知、进化演变、技术开发、智能应用等方向。
英文课程简介：
This will be an advanced course in Brain and Cognitive Sciences, focusing on developing the research-oriented knowledge structure and skill. It emphasizes the research frontiers of neuroscience. The lectures will be given in units, covering neural specification, neural development, synapse, synapse plasticity, neural evolution, behavior and circuit, machine learning and artificial intelligence. The course emphasizes and encourages student participation, and will be taught in English.
