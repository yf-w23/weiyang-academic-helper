课程简介：
会计是生产和提供关于一个组织的财务信息的信息系统，其最终成果是经审计的财务报表，以向组织的各利益相关者公允地反映该组织的财务状况、经营成果和现金流量等方面的信息。本课程的教学目标在于，以工商管理硕士研究生（MBA）为对象，讲解盈利组织（即企业）会计的基本概念、基本原则和基本方法，使MBA学生能够理解和初步分析应用企业财务报表，了解会计与公司治理之关系，理解和关注企业利益相关者的信息需求，理解企业履行全面和公允披露财务信息责任的必要性和重要性。 本课程的主要内容包括：（1）财务会计的基本概念和基本原则，包括会计基本前提、会计目标、会计信息质量要求、会计要素及其确认和计量的基本原则。（2）资产负债表、利润表和现金流量表及它们的相关附属报表的基本格式、逻辑关系与内容。（3）财务会计的基本方法，包括会计确认和计量方法、会计记录的借贷记账法以及财务报表的编制方法等。（4）资产负债表和利润表各项目的含义及其具体会计处理方法。（5）财务报表的分析应用。 本课程将以课堂讲授为主，并结合案例分析、课堂讨论以及小组报告等方式。
英文课程简介：
Accounting is an information system that produces and provides an organization’s financial information, resulting in the audited financial reports. The aim is to reflect the organization’s financial position, operating results and cash flow to the stakeholders fairly. The teaching goal of accounting course is to show MBA how to understand and analyze firms’ financial statements, to further understand the relationship between accounting and corporate governance, to pay more attention to the information demand of firms, and to catch on the necessity and importance of responsibility for firms to disclose financial statements comprehensively and fairly by explaining the basic concepts, principles and methods of profit-making enterprises. The contents of accounting courses are: (1)The basic concepts and principles of financial accounting including accounting basic premise, accounting objectives, the accounting information quality requirements, accounting elements and their recognition and measurement of the basic principles; (2)Balance sheet, income statement and cash flow statement and the relationship between these financial reports; (3)The basic methods of financial accounting including accounting recognition and measurement methods, the accounting records and financial statements of lending attributes establishment method; (4)The elements of balance sheet and income statement and their specific accounting methods; (5)The analytical applications of financial statements.
