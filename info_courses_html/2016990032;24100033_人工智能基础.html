课程内容简介：
人工智能的目标是通过深刻的认知基础、严格的数学工具与高效的计算方法，解决自然科学与工程技术中的复杂实际问题。近年来，人工智能迎来新一轮发展浪潮，成为全球信息技术制高点之一。本课程将从基本原理、经典模型、高效算法、实际应用等多个角度，重点介绍人工智能的若干关键技术。内容包括：1. 搜索（启发式搜索、高级搜索、对抗搜索）；2. 学习（监督学习、强化学习、深度学习、大模型）；3. 推理（概率图模型、期望最大化、变分方法、状态空间模型）；4. 前沿（具身智能、科学智能等）。本课程以讲授与实践相结合，帮助学生掌握扎实的人工智能理论基础和实践能力。
Course Description
The goal of Artificial Intelligence (AI) is to solve complex real problems of science and engineering through profound cognitive foundations, rigorous mathematical tools and efficient computational methods. In recent years, AI has come into a new wave of advancements and become one of the peaks in global information technology. This course will selectively introduce key AI technologies through fundamental principles, classical models, efficient algorithms, and practical applications. Main topics include: (1) Search (heuristic search, advanced search, adversarial search); (2) Learning (supervised learning, reinforcement learning, deep learning, foundation model); (3) Reasoning (probabilistic graphical model, expectation maximization, variational inference, state space model); (4) Frontiers (embodied AI, scientific AI, etc). This course features lectures and projects to help students lay theoretical foundations and form practical abilities of AI.
