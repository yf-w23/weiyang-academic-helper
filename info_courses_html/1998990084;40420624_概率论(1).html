课程内容简介：
1.随机事件,σ-域, 最小σ域, 最小单调类, 最小λ类,单调类定理,概率空间, 条件概率与独立性, (可列)乘积概率空间与独立随机试验. 2.随机变量(向量)及其结构,概率分布(概率模型)包括重要的常见20种概率分布及其它们之间关系和性质, 条件分布与独立性, 随机变量函数分布问题. 3.L-S 测度，概率测度积分及其性质(包括积分变换定理),期望(方差),条件期望(方差),条件数学期望(方差)及其性质,全期望(方差)公式,特征函数与概率分布关系, 多元正态分布及其性质,抽样分布定理. 4.概率极限理论(包括4种收敛充要条件刻画及其关系,随机级数收敛,一致可积性刻画,重点是分布收敛与特征函数收敛等价性问题),B-C引理,Chebyshev不等式与Kolmogorov极大值不等式,中心极限定理,(强)大数定理. 5.Poisson过程与Brownian Motions,离散时间鞅论介绍.
Course Description
1.Random events, fields and σ–fields, monotone classes,probability space,conditional probability and independence. 2.Random variables and random vectors, probability distributions (important distributions cover Binomial distribution, Poisson distribution, Uniform distribution, Cauchy distribution, Laplace distribution, Normal distribution, Gama-distribution,Pascal-distribution,T-distribution,F-distribution,Chi square-distribution, Beta- distribution and their properties), conditional distribution and independence. 3.L-S measure, Probability and integral,expectation and conditional expectation,conditional expectation given a sigma-field,characteristic functions and generating function, multivariate normal distributions and sampling distribution theorem. 4.Convergences of random variables(convergences a.s.,convergences in probability, weak convergences),(strong)law of large numbers, random series, central limit Theorem. 5.Poisson process and Brownian motion, discrete time martingales and Markov chains .
