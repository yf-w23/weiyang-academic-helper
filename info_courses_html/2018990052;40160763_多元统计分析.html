课程内容简介：
本课程讲授多元统计方法的基本原理，主要包括基于多元正态分布的单总体、双总体均值检验，针对独立同分布的多元数据的降维、聚类、分类的基本方法。授课过程中将结合统计软件R，在讲解理论方法的基础上介绍这些方法在数据分析中的应用。
Course Description
In this course, the fundamental techniques in multivariate statistical analysis will be introduced, mainly including the inference for one population mean or two population mean based on multivariate normal distribution, the basic methods for dimension reduction, clustering and classification based on i.i.d. multivariate data. The application of multivariate techniques in R will also be covered.
进度安排：

1、  Introduction to Multivariate Data (1)

a)     What is multivariate data? Matrix form

b)     Modeling of Multivariate Data

c)      Descriptive statistics

d)     Geometry of random sample

e)     Statistical distance

f)       Exploratory data analysis (and demo in R)

2、  Matrix Algebra and application in MVA (1)

a)     Vector

b)     Matrix and matrix operation

c)      Geometric Interpretation of descriptive statistics

d)     Generalized variance

e)     Other Matrix Decomposition and Some Inequalities

                  i.           Singular value decomposition

                ii.           Cauchy-Schwarz Inequality / Extended Cauchy-Schwarz Inequality

              iii.           Maximization lemma, maximization of quadratic forms

               iv.           Cholesky decomposition

f)       Vector and Matrix Calculus 

g)      Theoretical results of sample statistics, linear combinations of random variables

3、  Multivariate Normal Distribution (1)

a)     Definition

b)     Properties of multivariate normal distribution

c)      Modeling with multivariate normal distribution,

                  i.           Modeling by random sampling

                ii.           MLE

              iii.           Unbiased estimator

               iv.           Sufficient statistics and their large sample behavior

d)     How to assess the normality assumption; outlier detection

4、  Inference for one population mean (1)

a)     Multivariate Normal Distribution

                  i.           Hypothesis test: Hotelling’s T2

                ii.           Properties of Hotelling’s T2 , relations to LRT

              iii.           Confidence regions and simultaneous comparisons of component means

b)     Large Sample Inferences about a population mean vector

5、  Inference for two population mean (1)

a)     Multivariate Normal Distribution

                  i.           Paired sample

                ii.           Equal variance

b)     Large Sample Inferences

c)      Assessing the Assumption

d)     Outlier detection

6、 Principal Component Analysis (1.5)

a.    Motivation

b.    Population principal component

c.     Sample principal component

d.    Practical issues in PCA analysis

e.    Large sample inference

f.      PCA and singular value decomposition 

7、Multidimensional Scaling (0.5)

a. connections with PCA

7、8、Factor Analysis (2)

a.    Motivation

b.    Orthogonal factor model

c.     Model of estimation: PC approach and MLE approach

d.    Factor rotation

e.    Factor score

f.      Comparison between FA and PCA

g.    Extensions in high dimensional data (optional)

8、9、Canonical correlation analysis (1)

a.    Motivation

b.    Canonical variables

c.     How to find CCA

d.    Sample canonical variables and canonical correlations

e.    Additional sample descriptive measures

f.      Extensions in most recent research (optional)

9、10、Discriminant analysis (1)

a.    Motivation

b.    Separation and classification for two populations

c.     Classification with two multivariate normal populations

d.    Fisher’s discriminant analysis

e. Sparse LDA and sparse QDA (optional)

11、 Clustering Analysis (1)

a)     Similarity measures

b)     Hierarchical clustering methods

c)      Nonhierarchical clustering methods

                  i.           K-means method
