课程简介：
本课程将通过课堂讲授和讨论，帮助学生学习量子计算机运行的基本原理，建造量子计算机的基本方法和要素，研讨实现量子计算机的各种不同物理体系的优缺点，讨论各种实现方法的最前沿进展和面临的问题，探讨最可能实现规模化量子计算的物理系统。本课程可以作为开展量子计算机研究的基础课程，也可做为通识教育的一部分，帮助学生了解掌握信息处理的最新前沿进展。
英文课程简介：
This course will teach the principles of how a quantum computer works through class teaching, literature survey and seminar discussion. We will review the latest developments of practical quantum computers, discuss the basic requirements of building a quantum computer, differences and merits of different physical realizations. By taking this class, students can learn the basic knowledge about scalable quantum computer. It can be taken as the first step in starting a research project in quantum information process, as well as a part of general studies.
