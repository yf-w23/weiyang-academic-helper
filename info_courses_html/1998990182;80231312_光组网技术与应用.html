课程简介：
本课程为清华大学-华为“智能基座”联接领域共建课程，讲述国际最先进的光组网技术。光通信是信息社会的基础设施。我国的光组网架构是我国通信领域国际竞争力的一个重要标志，在国民经济、国防建设、科学研究等领域发挥着不可替代的作用。本课程首先讲述光组网的基本概念、基本架构、原理和算法，分析光网络故障处理策略，介绍人工智能光组网关键技术；然后介绍数据中心/高性能计算、无线光接入、智能电力系统、智能交通、海缆通信、卫星通信，以及时频同步等重要应用领域中的光组网应用技术。通过本课程的学习，学生将掌握光组网的基本理论、关键技术和协议算法；了解不同应用领域光组网的特殊性与组网方式；具备基于应用需求设计光网络、优化光组网技术的能力。
英文课程简介：
Optical communication has developed to be the infrastructure of the information society. China's optical networking is an important symbol for China to obtain optical communication international competitiveness, and plays more and more important role in China's economy, scientific research and other fields. This course first introduces the basic architecture, principle and algorithm of optical networking, analyzes optical network protection and restoration, and introduces artificial intelligence optical networking; Then the course turns to the application technology of optical networking in key fields, including data center/high-performance computing, wireless optical access, intelligent power system, intelligent transportation, submarine cable communication, satellite communication, and time-frequency synchronization, etc.. With this course, students will master the basic theory, key technology and protocol algorithm of optical networking, understand the particularity and networking modes of optical networking in different application fields, and have the ability to design optical network and optimize optical networking technology based on application requirements.
