课程内容简介：
皮肤作为人体最大的器官，对其进行科学护理，不仅属于社会文化层面的审美需求，更是生理层面预防疾病、维护身体健康的需求。本课程以皮肤科学与化妆品学为基础，构建从理论到实践的教学体系，分为基础篇与应用篇两大部分：基础篇讲解皮肤结构与生理功能、化妆品成分与配方科学；应用篇涵盖皮肤清洁、保湿与润肤、抗衰除皱、防晒、美白淡斑及祛痘等实际皮肤护理的相关原理和具体方法。课程旨在促进同学们掌握科学护肤的核心知识与实践方法，全面提升科学护肤的能力。
Course Description
The skin, as the largest organ of the human body, requires scientific care not only to meet sociocultural aesthetic demands but also to prevent diseases and maintain physical health at a physiological level. This course is based on skin science and cosmetology, building a teaching system that integrates theoretical knowledge with practical application. It consists of two main parts: a foundational section and an applied section. The foundational section covers the structure and physiological functions of the skin, as well as cosmetic ingredients and formulation science. The applied section includes the principles and specific methods of practical skin care, such as cleansing, moisturizing, anti-aging and wrinkle reduction, sun protection, whitening and spot fading, and acne treatment. The course aims to help students master core knowledge and practical methods of scientific skin care, comprehensively enhancing their ability to care for their skin in a scientifically informed manner.
