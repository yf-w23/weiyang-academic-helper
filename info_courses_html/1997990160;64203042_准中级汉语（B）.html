课程简介：
本课程共32学时，是面向全校国际研究生和国际交换生开设的汉语准中级课程。这门课适合通过HSK3级且掌握至少400汉字、1000词汇量和初级语法项目的汉语学习者选修，如果学习者开始就发现对教材生词表中一半以上的词语不熟悉，听课会感到困难。与初级汉语相比，该级别教材包含更长的汉语文章，其词汇丰富、语法复杂、内容广泛，主要涉及学习与生活、人物外貌、性格、爱好、心理活动以及各地名人和风俗等。课上教师将主要用汉语精讲教材内容，并配合语言类的各项练习。学完该课程后，学生将有以下几方面收获： 1、掌握更多汉字和词汇，总认读词汇量有望达到1500。 2、接触书面语表达，特别是提高近义词的辨析能力。 3、练习使用复杂但常见的语言结构，进行口头和笔头成段表达。
英文课程简介：
This Pre-intermediate-level Chinese language course consists of 32 class-teaching-hours and is designed for international graduate students and exchange students with basic Chinese language literacy and oral-aural skills. This course is most suitable for learners who can recognize at least 400 Chinese characters, have a Chinese vocabulary of about 1000 words, and grasp basic Chinese grammar with HSK 3 level. If learners find that more than half of the words in the vocabulary list of the textbook are unfamiliar, it may be difficult to follow the lectures for them. Compared to the elementary level, this course’s teaching material / textbook includes longer Chinese articles, a richer vocabulary, and more complex grammar, covering a wider range of topics such as school life, daily life, physical appearance, personality, hobbies, psychological activities, and notable people and cultures from different regions. In class, the teacher will explain the texts in Mandarin and provide various language exercises. Students can expect the following learning outcomes upon completing the course: 1. Acquisition of more Chinese characters and vocabulary, reaching a recognized total of 1500. 2. Exposure to written Chinese expressions with a focus on distinguishing synonyms. 3. Practice using complex yet common language structures for paragraph and short essay writing and presentation.

教学安排根据实际情况适时调整快慢，完成6到8课的学习。The teaching arrangement will be adjusted according to the actual situation. About 6 to 8 texts/lessons could be covered.

1、老师上课基本上使用汉语，几乎不用其他语种解释。

2、要求学生保证出勤率：迟到超过3次开始扣分甚至降级，缺勤超过4次劝退或成绩不及格。伤病等情况可特殊考虑，但须及时提供有效的医院证明或官方文件说明。

3、家庭作业要按时完成并及时提交，漏交、迟交和补交将影响成绩，逾期两周后补交的作业不再计成绩。

4、鼓励学生在平时作业中提交一部分手写的内容。仅仅用电脑生成中文文本的作业可能会面临最高10%的降级。

5、从第3周到第16周可与助教或老师进行口语会话练习，至少完成5次（每次25-30分钟），通过开课初期口试的同学可免除此项作业。

6、我们的考试是闭卷，要求用纸笔手写作答。最终课程成绩按A、B、C、D、F等级制评分。平时成绩以5、4、3、2/1、0记录。

1.  The course is mainly lectured in Mandarin, with few explanations in other languages.

2. Student’s attendance will be kept track. If a student is late more than 3 times, their scores will be subject to deduction or they may face a downgrade. If a student has more than 4 absences during the semester, they will be asked to withdraw or may receive a failing mark for the course. Special circumstances such as injuries or illness can be considered, but valid hospital or official documentation must be provided in a timely manner.

3. Routine homework must be completed and submitted on time. Late submissions and make-up assignments will affect your grade. Homework submitted more than two weeks past the deadline will not be accepted for grading.

4.  Students are encouraged to include some work handwritten in pen or pencil (not keyboard-typed or AI-generated) in their assignments. Fully digital submissions with no original handwriting may be subject to a grade reduction of up to 10%.. 

5.  From Week 3 till Week 16, students may have Chinese oral practice with the TA, with a minimum of 5 sessions (about 25-30 minutes each) required. Students who pass the oral exam early in the course are exempted from this assignment.

6. Our exams are closed-book and require handwritten answers using pen on paper. Final grades will be recorded as A, B, C, D, F. Routine homework will be recorded as 5, 4, 3, 2/1, 0 score points from the top to the bottom.
