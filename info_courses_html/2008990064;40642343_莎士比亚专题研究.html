课程内容简介：
1.文本精读与批评分析：精读5-6部莎士比亚戏剧原文，通过文学批评视角，分析其中的艺术手法，并审视作品与伊丽莎白一世及詹姆斯一世时期社会历史背景的关联。 2.创意表达与阐释：创作一件基于莎剧的创意作品（如戏剧改编、艺术创作、多媒体项目等），以独特而个人的艺术形式，展现对剧本的当代解读与创新性理解。 3.学术研究与论文撰写：开展一项独立的研究项目，内容包括文献综述、选题定题、研究计划与论文大纲的研讨，并最终撰写一篇学术论文。旨在培养对莎士比亚戏剧及其文化语境的深度分析能力和批判性独立思考。
Course Description
In this course students read Shakespeare’s plays in the original and explore the stage conventions and cultural trends of his era. Through in-class discussions, critical perspectives, translations and multimedia resources, the course deepens their understanding of the richness of Shakespeare’s text. Approaching Shakespeare as an aesthetic journey and a profound exploration of reality, students are encouraged to create their own connections with the Bard through discussions, performances, writing and other creative activities. The course culminates in an independent research project in the field of Shakespeare studies, guiding students to produce a scholarly paper.
