课程简介：
该课程是为具有药学、医学、生物学等相关背景的本科教育背景的学生开设的一门涉及多学科领域的研究生选修课程，以加强药品医疗器械全生命周期管理的法律法规知识和科学规律理解为主要培养目的。课程将介绍美国和中国药品医疗器械监管的法律法规和最新的趋势，以药品医疗器械全生命周期质量管理为核心，涉及药物研究、开发、上市评价和上市后全生命周期管理的法律法规体系，通过理论学习、案例教学等模式，系统了解监管法规、理解药品医疗器械全生命周期质量管理的要求。 课程可以向本科生开放。
英文课程简介：
This program in Regulatory Science is an intensive, interdisciplinary program designed for those graduates with the background of biological, pharmaceutical and biomedical sciences. The purpose of the grogram aims at enhanceing regulatory policy knowledge and skills to meet the needs of managing regulated biomedical products in the real market. This program will expose the latest trends in domestic and global regulation as well as quality practices in the biotechnology, pharmaceutical, and medical device industries. It will focus on the quality management of drug and medical devices throughout their life cycle. In class, the audients will use real-world examples and projects to gain greater knowledge of the foundations of regulatory, quality, and clinical sciences. You will also come to understand the complexities of blending engineering, pharmaceutical, and regulatory sciences on a global scale. This program is also suitable for undergraduate students.
