课程简介：
本课程包括四个方面的内容,全面介绍航空宇航主要电子系统,航空宇航电子设备的机电热一体化设计,航空宇航电子产品的可靠性可维修性和安全性设计以及航空宇航电子的前沿技术. 第一部分 绪论,介绍航空宇航电子学基本概念,历史现状和发展以及课程安排和要求. 第二部分 介绍航空宇航主要电子系统,包括:飞行控制,发动机控制,航空电子(通信,导航),电子载荷(机载星载雷达)及其它电子系统(地形感知)等. 第三部分 航空宇航电子设备机电热一体化设计,包括数字,模拟,微波等典型电路设计,机械结构和力学设计和分析,热设计和分析,电磁兼容设计和分析等方面的设计分析原则方法,并结合实例讲授计算机辅助设计软件,并开展实验课程. 第四部分 突出航空航天高可靠性和可维修性的特点,讲授可靠性设计分析的基本概念方法和电路设计实例. 第五部分 结合航空宇航电子领域的最新动态介绍最新的电子技术和系统.
英文课程简介：
This course aims to a comprehensive introduction to four aspects, including aviation and aerospace electronic systems, electromechanical thermal integration design for aviation and aerospace electronic equipment, the reliability, maintainability and safety design for aviation aerospace electronic products and aviation aerospace electronic cutting-edge technology. Part I Introduction, introduce aviation and aerospace electronics basic concepts, history, current situation and development as well as curriculum and requirements. Part II Introduce of aviation and aerospace electronic systems：flight control, engine control, avionics (communications, navigation), electronic payload (Airborne & space borne radar) and other electronic systems (terrain perception). Part III The electromechanical thermal integration design for aviation and aerospace electronic equipments, includes designs of digital, analog, microwave typical circuit, principles and methods of mechanical, structural and mechanical design and analysis, thermal design and analysis, electromagnetic compatibility design and analysis, to teach computer-aided design software with examples and to carry out experimental courses. Part IV With emphasis on the aerospace high reliability and maintainability characteristics, teach basic concepts and methods for the analysis of the reliability design and circuit design examples. Part V Introduce the latest electronic technology and systems, combined with the latest new developments in aviation and aerospace electronics field.
