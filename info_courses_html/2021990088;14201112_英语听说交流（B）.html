课程内容简介：
本课程面向中级英语语言水平的学生（英语分级考试成绩为二级），聚焦听力理解、日常口头对话、演说及学术话题讨论能力的培养。本课程鼓励学生强化语言迁移能力，提升表达自信度，培养语言、思辨、跨文化传播、研究及自主学习能力。主要内容包括：（1）较为准确理解语速为130-150词/分钟的听力材料；（2）能够较得体地参与课堂讨论及会议发言，如组织陈述个人观点，总结、分析及评价他人观点；（3）应用语音知识如重音、连读、失爆破、节奏等进行意义沟通。课程设计基于“输出驱动、输入促成”的产出导向法教学理念，采取形成性评估，学期中进行阶段性听力测试，学生需完成若干独立口语任务和小组合作口语任务。
Course Description
Listening and Speaking for Academic Purposes (B) is a learning-centered course at the intermediate level to help students develop listening and speaking skills and thus enhance global competence especially in academic activities. During the semester, students listen to short lectures or conversations from different disciplines, building skills in note-taking to grasp the main ideas and supporting details. Listening assignments are mainly completed after class, which are checked in listening tests. Speaking activities are organized in class, which are intended to develop skills for retelling, summarizing, reviewing and evaluating what is listened to or watched and expressing one’s viewpoints on certain topics in academic contexts such as class discussion or seminar.
