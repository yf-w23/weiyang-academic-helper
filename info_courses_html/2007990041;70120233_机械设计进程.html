课程简介：
课程简介 本课程根据研究生《机械设计进程》(Machine Design Process)的教学需要，完成覆盖清华大学机械工程学院研究生48学时教学内容的全部英文讲义。涉及空间机构的结构设计、空间机构的旋量运动学与静力学分析、机械系统动力学、柔性机械系统设计、机械创新设计进程。 教学活动包括课堂讲授、课后作业与项目训练、课堂讨论。具体教学形式还可能包括其它内容。
英文课程简介：
According to the teaching plan of Machine Design Process for graduate students, this course has completed all the English lecture notes covering 48 lectures of teaching content for graduate students in the School of Mechanical Engineering of Tsinghua University. It involves the structure design of space mechanism, the screw kinematics and statics analysis of space mechanism, the dynamics of mechanical system, the design of flexible mechanical system and the process of mechanical innovation design. The teaching activities include but not restrict to class lecture, homework, project training, and class discussion.
