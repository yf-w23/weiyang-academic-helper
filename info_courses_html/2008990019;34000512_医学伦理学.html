课程内容简介：
该课程是医学院医学专业学生的必修课程。医学伦理学是医学及其相关专业必修课程的基础，是接受医学教育、实践医学活动必经的桥梁，是医学科技与医学人文联系的纽带。其主要任务是研究各个时代的医德关系和医德现象，揭示医德的起源、本质、作用和发展规律，研究医德的原则、规范和范畴，医德评价的标准和方法，同时也阐述现代医疗实践和医学科研中提出的各种伦理难题，以保证医疗卫生、医学科研和教育的发展方向。该课程以理论教学为主，结合讨论式教学，力争通过观察法、调查法、文献法等多种渠道，使学生认识并解决医疗及医学科研中遇到的种种伦理问题，理解医学的科学和人文双重性质和要求，提高医学生的医生职业价值观，培养良好的职业素质。
Course Description
Medical ethics is the interdisciplinary subject of the humanities and natural sciences. It is the basis of the policy, strategy and legislation of modern life science and technology, as well as medical and health development. The subject is practical, knowledgeable and global. This course aims to study medical ethics and medical phenomenon, reveal the origin, essence, function and development law of medical ethics, study the standard and method of medical ethics evaluation, discuss the moral problems in modern medical life through combining the various realistic problems in the process of medical system reform in China, answer difficult ethics problems raised in modern medical practice and medicine developments. Through educating and guiding students to improve medical ethics culture, we hope to cultivate students become medical workers with noble medical ethics in the future. This course is required course for students of MD Program. Various teaching forms, such as theory teaching, case discussion and practice, will be integrated into the course. Active thinking and combination with diversified knowledges will be encouraged in the teaching process.
