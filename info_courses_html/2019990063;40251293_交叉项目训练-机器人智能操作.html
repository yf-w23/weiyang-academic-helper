课程内容简介：
本课程面向工科专业本科生，旨在培养学生了解人工智能与机器人结合的最新进展，通过动手实践，掌握开发智能机器人的能力。本课程包括三部分：(a) 理论课 - 讲授机器人相关的理论知识；(b) 课程项目 - 搭建机械臂操作系统，编程实现算法，控制机器人执行抓取任务，进行比赛来展示其性能；(c) 专题讲座 - 介绍机器人领域最新最热门的进展。 课程将教学与最新科研进展结合，教学内容包括大量机器人领域旗舰期刊与会议的最佳论文与视频介绍（Science Robotics, IJRR, TRO, ICRA, IROS）；通过设计课程项目，强化学生对于理论知识的理解，鼓励学生去追踪热点，采用最新的技术与算法去解决问题，综合提高学生的理论深度、动手能力、解决问题的能力与发散思维。 课程的实验实践部分设计为机器人比赛：智能机器人抓取挑战赛。该环节鼓励学生应用最新的人工智能与机器学习技术去提升机器人的速度、精度、泛化能力，最终通过评估所搭建机器人的任务完成度、自主能力与执行速度来判定成绩。在项目过程中，通过设置一系列的里程碑（milestone），循序渐进地帮助学生按照模块、功能来完成项目任务。
Course Description
This course is designed to educate undergraduate students majoring in Engineering. It trains students to have a systematical understanding of robotics, master theoretical knowledge, and develop the ability to build a real robotic system. The course consists of three modules: (a) Theoretical Lesson – to teach students theoretical knowledge; (b) Course Project – to allow students to build a robotic manipulator, realize a control algorithm for grasping tasks and demonstrate its performance in the competition; (c) Seminar – to introduce the latest research outcomes in the robotics field. The course combines the breakthrough research results with the teaching and includes the introduction of papers and supplemental videos which won the “Best Paper Award” in the top journals and conferences in the robotics field (Science Robotics, IJRR, TRO, ICRA, IROS). This course will provide students with the opportunity to recognize, evaluate, customize, and interact with robotic systems through productive teaching methods and cutting-edge teaching content. Students will also focus on popular research topics and identify and explore robotics and automation technology that may affect human society. The Course Project is designed to include a series of milestone tasks, where students can refer to their background and interests to develop the robot manipulator and use the new algorithm of AI and machine learning to further improve the performance of robots.
