课程内容简介：
随着全球变暖、环境污染、自然生境退化、生物多样性丧失、人与野生动物冲突等问题的加剧，公众对生态、生物多样性、自然保护等概念的关注也越来越多，这些概念的阐释与问题的解决都离不开生态学研究。生态学是研究有机体及其所处环境的相互关系的科学，包含个体生态学、种群生态学、群落生态学和生态系统生态学等多个层次，发展出了应用生态学、分子生态学和景观生态学等多个分支。本课程面向生态学初学者，对生态学的基本理论、研究方法和实际案例等进行介绍，以期帮助初学者建立生态学的基本知识框架、激发对生态学研究的兴趣，更重要的是希望初学者能亲身参与到生物多样性与生态环境的保护之中，成为生态文明的实际建设者。
Course Description
With the intensification of many ecological problems (e.g. global warming, environment pollution, habitat degradation, biodiversity loss, and human-wildlife conflict), more and more citizens pay close attention to ecology, biodiversity, nature conservation, etc. Ecology is the study of interactions between organisms and their environment. It is composed of several levels (e.g. autecology, population ecology, community ecology, ecosystem ecology, etc.), and has many subdisciplines (e.g. applied ecology, molecular ecology, landscape ecology, etc.). This course of ecology is for beginners with lectures on ecological theories, methods, and cases, aiming at helping beginners establish their knowledge framework of ecology and get involved in environment and biodiversity conservation.
