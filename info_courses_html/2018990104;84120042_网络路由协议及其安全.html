课程简介：
网络路由是当前互联网最重要的技术基础和研究热点之一，对路由协议及其安全的了解，为网络空间安全的学习和研究打下基础。本课程从路由算法和路由协议的基础知识入手、详细学习主要的域内和域间IPv4/IPv6路由协议及其安全问题与安全机制：距离向量类路由协议及其安全；链路状态路由协议及其安全；路径向量类路由协议及其安全；学习和介绍互联网路由体系结构和寻址安全的前沿研究：学习BGP路由安全；源地址验证和追溯技术；介绍新型路由结构及其安全。
英文课程简介：
Network routing is the major technical foundation and one of the most important research topics in the Internet. Based on an introduction to the foundation of routing algorithm and routing protocol, this course teaches the technical details of the major intra-domain and inter-domain IPv4/IPv6 routing protocols. The course also focuses on advanced research topics in routing and addressing area: BGP routing security, source address validation and traceback, new routing studies and their security. By attending this course, the students are able to learn and master the theory and technology of future Internet to promote their later research. On the other hand, by reading a large amount of related references and literatures, and finishing the assigned projects, students may be trained with the capabilities of problem statement, analyzing, and solving.
