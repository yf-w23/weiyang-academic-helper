课程简介：
本课程的目标是让学生熟悉金融投资分析理论和技术，特别是人工智能/机器学习技术在投资中的应用。这门课程适合想了解资产管理业务和量化投资的同学，尤其是那些希望了解如何将量化分析和人工智能技术应用在资产管理行业中的同学。课程涵盖了金融投资相关诸多主题，包括主动投资、量化投资流程、多因子模型、投资组合构建方法、绩效评估以及人工智能/机器学习技术在金融投资中的应用。这是一门非常实践性的课程。同学们将需要评估、设计、构建和回测量化策略。本课程还要求同学阅读一些相关研究和书籍，并将这些内容融入到他们的投资策略中。本课程没有期末考试，但是将会有一些作业和一个期末课程报告。
英文课程简介：
This course aims to acquaint students with the theories and techniques of equity analysis using artificial intelligence, particularly machine learning technologies. Designed for those interested in asset management and equity investing, the course is ideal for students aspiring to apply quantitative and machine learning methods within the asset management industry. The course covers a wide range of equity investing topics, including active investing, the quantitative investment process, multi-factor stock selection models, portfolio construction methods, performance evaluation, and the application of AI and machine learning technologies in equity investing. This is a highly hands-on course, requiring students to evaluate, design, build, and backtest equity investment strategies. Additionally, students will engage with various research studies and books, integrating their insights into their strategies. There is no final exam; instead, the course includes several assignments and a final project.
