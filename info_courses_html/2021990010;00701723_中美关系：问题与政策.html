课程内容简介：
本课程是有关当代中美关系中若干重大问题的通识课程。这些问题包括中国人的美国观与美国人的中国观、中国的对美战略与美国的对华战略、中美关系当中的政治人权议题、中美关系中的人文交流、中美关系中的经贸问题、中美关系中的军事安全问题、中美关系中的台湾问题、中美关系中的海洋问题等议题。 本课程的主要特点是： 第一，强调培养学生的换位思考和共智能力。本课程的基本前提假定是：中国与美国各自都拥有自己引以为傲的历史传统和价值体系，也都为人类和世界做出过巨大的贡献。那么，这样两个伟大的国家为何会产生激烈的矛盾和冲突？本课程强调，一个具备国际眼光的优秀公民（无论国籍）应当具备换位思考并理解其他国际行为者的能力。我们不一定需要赞同但是需要理解其他国家及其公民的观念与利益。本课程就是围绕这一理念设计的； 第二，本课程特邀美国乔治·布什美中关系基金会总裁方大为（David Firestein）承担本课程1/3的教学任务，并主要负责为学生提供看问题的美国视角。这一部分授课采用英文教学； 第三，本课程是政策导向的课程，聚焦当前中美关系中最前沿也是最突出的问题，突出动态色彩。同时，课程也会兼顾各个专题的历史和来龙去脉。
Course Description
This course is a course on major issues in contemporary U.S.-China relations. These issues include but do not limited to Chinese views of America and American views of China, China's strategy toward the United States and American strategy toward China, political and human rights issues in Sino-US relations, people to people exchanges, economic and trade issues, military security issues, Taiwan issues, maritime security issues, and other topics. The main features of this course are: (1) it emphasizes on the students' ability of intellectual empathy. The basic premise of this course assumes that China and the United States each have their historical traditions and value systems, and both have made great contributions to humanity and the world. Why, then, have such two great nations come into intense conflict and contradiction? This course emphasizes that a good citizen with an international perspective (regardless of nationality) should have the ability of being intellectually empathetic to other international actors. We do not necessarily need to agree with but have to understand each other. (2) I will invite my long-time colleague, Mr. David Firestein, President of the George W. Bush Foundation for U.S.-China Relations, to teach 1/3 of the course and is primarily responsible for providing students with an American perspective on each issue. This part of the course will be taught in English. (3) the course is policy-oriented, focusing on the most current and salient issues in U.S.-China relations. However, the course will also take into account the history and ins and outs of each topic.
