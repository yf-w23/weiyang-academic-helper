耳鼻咽喉科学进展

课程简介：
本课程的教学以大课讲授为主要形式，主要阐述耳鼻咽喉科特点及新进展。分四方面：耳科学：外、中耳疾病的诊疗进展、人工听觉植入技术的进展、人工材料在中耳手术中的应用、侧颅底外科的进展、突发性聋的诊疗进展、眩晕疾病的诊疗进展。鼻科学：鼻科和前颅底外科的进展、修复材料的进展、导航技术的进展。咽喉科学：新技术在咽喉手术中的应用、阻塞性睡眠呼吸暂停综合的诊疗进展、上气道科研领域新进展。 嗓音外科的进展。新技术在食管、气管疾病的应用（激光、氩气刀等）。头颈外科学：甲状腺治疗进展、头颈肿瘤综合诊疗的进展。
英文课程简介：
1. Characteristics and new progress of otolaryngology. 2. Progress of Otology: advances in cochlear implantation, application of artificial material in middle ear surgery, research progress on hereditary deafness, progress of lateral skull base surgery, advances in diagnosis and treatment of vertigo disease, application prospects of stem cell technology in deafness treatment, advances in diagnosis and treatment of sudden deafness. 3.Progress of Rhinology: progress of skull base surgery, repair material，navigation technology and genetics in Rhinology. 4.Progress of Pharyngology: application of new technology in the pharynx and larynx surgery, diagnosis and treatment progress of obstructive sleep apnea hypopnea syndrome. 5.Progress of Laryngology：Progress of phoniatrics surgery. 6.Progress of Esophagus and Trachea Surgery: application of new technology in esophagus and trachea disease. 7.Progress of Head and Neck Surgery: advances in thyroid treatment and comprehensive diagnosis and treatment of head and neck neoplasms.
