课程内容简介：
《材料科学与工程基础》作为材料科学与工程学科的核心课程，属于专业基础课，为为先书 院本科生并面向未央书院、致理书院、化工系、化学系、机械工程学院、航空航天学院、工 程物理系等院系本科生开设，把金属及合金、陶瓷和玻璃、聚合物、半导体、复合材料等各 类材料综合在一起，归纳出共性规律进行讨论，突出共性，增加内涵，内容包括原子结构与 键合、晶体学基础、固体材料结构、晶体的塑性形变、晶体缺陷、表面与界面、材料热力学 与相图、固体中的扩散、凝固与结晶、回复与再结晶和固态相变等章节，讲授材料科学的基 本概念和基础理论，是学生学习其他专业课的基础，也是今后从事材料应用、开发和研究等 的基础。
Course Description
Fundamental of Material Science and Engineering is a core course of materials science and engineering, belonging to the fundamental couse of the materials. This course is mainly set for the undergraduate students of Weixian College, Weiyang College, Zhili College, chemical engineering, chemistry, mechanical engineering, aerospace engineering, and engineering physics. Different kinds of materials, metals, alloys, ceramics, glass, polymer, semiconductor, and composites, etc., are involved in the course, and their general rule are summarized. The contents of atomic structure and bonding, fundamental of crystallinity, structure of solid materials, plastic deformation of crystals, defects, surface and interface, thermodynamics of materials, phase diagram, diffusion in solid materials, solidification and crystallization, recovery and recrystallization, and solid phase transition are systematically discussed. The basic concepts and fundamental theories on materials science are introduced in the course. These knowledges are not only the basis for studying other specialized course, but also the basis for the students who embark the job revlevant to R&D of materials.
