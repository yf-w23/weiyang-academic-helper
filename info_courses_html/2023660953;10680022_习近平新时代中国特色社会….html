课程内容简介：
本课程为思想政治理论课，系统讲授习近平新时代中国特色社会主义思想的核心要义、精神实质、科学内涵、实践要求。在教学中坚持马克思主义立场观点和方法，注重理论与实践相结合，强调师生互动交流，力图融合不同学科的视角，解读新时代中国特色社会主义的伟大实践。 本课程有以下特色：大课讲授强调交流互动，理解掌握总书记思想要义；平时作业重视逻辑思辨，培养分析问题和写作能力；期末作业要求走出书斋，了解基层社会的生动实践。授课教师拥有经济学、政治学、马克思主义理论、法学、管理学的交叉学科背景。欢迎关注中国经济社会现实问题、希望学习社会科学的研究方法和思维方式的同学选课。
Course Description
This course systematically teaches the core essence, spiritual essence, scientific connotation, and practical requirements of Xi Jinping's Thought on Socialism with Chinese Characteristics for a New Era. It adheres to the Marxist position, viewpoints and methods in teaching, focuses on the combination of theory and practice, emphasizes the interaction between teachers and students, and tries to integrate the perspectives of different disciplines to interpret the great practice of socialism with Chinese characteristics in the new era. The course has the following characteristics: the lectures in the large classes emphasize communication and interaction to help students understand the essence of the ideas; the midterm assignments attach importance to logical thinking and cultivate students' ability to analyze problems and write; and the final assignments require students to understand the real-world social practices. The lecturers have interdisciplinary backgrounds in economics, political science, Marxist theory, law and management. Students who are concerned about the economic and social realities in China and wish to learn the research methods and ways of thinking in social sciences are welcome to take the course.
