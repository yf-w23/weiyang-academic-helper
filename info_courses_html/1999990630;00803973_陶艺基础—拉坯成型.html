课程内容简介：
本课程的教学目的，重在通过拉坯制作陶瓷的亲身体验，在学习中锻炼同学们淡定的心态，提升自身定力，拓宽知识面，培养学生的综合素养，助力未来的学术成长。学习内容主要包括：拉坯、修坯。讲解拉坯工具、原料和不同粘土的特性后，通过系统的、循序渐进的学习和实践，探讨通过拉坯制作陶瓷的步骤和技法，重点学习拉制杯、碗、盘、罐和瓶，并演示在拉坯机上做水流、把手、钮和盖的方法。课程期间，穿插以赏析的方式，选用古今经典陶瓷作品图片和实物，从艺术和技术方面进行较为深入的评析，为同学们的学习和创作提供借鉴和参考。本课程注重学习互动，注重在中国优秀传统文化中吸取营养，可以充分发挥自己的想象力和创造力，拉制具有个性的陶瓷作品，得到艺术的享受。
Course Description
This course is intended to train students’ patience and perseverance, broaden their knowledge, and develop their overall qualities, which will contribute to their academic growth in the future. The content mainly comprises of throwing and trimming. After explaining the tools, the materials and the properties of different clays, the course explores the steps and techniques of making ceramics through throwing, especially cups, bowls, plates, jugs, and vases and demonstrates the methods of making spouts, handles, knobs, and lids on the wheel. During the course, from time to time, the students are exposed to pictures and objects of selected ancient and modern classical ceramic works, together with in-depth analysis in aspects of art and techniques, which will provide them with references in their future study and creation. This course lays emphasis on interactions between the teacher and the students and on drawing nutrition from the best parts of traditional Chinese culture; the students are allowed to bring their imagination and creativity into full play, to create their unique ceramic works and to enjoy the study on the artistic level.
