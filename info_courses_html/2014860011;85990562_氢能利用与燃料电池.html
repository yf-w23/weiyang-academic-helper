氢能利用与燃料电池

课程简介：
“氢”是一种洁净的二次能源载体，可为燃料电池提供氢源，有望成为“后化石能源时代”能源主体。发展氢能与氢燃料电池汽车对构建低碳新能源体系具有巨大的战略意义。本课程将从氢能的产生、储存、输运，到加氢站、燃料电池以及新能源汽车等氢能产业链中的各个环节做全面的介绍，既介绍相关科学研究的进展，又结合相关产业（尤其是粤港澳大湾区氢能产业）的实际发展情况，从而更全面的培养相关技术和管理人员，满足相关人员对了解氢能产业和研发的迫切需求。课程主要内容有：导论、氢的物理化学性质（安全性）、氢气的制取方法(碱性水电解制氢、PEM电解水制氢及AEM电解水制氢等）、储氢技术与储氢材料、燃料电池（基本原理、主要部件和关键材料等）、电解水制氢和燃料电池电堆（系统设计、故障诊断和耐久性等）、氢能与燃料电池产业发展等。课程教学语言为英语教学。
英文课程简介：
Hydrogen represents a clean energy carrier that can provide a fuel for fuel cells and is expected to become the main energy resource in the post-fossil energy era. The development of hydrogen energy and hydrogen fuel cell vehicles is of great strategic significance for building a low-carbon society. This course will give a comprehensive introduction to hydrogen energy industry, including hydrogen production, hydrogen storage and transportation, hydrogen fuel cells and their key materials. In addition to the principles and fundamental research progress, this course will introduce the development of hydrogen energy industry especially in the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area, so as to achieve comprehensive understanding of the hydrogen energy research and development. The main content includes: introduction, physical and chemical properties of hydrogen, hydrogen production, hydrogen storage, transportation and safety, basic principles of fuel cells, fuel cell components and key materials, fuel cell stack and system design, fuel cell diagnosis and durability, hydrogen energy and fuel cell industry, etc. The course language is English.
