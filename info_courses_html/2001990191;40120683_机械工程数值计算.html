课程内容简介：
理论、实验、计算是现代科学发展的重要研究手段，数值计算则是解决复杂工程问题的重要方法。《机械工程数值计算》作为一门重要的专业必修课，首先从数值计算基础知识入手，介绍线性方程组、特征值、常微分方程和偏微分方程的一般数值方法，在此基础上系统讲授机械工程中力学分析的基本原理及求解方法，主要包括：结构的数值分析(有限元法）基础、传热的数值分析基础、以及流体的数值分析基础。课程阐述数理基础、建模过程、求解原理和工程应用等内容，分析它们之间的相互联系及发展规律，揭示其中的科学本质，具有内容上的完整性和结构上的严谨性。 本课程可以让学生理解如何应用已有的数理基础知识解决实际机械工程问题，是一门衔接数理基础课程的专业课程。通过课堂讲授及讨论、上机建模练习与指导、课程实验设计与分析、研究型作业布置等多种教学环节和教学手段，帮助学生理解数值计算的数学原理，掌握机械工程领域的常用数值计算方法，从而实现最终的教学目标。课程的主要教学环节包括：课堂讲授、课堂讨论、建模训练、网络教学、自主专题研究。成绩评定标准：平时作业+考勤 20%; 上机练习和实验 30%; 期末考试50 %。
Course Description
Theory, experiments, and numerical computation are all important research methods for modern science. Among them, numerical computation is especially crucial to solve complex engineering problems. “Numerical Computation for Mechanical Engineering” is an important compulsory course. First, starting from the basics of numerical calculation, we introduce the general numerical methods of linear equations, eigenvalues, ordinary differential equations and partial differential equations. On this basis, the principles and solution methods of mechanical analysis are systematically taught, mainly including the numerical analysis of structure (finite element method), heat transfer and fluid. The course expounds basic knowledge of the mathematical foundation, modeling process, solving principles and engineering applications, analyzes the interconnections between them, and reveals the scientific essence therein. This course allows students to understand how to apply existing basic knowledge of mathematics and physics to solve practical problems, and is a professional course that bridges the basic courses of mathematics and engineering. Through lectures and discussions, modeling exercises and guidance, experimental design and analysis, research-based homework assignments and other teaching methods, we can help students understand the mathematical principles of numerical calculation, and master its common methods in the field of mechanical engineering.
