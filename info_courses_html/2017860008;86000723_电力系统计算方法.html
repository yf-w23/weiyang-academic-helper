课程简介：
本课程介绍潮流计算的各种计算方法及其收敛性分析。学生应能使用Matlab完成满足计算速度和精度要求的潮流计算。这些是电力系统中常见的实际问题，是电力系统专业学生必须掌握的，也是对电力系统和数值计算方法感兴趣的其他专业学生必须理解的。
英文课程简介：
This course introduces various calculation methods and convergence analysis of power flow calculation. Students should be able to use Matlab to complete power flow calculations that meet the requirements of computational speed and accuracy. These are common practical problems in the power system, which must be mastered by students majoring in power systems, as well as understood by students in other majors who are interested in power systems and numerical calculation methods.
