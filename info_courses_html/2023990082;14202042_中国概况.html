课程内容简介：
本课程专为国际本科留学生开设。课程内容涉及中国的地理、历史、政治制度、经济、科技、教育、传统思想、文学艺术、传统习俗、多民族文化等多个方面。通过修读此课程，国际学生可以了解中国国情概况，为更好地理解中国、修读各自的专业以及在中国生活提供帮助。
Course Description
This course is specifically designed for international undergraduate students. The curriculum covers various aspects of China, including geography, history, political systems, economy, science and technology, education, traditional philosophy, literary arts, traditional customs, and multiculturalism of different ethnic groups. By taking this course, international students can gain an overview of China's national conditions, which will help them to better understand China, pursue their respective majors, and adapt to life in China.
