课程简介：

英文课程简介：
This course aims to teach the principles and classic algorithms of radar imaging. This course is open for graduate students and senior undergraduate students of Tsinghua University. The contents of this course include 20 hours teaching and 12 hours experiments with millimeter wave (mmw) radar kits produced by Infineon. In each experiment, all the students are divided into some groups, one mmw radar kit per group. The group members are required to work in collaboration for designing the experiments, collecting and analyzing data and accomplishing the technical reports, in which the individual contribution to each experiment is required to be clearly clarified. The grade of this course is composed of the scores of all the experimental reports and the final exam. Prerequisites for this course include “Signals and Systems” and “Digital Signal Processing”, and Matlab or C language programming skills are required.
