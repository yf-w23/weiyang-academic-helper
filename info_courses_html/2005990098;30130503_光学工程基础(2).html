课程内容简介：
本课程从光的电磁理论出发，讲授光的电磁理论及应用技术。对于光在传播过程中所发生的各种现象的规律及其应用作较全面的论述。课程将着重说明光波传输特性的物理本质，及其在应用中应注意的问题，课程特别注意理论与实用的结合。课程中结合大量的应用实例，使学生通过本课程学习初步学会如何把光学原理用于解决现代工程技术中的问题。 本课程是专业基础课，一方面为后续课程（光电子技术基础，激光原理，激光技术，信息光学，付立叶光学，非线性光学）奠定理论基础。同时本课程的内容也是从事光学工作人员的必备基本知识。
Course Description
This course mainly introduces the electromagnetic properties of light, the rules of light propagation in isotropic medium interface, light wave superposition and analysis, Interference of light waves and typical interference devices and application, principle and application of light diffraction, the polarization of light and its propagation in the crystal, etc. Course pays attention to the combination of theory and practical, focus on the physical nature of light transmission characteristics, and the problems that should be paid attention to in the application. Through this course, students will learn how to apply optical principles to solve problems in modern engineering technology. This course is a professional basic course. On the one hand, for the subsequent courses (optoelectronic technology, laser principle and laser technology, information optics, Fourier optics, nonlinear optics) lay the theory foundation. At the same time, the content of this course is also a basic knowledge for optical workers.
