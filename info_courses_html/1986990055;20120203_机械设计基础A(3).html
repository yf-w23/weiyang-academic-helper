课程内容简介：
机械设计课程通过对典型零部件设计问题的分析，使学生掌握通用零部件的设计原理、方法和机械设计的一般规律，初步具有机械系统的综合设计能力，树立创新意识，培养机械创新设计能力，培养应用设计资料的能力，了解机械设计理论与方法的最新发展。在机械系统整体分析的基础上，着重分析传动类零件（齿轮传动、蜗杆传动等）支承类零件（轴、滚动轴承）及连接类零件（螺纹连接、轴毂连接等）的工作能力设计及结构设计问题。
Course Description
The course is about design of operational capability (strength, stiffness, life, etc.) of machine elements and parts, as well as structure design of mechanical system. The design of operational capability includes modeling and analysis of machine elements and parts based upon extensive application of physics, mathematics and core mechanical engineering principles (solid mechanics, fluid mechanics, manufacturing, estimation, computer simulation, etc.). The structural design of mechanical systems includes the design of assembly and functional structure in mechanical system. The fundamental concepts and engineering skills for mechanical design will be introduced. The students will learn the theory, method and tool about mechanical design.
