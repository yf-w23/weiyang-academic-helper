课程简介：
以现代光学的基本观念和处理方法来讨论传统的物理光学现象，并用傅立叶光学的基本概念贯穿整个课程。内容涉及光的干涉、衍射、偏振，部分相干性理论，晶体光学和高斯光学。在衍射理论方面，对菲涅耳衍射和夫琅和费衍射做较为详细的讨论；并对近年来新出现的无衍射光束做介绍；对晶体光学的理论以及电光、声光和磁光效应做较为深入的分析探讨；对应用广泛的高斯光束光学的理论基础进行详细讲解。
英文课程简介：
The important phenomena such as interference, diffraction, and polarization are accounted for by basic concept of modern optics. Fourier Optics processing method runs through the entire course. The content mainly involves interference, diffraction, polarization, theory of partial coherent light, and crystal optics. Fraunhofer and Fresnel diffraction are discussed carefully and Besell beam is introduced briefly. The study of these phenomena lays the foundation for an understanding of such devices and concepts as interferometers, thin-film interference, gratings, holograms, polarizers, electro-optics, acousto-optic, magneto-optic effects, and laser beam divergence in the near and far field.
