课程简介：
这是一门聚焦于《老子》原著中的经典领导力哲理的课程，包括原著导读，领导力哲理鉴析等。《老子》是写给古代君王的治世策论，其中蕴含丰富的领导力哲理，即使是在现代社会，也不为过时，而且还大有裨益。通过这门课的学习，不仅能使学生了解领导力哲理的经典论述，而且还能激发他们的自我增强领导力的意识。 本课程使用的《老子》原文版本不同于传统的道德经，而是德篇在前道篇在后的《帛书老子》。这是目前能得到的最早最全的《老子》版本。课程中，《帛书老子》将被恢复到原始的无标点符号无段落的状态，并重新赋予标点符号和段落。由于老子行文精炼，字字珠玑，这样得到的每一段都像一段散文诗。这样的散文诗将被逐段逐句地加以解读，以析取蕴含其中具有现实意义的领导力哲理。 通过这门课程的学习，学生在之后的实践中，遇到与人相关的领导力问题时，不仅能用学到的领导力哲理勉力并激发自己，而且还能自我解读这部古代文化哲学经典，从中吸取营养，实现领导力的可持续自我提升。
英文课程简介：
This is a course focusing on the classic leadership philosophies in the original work of Laozi, including to unscramble the original work, and extract the leadership philosophies in it, etc. The Laozi was a book written for ancient kings to govern the world, and rich leadership philosophies was contained in it. Even in modern society, these are not outdated, and also of great benefit to us. Through the study of this course, students can not only understand the classic discourse of the leadership philosophies, but also stimulate their awareness to enhance leadership by themselves. The original version of the Laozi used in this course, different from the popular Tao Teh Ching, is the Silk Book Laozi, in which the Teh Section is the first and the Tao Section is after it. This is the earliest and most complete version of Laozi available now. In this course, the Silk Book Laozi will be restored to its original state without punctuation, also no paragraph. After that, it will be given punctuations and paragraphs again. In this way, each paragraph is like a prose poem, because Laozi's writing was refined, every word a gem. Such prose poems will be interpreted line by line to extract the leadership philosophies with realistic significance contained in each paragraph. Through the study of this course, when students encounter leadership problems related to people in later practice, they can not only encourage and motivate themselves with the leadership philosophies they have learned, but also interpret this ancient philosophy classic by themselves, absorb the leadership nutrition from it, and achieve sustainable self-improvement of leadership.

进度安排：

本课程全周上课，每周一课，每课两学时。第一堂课介绍帛书老子的相关历史背景，并启动原著的导读解读。之后的课堂上，会逐行解读段落原文，鉴析段落中隐喻或表达的领导力哲理。在学期中和学期末，还有专门课堂讨论原文中的关键解读和重点论述。
