课程简介：
可持续能源中的材料与器件这门课程将介绍可持续发展能源、材料、纳米技术及器件，涉及太阳能电池材料及器件、光电化学材料及器件、压电材料及器件、热电材料及器件、木头及织物器件、柔性电子材料及器件，先进电池材料及器件， 燃料电池及大规模储能技术，3D 打印技术及器件发展等。围绕与现代生活和科技发展密切相关的可持续发展能源材料及器件，举例剖析在研究开发中遇到的实际问题和改进的方法。该课程还将跟踪国内外可持续发展能源、材料、技术及器件研究及应用开发的动态，介绍可持续发展能源、材料及器件最新研究成果和发展方向。
英文课程简介：
This is a comprehensive course with contents at the intersection of nanoscale science and engineering, materials and devices, and energy science and technology. The aim of this course is to provide students with basic knowledge and information in sustainable and renewable energy, and the important approaches using materials and nanotechnology, thus inspiring their further interest in related research and development. Topics to be discussed include sustainability, energy landscape, and nanotechnology, fundamentals of nanoscale materials and structures, solar cell devices and nanotechnology development, solar fuel, piezoelectric devices, thermoelectrics, flexible devices for sustainable energy, 3D printing for sustainable energy, fuel cell, microbial fuel cells, and so on. In addition, the lecturer will use his own research experience to inspire the students understand the challenges facing sustainable energy and the proposed strategies for these challenges. Examples from research projects will be introduced to make the connection between teaching and research, and to cultivate their research interest and understanding on how to do research.
