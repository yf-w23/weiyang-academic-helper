课程内容简介：
中国力争于2030年前实现碳达峰、2060年前实现碳中和。“双碳目标”的提出将在未来近三十年间对社会科学技术、工程实践及社会生活方式提出新的转型要求。本课程培养学生工程领域绿色低碳设计的基本理念，使学生系统掌握工程绿色低碳设计的方法体系、实施策略与实践技能，提高学生对环境保护的认识和责任感，培养学生运用绿色低碳知识解决实际问题的能力。课程主要包括以下内容：碳中和与绿色低碳设计、绿色设计语言、绿色低碳评估方法、绿色低碳设计、跨系统多主体协同设计、案例分析思辨、项目实践挑战等。项目实践环节包括低碳工程设计工具实操、分组设计“零碳社区”、路演评估等。课程培养学生可持续发展理念、批判性思维，以及从跨学科角度创新解决问题的能力。
Course Description
As part of a global response to climate change, China has committed at the UN General Assembly in 2020 to achieve carbon peak and carbon neutrality by 2030 and 2060 respectively. The introduction of the “dual carbon goals” will bring about new transformation requirements in social science and technology, engineering and social lifestyles over a period of nearly three decades. This course cultivates students' foundational concepts of green and low-carbon engineering design, equipping them with a systematic mastery of the methodological framework, implementation strategies, and practical skills in green and low-carbon engineering design. It aims to enhance students' environmental awareness and sense of responsibility while fostering their ability to apply green and low-carbon knowledge to solve real-world problems. The course primarily covers the following topics: carbon neutrality and green low-carbon design, green design language, green low-carbon assessment methods, green low-carbon design, cross-system multi-agent collaborative design, case study analysis and critical thinking, and project practice challenges. The project practice component includes hands-on training with low-carbon engineering design tools, group design of a "zero-carbon community," and roadshow evaluations. The course nurtures students' sustainability mindset, critical thinking, and interdisciplinary problem-solving and innovation capabilities.
