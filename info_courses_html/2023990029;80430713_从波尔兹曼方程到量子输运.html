课程简介：
非平衡态统计物理是包括凝聚态物理、原子分子物理、高能核物理在内的多个物理学科共同关注的前沿课题。本课程涵盖基于经典背景的波尔兹曼方程以及对应的量子输运过程，并在二者之间建立关联，旨在建立非平衡物理体系的基本图像、并掌握相应理论工具。 课程内容包括： 第一章（1-2周）分子运动学：引言；几率、统计分布；两体碰撞。 第二章（3-5周）玻尔兹曼方程：刘维尔方程；推导玻尔兹曼方程；微观分布函数与宏观热力学量的关系；流体方程；H原理与平衡态解。 第三章（6-9周）求解玻尔兹曼方程的(近似)方法：线性化玻尔兹曼方程；chapmann enskog、grad矩量展开方法；test particle方法；输运系数（导热系数、粘滞系数）的计算。 第四章（10-14周）量子输运：有限温度的量子场体系、量子分布函数；从量子输运方程到玻尔兹曼方程；线性响应与输运系数的量子形式；如何在量子场态实时演化中计算上述物理量。 第五章(15周)在相对论重离子碰撞中应用。
英文课程简介：
Non-equilibrium statistical physicsis a frontier topic of common interest across multiple disciplines, including condensed matter physics, atomic and molecular physics, and high-energy nuclear physics. This course covers the Boltzmann equation based on a classical background and corresponding quantum transport processes, establishing connections between the two. It aims to build a fundamental understanding of non-equilibrium physical systems and equip students with relevant theoretical tools. Course Content Includes: Chapter 1 (Weeks 1-2), Molecular Kinetics: Introduction; Probability and Statistical Distributions; Two-Body Collisions. Chapter 2 (Weeks 3-5), The Boltzmann Equation: The Liouville Equation; Derivation of the Boltzmann Equation; Relation between Microscopic Distribution Functions and Macroscopic Thermodynamic Quantities; Fluid Equations; H-Theorem and Equilibrium Solutions. Chapter 3 (Weeks 6-9), (Approximate) Methods for Solving the Boltzmann Equation: Linearized Boltzmann Equation; Chapman-Enskog and Grad Moment Methods; Test Particle Method; Calculation of Transport Coefficients (Thermal Conductivity, Viscosity). Chapter 4 (Weeks 10-14), Quantum Transport: Quantum Field Systems at Finite Temperature; Quantum Distribution Functions; From Quantum Transport Equations to the Boltzmann Equation; Linear Response and Quantum Formulations of Transport Coefficients; Calculating these Quantities in Real-Time Evolution of Quantum Field States. Chapter 5 (Week 15): Application in Relativistic Heavy-Ion Collisions
