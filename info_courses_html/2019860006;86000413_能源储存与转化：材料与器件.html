课程简介：
“能源储存与转化：材料与器件”课程主要面向一年级/二年级研究生(硕士、博士），全英文授课，旨在介绍能源储存与转化的基础知识和研究进展。具体将涵盖以下四大类内容：（1）能源储存与转化技术介绍；（2）新型能源的开发与利用；（3）能源储存与转化技术、器件与材料的研究进展；（4）用于研究能源储存与转化技术的科学方法。课程3学分，共计48学时，每周3学时。通过该课程，学生将对能源储存与转化的材料、器件有基本的认知，并初步理解其中涉及的化学、电化学过程；结合所学知识，具备完成文献调研和报告的能力。 课程考核评价将基于以下四个方面：（1）出席率（占成绩15%）；（2）课堂讨论（占成绩15%）；（3）作业（占成绩20%）；（4）最终课堂展示报告（占成绩50%）。
英文课程简介：
This course is designed for graduate students (MS and PhD) who have interests in technology and research about materials and devices for energy storage and conversion. In the classroom lecture sessions, lectures on basics and state-of-the-art of materials and devices for energy storage and conversion will be delivered. This course will cover four areas on energy storage and conversion: (1) Intorduction to typical energy storage and conversion systems, (2) advanced power sources, (3) advanced energy storage and conversion techniques, and (4) development of characterization techniques. Materials and devices applied in energy storage and conversion are the focus of this course. Open discussion will be encouraged during this course. This is a 3-credit course with 48 teaching hours (3 hours per week). Upon completion of this course, students should be able to comprehend and summarize the primary categories of energy storage and conversion devices, integrate knowledge about energy storage and conversion, and effectively present information about research project and proposal, etc. The grading of the course is based on four parts: Participation (15%), Assignments (20%), Discussion During Class (15%), and Seminar Presentation (50% = 25% students + 10% TA + 15% Instructor).
