课程简介：
本课程的主要任务，一是讲解中国特色社会主义的基本属性与任务，讲明社会主义理论与实践的内涵及其中国体现；二是讲解中国的基本国情、现代化历史、发展经验、当代问题与改革方向；三是就中国特色社会主义的经济建设、政治建设、社会建设、文化建设等专题展开讲解；四是贯穿中国共产党的建设及领导地位论证议题于教学过程当中；五是讲述中国发展的改革动力、中国方案与改革的总体设计。 除此之外，在科学社会主义的理论与实践部分，本课程在新开课课程体系建设及教学改革中，还将根据清华大学研究生的具体情况，适当地在课程中加大了对社会主义理念和基本逻辑的学理解释以及对各个主要国家社会主义运动和具体历史过程的动态解读与客观分析，融入了清华校史、党史、革命史的内容，使学生能够同时在更深的学理层面和更易引发情感共鸣的认识层次，对马克思主义理论和科学社会主义理论以及中国特色的社会主义建设形成更深刻的理解，产生更自觉的认同。
英文课程简介：
Specifically, the main tasks of this course are to explain the basic attributes and tasks of socialism with Chinese characteristics, and explain the connotation of socialist theory and practice and its Chinese embodiment; the second is to explain China's basic national conditions, modernization history, and development experience , contemporary issues and reform directions; third, to explain the economic construction, political construction, social construction, cultural construction and other topics of socialism with Chinese characteristics; fourth, to run through the construction of the Communist Party of China and leadership demonstration issues in the teaching process; fifth, describes the reform momentum of China's development, the overall design of China's plan and reform. In addition, in the theoretical and practical part of scientific socialism, in the construction of the new curriculum system and teaching reforms, this course will also appropriately increase the concept of socialism in the curriculum according to the specific conditions of the graduate students of Tsinghua University. The theoretical explanation of basic logic and the dynamic interpretation and objective analysis of each major country’s socialist movements and specific historical processes are integrated into the history of Tsinghua, the history of party and revolution, so that at the same time students can be at a deeper academic level and more easily to understand, which could arouse emotional resonance with a deeper understanding of Marxist theories, scientific socialism theories, and the construction of socialism with Chinese characteristics, and develop a more conscious identity.
