课程内容简介：
讲授系统设计与管理的原理、方法和工具，将理论和实践教学相结合。培养学生面向复杂工业系统的系统思维，从“系统观、全局观”出发，理解现代工业系统的跨学科属性和全生命周期，在系统设计开发过程中平衡创造性、敏捷性、可靠性和成本等多维度需求；使学生们掌握基于模型的系统工程方法，包括系统建模语言、运筹与统计模型、图形化模型、仿真模型在现代工业系统的需求分析、设计、决策、实施、验证、评价等方面的应用；培养学生跨学科开发的沟通、合作和创新能力，模拟现代工业系统开发过程中的跨学科环境和挑战，强调跨学科数字化模型与系统化模型的衔接，强调跨学科创新能力的培养。
Course Description
The course content of "System Design and Management" comprehensively covers the whole life cycle development process of system such as demand analysis, conceptual design, structure design, prototype manufacturing, integration testing and operation and maintenance, and provides advanced development and manufacturing tool support during the R&D and manufacturing process. The course relies on the long-term cooperation between the Department of Industrial Engineering and China’s high-end equipment manufacturing industry (including aviation, aerospace, high-speed rail, shipbuilding, etc.), and refines the forward-looking common problems of these industries in system design and development, and integrates them into the lectures, speeches, seminars, visiting and practicing. Students need to solve a simplified version of the innovation challenge task based on the challenging problems in the above-mentioned industries through forming an interdisciplinary team. Students of all majors will go through the key processes of industrial product design such as mission analysis, demand analysis, conceptual design, prototype trial production, integration testing, and service design in operation.
