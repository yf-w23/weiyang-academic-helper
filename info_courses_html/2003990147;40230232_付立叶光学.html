课程内容简介：
本课程主要讲述傅里叶分析在光学领域的理论与应用，主要涉及衍射理论、透镜成像、光学信息处理以及全息术方面的应用。通过本课程的学习要求能基本掌握傅里叶光学的基本理论及其分析计算方法，了解傅里叶光学在衍射元件、微纳光学、光计算等领域创新应用。课程内容主要包括傅里叶变换和卷积，光的衍射理论基础，透镜的傅里叶变换和成像性质，全息术的基本原理，全息干涉计量，空间滤波与光信息处理，光学成像系统的频谱分析，并介绍一些前沿应用实例，包含1次全息实验。傅里叶光学是“用光做傅里叶变换”的天然系统，为信号处理提供了高速、并行的物理实现方案。课程包含1次全息实验。学习傅里叶光学为现代前沿信息光电子学提供了核心的频域视角与分析范式，是实现光学神经网络、超分辨率成像及高速全光信息处理等颠覆性技术的理论钥匙。
Course Description
Fourier Optics primarily covers the theory and applications of Fourier analysis in the field of optics, focusing on diffraction theory, lens imaging, optical information processing, and holography. Through this course, students are expected to gain a fundamental understanding of the basic theory of Fourier optics and its analytical and computational methods, as well as explore innovative applications of Fourier optics in diffraction elements, micro-nano optics, optical computing, and related fields. The course content includes Fourier transforms and convolution, the theoretical foundation of light diffraction, Fourier transforms and imaging properties of lenses, the basic principles of holography, holographic interferometry, spatial filtering and optical information processing, spectral analysis of optical imaging systems, and introduces some cutting-edge application examples, including one holographic experiment. Fourier optics is a natural system for performing Fourier transforms with light, providing a high-speed, parallel physical implementation for signal processing. The course includes one holographic experiment. Studying Fourier optics offers a core frequency-domain perspective and analytical framework for modern cutting-edge optoelectronics, serving as the theoretical key to achieving disruptive technologies such as optical neural networks, super-resolution imaging, and high-speed all-optical information processing.
