课程内容简介：
本课程主要让学生了解3D打印技术的原理及发展趋势，并尝试运用该技术进行产品创新性设计与制造。3D打印是一种通过材料逐层添加制造三维物体的变革性、数字化增材制造技术，它将信息、材料、生物等技术融合渗透，将对未来制造业生产模式与人类生活方式产生重要影响。通过本课程的学习，学生可以对3D打印技术的原理、工艺、特点及应用领域有全面系统的了解，并能够运用该技术操作相关3D打印设备，进行团队协作式的创新型设计和制作，培养学生的团队精神和创新思维。通过对3D打印技术的应用领域进行主题调研，深入探究新技术对未来社会所产生的深远影响（技术革新、知识产权保护、环保污染等伦理思考），体验跨学科学习魅力，提高自主学习能力和思辨能力。
Course Description
This course is mainly to understand the principle and development of 3D printing technology, and try to use the technology for innovative design and manufacturing of products, and trigger their ethical thinking at the same time. 3D printing is a transformative and digital additive manufacturing technology that makes 3D objects by adding materials layer by layer. It integrates information, materials, biology, control and other technologies, and will have an important impact on the future manufacturing production mode and human life style. Through the study of this course, students can understand the basic principles, characteristics and application of advanced 3D printing technology, feel the profound technology and social impact, and can use this technology to operate 3D printers. At the same time, carry out team collaborative innovative design and manufacturing, cultivate students' team spirit and innovative thinking. Moreover, carry out such technology as intellectual property protection, environmental pollution and a series of ethical thinking.
