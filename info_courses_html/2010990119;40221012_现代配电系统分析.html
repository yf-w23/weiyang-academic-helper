课程内容简介：
传统配电系统是电网的末端，完成电能从输电网到终端用户的配送。配电系统一般以开环结构运行，配电系统的运行控制相对简单。而随着分布式电源的广泛接入，改变了配电网的潮流流向和运行控制方式，对配电系统的运行分析提出了新的要求。另外，随着智能电网的发展，配电系统中安装了大量的测量装置、智能表计和控制设备，使得配电网的可观性和自动化水平得到了显著提升。需求侧响应、电动汽车接入和储能系统的应用，丰富和拓展了配电系统分析的内容。本课程紧密结合配电系统的发展，主要涵盖以下内容： 1. 配电系统的结构、运行方式和特点分析； 2. 配电系统建模，包括基本的元件和分布式电源模型； 3. 配电网潮流计算方法； 4. 配电系统故障分析方法； 5. 配电系统可靠性评估方法； 6. 配电系统状态估计； 7. 配电系统电压控制与无功优化； 8. 主动配电网与智能电网。 以上每部分内容均专门讲授分布式电源接入配电网后的计算、分析和评估方法。在配电系统潮流计算、故障分析和可靠性评估等内容的基础上，讲授现代配电网（智能配电网）的状态估计和运行优化方法。
Course Description
Traditionally, a distribution system is responsible for delivering electricity from the transmission system to the consumers. It is usually operated with an open-loop topology, which is relatively simple for operational control and protection. With the development of distributed generations, especially the solar and wind power generations, the power flow direction and control mode of distribution systems are changing. This new situation brings challenges for the operation and analysis of the distribution systems. Furthermore, with the advancement of smart grid, the distribution systems are equipped with more and more measuring instruments, smart meters and control devices, which promotes the observability and automation levels of distribution systems. The contents of distribution system analysis have been expanded and enriched with the progress of demand side response, electric vehicle integration and the applications of energy storage systems. This course is designed to cover the evolution of distribution systems. The contents mainly include: 1, Topology, operating mode and characteristics of distribution systems, 2, Modeling of distribution systems, including the model of basic elements and distributed generators, 3, Power flow calculation for distribution systems, 4, Fault analysis of distribution systems 5, Reliability evaluation methods for distribution systems 6, State estimation of distribution systems 7, Voltage control and reactive power optimization of distribution systems 8, Active distribution system and smart grid.
