课程内容简介：
自动化系结合学科自身的发展特点，依托本系在脑与认知、生物信息、人工智能等具有交叉 特点的科研方向的积累，现设立“交叉方向”课程。该课程通过上述几个方向在国际和国内 有影响的赛事吸引学生，使学生从低年级开始关注并参与该课程，尽早进入交叉方向的研 究。该课程作为培养创新能力的环节，意在：鼓励交叉方向的发展，为教师提供更多的支 持；为学生开阔眼界，寻找感兴趣的方向提供更多选择；引导学生感受科研精神，体验科研 过程，进行科研训练。作为交叉项目综合训练方向之一，脑科学与人工智能是脑与认知科 学、信息学、人工智能等学科高度交叉的新型领域，强调理学思维融合工科实践，将生物机 理、数学建模、算法设计与实现、系统应用等有机结合，实现多种智能任务的执行和优化， 解决一系列实际问题。本课程的教学内容包括脑启发的感知模型、认知与决策模型、面向脑 科学研究的人工智能算法与模型等基础知识，以及脑启发的神经网络基本单元、突触学习、 图像智能处理、神经拟态数据分析等试验环节。通过本课程的学习，学生将对脑科学与人工 智能的基本知识和应用有初步了解，培养从事交叉学科研究的兴趣与技能。
Course Description
Based on the characteristics of Department of Automation, and its advantage on brain and cognitive sciences, bioinformatics, artificial intelligence and other interdisciplines, we set up a new interdisciplinary course. This course aims to provide an opportunity for the undergraduates to participate in interdisciplinary research, and improve their vision and creativity. As a new interdisciplinary course, Brain Science and Artificial Intelligence covers several fields, such as brain and cognitive sciences, informatics, and artificial intelligence. It requires the combination of scientific thinking and engineering practice, by introducing how to use biological mechanisms, modeling, algorithm design and implementation, system development and practice to perform and optimize several intelligent tasks in real-world scenarios. This course will introduce brain inspired perception, cognition, and decision models, and AI algorithms and models empowering research on brain science. In particular, the topics include brain inspired basic units of neural networks, learning of synapses, image processing, neuro-morphic data analysis, and other experiments. By participating in this course, the students will learn the basic knowledge about brain science and artificial intelligence, and skills for interdisciplinary research.
